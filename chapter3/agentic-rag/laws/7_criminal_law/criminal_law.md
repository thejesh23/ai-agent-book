## Part One General Provisions

### Chapter I Tasks, Basic Principles, and Scope of Application of the Criminal Law

Article 1 This Law is enacted in accordance with the Constitution and in light of China's concrete experience and actual circumstances in fighting crime, for the purpose of punishing crime and protecting the people.

Article 2 The task of the Criminal Law of the People's Republic of China is to use criminal penalties to combat all criminal acts in order to safeguard national security, protect the people's democratic dictatorship and the socialist system, protect state property and property collectively owned by the working masses, protect citizens' privately owned property, protect citizens' personal rights, democratic rights, and other rights, maintain social and economic order, and ensure the smooth progress of socialist construction.

Article 3 For acts that are expressly defined as criminal acts in law, the offenders shall be convicted and punished in accordance with law; for acts that are not expressly defined as criminal acts in law, the offenders shall not be convicted or punished.

Article 4 The law shall be equally applied to anyone who commits a crime. No one is permitted to have privileges beyond the law.

Article 5 The severity of punishments shall be commensurate with the crime committed and the criminal responsibility borne by the offender.

Article 6 This Law shall apply to anyone who commits a crime within the territory of the People's Republic of China, except as otherwise specifically provided by law.

This Law shall also apply to anyone who commits a crime on board a ship or aircraft of the People's Republic of China.

If a criminal act or its consequence occurs within the territory of the People's Republic of China, the crime shall be deemed to have been committed within the territory of the People's Republic of China.

Article 7 This Law shall apply to any citizen of the People's Republic of China who commits a crime prescribed by this Law outside the territory of the People's Republic of China; however, if the maximum punishment prescribed by this Law is fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, the matter may be disregarded.

This Law shall apply to any state functionary or member of the armed forces of the People's Republic of China who commits a crime prescribed by this Law outside the territory of the People's Republic of China.

Article 8 This Law may be applied to any foreigner who commits a crime outside the territory of the People's Republic of China against the state or a citizen of the People's Republic of China, if the minimum punishment prescribed by this Law for that crime is fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years; however, this shall not apply if the act is not punishable according to the laws of the place where it was committed.

Article 9 This Law shall apply to crimes stipulated in international treaties to which the People's Republic of China is a party or has acceded, within the scope of treaty obligations that the People's Republic of China assumes, where the People's Republic of China exercises criminal jurisdiction over such crimes.

Article 10 Anyone who commits a crime outside the territory of the People's Republic of China and bears criminal responsibility according to this Law may still be investigated for criminal responsibility according to this Law, even if they have been tried in a foreign country. However, if they have already received criminal punishment in the foreign country, the punishment may be mitigated or remitted.

Article 11 The criminal responsibility of foreigners who enjoy diplomatic privileges and immunities shall be resolved through diplomatic channels.

Article 12 For acts committed after the founding of the People's Republic of China and before the entry into force of this Law, if the law in force at the time did not deem them crimes, the law in force at the time shall apply. If the law in force at the time deemed them crimes, criminal responsibility shall be investigated according to the law in force at the time, provided that the matter is subject to prosecution under the provisions of Section 8, Chapter IV of the General Provisions of this Law. However, if this Law does not deem them crimes or imposes a lighter punishment, this Law shall apply.

Judgments that have become legally effective before the entry into force of this Law, rendered according to the law in force at the time, shall remain valid.

### Chapter II Crime

#### Section 1 Crimes and Criminal Responsibility

Article 13 All acts that endanger national sovereignty, territorial integrity, and security; split the state; subvert the people's democratic dictatorship and overthrow the socialist system; disrupt social and economic order; infringe upon state property or property collectively owned by the working masses; infringe upon citizens' privately owned property; infringe upon citizens' personal rights, democratic rights, and other rights; and other acts that endanger society, which are punishable by criminal penalties according to law, are crimes. However, if the circumstances are clearly minor and the harm is not great, the act shall not be deemed a crime.

Article 14 An intentional crime is one where the actor clearly knows that their act will cause a harmful consequence to society and hopes for or allows that consequence to occur, thereby constituting a crime.

Intentional crimes shall bear criminal responsibility.

Article 15 A negligent crime is one where the actor should have foreseen that their act might cause a harmful consequence to society but failed to do so due to negligence, or foresaw it but believed it could be avoided, leading to that consequence.

Negligent crimes bear criminal responsibility only when the law so provides.

Article 16 An act that objectively causes a harmful consequence but is not due to intent or negligence, but rather due to irresistible or unforeseeable causes, is not a crime.

Article 17 A person who has reached the age of sixteen and commits a crime shall bear criminal responsibility.

A person who has reached the age of fourteen but not the age of sixteen and commits the crimes of intentional homicide, intentional injury causing serious injury or death, rape, robbery, drug trafficking, arson, explosion, or placing dangerous substances shall bear criminal responsibility.

A person who has reached the age of twelve but not the age of fourteen and commits the crimes of intentional homicide or intentional injury, causing death or causing serious injury resulting in severe disability through particularly cruel means, with abhorrent circumstances, and whose prosecution is approved by the Supreme People's Procuratorate, shall bear criminal responsibility.

A person under the age of eighteen who is investigated for criminal responsibility according to the provisions of the preceding three paragraphs shall be given a lighter or mitigated punishment.

If a person is not subject to criminal punishment because they have not reached the age of sixteen, their parents or other guardians shall be ordered to discipline them; when necessary, specialized correctional education may be carried out in accordance with law.

Article 17-1 A person who has reached the age of seventy-five and commits an intentional crime may be given a lighter or mitigated punishment; if they commit a negligent crime, they shall be given a lighter or mitigated punishment.

Article 18 If a mental patient causes a harmful consequence when they are unable to recognize or control their own conduct, and this is confirmed through legal procedures, they shall not bear criminal responsibility. However, their family members or guardians shall be ordered to keep them under strict surveillance and arrange for medical treatment; when necessary, the government may compel them to receive medical treatment.

An intermittent mental patient who commits a crime when in a normal mental state shall bear criminal responsibility.

A mental patient who has not completely lost the ability to recognize or control their own conduct and commits a crime shall bear criminal responsibility, but may be given a lighter or mitigated punishment.

A person who commits a crime while intoxicated shall bear criminal responsibility.

Article 19 A deaf-mute or blind person who commits a crime may be given a lighter, mitigated, or remitted punishment.

Article 20 An act that is taken to stop an ongoing unlawful infringement in order to protect the state, public interest, or the personal, property, or other rights of oneself or others, and that causes harm to the unlawful infringer, constitutes justifiable defense and shall not bear criminal responsibility.

If justifiable defense clearly exceeds the necessary limits and causes serious harm, criminal responsibility shall be borne, but the punishment shall be mitigated or remitted.

Taking defensive action against ongoing violent crimes such as assault, homicide, robbery, rape, kidnapping, and other violent crimes that seriously endanger personal safety, resulting in injury or death to the unlawful infringer, does not constitute excessive defense and shall not bear criminal responsibility.

Article 21 An act of emergency avoidance taken in an unavoidable manner to avert an ongoing danger to the state, public interest, or the personal, property, or other rights of oneself or others, and that causes harm, shall not bear criminal responsibility.

If emergency avoidance exceeds the necessary limits and causes undue harm, criminal responsibility shall be borne, but the punishment shall be mitigated or remitted.

The provisions of the first paragraph regarding avoiding personal danger shall not apply to persons who have a specific responsibility in their duties or profession.

#### Section 2 Preparation, Attempt, and Discontinuation of Crime

Article 22 Preparing tools or creating conditions for the purpose of committing a crime constitutes preparation for a crime.

For an offender who prepares for a crime, a lighter, mitigated, or remitted punishment may be imposed compared to that for a completed offense.

Article 23 An attempt to commit a crime occurs when the offender has already started to commit the crime but fails due to reasons independent of their will.

For an attempted offender, a lighter or mitigated punishment may be imposed compared to that for a completed offense.

Article 24 Discontinuation of a crime occurs when, during the process of committing a crime, the offender voluntarily abandons the crime or voluntarily and effectively prevents the criminal consequence from occurring.

For an offender who discontinues a crime, if no harm is caused, the punishment shall be remitted; if harm is caused, the punishment shall be mitigated.

#### Section 3 Joint Crimes

Article 25 A joint crime refers to an intentional crime committed by two or more persons jointly.

A negligent crime committed by two or more persons jointly shall not be treated as a joint crime; those who should bear criminal responsibility shall be punished separately according to the crimes they have committed.

Article 26 A principal offender is one who organizes or leads a criminal group to carry out criminal activities or plays a major role in a joint crime.

A criminal group is a relatively stable criminal organization formed by three or more persons for the purpose of jointly committing crimes.

The ringleader who organizes or leads a criminal group shall be punished for all the crimes committed by the group.

For principal offenders other than those specified in the third paragraph, they shall be punished for all the crimes they participated in, organized, or directed.

Article 27 An accomplice is one who plays a secondary or auxiliary role in a joint crime.

An accomplice shall be given a lighter, mitigated, or remitted punishment.

Article 28 A person who is coerced into participating in a crime shall be given a mitigated or remitted punishment according to the circumstances of their crime.

Article 29 A person who instigates another to commit a crime shall be punished according to the role they play in the joint crime. A person who instigates a person under the age of eighteen to commit a crime shall be given a heavier punishment.

If the instigated person does not commit the instigated crime, the instigator may be given a lighter or mitigated punishment.

#### Section 4 Unit Crimes

Article 30 A company, enterprise, institution, state organ, or organization that commits an act endangering society, which is stipulated by law as a unit crime, shall bear criminal responsibility.

Article 31 Where a unit commits a crime, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly responsible persons shall be subject to criminal penalties. Where this Part and other laws provide otherwise, those provisions shall apply.

### Chapter III Punishments

#### Section 1 Types of Punishments

Article 32 Punishments are divided into principal punishments and supplementary punishments.

Article 33 The types of principal punishments are as follows:

(1) Public surveillance;
(2) Criminal detention;
(3) Fixed-term imprisonment;
(4) Life imprisonment;
(5) The death penalty.

Article 34 The types of supplementary punishments are as follows:

(1) Fines;
(2) Deprivation of political rights;
(3) Confiscation of property.

Supplementary punishments may also be applied independently.

Article 35 For foreigners who commit crimes, deportation may be applied independently or as a supplementary punishment.

Article 36 Where a victim suffers economic losses due to a criminal act, the criminal offender shall, in addition to receiving criminal punishment according to law, be sentenced to compensate for the economic losses based on the circumstances.

If a criminal offender who bears civil compensation liability is also sentenced to a fine, and their property is insufficient to pay both in full, or if they are sentenced to confiscation of property, they shall first bear civil compensation liability to the victim.

Article 37 For a person whose criminal circumstances are minor and who does not need to be sentenced to criminal punishment, criminal punishment may be remitted. However, depending on the circumstances of the case, they may be reprimanded or ordered to make a statement of repentance, apologize, compensate for losses, or be subject to administrative penalties or sanctions by the competent authorities.

Article 37-1 Where a person is sentenced to criminal punishment for committing a crime by taking advantage of their professional convenience, or for committing a crime that violates specific duties required by their profession, the people's court may, based on the circumstances of the crime and the need to prevent recidivism, prohibit them from engaging in the relevant profession for a period of three to five years starting from the date of completion of the criminal punishment or the date of parole.

If a person prohibited from engaging in a relevant profession violates the decision made by the people's court according to the provisions of the preceding paragraph, the public security organ shall impose punishment according to law; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be convicted and punished according to the provisions of Article 313 of this Law.

If other laws or administrative regulations have other prohibitive or restrictive provisions regarding their engagement in the relevant profession, those provisions shall apply.

#### Section 2 Public Surveillance

Article 38 The term of public surveillance shall be not less than three months but not more than two years.

When sentencing a person to public surveillance, the court may, based on the circumstances of the crime, simultaneously prohibit the offender from engaging in specific activities, entering specific areas or venues, or contacting specific persons during the execution period.

For criminal offenders sentenced to public surveillance, community correction shall be implemented according to law.

If a person violates the prohibition order specified in the second paragraph, the public security organ shall impose punishment according to the provisions of the Public Security Administration Punishments Law of the People's Republic of China.

Article 39 A criminal offender sentenced to public surveillance shall, during the execution period, comply with the following provisions:

(1) Observe laws and administrative regulations, and submit to supervision;
(2) Without the approval of the executing authority, shall not exercise the rights to freedom of speech, press, assembly, association, procession, or demonstration;
(3) Report their own activities as required by the executing authority;
(4) Comply with the regulations of the executing authority regarding receiving visitors;
(5) Report to the executing authority for approval before leaving the city or county where they reside or moving residence.

For criminal offenders sentenced to public surveillance, equal pay for equal work shall be applied in labor.

Article 40 When the term of public surveillance expires, the executing authority shall immediately announce the termination of public surveillance to the offender themselves, their work unit, or the residents of their place of residence.

Article 41 The term of public surveillance shall be calculated from the date the judgment begins execution; if the offender is detained before the judgment is executed, one day of detention shall be offset against two days of the term.

#### Section 3 Criminal Detention

Article 42 The term of criminal detention shall be not less than one month but not more than six months.

Article 43 A criminal offender sentenced to criminal detention shall be executed by the public security organ in the vicinity.

During the execution period, a criminal offender sentenced to criminal detention may return home for one to two days each month; if they participate in labor, they may be given appropriate remuneration.

Article 44 The term of criminal detention shall be calculated from the date the judgment begins execution; if the offender is detained before the judgment is executed, one day of detention shall be offset against one day of the term.

#### Section 4 Fixed-term Imprisonment and Life Imprisonment

Article 45 The term of fixed-term imprisonment shall be not less than six months but not more than fifteen years, except as provided in Articles 50 and 69 of this Law.

Article 46 A criminal offender sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment or life imprisonment shall be executed in a prison or other execution venue; anyone with the ability to work shall participate in labor, receive education, and undergo reform.

Article 47 The term of fixed-term imprisonment shall be calculated from the date the judgment begins execution; if the offender is detained before the judgment is executed, one day of detention shall be offset against one day of the term.

#### Section 5 The Death Penalty

Article 48 The death penalty shall only be applied to criminal offenders who have committed the most heinous crimes. For a criminal offender who should be sentenced to the death penalty, if immediate execution is not necessary, a sentence of death with a two-year reprieve may be pronounced at the same time.

Except for judgments of the death penalty rendered by the Supreme People's Court according to law, all death penalty judgments shall be submitted to the Supreme People's Court for approval. A sentence of death with a reprieve may be rendered or approved by a Higher People's Court.

Article 49 The death penalty shall not be applied to a person who has not reached the age of eighteen at the time of the crime or to a woman who is pregnant at the time of trial.

The death penalty shall not be applied to a person who has reached the age of seventy-five at the time of trial, except where death is caused by particularly cruel means.

Article 50 For a person sentenced to death with a reprieve, if no intentional crime is committed during the period of the reprieve, the sentence shall be commuted to life imprisonment upon the expiration of the two-year period; if there is a major meritorious performance, the sentence shall be commuted to twenty-five years of fixed-term imprisonment upon the expiration of the two-year period; if an intentional crime is committed with abhorrent circumstances, the death penalty shall be executed upon approval by the Supreme People's Court; for an intentional crime for which the death penalty is not executed, the period of the death sentence with a reprieve shall be recalculated and reported to the Supreme People's Court for record.

For a recidivist sentenced to death with a reprieve, or for a criminal offender sentenced to death with a reprieve for intentional homicide, rape, robbery, kidnapping, arson, explosion, placing dangerous substances, or organized violent crimes, the people's court may, based on the circumstances of the crime and other factors, simultaneously decide to restrict the commutation of their sentence.

Article 51 The period of a death sentence with a reprieve shall be calculated from the date the judgment is confirmed. The term of fixed-term imprisonment commuted from a death sentence with a reprieve shall be calculated from the date the period of the reprieve expires.

#### Section 6 Fines

Article 52 When imposing a fine, the amount of the fine shall be determined based on the circumstances of the crime.

Article 53 A fine shall be paid in a lump sum or in installments within the period specified in the judgment. If the fine is not paid upon expiration, payment shall be compelled. If the full amount of the fine cannot be paid, the people's court shall recover the property at any time when it discovers that the person subject to execution has property that can be executed.If payment is genuinely difficult due to irresistible disasters or other reasons, the payment may be deferred, reduced, or exempted upon a ruling by the People's Court.

#### Section 7: Deprivation of Political Rights

**Article 54** Deprivation of political rights means depriving the following rights:

(1) The right to vote and to stand for election;
(2) The rights to freedom of speech, press, assembly, association, procession, and demonstration;
(3) The right to hold a position in a state organ;
(4) The right to hold a leading position in a state-owned company, enterprise, institution, or people's organization.

**Article 55** The term of deprivation of political rights shall be not less than one year and not more than five years, except as provided in Article 57 of this Law.

Where deprivation of political rights is imposed as a supplementary punishment for public surveillance, the term of deprivation of political rights shall be the same as the term of public surveillance, and the punishments shall be executed concurrently.

**Article 56** Criminals who endanger national security shall be sentenced to deprivation of political rights as a supplementary punishment. Criminals who commit intentional homicide, rape, arson, explosion, poisoning, robbery, or other crimes seriously disrupting social order may be sentenced to deprivation of political rights as a supplementary punishment.

Where deprivation of political rights is imposed independently, it shall be applied in accordance with the provisions of the Specific Provisions of this Law.

**Article 57** Criminals sentenced to death or life imprisonment shall be deprived of political rights for life.

When a death sentence with a reprieve is commuted to fixed-term imprisonment, or a life sentence is commuted to fixed-term imprisonment, the term of the supplementary punishment of deprivation of political rights shall be changed to not less than three years and not more than ten years.

**Article 58** The term of the supplementary punishment of deprivation of political rights shall be calculated from the date of completion of the principal punishment (fixed-term imprisonment or criminal detention) or from the date of parole. The effect of deprivation of political rights shall naturally apply during the period of execution of the principal punishment.

A criminal deprived of political rights shall, during the period of execution, abide by laws, administrative regulations, and the regulations of the public security department of the State Council concerning supervision and control, submit to supervision, and shall not exercise any of the rights specified in Article 54 of this Law.

#### Section 8: Confiscation of Property

**Article 59** Confiscation of property means confiscating part or all of the property personally owned by a criminal. Where all of the property is confiscated, necessary living expenses shall be reserved for the criminal and his dependent family members.

When imposing confiscation of property, property belonging to or due to the family members of the criminal shall not be confiscated.

**Article 60** Where legitimate debts incurred by the criminal before the confiscation of property need to be repaid with the confiscated property, they shall be repaid upon the request of the creditor.

### Chapter IV: Specific Application of Punishments

#### Section 1: Sentencing

**Article 61** When deciding a punishment for a criminal, the sentence shall be imposed in accordance with the relevant provisions of this Law, based on the facts, nature, and circumstances of the crime, and the degree of harm to society.

**Article 62** Where a criminal has circumstances specified in this Law for a heavier or lighter punishment, the sentence shall be imposed within the limits of the legally prescribed punishment.

**Article 63** Where a criminal has circumstances specified in this Law for a mitigated punishment, the sentence shall be imposed below the legally prescribed punishment. Where this Law provides for several sentencing ranges, the sentence shall be imposed within the next lower sentencing range.

Even if a criminal does not have the circumstances for a mitigated punishment specified in this Law, a sentence below the legally prescribed punishment may still be imposed, based on the special circumstances of the case, subject to the approval of the Supreme People's Court.

**Article 64** All property illegally obtained by a criminal shall be recovered or ordered to be returned or compensated. The lawful property of the victim shall be promptly returned. Contraband and property of the criminal used for the crime shall be confiscated. Confiscated property and fines shall be turned over to the state treasury and shall not be misappropriated or disposed of without authorization.

#### Section 2: Recidivism

**Article 65** A criminal who has been sentenced to a punishment of fixed-term imprisonment or heavier and, within five years after the completion of the punishment or after receiving a pardon, commits another crime punishable by fixed-term imprisonment or heavier, is a recidivist and shall be given a heavier punishment. However, this does not apply to negligent crimes or crimes committed by persons under the age of eighteen.

The period specified in the preceding paragraph shall be calculated from the date of completion of parole for criminals who have been paroled.

**Article 66** Criminals who commit crimes of endangering national security, terrorist activities, or organized crimes of a mafia-like nature shall be deemed recidivists if they commit any of the aforementioned categories of crimes again at any time after the completion of the punishment or after receiving a pardon.

#### Section 3: Voluntary Surrender and Meritorious Service

**Article 67** Voluntary surrender after committing a crime and truthfully confessing one's crime constitutes voluntary surrender. Criminals who voluntarily surrender may be given a lighter or mitigated punishment. Where the crime is relatively minor, the punishment may be exempted.

Criminal suspects, defendants, and convicted criminals serving sentences who are subject to compulsory measures and truthfully confess other crimes of their own that are not yet known to the judicial authorities shall be deemed to have voluntarily surrendered.

Even if a criminal suspect does not have the circumstances of voluntary surrender specified in the preceding two paragraphs, he may be given a lighter punishment if he truthfully confesses his crime. If his truthful confession avoids particularly serious consequences, the punishment may be mitigated.

**Article 68** Criminals who expose the criminal conduct of others, which is verified, or provide important clues that lead to the solving of other cases, thereby performing meritorious service, may be given a lighter or mitigated punishment. Those who perform major meritorious service may be given a mitigated punishment or be exempted from punishment.

#### Section 4: Combined Punishment for Multiple Crimes

**Article 69** Where a person commits multiple crimes before a judgment is pronounced, except where the crimes are punishable by death or life imprisonment, the sentence to be executed shall be decided, based on the circumstances, at a level below the total term of the sentences for all crimes but above the highest term among the individual sentences. However, the maximum term of public surveillance shall not exceed three years, the maximum term of criminal detention shall not exceed one year, and the maximum term of fixed-term imprisonment shall not exceed twenty years if the total term is less than thirty-five years, or twenty-five years if the total term is thirty-five years or more.

Where multiple crimes include sentences of fixed-term imprisonment and criminal detention, the fixed-term imprisonment shall be executed. Where multiple crimes include sentences of fixed-term imprisonment and public surveillance, or criminal detention and public surveillance, the public surveillance shall still be executed after the fixed-term imprisonment or criminal detention is completed.

Where multiple crimes include supplementary punishments, the supplementary punishments shall still be executed. If the supplementary punishments are of the same type, they shall be executed concurrently; if they are of different types, they shall be executed separately.

**Article 70** Where, after a judgment is pronounced but before the punishment is completed, it is discovered that the convicted criminal committed other crimes before the judgment was pronounced that were not adjudicated, a judgment shall be made for the newly discovered crime, and the sentence to be executed shall be decided in accordance with the provisions of Article 69 of this Law, based on the punishments imposed by the two judgments. The term already served shall be deducted from the term decided by the new judgment.

**Article 71** Where, after a judgment is pronounced but before the punishment is completed, the convicted criminal commits another crime, a judgment shall be made for the new crime, and the sentence to be executed shall be decided in accordance with the provisions of Article 69 of this Law, based on the unexecuted portion of the punishment for the previous crime and the punishment imposed for the new crime.

#### Section 5: Probation

**Article 72** For a criminal sentenced to criminal detention or fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, if the following conditions are met, probation may be declared. For those under the age of eighteen, pregnant women, or persons who have reached the age of seventy-five, probation shall be declared:

(1) The circumstances of the crime are relatively minor;
(2) There is a demonstration of repentance;
(3) There is no danger of re-offending;
(4) Declaring probation will not have a significant adverse impact on the community where the criminal resides.

When declaring probation, the court may, based on the circumstances of the crime, simultaneously prohibit the criminal from engaging in specific activities, entering specific areas or places, or contacting specific persons during the probation period.

If a criminal declared probation is also sentenced to a supplementary punishment, the supplementary punishment shall still be executed.

**Article 73** The probation period for criminal detention shall be not less than the original term of the sentence and not more than one year, but shall not be less than two months.

The probation period for fixed-term imprisonment shall be not less than the original term of the sentence and not more than five years, but shall not be less than one year.

The probation period shall be calculated from the date the judgment is finalized.

**Article 74** Probation shall not apply to recidivists or ringleaders of criminal groups.

**Article 75** A criminal declared probation shall comply with the following provisions:

(1) Abide by laws and administrative regulations and submit to supervision;
(2) Report his activities in accordance with the regulations of the supervisory authority;
(3) Comply with the regulations of the supervisory authority concerning meeting visitors;
(4) Obtain approval from the supervisory authority before leaving the city or county where he resides or moving to another place.

**Article 76** For a criminal declared probation, community correction shall be implemented during the probation period in accordance with the law. If there is no circumstance specified in Article 77 of this Law, the original punishment shall no longer be executed upon the expiration of the probation period, and this shall be publicly announced.

**Article 77** If a criminal declared probation commits a new crime during the probation period, or if it is discovered that he committed other crimes before the judgment was pronounced that were not adjudicated, the probation shall be revoked, a judgment shall be made for the new crime or the newly discovered crime, and the sentence to be executed shall be decided in accordance with the provisions of Article 69 of this Law, based on the punishments for the previous crime and the subsequent crime.

If a criminal declared probation, during the probation period, violates laws, administrative regulations, or the regulations of the relevant department of the State Council concerning the supervision and management of probation, or violates the prohibition order in the court judgment, and the circumstances are serious, the probation shall be revoked, and the original punishment shall be executed.

#### Section 6: Commutation of Sentence

**Article 78** For a criminal sentenced to public surveillance, criminal detention, fixed-term imprisonment, or life imprisonment, if, during the execution of the sentence, he earnestly observes prison regulations, accepts education and reform, and truly demonstrates repentance, or performs meritorious service, his sentence may be commuted. If he performs one of the following major meritorious acts, his sentence shall be commuted:

(1) Preventing others from committing major criminal activities;
(2) Reporting major criminal activities inside or outside the prison, which is verified;
(3) Making inventions or major technical innovations;
(4) Sacrificing himself to save others in daily production or life;
(5) Performing outstanding deeds in resisting natural disasters or eliminating major accidents;
(6) Making other major contributions to the state and society.

The actual term of the sentence to be served after commutation shall not be less than:

(1) For public surveillance, criminal detention, or fixed-term imprisonment, one-half of the original term of the sentence;
(2) For life imprisonment, thirteen years;
(3) For a criminal sentenced to death with a reprieve whose commutation is restricted by the People's Court in accordance with the provisions of Article 50, paragraph 2, of this Law, if the sentence is commuted to life imprisonment after the expiration of the reprieve period, the actual term shall not be less than twenty-five years; if commuted to twenty-five years of fixed-term imprisonment, the actual term shall not be less than twenty years.

**Article 79** For the commutation of a criminal's sentence, the executing authority shall submit a commutation proposal to an intermediate People's Court or higher. The People's Court shall form a collegial panel to conduct a hearing and, if it finds that the criminal truly demonstrates repentance or has performed meritorious service, shall issue a ruling to commute the sentence. No commutation shall be granted without going through the statutory procedures.

**Article 80** The term of a life sentence commuted to fixed-term imprisonment shall be calculated from the date of the ruling on commutation.

#### Section 7: Parole

**Article 81** A criminal sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment who has served more than one-half of the original term of the sentence, or a criminal sentenced to life imprisonment who has actually served more than thirteen years, may be granted parole if he earnestly observes prison regulations, accepts education and reform, truly demonstrates repentance, and has no danger of re-offending. Under special circumstances, subject to the approval of the Supreme People's Court, the above restrictions on the term served may be disregarded.

Parole shall not be granted to recidivists or to criminals sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment for intentional homicide, rape, robbery, kidnapping, arson, explosion, spreading dangerous substances, or organized violent crimes.

When deciding on parole for a criminal, the impact of the parole on the community where the criminal will reside shall be considered.

**Article 82** Parole for criminals shall be carried out in accordance with the procedures specified in Article 79 of this Law. No parole shall be granted without going through the statutory procedures.

**Article 83** The parole period for fixed-term imprisonment is the unserved portion of the sentence. The parole period for life imprisonment is ten years.

The parole period shall be calculated from the date of parole.

**Article 84** A criminal declared parole shall comply with the following provisions:

(1) Abide by laws and administrative regulations and submit to supervision;
(2) Report his activities in accordance with the regulations of the supervisory authority;
(3) Comply with the regulations of the supervisory authority concerning meeting visitors;
(4) Obtain approval from the supervisory authority before leaving the city or county where he resides or moving to another place.

**Article 85** For a paroled criminal, community correction shall be implemented during the parole period in accordance with the law. If there is no circumstance specified in Article 86 of this Law, upon the expiration of the parole period, the original sentence shall be deemed to have been fully executed, and this shall be publicly announced.

**Article 86** If a paroled criminal commits a new crime during the parole period, the parole shall be revoked, and combined punishment for multiple crimes shall be imposed in accordance with the provisions of Article 71 of this Law.

If, during the parole period, it is discovered that the paroled criminal committed other crimes before the judgment was pronounced that were not adjudicated, the parole shall be revoked, and combined punishment for multiple crimes shall be imposed in accordance with the provisions of Article 70 of this Law.

If a paroled criminal, during the parole period, violates laws, administrative regulations, or the regulations of the relevant department of the State Council concerning the supervision and management of parole, but the conduct does not constitute a new crime, the parole shall be revoked in accordance with statutory procedures, and the criminal shall be taken back into custody to serve the unexecuted portion of the sentence.

#### Section 8: Statute of Limitations

**Article 87** Crimes shall no longer be prosecuted after the following periods:

(1) Five years, if the maximum legally prescribed punishment is fixed-term imprisonment of less than five years;
(2) Ten years, if the maximum legally prescribed punishment is fixed-term imprisonment of five years or more but less than ten years;
(3) Fifteen years, if the maximum legally prescribed punishment is fixed-term imprisonment of ten years or more;
(4) Twenty years, if the maximum legally prescribed punishment is life imprisonment or death. If prosecution is deemed necessary after twenty years, it must be submitted to the Supreme People's Procuratorate for approval.

**Article 88** If, after a case is filed for investigation by the People's Procuratorate, public security organ, or state security organ, or after a case is accepted by the People's Court, the criminal evades investigation or trial, the statute of limitations shall not apply.

If the victim files a complaint within the statute of limitations, and the People's Court, People's Procuratorate, or public security organ should file a case but fails to do so, the statute of limitations shall not apply.

**Article 89** The statute of limitations shall be calculated from the date the crime is committed. If the criminal act is continuous or ongoing, it shall be calculated from the date the criminal act ends.

If another crime is committed within the statute of limitations, the statute of limitations for the previous crime shall be calculated from the date the subsequent crime is committed.

### Chapter V: Other Provisions

**Article 90** Where the provisions of this Law cannot be fully applied in ethnic autonomous areas, the people's congress of the autonomous region or province may, based on the political, economic, and cultural characteristics of the local ethnic groups and the basic principles of this Law, formulate adaptive or supplementary provisions, which shall be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for approval before implementation.

**Article 91** For the purposes of this Law, "public property" refers to the following property:

(1) State-owned property;
(2) Property collectively owned by the working masses;
(3) Property from social donations or special funds used for poverty alleviation and other public welfare undertakings.

Private property under the management, use, or transport of state organs, state-owned companies, enterprises, collective enterprises, or people's organizations shall be deemed public property.

**Article 92** For the purposes of this Law, "privately owned property of citizens" refers to the following property:

(1) Citizens' lawful income, savings, houses, and other means of subsistence;
(2) Means of production lawfully owned by individuals or families;
(3) Lawful property of individual businesses and private enterprises;
(4) Shares, stocks, bonds, and other property lawfully owned by individuals.

**Article 93** For the purposes of this Law, "state functionaries" refers to persons who engage in public service in state organs.

Persons who engage in public service in state-owned companies, enterprises, institutions, or people's organizations, persons who are dispatched by state organs, state-owned companies, enterprises, or institutions to engage in public service in non-state-owned companies, enterprises, institutions, or social organizations, and other persons who engage in public service in accordance with the law, shall be deemed state functionaries.

**Article 94** For the purposes of this Law, "judicial officers" refers to persons with responsibilities for investigation, prosecution, adjudication, and supervision.

**Article 95** For the purposes of this Law, "serious injury" refers to any of the following injuries:

(1) Causing disability or disfigurement of a person's limbs;
(2) Causing loss of hearing, sight, or function of other organs;
(3) Other serious harm to a person's physical health.

**Article 96** For the purposes of this Law, "violation of state regulations" refers to violation of laws and decisions enacted by the National People's Congress and its Standing Committee, and administrative regulations, administrative measures, decisions, and orders enacted by the State Council.

**Article 97** For the purposes of this Law, "ringleader" refers to a criminal who plays the role of organizing, planning, or directing in a criminal group or a mass crime.

**Article 98** For the purposes of this Law, "handled only upon complaint" means that the case is handled only if the victim files a complaint. If the victim is unable to file a complaint due to coercion or intimidation, the People's Procuratorate or the victim's close relatives may also file the complaint.

**Article 99** For the purposes of this Law, the terms "not less than," "not more than," and "within" include the given number.

**Article 100** A person who has been criminally punished in accordance with the law shall, when joining the military or seeking employment, truthfully report to the relevant unit that he has been criminally punished and shall not conceal it.

A person who was under the age of eighteen at the time of the crime and was sentenced to a punishment of fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years is exempted from the reporting obligation specified in the preceding paragraph.

**Article 101** The General Provisions of this Law apply to other laws that provide for criminal punishments, unless those other laws have special provisions.

## Part Two: Specific Provisions

### Chapter I: Crimes of Endangering National Security

**Article 102** Whoever colludes with a foreign state to endanger the sovereignty, territorial integrity, or security of the People's Republic of China shall be sentenced to life imprisonment or fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years.

Whoever colludes with an overseas institution, organization, or individual to commit the crime in the preceding paragraph shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

**Article 103** Whoever organizes, plans, or implements the splitting of the state or undermining national unity shall, if he is a ringleader or if his crime is major, be sentenced to life imprisonment or fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years; if he is an active participant, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if he is an other participant, to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Whoever incites the splitting of the state or undermining national unity shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights; if he is a ringleader or if his crime is major, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

**Article 104** Whoever organizes, plans, or implements an armed rebellion or an armed riot shall, if he is a ringleader or if his crime is major, be sentenced to life imprisonment or fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years; if he is an active participant, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if he is an other participant, to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Whoever instigates, coerces, lures, or bribes state functionaries, members of the armed forces, people's police, or militiamen to engage in an armed rebellion or an armed riot shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.Article 105: Those who organize, plan, or carry out acts to subvert the state power or overthrow the socialist system shall, if they are ringleaders or whose crimes are grave, be sentenced to life imprisonment or fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years; those who actively participate shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 10 years; and other participants shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Those who incite subversion of the state power or overthrow of the socialist system by spreading rumors, libel, or other means shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 5 years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights; ringleaders or those whose crimes are grave shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 5 years.

Article 106: Those who collude with any foreign institution, organization, or individual to commit the crimes stipulated in Articles 103, 104, and 105 of this Chapter shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of those respective articles.

Article 107: Where any domestic or foreign institution, organization, or individual provides funding to commit the crimes stipulated in Articles 102, 103, 104, and 105 of this Chapter, the persons directly responsible shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 5 years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 5 years.

Article 108: Those who defect to the enemy and turn traitor shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 10 years; if the circumstances are serious, or if they lead armed personnel, members of the People's Police, or militiamen in defecting to the enemy and turning traitor, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years or life imprisonment.

Article 109: Any functionary of a state organ who, during the performance of official duties, abandons his post and defects abroad, or defects while abroad, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 5 years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 5 years but not more than 10 years.

Any state functionary who has access to state secrets and defects abroad or defects while abroad shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 110: Anyone who commits any of the following acts of espionage that endanger national security shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years or life imprisonment; if the circumstances are relatively minor, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 10 years:

(1) Joining an espionage organization or accepting a mission assigned by an espionage organization or its agent;

(2) Directing the enemy at bombing targets.

Article 111: Anyone who steals, spies into, buys, or illegally provides state secrets or intelligence for any foreign institution, organization, or individual shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 5 years but not more than 10 years; if the circumstances are especially serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years or life imprisonment; if the circumstances are relatively minor, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 5 years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Article 112: Anyone who supplies weapons, ammunition, or military materials to the enemy during wartime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years or life imprisonment; if the circumstances are relatively minor, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 10 years.

Article 113: In the crimes of endangering national security stipulated in this Chapter, except for those stipulated in the second paragraph of Article 103, Article 105, Article 107, and Article 109, if the crimes cause especially grave harm to the state and the people, or the circumstances are especially flagrant, the death penalty may be imposed.

Those who commit crimes stipulated in this Chapter may also be sentenced to confiscation of property.

### Chapter 2: Crimes of Endangering Public Security

Article 114: Anyone who commits arson, breaches dikes, causes explosions, spreads poisonous, radioactive, or infectious disease pathogens, or uses other dangerous means to endanger public security, if no serious consequences have been caused, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 10 years.

Article 115: Anyone who commits arson, breaches dikes, causes explosions, spreads poisonous, radioactive, or infectious disease pathogens, or uses other dangerous means to cause serious injury or death, or cause major losses to public or private property, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years, life imprisonment, or death.

Anyone who negligently commits the crime in the preceding paragraph shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years; if the circumstances are relatively minor, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention.

Article 116: Anyone who sabotages a train, motor vehicle, tram, ship, or aircraft, creating a danger of overturning or destruction sufficient to endanger public safety, if no serious consequences have been caused, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 10 years.

Article 117: Anyone who sabotages tracks, bridges, tunnels, highways, airports, waterways, lighthouses, signs, or engages in other destructive activities, creating a danger of overturning or destruction sufficient to endanger public safety, if no serious consequences have been caused, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 10 years.

Article 118: Anyone who sabotages electric power, gas, or other flammable or explosive equipment, endangering public security, if no serious consequences have been caused, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 10 years.

Article 119: Anyone who sabotages means of transportation, transportation facilities, electric power equipment, gas equipment, or flammable or explosive equipment, causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years, life imprisonment, or death.

Anyone who negligently commits the crime in the preceding paragraph shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years; if the circumstances are relatively minor, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention.

Article 120: Anyone who organizes or leads a terrorist organization shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years or life imprisonment, and shall also be sentenced to confiscation of property; those who actively participate shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 10 years, and shall also be fined; other participants shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights, and may also be fined.

Those who commit the crime in the preceding paragraph and also commit crimes such as murder, explosion, or kidnapping shall be punished in accordance with the provisions on combined punishment for multiple crimes.

Article 120-1: Anyone who provides financial support to a terrorist organization or an individual who carries out terrorist activities, or provides financial support for terrorist training, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 5 years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 5 years, and shall also be fined or have their property confiscated.

Anyone who recruits or transports personnel for a terrorist organization, the commission of terrorist activities, or terrorist training shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where a unit commits the crimes in the preceding two paragraphs, the unit shall be fined, and the persons directly in charge and other persons directly responsible shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Article 120-2: Anyone who falls under any of the following circumstances shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 5 years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 5 years, and shall also be fined or have their property confiscated:

(1) Preparing weapons, dangerous articles, or other tools for the commission of terrorist activities;

(2) Organizing terrorist training or actively participating in terrorist training;

(3) Contacting foreign terrorist organizations or individuals for the commission of terrorist activities;

(4) Planning or making other preparations for the commission of terrorist activities.

Where the conduct in the preceding paragraph also constitutes another crime, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions for the heavier punishment.

Article 120-3: Anyone who produces or disseminates books, audio or video materials, or other items that promote terrorism or extremism, or promotes terrorism or extremism through lectures, information dissemination, or other means, or incites the commission of terrorist activities, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 5 years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 5 years, and shall also be fined or have their property confiscated.

Article 120-4: Anyone who uses extremism to incite or coerce the masses to undermine the implementation of the systems of marriage, justice, education, and social management established by state laws shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 7 years, and shall also be fined or have their property confiscated.

Article 120-5: Anyone who forces another person to wear or display clothing or symbols that promote terrorism or extremism in a public place by violence, coercion, or other means shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined.

Article 120-6: Anyone who knowingly and illegally possesses books, audio or video materials, or other items that promote terrorism or extremism, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or only, be fined.

Article 121: Anyone who hijacks an aircraft by violence, coercion, or other means shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years or life imprisonment; if serious injury, death, or severe damage to the aircraft is caused, the offender shall be sentenced to death.

Article 122: Anyone who hijacks a ship or motor vehicle by violence, coercion, or other means shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 5 years but not more than 10 years; if serious consequences are caused, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years or life imprisonment.

Article 123: Anyone who uses violence against a person on board an aircraft in flight, endangering flight safety, if no serious consequences have been caused, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 5 years or criminal detention; if serious consequences are caused, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 5 years.

Article 124: Anyone who sabotages radio and television facilities or public telecommunications facilities, endangering public security, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years; if serious consequences are caused, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 7 years.

Anyone who negligently commits the crime in the preceding paragraph shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years; if the circumstances are relatively minor, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention.

Article 125: Anyone who illegally manufactures, trades in, transports, mails, or stores firearms, ammunition, or explosives shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 10 years; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years, life imprisonment, or death.

Anyone who illegally manufactures, trades in, transports, or stores poisonous, radioactive, or infectious disease pathogens, endangering public security, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where a unit commits the crimes in the preceding two paragraphs, the unit shall be fined, and the persons directly in charge and other persons directly responsible shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Article 126: Where an enterprise designated or determined by law for the manufacture or sale of firearms violates the regulations on the control of firearms and commits any of the following acts, the unit shall be fined, and the persons directly in charge and other persons directly responsible shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 5 years; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 5 years but not more than 10 years; if the circumstances are especially serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years or life imprisonment:

(1) Manufacturing or allocating firearms beyond the prescribed quota or not in accordance with the prescribed types for the purpose of illegal sale;

(2) Manufacturing firearms without serial numbers, with duplicate serial numbers, or with false serial numbers for the purpose of illegal sale;

(3) Illegally selling firearms or selling firearms manufactured for export within the country.

Article 127: Anyone who steals or forcibly seizes firearms, ammunition, or explosives, or steals or forcibly seizes poisonous, radioactive, or infectious disease pathogens, endangering public security, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 10 years; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years, life imprisonment, or death.

Anyone who robs firearms, ammunition, or explosives, or robs poisonous, radioactive, or infectious disease pathogens, endangering public security, or steals or forcibly seizes firearms, ammunition, or explosives from state organs, military personnel, police officers, or militiamen, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 10 years, life imprisonment, or death.

Article 128: Anyone who violates the regulations on the control of firearms and illegally possesses or privately stores firearms or ammunition shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years, criminal detention, or public surveillance; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years.

Any person who is legally equipped with firearms for official use and illegally leases or lends out firearms shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Any person who is legally equipped with firearms and illegally leases or lends out firearms, causing serious consequences, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Where a unit commits the crimes in the second or third paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the persons directly in charge and other persons directly responsible shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Article 129: Any person who is legally equipped with firearms for official use and loses his firearms without timely reporting, causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention.

Article 130: Anyone who illegally carries firearms, ammunition, controlled knives, or explosive, flammable, radioactive, poisonous, or corrosive articles into a public place or on a public means of transportation, endangering public security, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Article 131: Any aviation personnel who violates rules and regulations, causing a major flight accident with serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention; if the accident causes the crash of an aircraft or the death of persons, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years.

Article 132: Any railway employee who violates rules and regulations, causing a railway operation safety accident with serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention; if the consequences are especially serious, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years.

Article 133: Anyone who violates traffic and transportation management regulations, thereby causing a major accident resulting in serious injury or death, or causing major losses to public or private property, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention; if the person flees after a traffic accident or there are other especially flagrant circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years; if the flight causes the death of another person, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 7 years.

Article 133-1: Anyone who drives a motor vehicle on a road under any of the following circumstances shall be sentenced to criminal detention and shall also be fined:

(1) Engaging in drag racing, if the circumstances are flagrant;

(2) Driving a motor vehicle while intoxicated;

(3) Engaging in school bus services or passenger transport, severely exceeding the rated number of passengers, or severely exceeding the prescribed speed limit;

(4) Transporting hazardous chemicals in violation of the regulations on the safety management of hazardous chemicals, endangering public safety.

Where the owner or manager of a motor vehicle bears direct responsibility for the conduct in items (3) or (4) of the preceding paragraph, they shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where the conduct in the preceding two paragraphs also constitutes another crime, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions for the heavier punishment.

Article 133-2: Anyone who uses violence against the driver of a public transport vehicle in motion, or seizes or controls the driving control device, interfering with the normal operation of the public transport vehicle and endangering public safety, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 1 year, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or only, be fined.

Where the driver specified in the preceding paragraph abandons his post on a moving public transport vehicle and engages in mutual fighting or assaults another person, endangering public safety, they shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where the conduct in the preceding two paragraphs also constitutes another crime, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions for the heavier punishment.

Article 134: Anyone who violates the provisions on safety management in production or operations, thereby causing a major accident resulting in serious injury or death, or causing other serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially flagrant, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years.

Anyone who forces another person to work in violation of regulations and take risks, or who, knowing that there is a major accident hazard, fails to eliminate it and still takes risks in organizing operations, thereby causing a major accident resulting in serious injury or death, or causing other serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 5 years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially flagrant, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 5 years.

Article 134-1: Anyone who violates the provisions on safety management in production or operations under any of the following circumstances, creating a real danger of a major accident resulting in serious injury or death or other serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 1 year, criminal detention, or public surveillance:

(1) Shutting down or destroying monitoring, alarm, protection, or life-saving equipment or facilities directly related to production safety, or tampering with, concealing, or destroying their related data or information;

(2) Refusing to comply with an order lawfully issued to suspend production or business, stop construction, stop using relevant equipment, facilities, or premises, or immediately take corrective measures to eliminate dangers due to the existence of a major accident hazard;

(3) Engaging in highly dangerous production operations such as mining, metal smelting, building construction, and the production, operation, or storage of dangerous articles without the legally required approval or permission for matters related to production safety.

Article 135: Where production safety facilities or conditions do not meet state regulations, thereby causing a major accident resulting in serious injury or death, or causing other serious consequences, the persons directly in charge and other persons directly responsible shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially flagrant, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years.

Article 135-1: Where the holding of a large-scale mass event violates safety management regulations, thereby causing a major accident resulting in serious injury or death, or causing other serious consequences, the persons directly in charge and other persons directly responsible shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially flagrant, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years.

Article 136: Anyone who violates the regulations on the control of explosive, flammable, radioactive, poisonous, or corrosive articles, causing a major accident in production, storage, transportation, or use, resulting in serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention; if the consequences are especially serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years.

Article 137: Where a construction unit, design unit, construction unit, or project supervision unit violates state regulations by lowering the quality standards of the project, causing a major safety accident, the persons directly responsible shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 5 years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined; if the consequences are especially serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 5 years but not more than 10 years, and shall also be fined.

Article 138: Anyone who, knowing that a school building or educational or teaching facility is dangerous, fails to take measures or report in a timely manner, thereby causing a major accident resulting in serious injury or death, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention; if the consequences are especially serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years.

Article 139: Anyone who violates fire control management regulations and, after being notified by a fire control supervision agency to take corrective measures, refuses to implement them, causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention; if the consequences are especially serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years.

Article 139-1: Where, after a safety accident occurs, a person responsible for reporting fails to report or falsely reports the accident situation, delaying the rescue of the accident, if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 3 years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 3 years but not more than 7 years.

### Chapter 3: Crimes of Disrupting the Order of the Socialist Market Economy

#### Section 1: Crimes of Producing and Selling Counterfeit and Substandard Goods

Article 140: Where a producer or seller mixes impurities or imitations into a product, passes a fake product off as a genuine one, passes a defective product off as a high-quality one, or passes a substandard product off as a standard one, if the sales amount is not less than 50,000 yuan but less than 200,000 yuan, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than 2 years or criminal detention, and shall also, or only, be fined not less than 50% but not more than 2 times the sales amount; if the sales amount is not less than 200,000 yuan but less than 500,000 yuan, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 2 years but not more than 7 years, and shall also be fined not less than 50% but not more than 2 times the sales amount; if the sales amount is not less than 500,000 yuan but less than 2,000,000 yuan, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than 7 years, and shall also be fined not less than 50% but not more than 2 times the sales amount; if the sales amount is not less than 2,000,000 yuan, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of 15 years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50% but not more than 2 times the sales amount or have their property confiscated.Article 141: Whoever produces or sells counterfeit drugs shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also be fined; if it causes serious harm to human health or if there are other serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years and shall also be fined; if it causes death or if there are other especially serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death, and shall also be fined or have property confiscated.

Where personnel of a drug-using entity knowingly provide counterfeit drugs to others, they shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 142: Whoever produces or sells substandard drugs, causing serious harm to human health, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years and shall also be fined; if the consequences are especially serious, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined or have property confiscated.

Where personnel of a drug-using entity knowingly provide substandard drugs to others, they shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 142-1: Whoever violates drug administration regulations and commits any of the following acts, if it is sufficient to seriously endanger human health, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if it causes serious harm to human health or if there are other serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years and shall also be fined:

(1) Producing or selling drugs prohibited by the drug regulatory department of the State Council;
(2) Producing or importing drugs without obtaining relevant drug approval certificates, or knowingly selling such drugs;
(3) Providing false certificates, data, materials, samples, or using other deceptive means in drug registration applications;
(4) Fabricating production or inspection records.

Where an act under the preceding paragraph also constitutes a crime under Article 141 or Article 142 of this Law or any other crime, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions for the heavier punishment.

Article 143: Whoever produces or sells food that fails to meet food safety standards, if it is sufficient to cause a serious food poisoning accident or any other serious foodborne disease, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also be fined; if it causes serious harm to human health or if there are other serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years and shall also be fined; if the consequences are especially serious, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined or have property confiscated.

Article 144: Whoever mixes food produced or sold with toxic or harmful non-food raw materials, or knowingly sells food mixed with such raw materials, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years and shall also be fined; if it causes serious harm to human health or if there are other serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years and shall also be fined; if it causes death or if there are other especially serious circumstances, the offender shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 141 of this Law.

Article 145: Whoever produces medical devices or medical hygiene materials that do not meet national or industry standards for protecting human health, or knowingly sells such products, if it is sufficient to seriously endanger human health, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also be fined not less than 50% but not more than two times the sales amount; if it causes serious harm to human health, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years and shall also be fined not less than 50% but not more than two times the sales amount; if the consequences are especially serious, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50% but not more than two times the sales amount or have property confiscated.

Article 146: Whoever produces electrical appliances, pressure vessels, flammable or explosive products, or other products that do not meet national or industry standards for protecting personal or property safety, or knowingly sells such products, if it causes serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years and shall also be fined not less than 50% but not more than two times the sales amount; if the consequences are especially serious, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years and shall also be fined not less than 50% but not more than two times the sales amount.

Article 147: Whoever produces counterfeit pesticides, counterfeit veterinary drugs, or counterfeit fertilizers, knowingly sells counterfeit or ineffective pesticides, veterinary drugs, fertilizers, or seeds, or passes off substandard pesticides, veterinary drugs, fertilizers, or seeds as qualified ones, causing relatively heavy losses to production, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 50% but not more than two times the sales amount; if it causes heavy losses to production, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years and shall also be fined not less than 50% but not more than two times the sales amount; if it causes especially heavy losses to production, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50% but not more than two times the sales amount or have property confiscated.

Article 148: Whoever produces cosmetics that do not meet hygiene standards, or knowingly sells such cosmetics, if it causes serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 50% but not more than two times the sales amount.

Article 149: Where the production or sale of products listed in Articles 141 through 148 of this Section does not constitute a crime under those respective articles, but the sales amount is not less than 50,000 yuan, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 140 of this Section.

Where the production or sale of products listed in Articles 141 through 148 of this Section constitutes a crime under those respective articles and also constitutes a crime under Article 140 of this Section, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions for the heavier punishment.

Article 150: Where a unit commits a crime under Articles 140 through 148 of this Section, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of those respective articles.

#### Section 2: Crimes of Smuggling

Article 151: Whoever smuggles weapons, ammunition, nuclear materials, or counterfeit currency shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years and shall also be fined or have property confiscated; if the circumstances are especially serious, the offender shall be sentenced to life imprisonment and shall also have property confiscated; if the circumstances are relatively minor, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years and shall also be fined.

Whoever smuggles cultural relics, gold, silver, or other precious metals that are prohibited from export by the State, or precious animals or their products that are prohibited from import or export by the State, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment and shall also have property confiscated; if the circumstances are relatively minor, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years and shall also be fined.

Whoever smuggles rare plants or their products, or other goods or articles prohibited from import or export by the State, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years and shall also be fined.

Where a unit commits a crime under this article, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the respective paragraphs of this article.

Article 152: Whoever, for the purpose of profit or dissemination, smuggles obscene films, videotapes, audio tapes, pictures, books, periodicals, or other obscene articles shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment and shall also be fined or have property confiscated; if the circumstances are relatively minor, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance and shall also be fined.

Whoever evades customs supervision and transports solid waste, liquid waste, or gaseous waste from abroad into the country, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years and shall also be fined.

Where a unit commits the crimes under the preceding two paragraphs, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs.

Article 153: Whoever smuggles goods or articles other than those specified in Articles 151, 152, and 347 of this Law shall, depending on the seriousness of the circumstances, be punished in accordance with the following provisions:

(1) If the amount of duties evaded on the smuggled goods or articles is relatively large, or if the offender has been given two administrative penalties for smuggling within one year and then smuggles again, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the amount of duties evaded.
(2) If the amount of duties evaded on the smuggled goods or articles is huge, or if there are other serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years and shall also be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the amount of duties evaded.
(3) If the amount of duties evaded on the smuggled goods or articles is especially huge, or if there are other especially serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment and shall also be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the amount of duties evaded or have property confiscated.

Where a unit commits the crime under the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are especially serious, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years.

For multiple acts of smuggling that have not been dealt with, the punishment shall be based on the cumulative amount of duties evaded on the smuggled goods or articles.

Article 154: The following smuggling acts, if constituting a crime under this Section, shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 153 of this Law:

(1) Without permission from the customs and without paying the duties due, selling for profit within the country bonded goods such as raw materials, parts, finished products, or equipment imported for processing, assembly, or compensation trade, which have been approved for import;
(2) Without permission from the customs and without paying the duties due, selling for profit within the country goods or articles imported with specific tax reductions or exemptions.

Article 155: The following acts shall be dealt with as smuggling crimes and punished in accordance with the relevant provisions of this Section:

(1) Directly purchasing from smugglers, in an illegal manner, goods prohibited from import by the State, or directly purchasing from smugglers, in an illegal manner, other smuggled goods or articles in relatively large quantities;
(2) Transporting, purchasing, or selling goods prohibited from import or export by the State in inland seas, territorial waters, boundary rivers, or boundary lakes, or transporting, purchasing, or selling goods or articles restricted from import or export by the State in relatively large quantities without lawful certificates.

Article 156: Whoever conspires with a smuggler and provides loans, funds, account numbers, invoices, certificates, or provides transportation, storage, mailing, or other conveniences shall be dealt with as an accomplice in the smuggling crime.

Article 157: Whoever smuggles while armed with weapons shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph of Article 151 of this Law.

Whoever resists customs seizure by means of violence or threats shall be punished in accordance with the provisions on combined punishment for multiple crimes for the crime of smuggling and the crime of obstructing a state organ functionary from lawfully performing duties as provided in Article 277 of this Law.

#### Section 3: Crimes of Obstructing the Management of Companies and Enterprises

Article 158: Whoever, in applying for company registration, uses false certificates or other deceptive means to falsely declare registered capital, deceives the company registration authority, and obtains company registration, if the amount of falsely declared registered capital is huge, the consequences are serious, or there are other serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 1% but not more than 5% of the falsely declared registered capital.

Where a unit commits the crime under the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 159: Where a company promoter or shareholder, in violation of the provisions of the Company Law, fails to deliver monetary or physical contributions or fails to transfer property rights, makes a false capital contribution, or withdraws the contributed capital after the company is established, if the amount is huge, the consequences are serious, or there are other serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 2% but not more than 10% of the amount of the false capital contribution or the withdrawn capital contribution.

Where a unit commits the crime under the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

Article 160: Whoever conceals important facts or fabricates major false content in issuance documents such as prospectuses, subscription forms, or methods for raising corporate or enterprise bonds, and issues stocks, corporate or enterprise bonds, depositary receipts, or other securities recognized by the State Council, if the amount is huge, the consequences are serious, or there are other serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the amount is especially huge, the consequences are especially serious, or there are other especially serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years and shall also be fined.

Where a controlling shareholder or actual controller organizes or instructs the commission of the act under the preceding paragraph, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 20% but not more than one time the amount of illegally raised funds; if the amount is especially huge, the consequences are especially serious, or there are other especially serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years and shall also be fined not less than 20% but not more than one time the amount of illegally raised funds.

Where a unit commits the crimes under the preceding two paragraphs, the unit shall be fined not less than 20% but not more than one time the amount of illegally raised funds, and the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Article 161: Where a company or enterprise legally obligated to disclose information provides false financial accounting reports or conceals important facts to shareholders or the public, or fails to disclose other important information that should be disclosed according to law, seriously harming the interests of shareholders or others, or if there are other serious circumstances, the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years and shall also be fined.

Where a controlling shareholder or actual controller of the company or enterprise specified in the preceding paragraph commits or organizes or instructs the commission of the act under the preceding paragraph, or conceals relevant matters leading to the occurrence of the circumstances specified in the preceding paragraph, the offender shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where the controlling shareholder or actual controller who commits the crime under the preceding paragraph is a unit, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Article 162: Where a company or enterprise, during liquidation, conceals property, makes false entries in the balance sheet or property list, or distributes company or enterprise property before paying off debts, seriously harming the interests of creditors or others, the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan.

Article 162-1: Whoever conceals or intentionally destroys accounting vouchers, accounting books, or financial accounting reports that should be preserved according to law, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan.

Where a unit commits the crime under the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 162-2: Where a company or enterprise, by concealing property, assuming fictitious debts, or by other means transferring or disposing of property, carries out a false bankruptcy, seriously harming the interests of creditors or others, the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan.

Article 163: Where an employee of a company, enterprise, or other unit, taking advantage of their position, demands property from others or illegally accepts property from others to seek benefits for others, if the amount is relatively large, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also be fined; if the amount is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years and shall also be fined; if the amount is especially huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment and shall also be fined.

Where an employee of a company, enterprise, or other unit, in economic transactions, takes advantage of their position, violates state regulations, and accepts various forms of kickbacks or handling fees for personal possession, the offender shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where a person engaged in public service in a state-owned company, enterprise, or other state-owned unit, or a person dispatched by a state-owned company, enterprise, or other state-owned unit to a non-state-owned company, enterprise, or other unit to engage in public service, commits the acts under the preceding two paragraphs, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Articles 385 and 386 of this Law.

Article 164: Whoever, for the purpose of seeking illegitimate benefits, gives property to an employee of a company, enterprise, or other unit, if the amount is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also be fined; if the amount is huge, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years and shall also be fined.

Whoever, for the purpose of seeking illegitimate commercial benefits, gives property to a foreign public official or an official of an international public organization shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where a unit commits the crimes under the preceding two paragraphs, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Where a briber voluntarily confesses the bribery act before being prosecuted, the punishment may be mitigated or the briber may be exempted from punishment.

Article 165: Where a director or manager of a state-owned company or enterprise, taking advantage of their position, operates for themselves or for others a business of the same type as that of the company or enterprise they serve, obtaining illegal benefits, if the amount is huge, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the amount is especially huge, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years and shall also be fined.

Article 166: Where an employee of a state-owned company, enterprise, or institution, taking advantage of their position, commits any of the following acts, causing heavy losses to the interests of the State, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if it causes especially heavy losses to the interests of the State, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years and shall also be fined:

(1) Entrusting the profitable business of the unit to relatives or friends for operation;
(2) Purchasing goods from a unit managed by relatives or friends at a price obviously higher than the market price, or selling goods to such a unit at a price obviously lower than the market price;
(3) Purchasing substandard goods from a unit managed by relatives or friends.

Article 167: Where a directly responsible supervisor of a state-owned company, enterprise, or institution, in the process of signing or performing a contract, is defrauded due to serious irresponsibility, causing heavy losses to the interests of the State, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if it causes especially heavy losses to the interests of the State, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 168: Where an employee of a state-owned company or enterprise, due to serious irresponsibility or abuse of power, causes bankruptcy or serious losses to the state-owned company or enterprise, resulting in heavy losses to the interests of the State, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if it causes especially heavy losses to the interests of the State, the offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.If a staff member of a state-owned institution commits the act described in the preceding paragraph, causing significant losses to national interests, they shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

If a staff member of a state-owned company, enterprise, or institution engages in malpractice for personal gain and commits the crimes described in the preceding two paragraphs, they shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Article 169 If a directly responsible manager of a state-owned company, enterprise, or its superior competent authority, engages in malpractice for personal gain by converting state-owned assets into shares at a low price or selling them at a low price, causing significant losses to national interests, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the losses to national interests are particularly significant, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 169a If a director, supervisor, or senior manager of a listed company violates their duty of loyalty to the company and, by taking advantage of their position, manipulates the listed company to engage in any of the following acts, causing significant losses to the interests of the listed company, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the losses to the interests of the listed company are particularly significant, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined:

(1) Providing funds, goods, services, or other assets to other units or individuals without compensation;
(2) Providing or accepting funds, goods, services, or other assets under obviously unfair conditions;
(3) Providing funds, goods, services, or other assets to units or individuals that are obviously insolvent;
(4) Providing guarantees for units or individuals that are obviously insolvent, or providing guarantees for other units or individuals without justifiable reasons;
(5) Abandoning claims or assuming debts without justifiable reasons;
(6) Using other methods to harm the interests of the listed company.

If the controlling shareholder or actual controller of a listed company instructs the directors, supervisors, or senior managers of the listed company to commit the acts described in the preceding paragraph, they shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

If the controlling shareholder or actual controller of a listed company that commits the crime described in the preceding paragraph is a unit, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

#### Section 4: Crimes of Disrupting Financial Management Order

Article 170 Whoever counterfeits currency shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined; under any of the following circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined or have their property confiscated:

(1) Being a principal offender in a counterfeit currency ring;
(2) Counterfeiting a particularly large amount of currency;
(3) Having other particularly serious circumstances.

Article 171 Whoever sells, purchases, or transports counterfeit currency, knowing it to be counterfeit, in a relatively large amount, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the amount is huge, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan; if the amount is particularly huge, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have their property confiscated.

If a staff member of a bank or other financial institution purchases counterfeit currency or, by taking advantage of their position, exchanges counterfeit currency for genuine currency, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the amount is huge or there are other serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan or have their property confiscated; if the circumstances are relatively minor, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan.

Whoever counterfeits currency and also sells or transports the counterfeit currency shall be convicted and given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of Article 170 of this Law.

Article 172 Whoever knowingly holds or uses counterfeit currency in a relatively large amount shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan; if the amount is huge, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the amount is particularly huge, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have their property confiscated.

Article 173 Whoever alters currency in a relatively large amount shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan; if the amount is huge, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan.

Article 174 Whoever establishes a commercial bank, stock exchange, futures exchange, securities company, futures brokerage company, insurance company, or other financial institution without the approval of the relevant state authority shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan.

Whoever forges, alters, or transfers the business license or approval document of a commercial bank, stock exchange, futures exchange, securities company, futures brokerage company, insurance company, or other financial institution shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

If a unit commits the crimes described in the preceding two paragraphs, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Article 175 Whoever, for the purpose of relending at a profit, obtains credit funds from a financial institution and relends them to others at a high interest rate, with a relatively large amount of illegal income, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the illegal income; if the amount is huge, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the illegal income.

If a unit commits the crime described in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 175a Whoever obtains loans, acceptance of bills, letters of credit, letters of guarantee, etc., from a bank or other financial institution by fraudulent means, causing significant losses to the bank or other financial institution, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if particularly significant losses are caused to the bank or other financial institution or there are other particularly serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

If a unit commits the crime described in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 176 Whoever illegally absorbs public deposits or does so in a disguised form, disrupting financial order, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the amount is huge or there are other serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined; if the amount is particularly huge or there are other particularly serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, and shall also be fined.

If a unit commits the crime described in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

If a person who has committed the acts described in the preceding two paragraphs actively returns the stolen goods or compensates for losses before a public prosecution is initiated, thereby reducing the occurrence of harmful consequences, they may be given a lighter or mitigated punishment.

Article 177 Whoever forges or alters financial instruments under any of the following circumstances shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan; if the circumstances are particularly serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have their property confiscated:

(1) Forging or altering drafts, promissory notes, or checks;
(2) Forging or altering other bank settlement vouchers such as collection authorization certificates, remittance certificates, or bank deposit certificates;
(3) Forging or altering letters of credit or their accompanying documents or papers;
(4) Forging credit cards.

If a unit commits the crime described in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 177a Whoever obstructs credit card management under any of the following circumstances shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan; if the quantity is huge or there are other serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan:

(1) Knowingly holding or transporting counterfeit credit cards, or knowingly holding or transporting counterfeit blank credit cards, in a relatively large quantity;
(2) Illegally holding credit cards of others in a relatively large quantity;
(3) Obtaining credit cards by fraud using false identity certificates;
(4) Selling, purchasing, or providing others with counterfeit credit cards or credit cards obtained by fraud using false identity certificates.

Whoever steals, purchases, or illegally provides others with credit card information shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

If a staff member of a bank or other financial institution, by taking advantage of their position, commits the crime described in the second paragraph, they shall be given a heavier punishment.

Article 178 Whoever forges or alters treasury bonds or other negotiable securities issued by the state, in a relatively large amount, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the amount is huge, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan; if the amount is particularly huge, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have their property confiscated.

Whoever forges or alters stocks or company or enterprise bonds, in a relatively large amount, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan; if the amount is huge, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan.

If a unit commits the crimes described in the preceding two paragraphs, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs.

Article 179 Whoever issues stocks or company or enterprise bonds without the approval of the relevant state authority, in a huge amount, with serious consequences, or under other serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than one percent but not more than five percent of the illegally raised funds.

If a unit commits the crime described in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

Article 180 If a person who has access to inside information on securities or futures trading, or who illegally obtains such inside information, buys or sells the securities, engages in futures trading related to the inside information, or discloses the information, or explicitly or implicitly suggests that others engage in the above trading activities, before the information that has a significant impact on the issuance of securities, securities or futures trading, or the price of securities or futures is made public, and the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the illegal income; if the circumstances are particularly serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the illegal income.

If a unit commits the crime described in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

The scope of inside information and persons with access to it shall be determined in accordance with laws and administrative regulations.

If an employee of a financial institution such as a stock exchange, futures exchange, securities company, futures brokerage company, fund management company, commercial bank, or insurance company, or a staff member of a relevant regulatory authority or industry association, uses other undisclosed information obtained by taking advantage of their position, other than inside information, to engage in securities or futures trading activities related to that information in violation of regulations, or explicitly or implicitly suggests that others engage in related trading activities, and the circumstances are serious, they shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Article 181 Whoever fabricates and disseminates false information that affects securities or futures trading, disrupting the securities or futures trading market, and causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan.

If an employee of a stock exchange, futures exchange, securities company, or futures brokerage company, or a staff member of the securities industry association, futures industry association, or securities and futures regulatory authority, intentionally provides false information or forges, alters, or destroys trading records to induce investors to buy or sell securities or futures contracts, causing serious consequences, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan; if the circumstances are particularly flagrant, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan.

If a unit commits the crimes described in the preceding two paragraphs, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

Article 182 Whoever manipulates the securities or futures market under any of the following circumstances, affecting the price or volume of securities or futures trading, and the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are particularly serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined:

(1) Acting alone or in collusion with others, concentrating capital advantages, shareholding or position advantages, or using information advantages to jointly or continuously buy or sell;
(2) Colluding with others to conduct securities or futures transactions with each other at a prearranged time, price, and manner;
(3) Conducting securities transactions between accounts under their actual control, or using themselves as the counterparty to buy and sell futures contracts;
(4) Frequently or in large quantities placing orders to buy or sell securities or futures contracts and then canceling the orders, without the intention of executing the transactions;
(5) Using false or uncertain material information to induce investors to conduct securities or futures transactions;
(6) Publicly making evaluations, predictions, or investment recommendations on securities, securities issuers, or futures trading targets, while simultaneously conducting reverse securities transactions or related futures transactions;
(7) Manipulating the securities or futures market by other methods.

If a unit commits the crime described in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 183 If an employee of an insurance company, by taking advantage of their position, intentionally fabricates an insurance accident that has not occurred to make a false claim and defrauds insurance money for their own possession, they shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 271 of this Law.

If an employee of a state-owned insurance company or a person assigned by a state-owned insurance company to a non-state-owned insurance company to perform public duties commits the act described in the preceding paragraph, they shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Articles 382 and 383 of this Law.

Article 184 If an employee of a bank or other financial institution, in the course of financial business activities, solicits or illegally accepts property from others to seek benefits for them, or, in violation of state regulations, accepts kickbacks or handling fees of various names for their own possession, they shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 163 of this Law.

If an employee of a state-owned financial institution or a person assigned by a state-owned financial institution to a non-state-owned financial institution to perform public duties commits the act described in the preceding paragraph, they shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Articles 385 and 386 of this Law.

Article 185 If an employee of a commercial bank, stock exchange, futures exchange, securities company, futures brokerage company, insurance company, or other financial institution, by taking advantage of their position, misappropriates funds of the unit or its clients, they shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 272 of this Law.

If an employee of a state-owned commercial bank, stock exchange, futures exchange, securities company, futures brokerage company, insurance company, or other state-owned financial institution, or a person assigned by such a state-owned institution to a non-state-owned institution as described in the preceding paragraph to perform public duties, commits the act described in the preceding paragraph, they shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 384 of this Law.

Article 185a If a commercial bank, stock exchange, futures exchange, securities company, futures brokerage company, insurance company, or other financial institution, in violation of its fiduciary duties, unauthorizedly uses client funds or other entrusted or trust property, and the circumstances are serious, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than 30,000 yuan but not more than 300,000 yuan; if the circumstances are particularly serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan.

If a public fund management institution such as a social security fund management institution or a housing provident fund management institution, or an insurance company, insurance asset management company, or securities investment fund management company, uses funds in violation of state regulations, the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 186 If an employee of a bank or other financial institution, in violation of state regulations, grants loans in a huge amount or causes significant losses, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan; if the amount is particularly huge or causes particularly significant losses, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan.

If an employee of a bank or other financial institution, in violation of state regulations, grants loans to a related party, they shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

If a unit commits the crimes described in the preceding two paragraphs, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs.

The scope of related parties shall be determined in accordance with the Commercial Bank Law of the People's Republic of China and relevant financial regulations.

Article 187 If an employee of a bank or other financial institution fails to record client funds received in the accounts, in a huge amount or causing significant losses, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the amount is particularly huge or causes particularly significant losses, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan.

If a unit commits the crime described in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 188 If an employee of a bank or other financial institution, in violation of regulations, issues letters of credit or other letters of guarantee, bills, deposit certificates, or credit certificates for others, and the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are particularly serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

If a unit commits the crime described in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible manager and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.Article 189 Where a staff member of a bank or any other financial institution, in handling negotiable instruments, accepts, pays or guarantees a negotiable instrument that is in violation of the Law on Negotiable Instruments, thus causing heavy losses, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the losses are especially heavy, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 190 Where a company, enterprise or any other unit, in violation of State regulations, arbitrarily deposits foreign exchange abroad or illegally transfers foreign exchange from inside the country to outside the country, if the amount involved is relatively large, the unit shall be fined not less than 5% but not more than 30% of the amount of foreign exchange evaded, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the amount involved is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, the unit shall be fined not less than 5% but not more than 30% of the amount of foreign exchange evaded, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Article 191 Whoever, for the purpose of disguising or concealing the source or nature of the proceeds obtained from drug-related crimes, crimes committed by organizations of a gangster nature, terrorist activities, smuggling crimes, crimes of embezzlement or bribery, crimes of disrupting the order of financial administration, or crimes of financial fraud, and the profits derived therefrom, commits any of the following acts shall, in addition to having the proceeds from the above-mentioned crimes and the profits derived therefrom confiscated, be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined:

(1) providing a fund account;

(2) converting property into cash, financial instruments or negotiable securities;

(3) transferring funds through transfer or other payment and settlement methods;

(4) cross-border transfer of assets;

(5) disguising or concealing the source or nature of the proceeds of crime and the profits derived therefrom by any other means.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

#### Section 5 Crimes of Financial Fraud

Article 192 Whoever, for the purpose of illegal possession, illegally raises funds by means of fraud, if the amount involved is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined; if the amount involved is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined or have his property confiscated.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 193 Whoever, under any of the following circumstances, for the purpose of illegal possession, defrauds a bank or any other financial institution of a loan, if the amount involved is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the amount involved is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan; if the amount involved is especially huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have his property confiscated:

(1) fabricating false reasons such as introducing funds or projects;

(2) using false economic contracts;

(3) using false supporting documents;

(4) using false property right certificates as security or repeatedly providing security exceeding the value of the mortgaged property;

(5) defrauding a loan by any other means.

Article 194 Whoever commits any of the following acts to engage in fraudulent activities involving financial instruments, if the amount involved is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the amount involved is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan; if the amount involved is especially huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have his property confiscated:

(1) knowingly using forged or altered bills of exchange, promissory notes or cheques;

(2) knowingly using invalid bills of exchange, promissory notes or cheques;

(3) fraudulently using another person's bills of exchange, promissory notes or cheques;

(4) issuing a rubber cheque or a cheque that does not match the reserved seal impression, thereby defrauding property;

(5) where the drawer of a bill of exchange or promissory note issues a bill of exchange or promissory note without a guarantee of funds, or makes false entries at the time of issue, thereby defrauding property.

Whoever uses forged or altered other bank settlement vouchers such as collection vouchers, remittance vouchers or bank deposit certificates shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 195 Whoever commits any of the following acts to engage in fraudulent activities involving letters of credit shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the amount involved is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan; if the amount involved is especially huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have his property confiscated:

(1) using forged or altered letters of credit or accompanying documents or instruments;

(2) using invalid letters of credit;

(3) fraudulently obtaining letters of credit;

(4) engaging in fraudulent activities involving letters of credit by any other means.

Article 196 Whoever commits any of the following acts to engage in fraudulent activities involving credit cards, if the amount involved is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the amount involved is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan; if the amount involved is especially huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have his property confiscated:

(1) using a forged credit card, or using a credit card fraudulently obtained with a false identity certificate;

(2) using an invalid credit card;

(3) fraudulently using another person's credit card;

(4) maliciously overdrawing.

"Malicious overdraft" as mentioned in the preceding paragraph means the act of a cardholder who, for the purpose of illegal possession, overdraws beyond the prescribed limit or within the prescribed period and fails to repay after being pressed by the issuing bank.

Whoever steals a credit card and uses it shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 264 of this Law.

Article 197 Whoever uses forged or altered treasury bonds or other negotiable securities issued by the State to engage in fraudulent activities, if the amount involved is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the amount involved is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan; if the amount involved is especially huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have his property confiscated.

Article 198 Whoever commits any of the following acts to engage in fraudulent activities involving insurance, if the amount involved is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan; if the amount involved is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the amount involved is especially huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan or have his property confiscated:

(1) where the policyholder intentionally fabricates the subject matter of the insurance to defraud insurance money;

(2) where the policyholder, the insured or the beneficiary fabricates a false cause for an insured incident or exaggerates the extent of the loss to defraud insurance money;

(3) where the policyholder, the insured or the beneficiary fabricates an insured incident that has not occurred to defraud insurance money;

(4) where the policyholder or the insured intentionally causes an insured incident involving property damage to defraud insurance money;

(5) where the policyholder or the beneficiary intentionally causes the death, disability or illness of the insured to defraud insurance money.

Where the acts listed in items (4) and (5) of the preceding paragraph also constitute other crimes, they shall be punished in accordance with the provisions on combined punishment for several crimes.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the first paragraph, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the amount involved is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; if the amount involved is especially huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years.

Where an appraiser, certifier or property assessor of an insured incident intentionally provides false supporting documents to facilitate fraud by others, he shall be punished as an accomplice to insurance fraud.

Article 199 (Deleted)

Article 200 Where a unit commits the crime provided for in Article 194 or Article 195 of this Section, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and may also be fined; if the amount involved is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined; if the amount involved is especially huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined.

#### Section 6 Crimes of Jeopardizing the Administration of Tax Revenue Collection

Article 201 Where a taxpayer, by means of deception or concealment, makes a false tax declaration or fails to make a declaration, thereby evading the payment of taxes, if the amount evaded is relatively large and accounts for not less than 10% of the tax payable, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined; if the amount evaded is huge and accounts for not less than 30% of the tax payable, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

Where a withholding agent, by means listed in the preceding paragraph, fails to pay or underpays the tax that has been withheld or collected, if the amount involved is relatively large, he shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where the acts mentioned in the preceding two paragraphs are committed repeatedly without having been dealt with, the amount shall be calculated on a cumulative basis.

Where a person who has committed the act mentioned in the first paragraph, after the tax authorities have lawfully issued a notice of recovery, pays the tax payable and the surcharge for overdue payment and has already been subjected to administrative punishment, he shall not be pursued for criminal liability; however, this shall not apply if he has been subjected to criminal punishment for tax evasion within the past five years or has been given administrative punishment by the tax authorities two or more times.

Article 202 Whoever refuses to pay taxes by means of violence or threat shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the amount of tax refused; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the amount of tax refused.

Article 203 Where a taxpayer who is in arrears with the tax payable, by means of transferring or concealing property, causes the tax authorities to be unable to recover the tax in arrears, if the amount involved is not less than 10,000 yuan but less than 100,000 yuan, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the amount of tax in arrears; if the amount involved is not less than 100,000 yuan, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the amount of tax in arrears.

Article 204 Whoever, by means of falsely declaring exports or other deceptive means, defrauds the State of export tax rebates, if the amount involved is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the amount defrauded; if the amount involved is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the amount defrauded; if the amount involved is especially huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than one time but not more than five times the amount defrauded or have his property confiscated.

Where a taxpayer, after paying the tax, uses the deceptive means mentioned in the preceding paragraph to defraud the tax paid, he shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 201 of this Law; where the amount defrauded exceeds the tax paid, the excess portion shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 205 Whoever falsely issues special VAT invoices or falsely issues other invoices for defrauding export tax rebates or for deducting tax credits shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the amount of tax falsely issued is relatively large or if there are other serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan; if the amount of tax falsely issued is huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have his property confiscated.

Where a unit commits the crime provided for in this Article, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the amount of tax falsely issued is relatively large or if there are other serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if the amount of tax falsely issued is huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment.

"Falsely issuing special VAT invoices or falsely issuing other invoices for defrauding export tax rebates or for deducting tax credits" means any of the following acts: issuing such invoices for others, issuing such invoices for oneself, having others issue such invoices for oneself, or introducing others to the issuance of such invoices.

Article 205a Whoever falsely issues invoices other than those specified in Article 205 of this Law, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years, criminal detention or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than two years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 206 Whoever forges or sells forged special VAT invoices shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention or public surveillance, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the quantity involved is relatively large or if there are other serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan; if the quantity involved is huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have his property confiscated.

Where a unit commits the crime provided for in this Article, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention or public surveillance; if the quantity involved is relatively large or if there are other serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if the quantity involved is huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment.

Article 207 Whoever illegally sells special VAT invoices shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention or public surveillance, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the quantity involved is relatively large, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan; if the quantity involved is huge, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have his property confiscated.

Article 208 Whoever illegally purchases special VAT invoices or purchases forged special VAT invoices shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan.

Whoever illegally purchases special VAT invoices or purchases forged special VAT invoices and then falsely issues or sells them shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Articles 205, 206 and 207 of this Law respectively.

Article 209 Whoever forges, without authorization, manufactures or sells forged or unauthorized manufactured other invoices that can be used for defrauding export tax rebates or for deducting tax credits shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention or public surveillance, and shall also be fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the quantity involved is huge, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan; if the quantity involved is especially huge, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan or have his property confiscated.

Whoever forges, without authorization, manufactures or sells forged or unauthorized manufactured invoices other than those specified in the preceding paragraph shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years, criminal detention or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 50,000 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than two years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan.

Whoever illegally sells other invoices that can be used for defrauding export tax rebates or for deducting tax credits shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Whoever illegally sells invoices other than those specified in the third paragraph shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the second paragraph.

Article 210 Whoever steals special VAT invoices or other invoices that can be used for defrauding export tax rebates or for deducting tax credits shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 264 of this Law.

Whoever, by means of deception, obtains special VAT invoices or other invoices that can be used for defrauding export tax rebates or for deducting tax credits shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 266 of this Law.

Article 210a Whoever knowingly holds forged invoices, if the quantity involved is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years, criminal detention or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the quantity involved is huge, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than two years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 211 Where a unit commits the crime provided for in Article 201, 203, 204, 207, 208 or 209 of this Section, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the respective Articles.

Article 212 Where a person who has committed the crime provided for in Articles 201 through 205 of this Section is sentenced to a fine or confiscation of property, before the execution of the sentence, the tax authorities shall first recover the tax and the export tax rebate defrauded.

#### Section 7 Crimes of Infringing upon Intellectual Property Rights

Article 213 Whoever, without permission from the owner of a registered trademark, uses a trademark that is identical to the registered trademark on the same kind of goods or services, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined.

Article 214 Whoever sells goods that he knows bear a counterfeit registered trademark, if the amount of illegal gains is relatively large or if there are other serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the amount of illegal gains is huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined.

Article 215 Whoever forges or without authorization manufactures another person's registered trademark identification or sells forged or unauthorized manufactured registered trademark identification, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined.

Article 216 Whoever counterfeits another person's patent, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.Article 217: Whoever, for the purpose of profit, commits any of the following acts of infringement upon copyright or rights related to copyright, where the amount of illegal gains is relatively large or there are other serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years and/or a fine; where the amount of illegal gains is huge or there are other particularly serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years and a fine:

(1) reproducing, distributing, or communicating to the public via information networks, without permission of the copyright owner, their written works, musical works, fine arts works, audiovisual works, computer software, or other works as provided for by laws and administrative regulations;

(2) publishing books for which another person has exclusive publishing rights;

(3) reproducing, distributing, or communicating to the public via information networks, without permission of the producer of sound recordings or video recordings, the sound recordings or video recordings produced by them;

(4) reproducing, distributing, or communicating to the public via information networks, without permission of the performer, sound recordings or video recordings containing their performance, or communicating their performance to the public via information networks;

(5) producing or selling fine arts works bearing the forged signature of another person;

(6) intentionally circumventing or destroying, without permission of the copyright owner or owner of rights related to copyright, the technical measures adopted by the right holder to protect copyright or rights related to copyright in their works, sound recordings, or video recordings.

Article 218: Whoever, for the purpose of profit, sells infringing reproduces that they clearly know are those specified in Article 217 of this Law, where the amount of illegal gains is huge or there are other serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years and/or a fine.

Article 219: Whoever commits any of the following acts of infringing upon trade secrets, where the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years and/or a fine; where the circumstances are particularly serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years and a fine:

(1) acquiring the trade secrets of the right holder by theft, bribery, fraud, coercion, electronic intrusion, or other improper means;

(2) disclosing, using, or allowing another person to use the trade secrets acquired by the means specified in the preceding subparagraph;

(3) disclosing, using, or allowing another person to use the trade secrets in their possession, in violation of a confidentiality obligation or the right holder's requirements for keeping trade secrets confidential.

Whoever acquires, discloses, uses, or allows another person to use a trade secret, knowing that the act is one of those specified in the preceding paragraph, shall be deemed to have infringed upon the trade secret.

For the purposes of this Article, "right holder" means the owner of the trade secret and the person authorized by the owner of the trade secret to use the trade secret.

Article 219a: Whoever steals, spies on, buys, or illegally provides trade secrets for institutions, organizations, or individuals outside the country shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years and/or a fine; where the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years and a fine.

Article 220: Where a unit commits any of the crimes specified in Articles 213 through 219a of this Section, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly responsible persons shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the respective Articles of this Section.

#### Section 8: Crimes of Disrupting the Order of the Market

Article 221: Whoever fabricates and spreads false facts to damage the business reputation or commodity reputation of another person, causing significant losses to the other person or having other serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years or criminal detention and/or a fine.

Article 222: Where an advertiser, advertising agent, or advertising publisher violates state regulations by using advertisements to make false publicity about goods or services, where the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years or criminal detention and/or a fine.

Article 223: Where bidders collude with each other in bidding prices, harming the interests of the tenderer or other bidders, where the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and/or a fine.

Where a bidder colludes with the tenderer in bidding, harming the legitimate interests of the state, the collective, or citizens, they shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 224: Whoever, under any of the following circumstances, with the intent of illegal possession, defrauds another party of property in the course of concluding or performing a contract, where the amount is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and/or a fine; where the amount is huge or there are other serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years and a fine; where the amount is especially huge or there are other particularly serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment and a fine or confiscation of property:

(1) concluding a contract in the name of a fictitious unit or under the name of another person;

(2) providing a guarantee with forged, altered, or invalidated negotiable instruments or other false certificates of property rights;

(3) without actual ability to perform, inducing the other party to continue to conclude and perform a contract by performing a small contract or partially performing a contract;

(4) absconding after receiving the goods, payment, advance payment, or guaranteed property delivered by the other party;

(5) defrauding the other party of property by other means.

Article 224a: Whoever organizes or leads pyramid selling activities, under the guise of promoting goods or providing services, requiring participants to pay fees or purchase goods or services to obtain membership, forming a hierarchy in a certain order, directly or indirectly using the number of persons developed as the basis for remuneration or rebates, inducing or coercing participants to continue developing others to participate, defrauding property, and disrupting the economic and social order, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and a fine; where the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years and a fine.

Article 225: Whoever, in violation of state regulations, commits any of the following illegal business operations, disrupting market order, where the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and/or a fine of not less than one time but not more than five times the illegal gains; where the circumstances are particularly serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years and a fine of not less than one time but not more than five times the illegal gains or confiscation of property:

(1) operating goods that are subject to monopoly or exclusive sale or other goods subject to trading restrictions as provided for by laws or administrative regulations without permission;

(2) buying or selling import or export licenses, certificates of origin for import or export, or other business licenses or approval documents as provided for by laws or administrative regulations;

(3) illegally operating securities, futures, or insurance business, or illegally engaging in fund payment and settlement business, without the approval of the relevant competent department of the state;

(4) other illegal business operations that seriously disrupt market order.

Article 226: Whoever, by violence or threat, commits any of the following acts, where the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and/or a fine; where the circumstances are particularly serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years and a fine:

(1) forcing another person to buy or sell goods;

(2) forcing another person to provide or accept services;

(3) forcing another person to participate in or withdraw from a tender or auction;

(4) forcing another person to transfer or acquire shares, bonds, or other assets of a company or enterprise;

(5) forcing another person to participate in or withdraw from a specific business activity.

Article 227: Whoever forges or scalps forged train tickets, ship tickets, stamps, or other valuable tickets, where the amount is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years, criminal detention, or public surveillance and/or a fine of not less than one time but not more than five times the face value of the tickets; where the amount is huge, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than two years but not more than seven years and a fine of not less than one time but not more than five times the face value of the tickets.

Whoever scalps train tickets or ship tickets, where the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance and/or a fine of not less than one time but not more than five times the face value of the tickets.

Article 228: Whoever, for the purpose of profit, in violation of land management laws and regulations, illegally transfers or scalps land use rights, where the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and/or a fine of not less than five percent but not more than twenty percent of the price of the illegally transferred or scalped land use rights; where the circumstances are particularly serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years and a fine of not less than five percent but not more than twenty percent of the price of the illegally transferred or scalped land use rights.

Article 229: Where a person from an intermediary organization responsible for asset appraisal, capital verification, certification, accounting, auditing, legal services, sponsorship, safety assessment, environmental impact assessment, environmental monitoring, etc., intentionally provides false certification documents, where the circumstances are serious, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and a fine; under any of the following circumstances, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years and a fine:

(1) providing false certification documents related to securities issuance, such as asset appraisal, accounting, auditing, legal services, or sponsorship, where the circumstances are particularly serious;

(2) providing false certification documents related to major asset transactions, such as asset appraisal, accounting, or auditing, where the circumstances are particularly serious;

(3) providing false certification documents such as safety assessment or environmental impact assessment for major projects or projects involving public safety, causing particularly significant losses to public property, the state, or the interests of the people.

Where a person commits the act specified in the preceding paragraph and simultaneously extorts property from another person or illegally accepts property from another person, constituting a crime, they shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions for the heavier punishment.

Where a person specified in the first paragraph is seriously irresponsible, resulting in the issuance of certification documents that are materially false and causing serious consequences, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and/or a fine.

Article 230: Whoever, in violation of the provisions of the Law on Import and Export Commodity Inspection, evades commodity inspection, and without reporting for inspection, sells or uses imported goods that must be inspected by the commodity inspection authorities, or without reporting for inspection and passing inspection, exports exported goods that must be inspected by the commodity inspection authorities, where the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and/or a fine.

Article 231: Where a unit commits any of the crimes specified in Articles 221 through 230 of this Section, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly responsible persons shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the respective Articles of this Section.

### Chapter 4: Crimes of Infringing upon Citizens' Rights of the Person and Democratic Rights

Article 232: Whoever intentionally kills another person shall be sentenced to death, life imprisonment, or fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years; where the circumstances are relatively minor, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Article 233: Whoever negligently causes the death of another person shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years; where the circumstances are relatively minor, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years. Where this Law provides otherwise, those provisions shall apply.

Article 234: Whoever intentionally inflicts injury upon the body of another person shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Whoever commits the crime specified in the preceding paragraph, causing serious injury to another person, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; where death is caused, or where serious injury is caused by especially cruel means, resulting in severe disability, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death. Where this Law provides otherwise, those provisions shall apply.

Article 234a: Whoever organizes another person to sell human organs shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years and a fine; where the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years and a fine or confiscation of property.

Whoever removes an organ without the consent of the person, or removes an organ from a person under the age of 18, or forces or deceives another person into donating an organ, shall be convicted and punished in accordance with Articles 234 and 232 of this Law.

Whoever, contrary to the wishes of the deceased expressed during their lifetime, removes an organ from the corpse of the deceased, or, where the deceased did not express consent during their lifetime, in violation of state regulations and contrary to the wishes of their close relatives, removes an organ from the corpse of the deceased, shall be convicted and punished in accordance with Article 302 of this Law.

Article 235: Whoever negligently injures another person, causing serious injury, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention. Where this Law provides otherwise, those provisions shall apply.

Article 236: Whoever rapes a woman by violence, coercion, or other means shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Whoever has sexual intercourse with a female child under the age of 14 shall be deemed to have committed rape and shall be given a heavier punishment.

Whoever rapes a woman or has sexual intercourse with a female child, under any of the following circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death:

(1) the circumstances of the rape of a woman or sexual intercourse with a female child are heinous;

(2) raping multiple women or having sexual intercourse with multiple female children;

(3) raping a woman or having sexual intercourse with a female child in a public place in the presence of others;

(4) gang rape by two or more persons;

(5) having sexual intercourse with a female child under the age of 10 or causing injury to the female child;

(6) causing serious injury or death to the victim or causing other serious consequences.

Article 236a: Where a person who has special responsibilities such as guardianship, adoption, care, education, or medical treatment towards a minor female who has reached the age of 14 but is under the age of 16 has sexual relations with that minor female, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years; where the circumstances are heinous, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Where a person commits the act specified in the preceding paragraph and also constitutes the crime specified in Article 236 of this Law, they shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions for the heavier punishment.

Article 237: Whoever forcibly molests another person or insults a woman by violence, coercion, or other methods shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

Whoever commits the crime specified in the preceding paragraph by gathering a crowd or in a public place in the presence of others, or where there are other heinous circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Whoever molests a child shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years; under any of the following circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years:

(1) molesting multiple children or molesting a child multiple times;

(2) gathering a crowd to molest a child, or molesting a child in a public place in the presence of others, where the circumstances are heinous;

(3) causing injury to the child or other serious consequences;

(4) the means of molestation are heinous or there are other heinous circumstances.

Article 238: Whoever unlawfully detains another person or unlawfully deprives another person of their personal freedom by other means shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights. Where there are circumstances of beating or humiliation, a heavier punishment shall be imposed.

Whoever commits the crime specified in the preceding paragraph, causing serious injury to another person, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; where death is caused, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years. Where violence is used to cause injury or death, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with Articles 234 and 232 of this Law.

Whoever unlawfully detains or confines another person for the purpose of recovering a debt shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs.

Where a functionary of a state organ commits any of the crimes specified in the preceding three paragraphs by abusing their powers, they shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the preceding three paragraphs.

Article 239: Whoever kidnaps another person for the purpose of extorting property, or kidnaps another person as a hostage, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment and a fine or confiscation of property; where the circumstances are relatively minor, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years and a fine.

Whoever commits the crime specified in the preceding paragraph and kills the kidnapped person, or intentionally injures the kidnapped person, causing serious injury or death, shall be sentenced to life imprisonment or death and confiscation of property.

Whoever steals an infant or young child for the purpose of extorting property shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs.

Article 240: Whoever abducts and traffics in a woman or child shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years and a fine; under any of the following circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment and a fine or confiscation of property; where the circumstances are particularly serious, shall be sentenced to death and confiscation of property:

(1) being a ringleader of a gang engaged in abducting and trafficking in women or children;

(2) abducting and trafficking in three or more women or children;

(3) raping the abducted woman;

(4) enticing or forcing the abducted woman into prostitution, or selling the abducted woman to another person to force her into prostitution;

(5) kidnapping a woman or child by violence, coercion, or anesthesia for the purpose of selling;

(6) stealing an infant or young child for the purpose of selling;

(7) causing serious injury or death to the abducted woman or child or their relatives, or causing other serious consequences;

(8) selling a woman or child outside the country.

"Abducting and trafficking in a woman or child" means any of the acts of abducting, kidnapping, buying, selling, transporting, or transshipping a woman or child for the purpose of selling.

Article 241: Whoever buys an abducted woman or child shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Whoever buys an abducted woman and forcibly has sexual relations with her shall be convicted and punished in accordance with Article 236 of this Law.

Whoever buys an abducted woman or child and unlawfully deprives or restricts their personal freedom, or commits acts of injury or humiliation, shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the relevant provisions of this Law.

Whoever buys an abducted woman or child and also commits the criminal acts specified in the second and third paragraphs shall be punished in accordance with the provisions on combined punishment for multiple crimes.

Whoever buys an abducted woman or child and then sells them shall be convicted and punished in accordance with Article 240 of this Law.

Whoever buys an abducted woman or child, does not abuse the bought child, and does not obstruct the rescue of the child, may be given a lighter punishment; whoever, in accordance with the wishes of the bought woman, does not obstruct her return to her original place of residence, may be given a lighter or mitigated punishment.

Article 242: Whoever obstructs, by violence or threat, a functionary of a state organ from rescuing a bought woman or child shall be convicted and punished in accordance with Article 277 of this Law.

The ringleader who gathers a crowd to obstruct a functionary of a state organ from rescuing a bought woman or child shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; other participants who use violence or threats shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 243: Whoever fabricates facts to falsely accuse another person, with the intent of subjecting that person to criminal prosecution, where the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance; where serious consequences are caused, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Where a functionary of a state organ commits the crime specified in the preceding paragraph, they shall be given a heavier punishment.

The provisions of the preceding two paragraphs shall not apply to cases where the accusation is not intentionally false but is a mistaken accusation or an unsubstantiated report.

Article 244: Whoever forces another person to work by violence, threat, or restriction of personal freedom shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and a fine; where the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years and a fine.

Whoever, knowing that another person is committing the act specified in the preceding paragraph, recruits or transports persons for them, or otherwise assists in forcing another person to work, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where a unit commits any of the crimes specified in the preceding two paragraphs, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly responsible persons shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Article 244a: Whoever, in violation of labor management laws and regulations, employs a minor under the age of 16 to engage in ultra-intensive physical labor, or to engage in work at heights or underground, or to engage in labor in explosive, flammable, radioactive, toxic, or other hazardous environments, where the circumstances are serious, the directly responsible person shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and a fine; where the circumstances are particularly serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years and a fine.

Where the act specified in the preceding paragraph causes an accident and also constitutes another crime, the offender shall be punished in accordance with the provisions on combined punishment for multiple crimes.

Article 245: Whoever unlawfully searches the body or residence of another person, or unlawfully enters the residence of another person, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Where a judicial officer abuses their powers and commits the crime specified in the preceding paragraph, they shall be given a heavier punishment.

Article 246: Whoever publicly insults another person by violence or other methods, or fabricates facts to defame another person, where the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.The crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph shall be handled only upon complaint, except where it seriously endangers social order or national interests.

If a victim files a complaint with a people's court regarding an act specified in the first paragraph committed through an information network, but has genuine difficulty in providing evidence, the people's court may request the public security authority to provide assistance.

Article 247 Any judicial officer who extorts confessions by torture or uses violence to obtain witness testimony from a criminal suspect or defendant shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention. If the act causes injury, disability, or death, the offender shall be convicted and given a heavier punishment in accordance with Articles 234 and 232 of this Law.

Article 248 Any custodial officer of a prison, detention center, or jail who beats or maltreats a detainee, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years. If the act causes injury, disability, or death, the offender shall be convicted and given a heavier punishment in accordance with Articles 234 and 232 of this Law.

If a custodial officer instigates a detainee to beat or maltreat another detainee, the punishment shall be imposed in accordance with the preceding paragraph.

Article 249 Whoever incites ethnic hatred or ethnic discrimination, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights; if the circumstances are especially serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Article 250 Whoever publishes content in publications that discriminates against or insults ethnic minorities, if the circumstances are flagrant and cause serious consequences, the persons directly responsible shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Article 251 Any state functionary who unlawfully deprives a citizen of their freedom of religious belief or infringes upon the customs and habits of an ethnic minority, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years or criminal detention.

Article 252 Whoever conceals, destroys, or unlawfully opens another person's letters, infringing upon the citizen's right to freedom of correspondence, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than one year or criminal detention.

Article 253 Any postal worker who privately opens, conceals, or destroys mail or telegrams shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years or criminal detention.

If the crime in the preceding paragraph is committed to steal property, the offender shall be convicted and given a heavier punishment in accordance with Article 264 of this Law.

Article 253a Whoever, in violation of relevant state regulations, sells or provides citizens' personal information to others, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine shall also be imposed.

Whoever, in violation of relevant state regulations, sells or provides citizens' personal information obtained during the performance of duties or provision of services to others shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the preceding paragraph.

Whoever steals or otherwise unlawfully obtains citizens' personal information shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

If a unit commits any of the crimes mentioned in the preceding three paragraphs, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the persons directly in charge and other persons directly responsible shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the respective paragraphs.

Article 254 Any state functionary who abuses their power or uses public office for private gain to retaliate against or frame a complainant, petitioner, critic, or whistleblower shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than two years but not more than seven years.

Article 255 Any leader of a company, enterprise, institution, state organ, or organization who retaliates against an accountant or statistician for lawfully performing their duties and resisting acts that violate the Accounting Law or the Statistics Law, if the circumstances are flagrant, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 256 Whoever, in the election of deputies to people's congresses at various levels or leaders of state organs, disrupts the election or obstructs voters and deputies from freely exercising their right to vote and stand for election by means of violence, threats, deception, bribery, falsification of election documents, or false reporting of vote counts, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or deprivation of political rights.

Article 257 Whoever uses violence to interfere with another person's freedom of marriage shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years or criminal detention.

If the crime in the preceding paragraph causes the death of the victim, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than two years but not more than seven years.

The crime in the first paragraph shall be handled only upon complaint.

Article 258 Whoever has a spouse and commits bigamy, or knowingly marries a person who has a spouse, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years or criminal detention.

Article 259 Whoever knowingly cohabits with or marries the spouse of an active-duty military member shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Whoever uses their position or a subordinate relationship to coerce and rape the wife of an active-duty military member shall be convicted and punished in accordance with Article 236 of this Law.

Article 260 Whoever maltreats a family member, if the circumstances are flagrant, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

If the crime in the preceding paragraph causes serious injury or death to the victim, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than two years but not more than seven years.

The crime in the first paragraph shall be handled only upon complaint, except where the victim is unable to file a complaint or is unable to do so due to coercion or intimidation.

Article 260a Any person who has a duty to care for or supervise a minor, elderly person, sick person, or disabled person and maltreats the person under their care or supervision, if the circumstances are flagrant, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

If a unit commits the crime in the preceding paragraph, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the persons directly in charge and other persons directly responsible shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

If an act in the first paragraph also constitutes another crime, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions for the heavier punishment.

Article 261 Whoever has a duty to support an elderly person, minor, sick person, or other person without the ability to live independently but refuses to do so, if the circumstances are flagrant, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Article 262 Whoever abducts a minor under the age of 14, removing them from their family or guardian, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

Article 262a Whoever organizes a person with a disability or a minor under the age of 14 to beg by means of violence or threats shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine shall also be imposed.

Article 262b Whoever organizes minors to engage in theft, fraud, robbery, extortion, or other activities that violate public security administration shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine shall also be imposed.

### Chapter 5: Crimes of Property Violation

Article 263 Whoever robs public or private property by violence, coercion, or other methods shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined; under any of the following circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death, and a fine or confiscation of property shall also be imposed:

(1) Robbery committed inside a dwelling;
(2) Robbery committed on a public transportation vehicle;
(3) Robbery of a bank or other financial institution;
(4) Multiple robberies or robbery involving a huge amount of property;
(5) Robbery causing serious injury or death;
(6) Robbery by impersonating military or police personnel;
(7) Robbery with a firearm;
(8) Robbery of military supplies or materials for emergency rescue, disaster relief, or relief.

Article 264 Whoever steals public or private property, if the amount is relatively large, or if the theft is committed multiple times, inside a dwelling, with a weapon, or by pickpocketing, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the amount is huge or there are other serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and a fine shall also be imposed; if the amount is especially huge or there are other especially serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and a fine or confiscation of property shall also be imposed.

Article 265 Whoever, for the purpose of profit, steals another person's communication line, copies another person's telecommunications code number, or knowingly uses telecommunications equipment or facilities that have been stolen or copied shall be convicted and punished in accordance with Article 264 of this Law.

Article 266 Whoever defrauds public or private property, if the amount is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the amount is huge or there are other serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and a fine shall also be imposed; if the amount is especially huge or there are other especially serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and a fine or confiscation of property shall also be imposed. Where this Law provides otherwise, those provisions shall apply.

Article 267 Whoever forcibly seizes public or private property, if the amount is relatively large, or if the seizure is committed multiple times, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the amount is huge or there are other serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and a fine shall also be imposed; if the amount is especially huge or there are other especially serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and a fine or confiscation of property shall also be imposed.

Whoever forcibly seizes property while carrying a weapon shall be convicted and punished in accordance with Article 263 of this Law.

Article 268 Whoever gathers a crowd to forcibly seize public or private property, if the amount is relatively large or there are other serious circumstances, the ringleaders and active participants shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the amount is huge or there are other especially serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and a fine shall also be imposed.

Article 269 Whoever commits theft, fraud, or forcible seizure and, in order to conceal stolen goods, resist arrest, or destroy criminal evidence, uses violence or threatens violence on the spot shall be convicted and punished in accordance with Article 263 of this Law.

Article 270 Whoever unlawfully takes possession of another person's property entrusted to them for safekeeping, if the amount is relatively large and they refuse to return it, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years, criminal detention, or a fine; if the amount is huge or there are other serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than two years but not more than five years, and a fine shall also be imposed.

Whoever unlawfully takes possession of another person's lost property or buried property, if the amount is relatively large and they refuse to hand it over, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

The crime in this Article shall be handled only upon complaint.

Article 271 Any employee of a company, enterprise, or other unit who, by taking advantage of their position, unlawfully takes possession of the unit's property, if the amount is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined; if the amount is huge, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and a fine shall also be imposed; if the amount is especially huge, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and a fine shall also be imposed.

If a person engaged in public service in a state-owned company, enterprise, or other state-owned unit, or a person assigned by a state-owned company, enterprise, or other state-owned unit to engage in public service in a non-state-owned company, enterprise, or other unit, commits the act in the preceding paragraph, they shall be convicted and punished in accordance with Articles 382 and 383 of this Law.

Article 272 Any employee of a company, enterprise, or other unit who, by taking advantage of their position, misappropriates the unit's funds for personal use or lends them to another person, if the amount is relatively large and the funds are not repaid within three months, or if the amount is relatively large and the funds are used for profit-making activities, even if within three months, or if the funds are used for illegal activities, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the amount of misappropriated funds is huge, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years; if the amount is especially huge, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years.

If a person engaged in public service in a state-owned company, enterprise, or other state-owned unit, or a person assigned by a state-owned company, enterprise, or other state-owned unit to engage in public service in a non-state-owned company, enterprise, or other unit, commits the act in the preceding paragraph, they shall be convicted and punished in accordance with Article 384 of this Law.

If a person who commits the act in the first paragraph returns the misappropriated funds before a public prosecution is initiated, the punishment may be mitigated or reduced. If the crime is relatively minor, the punishment may be reduced or waived.

Article 273 Whoever misappropriates funds or materials allocated for disaster relief, emergency rescue, flood control, special care for entitled groups, poverty alleviation, resettlement, or relief, if the circumstances are serious and cause significant damage to the interests of the state and the people, the persons directly responsible shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 274 Whoever extorts public or private property, if the amount is relatively large or the extortion is committed multiple times, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the amount is huge or there are other serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and a fine shall also be imposed; if the amount is especially huge or there are other especially serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, and a fine shall also be imposed.

Article 275 Whoever intentionally destroys public or private property, if the amount is relatively large or there are other serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or a fine; if the amount is huge or there are other especially serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 276 Whoever, out of personal grievance or revenge or for other personal motives, destroys machinery and equipment, injures draft animals, or otherwise sabotages production and business operations, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 276a Whoever evades paying workers' labor remuneration by transferring property, absconding, or other means, or who has the ability to pay but fails to pay, if the amount is relatively large and payment is still not made after being ordered by the relevant government department, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if serious consequences are caused, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine shall also be imposed.

If a unit commits the crime in the preceding paragraph, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the persons directly in charge and other persons directly responsible shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

If a person who commits the acts in the preceding two paragraphs pays the workers' labor remuneration before a public prosecution is initiated and assumes corresponding compensation liability in accordance with the law, and no serious consequences have been caused, the punishment may be mitigated or waived.

### Chapter 6: Crimes of Obstructing the Administration of Social Order

#### Section 1: Crimes of Disturbing Public Order

Article 277 Whoever obstructs a state functionary from lawfully performing their duties by means of violence or threats shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or a fine.

Whoever obstructs a deputy to the National People's Congress or a local people's congress at any level from lawfully performing their duties as a deputy by means of violence or threats shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Whoever obstructs a staff member of the Red Cross Society from lawfully performing their duties during natural disasters or emergencies by means of violence or threats shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Whoever intentionally obstructs a state security authority or public security authority from lawfully performing state security tasks, without using violence or threats, but causing serious consequences, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Whoever violently attacks a people's police officer who is lawfully performing their duties shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance; if a firearm, controlled knife, or other means such as driving a motor vehicle to ram is used, seriously endangering their personal safety, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 278 Whoever incites a crowd to violently resist the implementation of state laws and administrative regulations shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights; if serious consequences are caused, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 279 Whoever impersonates a state functionary to swindle and deceive shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Whoever impersonates a people's police officer to swindle and deceive shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 280 Whoever forges, alters, buys, sells, steals, robs, or destroys official documents, certificates, or seals of state organs shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and a fine shall also be imposed.

Whoever forges the seals of a company, enterprise, institution, or people's organization shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights, and shall also be fined.

Whoever forges, alters, buys, or sells resident identity cards, passports, social security cards, driver's licenses, or other documents that can be used to prove identity in accordance with the law shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine shall also be imposed.

Article 280a Whoever, in activities where identity documents are required to be provided in accordance with state regulations, uses forged, altered, or another person's resident identity card, passport, social security card, driver's license, or other documents that can be used to prove identity in accordance with the law, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to criminal detention or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

If an act in the preceding paragraph also constitutes another crime, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions for the heavier punishment.

Article 280b Whoever steals or fraudulently uses another person's identity to take the place of another person in obtaining higher education admission qualifications, civil service recruitment qualifications, or employment placement benefits shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined.

Whoever organizes or instigates another person to commit the act in the preceding paragraph shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

If a state functionary commits the acts in the preceding two paragraphs and also constitutes another crime, they shall be punished in accordance with the provisions on combined punishment for multiple crimes.

Article 281 Whoever illegally produces or sells special insignia such as people's police uniforms, vehicle license plates, police equipment, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

If a unit commits the crime in the preceding paragraph, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the persons directly in charge and other persons directly responsible shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 282 Whoever unlawfully obtains state secrets by means of theft, espionage, or bribery shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Whoever unlawfully possesses documents, materials, or other items classified as top secret or secret of the state and refuses to explain their source or purpose shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Article 283 Whoever illegally produces or sells specialized espionage equipment or specialized eavesdropping or covert photography equipment shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine shall also be imposed.

If a unit commits the crime in the preceding paragraph, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the persons directly in charge and other persons directly responsible shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 284 Whoever illegally uses specialized eavesdropping or covert photography equipment, causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Article 284a Whoever organizes cheating in a national examination prescribed by law shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine shall also be imposed.

Whoever provides cheating equipment or other assistance to another person for committing the crime in the preceding paragraph shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Whoever illegally sells or provides the test questions or answers of the examination specified in the first paragraph to another person for the purpose of cheating in the examination shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Whoever takes the examination specified in the first paragraph on behalf of another person, or causes another person to take the examination on their behalf, shall be sentenced to criminal detention or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.Article 285: Whoever, in violation of state regulations, intrudes into a computer information system in the fields of state affairs, national defense construction, or cutting-edge science and technology shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Whoever, in violation of state regulations, intrudes into a computer information system other than those specified in the preceding paragraph or uses other technical means to obtain data stored, processed, or transmitted in that computer information system, or illegally controls that computer information system, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

Whoever provides programs or tools specifically used for intruding into or illegally controlling computer information systems, or knowingly provides programs or tools to another person for committing illegal or criminal acts of intruding into or illegally controlling computer information systems, if the circumstances are serious, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where a unit commits any of the crimes mentioned in the preceding three paragraphs, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly liable persons shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the respective paragraphs.

Article 286: Whoever, in violation of state regulations, deletes, modifies, adds to, or interferes with the functions of a computer information system, causing the computer information system to fail to operate normally, if the consequences are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the consequences are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Whoever, in violation of state regulations, deletes, modifies, or adds to the data and application programs stored, processed, or transmitted in a computer information system, if the consequences are serious, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Whoever intentionally creates or disseminates destructive programs such as computer viruses, affecting the normal operation of a computer system, if the consequences are serious, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Where a unit commits any of the crimes mentioned in the preceding three paragraphs, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly liable persons shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Article 286a: Where a network service provider fails to perform the information network security management obligations prescribed by laws and administrative regulations, and refuses to take corrective measures after being ordered by the regulatory authority, if any of the following circumstances occurs, it shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined:

(1) Causing the widespread dissemination of illegal information;
(2) Causing the leakage of user information, resulting in serious consequences;
(3) Causing the loss of evidence in criminal cases, if the circumstances are serious;
(4) Having other serious circumstances.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly liable persons shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where an act mentioned in the preceding two paragraphs also constitutes another crime, it shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of the heavier punishment.

Article 287: Whoever uses a computer to commit crimes such as financial fraud, theft, embezzlement, misappropriation of public funds, theft of state secrets, or other crimes shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the relevant provisions of this Law.

Article 287a: Whoever uses an information network to commit any of the following acts, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined:

(1) Setting up websites or communication groups for committing fraud, teaching criminal methods, producing or selling prohibited or controlled items, or other illegal or criminal activities;
(2) Publishing information about the production or sale of drugs, firearms, obscene items, or other prohibited or controlled items, or other illegal or criminal information;
(3) Publishing information for committing fraud or other illegal or criminal activities.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly liable persons shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Where an act mentioned in the preceding two paragraphs also constitutes another crime, it shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of the heavier punishment.

Article 287b: Whoever knowingly provides technical support such as Internet access, server hosting, network storage, or communication transmission, or provides assistance such as advertising promotion or payment settlement, for another person's use of an information network to commit a crime, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly liable persons shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Where an act mentioned in the preceding two paragraphs also constitutes another crime, it shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of the heavier punishment.

Article 288: Whoever, in violation of state regulations, sets up or uses a radio station (or station) without authorization, or uses a radio frequency without authorization, interfering with the order of radio communications, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly liable persons shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 289: Where a crowd gathers to engage in "beating, smashing, and looting," causing injury, disability, or death, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Articles 234 and 232 of this Law. Where public or private property is destroyed or looted, in addition to ordering restitution, the principal offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 263 of this Law.

Article 290: Where a crowd gathers to disturb public order, if the circumstances are serious, causing work, production, business, teaching, scientific research, or medical treatment to be impossible, and causing serious losses, the principal offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years; other active participants shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Where a crowd gathers to assault a state organ, causing the work of the state organ to be impossible, and causing serious losses, the principal offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; other active participants shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Whoever repeatedly disrupts the work order of a state organ, and fails to correct after administrative punishment, causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Whoever repeatedly organizes or funds others to illegally gather, disturbing public order, if the circumstances are serious, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 291: Where a crowd gathers to disturb order at stations, docks, civil airports, shopping malls, parks, theaters, exhibition halls, sports grounds, or other public places, or to block traffic or disrupt traffic order, or to resist or obstruct state public security management personnel from performing their duties according to law, if the circumstances are serious, the principal offender shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Article 291a: Whoever releases false explosive, toxic, radioactive, or infectious disease pathogen substances, or fabricates terrorist information such as bomb threats, biological threats, or radiation threats, or knowingly disseminates fabricated terrorist information, seriously disrupting public order, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, or public surveillance; if serious consequences are caused, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Whoever fabricates false information about dangers, epidemics, disasters, or police situations, and disseminates it on information networks or other media, or knowingly disseminates the above false information on information networks or other media, seriously disrupting public order, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance; if serious consequences are caused, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 291b: Whoever throws objects from a building or other high place, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than one year, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

Where an act mentioned in the preceding paragraph also constitutes another crime, it shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of the heavier punishment.

Article 292: Where a crowd gathers to engage in affray, the principal offender and other active participants shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance; if any of the following circumstances exists, the principal offender and other active participants shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years:

(1) Repeatedly gathering a crowd to engage in affray;
(2) The affray involves a large number of people, is large in scale, and has a bad social impact;
(3) Gathering a crowd to engage in affray in a public place or on a major traffic route, causing serious chaos in public order;
(4) Gathering a crowd to engage in affray with weapons.

Where a crowd gathers to engage in affray, causing serious injury or death, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Articles 234 and 232 of this Law.

Article 293: Whoever commits any of the following acts of picking quarrels and provoking trouble, disrupting social order, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, or public surveillance:

(1) Beating another person at will, if the circumstances are flagrant;
(2) Chasing, intercepting, insulting, or intimidating another person, if the circumstances are flagrant;
(3) Forcibly taking or demanding, or arbitrarily damaging or occupying public or private property, if the circumstances are serious;
(4) Creating a disturbance in a public place, causing serious chaos in public order.

Whoever gathers others to repeatedly commit the acts mentioned in the preceding paragraph, seriously disrupting social order, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and may also be fined.

Article 293a: Whoever, under any of the following circumstances, collects illegal debts arising from high-interest lending or similar activities, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined:

(1) Using violence or coercion;
(2) Restricting another person's personal freedom or intruding into another person's residence;
(3) Intimidating, stalking, or harassing another person.

Article 294: Whoever organizes or leads a mafia-style organization shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years and shall also be subject to confiscation of property; those who actively participate shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and may also be fined or have their property confiscated; other participants shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights, and may also be fined.

Where personnel from a foreign mafia-style organization come to the People's Republic of China to develop organization members, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Where a state functionary harbors a mafia-style organization or connives at its illegal or criminal activities, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Where a person commits any of the crimes mentioned in the preceding three paragraphs and also commits other criminal acts, they shall be punished in accordance with the provisions on combined punishment for multiple crimes.

A mafia-style organization shall simultaneously possess the following characteristics:

(1) Forming a relatively stable criminal organization with a relatively large number of members, clear organizers and leaders, and basically fixed core members;
(2) Obtaining economic benefits in an organized manner through illegal or criminal activities or other means, possessing a certain economic strength to support the organization's activities;
(3) Using violence, threats, or other means to repeatedly engage in illegal or criminal activities in an organized manner, committing evil acts, oppressing and harming the masses;
(4) Through the commission of illegal or criminal activities, or by taking advantage of the harboring or connivance of state functionaries, dominating one area, forming illegal control or significant influence in a certain region or industry, and seriously disrupting economic and social order.

Article 295: Whoever teaches criminal methods shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, or public surveillance; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment.

Article 296: Where a gathering, procession, or demonstration is held without applying for permission as required by law, or without obtaining permission after application, or is not conducted in accordance with the start and end times, locations, and routes approved by the competent authority, and the order to disperse is disobeyed, seriously disrupting social order, the person in charge and the directly responsible person of the gathering, procession, or demonstration shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Article 297: Whoever, in violation of legal provisions, carries weapons, controlled knives, or explosives to participate in a gathering, procession, or demonstration shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Article 298: Whoever disturbs, assaults, or otherwise disrupts a lawfully held gathering, procession, or demonstration, causing public order chaos, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Article 299: Whoever, in a public place, intentionally insults the national flag or national emblem of the People's Republic of China by burning, damaging, defacing, soiling, trampling, or other means shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Whoever, in a public place, intentionally alters the lyrics or music score of the national anthem of the People's Republic of China, plays or sings the national anthem in a distorted or derogatory manner, or otherwise insults the national anthem, if the circumstances are serious, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 299a: Whoever insults, slanders, or otherwise infringes upon the reputation or honor of heroes and martyrs, harming the public interest, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Article 300: Whoever organizes or uses superstitious sects, cult organizations, or superstition to undermine the implementation of state laws and administrative regulations shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined or have their property confiscated; if the circumstances are relatively minor, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

Whoever organizes or uses superstitious sects, cult organizations, or superstition to deceive others, causing serious injury or death, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where a person commits the crime mentioned in the first paragraph and also commits other criminal acts such as rape of a woman or fraud for property, they shall be punished in accordance with the provisions on combined punishment for multiple crimes.

Article 301: Where a crowd gathers to engage in promiscuous activities, the principal offender or those who repeatedly participate shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Whoever lures a minor to participate in a crowd gathering for promiscuous activities shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 302: Whoever steals, insults, or intentionally destroys a corpse, skeleton, or ashes shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Article 303: Whoever, for the purpose of profit, gathers a crowd to engage in gambling or makes gambling their profession shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined.

Whoever operates a casino shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined.

Whoever organizes citizens of the People's Republic of China to participate in gambling abroad (or outside the territory), if the amount involved is huge or there are other serious circumstances, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 304: Where a postal worker, due to serious dereliction of duty, intentionally delays the delivery of mail, causing significant losses to public property, the state, or the interests of the people, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years or criminal detention.

#### Section 2: Crimes of Impairing Justice

Article 305: In criminal proceedings, if a witness, expert evaluator, recorder, or translator intentionally makes false testimony, evaluation, record, or translation concerning circumstances that are important to the case, with the intent to frame another person or conceal criminal evidence, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 306: In criminal proceedings, if a defender or agent ad litem destroys or forges evidence, helps a party destroy or forge evidence, or threatens or induces a witness to change their testimony against the facts or to give false testimony, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Where the testimony of a witness or other evidence provided, presented, or cited by a defender or agent ad litem is inaccurate, but not intentionally forged, it shall not be considered as forging evidence.

Article 307: Whoever uses violence, threats, bribery, or other means to prevent a witness from testifying or to instruct another person to give false testimony shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Whoever helps a party destroy or forge evidence, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Where a judicial officer commits any of the crimes mentioned in the preceding two paragraphs, they shall be given a heavier punishment.

Article 307a: Whoever files a civil lawsuit based on fabricated facts, disrupting judicial order or seriously infringing upon the lawful rights and interests of others, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly liable persons shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Where an act mentioned in the first paragraph illegally possesses another person's property or evades lawful debts, and also constitutes another crime, it shall be convicted and given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the heavier punishment.

Where a judicial officer, by taking advantage of their position, jointly commits any of the acts mentioned in the preceding three paragraphs with another person, they shall be given a heavier punishment; if it also constitutes another crime, they shall be convicted and given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the heavier punishment.

Article 308: Whoever retaliates against a witness shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 308a: Where a judicial officer, defender, agent ad litem, or other participant in proceedings discloses information that should not be disclosed in a case tried in camera, causing the information to be publicly disseminated or other serious consequences, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

Where an act mentioned in the preceding paragraph discloses state secrets, the offender shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 398 of this Law.

Whoever publicly discloses or reports the case information specified in the first paragraph, if the circumstances are serious, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly liable persons shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Article 309: Whoever commits any of the following acts disrupting court order shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or a fine:

(1) Gathering a crowd to make a din or assault the court;
(2) Beating a judicial officer or participant in proceedings;
(3) Insulting, slandering, or threatening a judicial officer or participant in proceedings, refusing to obey court orders, and seriously disrupting court order;
(4) Damaging court facilities, seizing or destroying litigation documents, evidence, or other acts disrupting court order, if the circumstances are serious.

Article 310: Whoever knowingly provides a hiding place or property to a criminal, or helps them escape or gives false testimony to shield them, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Where a person commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph and has prior collusion with the criminal, they shall be treated as a joint offender.

Article 311: Whoever knowingly knows that another person has committed espionage or terrorist or extremist crimes, and refuses to provide information when the judicial authorities investigate the relevant circumstances or collect relevant evidence, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Article 312: Whoever knowingly harbors, transfers, purchases, sells on commission, or otherwise conceals or disguises the proceeds of crime and the income generated therefrom shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

Where a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, a fine shall be imposed on the unit, and the directly responsible person in charge and other directly liable persons shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.Article 313: Whoever, being able to execute a legally effective judgment or order of a people's court, refuses to do so, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or a fine; if the circumstances are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

If a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisor and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 314: Whoever conceals, transfers, sells, or intentionally damages or destroys property that has been sealed up, distrained, or frozen by a judicial organ, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or a fine.

Article 315: Any criminal lawfully held in custody who commits any of the following acts disrupting the order of supervision, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years:

(1) beating a supervisory officer;

(2) organizing other persons under supervision to disrupt the order of supervision;

(3) gathering a crowd to create a disturbance, thereby disrupting the normal order of supervision; or

(4) beating, subjecting to corporal punishment, or instigating others to beat or subject to corporal punishment other persons under supervision.

Article 316: Any criminal, defendant, or criminal suspect lawfully held in custody who escapes shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

Whoever forcibly rescues a criminal, defendant, or criminal suspect under escort shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years.

Article 317: The ringleaders and active participants of an organized jailbreak shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years; other participants shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

The ringleaders and active participants of a riotous jailbreak or a gathering of people to forcibly rescue a prisoner with weapons shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment; if the circumstances are especially serious, shall be sentenced to death; other participants shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

#### Section 3: Crimes of Obstructing National Border (Frontier) Administration

Article 318: Whoever organizes others to secretly cross the national border (frontier) shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than two years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined; under any of the following circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined or have property confiscated:

(1) being the ringleader of a group organizing others to secretly cross the national border (frontier);

(2) repeatedly organizing others to secretly cross the national border (frontier) or organizing a large number of persons to do so;

(3) causing serious injury or death to the persons being organized;

(4) depriving or restricting the personal freedom of the persons being organized;

(5) resisting inspection by means of violence or threat;

(6) having a huge amount of illegal gains; or

(7) having other especially serious circumstances.

Whoever commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph and also commits crimes such as killing, injuring, raping, or trafficking in the persons being organized, or commits crimes such as killing or injuring inspection personnel, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions on combined punishment for multiple crimes.

Article 319: Whoever, under the pretext of labor export, economic and trade exchanges, or other names, practices fraud to obtain exit documents such as passports or visas for the purpose of organizing others to secretly cross the national border (frontier) shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined.

If a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisor and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 320: Whoever provides another person with a forged or altered passport, visa, or other entry-exit document, or sells a passport, visa, or other entry-exit document shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years, and shall also be fined.

Article 321: Whoever transports another person to secretly cross the national border (frontier) shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; under any of the following circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined:

(1) repeatedly engaging in transportation or transporting a large number of persons;

(2) using vessels, vehicles, or other means of transport that lack necessary safety conditions, sufficient to cause serious consequences;

(3) having a huge amount of illegal gains; or

(4) having other especially serious circumstances.

Whoever, in the course of transporting another person to secretly cross the national border (frontier), causes serious injury or death to the person being transported, or resists inspection by means of violence or threat, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years, and shall also be fined.

Whoever commits the crimes mentioned in the preceding two paragraphs and also commits crimes such as killing, injuring, raping, or trafficking in the persons being transported, or commits crimes such as killing or injuring inspection personnel, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions on combined punishment for multiple crimes.

Article 322: Whoever, in violation of the laws and regulations on national border (frontier) administration, secretly crosses the national border (frontier), if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than one year, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; whoever secretly crosses the national border (frontier) for the purpose of participating in a terrorist organization, receiving terrorist activity training, or carrying out terrorist activities shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than one year but not more than three years, and shall also be fined.

Article 323: Whoever intentionally damages boundary tablets, boundary markers, or permanent survey markers along the national border shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

#### Section 4: Crimes of Obstructing Cultural Relics Administration

Article 324: Whoever intentionally damages or destroys precious cultural relics under state protection or cultural relics designated as a major national protected cultural relic site or a provincial protected cultural relic site shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined.

Whoever intentionally damages or destroys a famous historical and cultural site under state protection, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

Whoever negligently damages or destroys precious cultural relics under state protection or cultural relics designated as a major national protected cultural relic site or a provincial protected cultural relic site, causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 325: Whoever, in violation of the laws and regulations on cultural relics protection, privately sells or gives as a gift to a foreigner any precious cultural relic in his collection that is prohibited from being exported by the state shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and may also be fined.

If a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisor and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 326: Whoever, for the purpose of profit, resells cultural relics the trading of which is prohibited by the state, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined.

If a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisor and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 327: Where a state-owned museum, library, or other unit, in violation of the laws and regulations on cultural relics protection, sells or privately gives as a gift any cultural relics in its collection under state protection to a non-state-owned unit or individual, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisor and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 328: Whoever illegally excavates and robs an ancient cultural site or ancient tomb of historical, artistic, or scientific value shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are relatively minor, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; under any of the following circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined or have property confiscated:

(1) illegally excavating and robbing an ancient cultural site or ancient tomb designated as a major national protected cultural relic site or a provincial protected cultural relic site;

(2) being the ringleader of a group illegally excavating and robbing ancient cultural sites or ancient tombs;

(3) repeatedly illegally excavating and robbing ancient cultural sites or ancient tombs; or

(4) illegally excavating and robbing an ancient cultural site or ancient tomb, and stealing precious cultural relics or causing serious damage to precious cultural relics.

Whoever illegally excavates and robs ancient human fossils or ancient vertebrate fossils of scientific value under state protection shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 329: Whoever forcibly seizes or steals archives owned by the state shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

Whoever, in violation of the Archives Law, privately sells or transfers archives owned by the state, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Whoever commits the acts mentioned in the preceding two paragraphs, which also constitute other crimes provided for in this Law, shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions on the heavier punishment.

#### Section 5: Crimes of Endangering Public Health

Article 330: Whoever, in violation of the provisions of the Law on the Prevention and Treatment of Infectious Diseases, commits any of the following acts, causing the spread of a Class A infectious disease or an infectious disease for which prevention and control measures applicable to Class A infectious diseases are determined according to law, or creating a serious risk of such spread, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the consequences are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years:

(1) the drinking water supplied by a water supply unit does not meet the hygienic standards prescribed by the state;

(2) refusing to carry out disinfection treatment of sewage, waste, premises, or articles contaminated by the pathogens of an infectious disease in accordance with the hygienic requirements put forward by the disease prevention and control institution;

(3) permitting or conniving at a patient of an infectious disease, a pathogen carrier, or a suspected patient of an infectious disease to engage in work that is prohibited by the health administration department of the State Council as being likely to cause the spread of the infectious disease;

(4) selling or transporting articles contaminated or likely to be contaminated by the pathogens of an infectious disease from an epidemic area without carrying out disinfection treatment; or

(5) refusing to implement the prevention and control measures proposed by the people's government at or above the county level or the disease prevention and control institution in accordance with the Law on the Prevention and Treatment of Infectious Diseases.

If a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisor and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

The scope of Class A infectious diseases shall be determined in accordance with the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Prevention and Treatment of Infectious Diseases and the relevant provisions of the State Council.

Article 331: Any person engaged in the experimentation, storage, carrying, or transportation of bacterial strains or virus strains of infectious diseases who, in violation of the relevant provisions of the health administration department of the State Council, causes the spread of such bacterial strains or virus strains, if the consequences are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the consequences are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 332: Whoever, in violation of the provisions on frontier health and quarantine, causes the spread of a quarantinable infectious disease or creates a serious risk of such spread shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

If a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisor and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 333: Whoever illegally organizes another person to sell blood shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, and shall also be fined; whoever compels another person to sell blood by means of violence or threat shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined.

Whoever commits the act mentioned in the preceding paragraph, causing injury to another person, shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 234 of this Law.

Article 334: Whoever illegally collects or supplies blood, or produces or supplies blood products, which do not meet the standards prescribed by the state and are sufficient to endanger human health, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined; if serious harm is caused to human health, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined; if especially serious consequences are caused, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined or have property confiscated.

Where a department approved by the competent state authority to collect or supply blood or produce or supply blood products fails to carry out testing in accordance with the regulations or violates other operational rules, causing harm to another person's health, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisor and other directly responsible personnel shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

Article 334-1: Whoever, in violation of the relevant state regulations, illegally collects human genetic resources of China or illegally transports, mails, or carries human genetic resource materials of China out of the country, endangering public health or public social interests, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

Article 335: Any medical worker who, due to gross irresponsibility, causes the death of a patient or seriously impairs the patient's health shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 336: Whoever, without obtaining the qualification for medical practice, unlawfully practices medicine, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if serious harm is caused to the patient's health, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined; if death of the patient is caused, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, and shall also be fined.

Whoever, without obtaining the qualification for medical practice, performs a tubal ligation reversal operation, a false sterilization operation, a pregnancy termination operation, or removes an intrauterine device for another person without authorization, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if serious harm is caused to the patient's health, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined; if death of the patient is caused, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, and shall also be fined.

Article 336-1: Whoever implants a gene-edited or cloned human embryo into a human or animal body, or implants a gene-edited or cloned animal embryo into a human body, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

Article 337: Whoever, in violation of the state regulations on animal and plant epidemic prevention and quarantine, causes a major animal or plant epidemic, or creates a risk of causing a major animal or plant epidemic, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

If a unit commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the directly responsible supervisor and other directly responsible personnel shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

#### Section 6: Crimes of Impairing the Protection of Environmental Resources

Article 338: Whoever, in violation of state regulations, discharges, dumps, or disposes of radioactive waste, waste containing pathogens of infectious diseases, toxic substances, or other hazardous substances, thereby seriously polluting the environment, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined; under any of the following circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years, and shall also be fined:

(1) discharging, dumping, or disposing of radioactive waste, waste containing pathogens of infectious diseases, or toxic substances in key protected areas determined according to law, such as drinking water source protection areas or core protection areas of nature reserves, where the circumstances are especially serious;

(2) discharging, dumping, or disposing of radioactive waste, waste containing pathogens of infectious diseases, or toxic substances into the waters of major rivers or lakes designated by the state, where the circumstances are especially serious;

(3) causing the loss of basic functions or permanent destruction of a large amount of permanent basic farmland; or

(4) causing multiple persons to suffer serious injury or serious illness, or causing persons to suffer serious disability or death.

Whoever commits the act mentioned in the preceding paragraph, which also constitutes another crime, shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions on the heavier punishment.

Article 339: Whoever, in violation of state regulations, imports solid waste from abroad and dumps, piles up, or disposes of it within the territory of China shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined; if a major environmental pollution accident is caused, resulting in heavy losses of public or private property or serious harm to human health, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined; if the consequences are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, and shall also be fined.

Whoever, without permission from the competent department of the State Council, imports solid waste from abroad as raw material, causing a major environmental pollution accident, resulting in heavy losses of public or private property or serious harm to human health, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined; if the consequences are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined.

Whoever, under the pretext of using as raw material, imports solid waste, liquid waste, or gaseous waste that cannot be used as raw material shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Paragraphs 2 and 3 of Article 152 of this Law.

Article 340: Whoever, in violation of the laws and regulations on the protection of aquatic resources, catches aquatic products in a prohibited fishing area, during a prohibited fishing period, or using prohibited fishing gear or methods, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or a fine.

Article 341: Whoever illegally hunts or kills precious or endangered wildlife under special state protection, or illegally purchases, transports, or sells such wildlife or their products, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, and shall also be fined or have property confiscated.

Whoever, in violation of hunting laws and regulations, hunts in a prohibited hunting area, during a prohibited hunting period, or using prohibited hunting gear or methods, thereby damaging wildlife resources, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or a fine.

Whoever, in violation of the laws and regulations on wildlife protection and management, illegally hunts, purchases, transports, or sells terrestrial wildlife that naturally grows and breeds in the wild environment, other than those specified in the first paragraph, for the purpose of consumption, if the circumstances are serious, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 342: Whoever, in violation of the laws and regulations on land administration, illegally occupies cultivated land, forestland, or other agricultural land, changes the use of the occupied land, in a relatively large amount, and causes a large amount of damage to the cultivated land, forestland, or other agricultural land, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

Article 342-1: Whoever, in violation of the laws and regulations on the administration of nature reserves, engages in reclamation or development activities or constructs buildings in a national park or national nature reserve, causing serious consequences or having other heinous circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

Whoever commits the act mentioned in the preceding paragraph, which also constitutes another crime, shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions on the heavier punishment.

Article 343: Whoever, in violation of the provisions of the Mineral Resources Law, mines without a mining license, enters a mining area designated by the state, a mining area of significant value to the national economy, or another person's mining area to mine without authorization, or mines a specific mineral species for which protective mining is prescribed by the state without authorization, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

Whoever, in violation of the provisions of the Mineral Resources Law, mines mineral resources using destructive mining methods, causing serious damage to mineral resources, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined.

Article 344: Whoever, in violation of state regulations, illegally fells or destroys precious trees or other plants under special state protection, or illegally purchases, transports, processes, or sells precious trees or other plants under special state protection or their products, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and shall also be fined.

Article 344-1: Whoever, in violation of state regulations, illegally introduces, releases, or discards invasive alien species, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.Article 345: Whoever illegally fells forests or other trees in relatively large quantities shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the quantity is huge, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine; if the quantity is especially huge, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years, and a fine.

Whoever, in violation of the provisions of the Forest Law, indiscriminately fells forests or other trees in relatively large quantities shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the quantity is huge, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine.

Whoever illegally purchases or transports trees that he knows to have been illegally or indiscriminately felled, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine.

Whoever illegally or indiscriminately fells forests or other trees in a nature reserve under State protection shall be given a heavier punishment.

Article 346: Where a unit commits any of the crimes provided for in Articles 338 through 345 of this Section, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the respective articles of this Section.

#### Section 7: Crimes of Smuggling, Trafficking, Transporting, and Manufacturing Narcotic Drugs

Article 347: Whoever smuggles, traffics, transports, or manufactures narcotic drugs, regardless of the quantity involved, shall be investigated for criminal liability and subjected to criminal punishment.

Whoever smuggles, traffics, transports, or manufactures narcotic drugs under any of the following circumstances shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of 15 years, life imprisonment, or death, and shall also have his property confiscated:

(1) smuggling, trafficking, transporting, or manufacturing opium of not less than 1,000 grams, heroin or methamphetamine of not less than 50 grams, or other narcotic drugs in large quantities;

(2) being the ringleader of a drug smuggling, trafficking, transporting, or manufacturing group;

(3) armed escorting of smuggling, trafficking, transporting, or manufacturing of narcotic drugs;

(4) violently resisting inspection, detention, or arrest, if the circumstances are serious; or

(5) participating in organized international drug trafficking activities.

Whoever smuggles, traffics, transports, or manufactures opium of not less than 200 grams but less than 1,000 grams, heroin or methamphetamine of not less than 10 grams but less than 50 grams, or other narcotic drugs in relatively large quantities shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years and shall also be fined.

Whoever smuggles, traffics, transports, or manufactures opium of less than 200 grams, heroin or methamphetamine of less than 10 grams, or other narcotic drugs in small quantities shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine.

Where a unit commits the crime provided for in the second, third, or fourth paragraph, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the respective paragraphs.

Whoever uses or instigates a minor to engage in smuggling, trafficking, transporting, or manufacturing of narcotic drugs, or sells narcotic drugs to a minor, shall be given a heavier punishment.

With respect to persons who have repeatedly committed the crime of smuggling, trafficking, transporting, or manufacturing narcotic drugs and have not been dealt with, the quantity of narcotic drugs involved shall be computed cumulatively.

Article 348: Whoever illegally possesses opium of not less than 1,000 grams, heroin or methamphetamine of not less than 50 grams, or other narcotic drugs in large quantities shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years or life imprisonment, and shall also be fined; whoever illegally possesses opium of not less than 200 grams but less than 1,000 grams, heroin or methamphetamine of not less than 10 grams but less than 50 grams, or other narcotic drugs in relatively large quantities shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine.

Article 349: Whoever harbors a criminal who smuggles, traffics, transports, or manufactures narcotic drugs, or whoever conceals, transfers, or keeps for such a criminal the narcotic drugs or the proceeds of crime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

If a drug enforcement officer or any other functionary of a State organ covers up or harbors a criminal who smuggles, traffics, transports, or manufactures narcotic drugs, he shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Whoever, prior to the commission of the crime provided for in the preceding two paragraphs, conspires with the criminal shall be punished as a joint offender in the crime of smuggling, trafficking, transporting, or manufacturing narcotic drugs.

Article 350: Whoever, in violation of State regulations, illegally produces, trades in, or transports acetic anhydride, ether, chloroform, or any other raw material or reagent used in the manufacture of narcotic drugs, or carries the above-mentioned substances into or out of the country, if the circumstances are relatively serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine; if the circumstances are especially serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years, and a fine or confiscation of property.

Whoever, knowing that another person is manufacturing narcotic drugs, produces or trades in or transports the substances provided for in the preceding paragraph for him shall be punished as a joint offender in the crime of manufacturing narcotic drugs.

Where a unit commits the crime provided for in the preceding two paragraphs, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs.

Article 351: Whoever illegally cultivates opium poppy, cannabis, or any other mother plants of narcotic drugs shall be forced to uproot them. Whoever does so under any of the following circumstances shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined:

(1) cultivating opium poppy of not less than 500 plants but less than 3,000 plants, or cultivating other mother plants of narcotic drugs in relatively large quantities;

(2) cultivating such plants again after being dealt with by the public security organ; or

(3) resisting the uprooting of such plants.

Whoever illegally cultivates opium poppy of not less than 3,000 plants or other mother plants of narcotic drugs in large quantities shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years, and shall also be fined or have his property confiscated.

Whoever illegally cultivates opium poppy or other mother plants of narcotic drugs but voluntarily uproots them before harvest may be exempted from punishment.

Article 352: Whoever illegally buys, sells, transports, carries, or possesses the seeds or seedlings of opium poppy or other mother plants of narcotic drugs that have not been inactivated, if the quantity is relatively large, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

Article 353: Whoever lures, instigates, or deceives another person into drug ingestion or injection shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine.

Whoever compels another person to ingest or inject narcotic drugs shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined.

Whoever lures, instigates, deceives, or compels a minor to ingest or inject narcotic drugs shall be given a heavier punishment.

Article 354: Whoever provides shelter for another person to ingest or inject narcotic drugs shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined.

Article 355: Any person who is lawfully engaged in the production, transportation, management, or use of narcotic drugs or psychotropic substances under State control and who, in violation of State regulations, provides narcotic drugs or psychotropic substances that are under State control and can cause addiction to persons who ingest or inject narcotic drugs shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine. If he provides such substances to criminals who smuggle or traffic in narcotic drugs, or provides them for the purpose of profit to persons who ingest or inject narcotic drugs, he shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 347 of this Law.

Where a unit commits the crime provided for in the preceding paragraph, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 355a: Whoever lures, instigates, or deceives an athlete into using stimulants to participate in major domestic or international sports competitions, or knowingly provides stimulants to an athlete who is to participate in such competitions, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined.

Whoever organizes or compels an athlete to use stimulants to participate in major domestic or international sports competitions shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 356: Whoever has been previously punished for the crime of smuggling, trafficking, transporting, manufacturing, or illegally possessing narcotic drugs and again commits any of the crimes provided for in this Section shall be given a heavier punishment.

Article 357: For the purposes of this Law, "narcotic drugs" refer to opium, heroin, methamphetamine (ice), morphine, cannabis, cocaine, and other narcotic drugs and psychotropic substances that are under State control and can cause addiction.

The quantity of narcotic drugs involved in the crimes of smuggling, trafficking, transporting, manufacturing, or illegally possessing narcotic drugs shall be calculated on the basis of the verified quantity, without conversion based on purity.

#### Section 8: Crimes of Organizing, Compelling, Luring, Sheltering, or Procuring Prostitution

Article 358: Whoever organizes or compels another person to engage in prostitution shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and a fine or confiscation of property.

Whoever organizes or compels a minor to engage in prostitution shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Whoever commits the crime provided for in the preceding two paragraphs and also commits the crimes of killing, injuring, raping, kidnapping, etc., shall be punished in accordance with the provisions on combined punishment for several crimes.

Whoever recruits, transports, or otherwise assists in organizing another person to engage in prostitution shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years, and a fine.

Article 359: Whoever lures, shelters, or procures another person to engage in prostitution shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years, and a fine.

Whoever lures a girl under the age of 14 to engage in prostitution shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years, and shall also be fined.

Article 360: Whoever, knowing that he or she is suffering from a serious venereal disease such as syphilis or gonorrhea, engages in prostitution or patronizes prostitutes shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined.

Article 361: Any person in a hotel, catering service, cultural entertainment, taxi service, or similar unit who, using the conditions of the unit, organizes, compels, lures, shelters, or procures another person to engage in prostitution shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Articles 358 and 359 of this Law.

If the principal responsible person of any of the units listed in the preceding paragraph commits the crime provided for in the preceding paragraph, he shall be given a heavier punishment.

Article 362: Any person in a hotel, catering service, cultural entertainment, taxi service, or similar unit who, when the public security organ investigates activities of prostitution or patronizing prostitutes, tips off the lawbreakers or criminals, if the circumstances are serious, shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 310 of this Law.

#### Section 9: Crimes of Producing, Selling, and Disseminating Obscene Materials

Article 363: Whoever, for the purpose of profit, produces, reproduces, publishes, sells, or disseminates obscene materials shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and a fine; if the circumstances are especially serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and a fine or confiscation of property.

Whoever provides another person with a book number to publish obscene books or periodicals shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if he provides a book number knowing that another person will use it to publish obscene books or periodicals, he shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 364: Whoever disseminates obscene books, periodicals, films, audio or video recordings, pictures, or other obscene materials, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Whoever organizes the showing of obscene films, videos, or other audio or video products shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and a fine.

Whoever produces or reproduces obscene films, videos, or other audio or video products and organizes their showing shall be given a heavier punishment in accordance with the provisions of the second paragraph.

Whoever disseminates obscene materials to a minor under the age of 18 shall be given a heavier punishment.

Article 365: Whoever organizes obscene performances shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and a fine.

Article 366: Where a unit commits any of the crimes provided for in Articles 363, 364, and 365 of this Section, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the respective articles.

Article 367: For the purposes of this Law, "obscene materials" refer to books, periodicals, films, videotapes, audiotapes, pictures, and other obscene materials that explicitly depict sexual acts or blatantly promote pornography.

Scientific works on human physiology or medical knowledge are not obscene materials.

Literary and artistic works of artistic value that contain erotic content are not deemed obscene materials.

### Chapter VII: Crimes of Impairing National Defense Interests

Article 368: Whoever, by means of violence or threat, obstructs a member of the armed forces from performing his duties according to law shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or a fine.

Whoever intentionally obstructs military operations of the armed forces, causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

Article 369: Whoever sabotages weapons, military installations, or military communications shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance; whoever sabotages important weapons, military installations, or military communications shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are especially serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death.

Whoever negligently commits the crime provided for in the preceding paragraph, causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if especially serious consequences are caused, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Whoever commits the crime provided for in the preceding two paragraphs during wartime shall be given a heavier punishment.

Article 370: Whoever knowingly provides substandard weapons or military installations to the armed forces shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are especially serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death.

Whoever negligently commits the crime provided for in the preceding paragraph, causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if especially serious consequences are caused, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Where a unit commits the crime provided for in the first paragraph, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph.

Article 371: Whoever gathers a crowd to assault a military restricted zone, seriously disrupting the order of the military restricted zone, shall, if he is the ringleader, be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; if he is an active participant, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Whoever gathers a crowd to disturb the order of a military administrative zone, if the circumstances are so serious that work in the military administrative zone cannot be carried on and heavy losses are caused, shall, if he is the ringleader, be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years; if he is an active participant, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights.

Article 372: Whoever impersonates a member of the armed forces to bluff and deceive shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Article 373: Whoever incites a member of the armed forces to desert from the unit, or knowingly employs a deserter from the armed forces, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance.

Article 374: Whoever engages in malpractices for personal gain in conscription work, accepting or sending unqualified recruits, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if especially serious consequences are caused, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 375: Whoever forges, alters, buys, sells, steals, or forcibly seizes the official documents, certificates, or seals of the armed forces shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, public surveillance, or deprivation of political rights; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Whoever illegally produces or buys or sells uniforms of the armed forces, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined.

Whoever forges, steals, buys, sells, or illegally provides or uses special signs such as vehicle license plates of the armed forces, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance, and shall also, or shall only, be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years, and a fine.

Where a unit commits the crime provided for in the second or third paragraph, it shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the respective paragraphs.

Article 376: If a reservist refuses or evades enlistment or military training during wartime, if the circumstances are serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

If a citizen refuses or evades military service during wartime, if the circumstances are serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years or criminal detention.

Article 377: Whoever intentionally provides false enemy information to the armed forces during wartime, causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if especially serious consequences are caused, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment.

Article 378: Whoever spreads rumors to create confusion and disturb the morale of the armed forces during wartime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years, criminal detention, or public surveillance; if the circumstances are serious, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Article 379: Whoever knowingly provides a deserter from the armed forces with a hiding place or property during wartime, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 380: Whoever refuses or intentionally delays military orders during wartime, if the circumstances are serious, the unit shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the crime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if serious consequences are caused, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Article 381: Whoever refuses military requisition or expropriation during wartime, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

### Chapter VIII: Crimes of Embezzlement and Bribery

Article 382: Any State functionary who, by taking advantage of his office, misappropriates, steals, swindles, or otherwise illegally takes possession of public property shall be guilty of embezzlement.

Any person who is entrusted by a State organ, State-owned company, enterprise, institution, or people's organization to manage or operate State-owned property and who, by taking advantage of his office, misappropriates, steals, swindles, or otherwise illegally takes possession of the State-owned property shall be deemed to have committed embezzlement.

Whoever colludes with the persons mentioned in the preceding two paragraphs to engage in embezzlement shall be punished as a joint offender.

Article 383: Whoever commits the crime of embezzlement shall be punished according to the seriousness of the circumstances in accordance with the following provisions:

(1) If the amount of embezzlement is relatively large or if there are other relatively serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and a fine.

(2) If the amount of embezzlement is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years, and a fine or confiscation of property.

(3) If the amount of embezzlement is especially huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, the sentence shall be fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment, and a fine or confiscation of property; if the amount is especially huge and the interests of the State and the people are especially seriously damaged, the sentence shall be life imprisonment or death, and confiscation of property.

Where a person has committed embezzlement on multiple occasions and has not been dealt with, the punishment shall be based on the cumulative amount of embezzlement.

Whoever commits the crime provided for in the first paragraph and, before the initiation of public prosecution, truthfully confesses his crime, sincerely repents, actively returns the embezzled property, and avoids or reduces the occurrence of damage, if the circumstances fall under item (1), may be given a lighter or mitigated punishment or be exempted from punishment; if the circumstances fall under item (2) or (3), may be given a lighter punishment.

Whoever commits the crime provided for in the first paragraph and, under the circumstances of item (3), is sentenced to death with a two-year reprieve, the People's Court may, based on the circumstances of the crime, simultaneously decide that after the two-year period of the death sentence with reprieve expires and the sentence is lawfully commuted to life imprisonment, he shall be subject to life imprisonment without commutation or parole.Article 384 Any state functionary who, taking advantage of his position, misappropriates public funds for personal use to engage in illegal activities, or misappropriates a relatively large amount of public funds for profit-making activities, or misappropriates a relatively large amount of public funds that is not repaid within three months, commits the crime of misappropriation of public funds and shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years. If the amount of misappropriated public funds is huge and not repaid, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment.

Whoever misappropriates for personal use funds or materials allocated for disaster relief, emergency rescue, flood control, special care for disabled servicemen and family members of revolutionary martyrs, poverty alleviation, resettlement of migrants, or social relief shall be given a heavier punishment.

Article 385 Any state functionary who, taking advantage of his position, extorts property from another person, or illegally accepts another person's property and seeks benefits for that person, commits the crime of acceptance of bribes.

Any state functionary who, in economic activities, violates state regulations by accepting various kinds of rebates or handling fees for personal ownership shall be punished as for acceptance of bribes.

Article 386 Any person who commits the crime of acceptance of bribes shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 383 of this Law, based on the amount of bribes accepted and the circumstances. Whoever extorts bribes shall be given a heavier punishment.

Article 387 Any state organ, state-owned company, enterprise, institution, or people's organization that extorts or illegally accepts another person's property and seeks benefits for that person, if the circumstances are serious, shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the offense shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

If any unit mentioned in the preceding paragraph, in economic activities, secretly accepts various kinds of rebates or handling fees off the books, it shall be punished as for acceptance of bribes, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the offense shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 388 Any state functionary who, taking advantage of the convenience of his own authority or position, through the conduct of another state functionary in the performance of his duties, seeks illegitimate benefits for a client and extorts or accepts property from the client shall be punished as for acceptance of bribes.

Article 388a Any close relative of a state functionary or any other person closely related to the state functionary who, through the conduct of the state functionary in the performance of his duties, or by taking advantage of the convenience of the state functionary's authority or position, through the conduct of another state functionary in the performance of his duties, seeks illegitimate benefits for a client and extorts or accepts property from the client, if the amount is relatively large or if there are other relatively serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also be fined; if the amount is huge or if there are other serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years and shall also be fined; if the amount is especially huge or if there are other especially serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years and shall also be fined or have his property confiscated.

Any former state functionary, or his close relative, or any other person closely related to him, who, by taking advantage of the convenience of the former state functionary's original authority or position, commits the act prescribed in the preceding paragraph, shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 389 Whoever, for the purpose of seeking illegitimate benefits, gives property to a state functionary, commits the crime of offering bribes.

Whoever, in economic activities, violates state regulations by giving property to a state functionary in a relatively large amount, or by giving various kinds of rebates or handling fees to a state functionary, shall be punished as for offering bribes.

Whoever gives property to a state functionary because of extortion and does not obtain illegitimate benefits shall not be regarded as offering bribes.

Article 390 Any person who commits the crime of offering bribes shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and shall also be fined; if he seeks illegitimate benefits through bribery and the circumstances are serious, or if he causes major losses to the interests of the state, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, or if he causes especially major losses to the interests of the state, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment and shall also be fined or have his property confiscated.

If the bribe-giver voluntarily confesses his bribery before being prosecuted, he may be given a lighter or mitigated punishment. Among them, if the crime is relatively minor, if he plays a key role in solving a major case, or if he has performed major meritorious service, he may be given a mitigated punishment or be exempted from punishment.

Article 390a Whoever, for the purpose of seeking illegitimate benefits, offers bribes to a close relative of a state functionary or any other person closely related to the state functionary, or to a former state functionary or his close relative or any other person closely related to him, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are serious, or if he causes major losses to the interests of the state, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years and shall also be fined; if the circumstances are especially serious, or if he causes especially major losses to the interests of the state, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than seven years but not more than ten years and shall also be fined.

If a unit commits the crime prescribed in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the offense shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also be fined.

Article 391 Whoever, for the purpose of seeking illegitimate benefits, gives property to a state organ, state-owned company, enterprise, institution, or people's organization, or, in economic activities, violates state regulations by giving various kinds of rebates or handling fees, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also be fined.

If a unit commits the crime prescribed in the preceding paragraph, the unit shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the offense shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 392 Whoever introduces a bribe to a state functionary, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention and shall also be fined.

If the person who introduces a bribe voluntarily confesses his act of introducing a bribe before being prosecuted, he may be given a mitigated punishment or be exempted from punishment.

Article 393 If a unit offers bribes for the purpose of seeking illegitimate benefits, or, in violation of state regulations, gives rebates or handling fees to a state functionary, and the circumstances are serious, the unit shall be fined, and the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the offense shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention and shall also be fined. If the illegal gains obtained through bribery are owned by an individual, the individual shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Articles 389 and 390 of this Law.

Article 394 Any state functionary who, in domestic official activities or in foreign exchanges, accepts gifts that should be turned over to the state according to state regulations but fails to do so, if the amount is relatively large, shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions of Articles 382 and 383 of this Law.

Article 395 If the property or expenditures of a state functionary clearly exceed his lawful income, and the difference is huge, the state functionary may be ordered to explain the source of the property. If he cannot explain the source, the difference shall be regarded as illegal income, and he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the difference is especially huge, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years. The difference in property shall be recovered.

Any state functionary who has deposits abroad shall declare them in accordance with state regulations. If the amount is relatively large and he conceals them without declaring, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are relatively minor, he shall be given administrative sanctions by his unit or the competent authority at a higher level as the case may be.

Article 396 Any state organ, state-owned company, enterprise, institution, or people's organization that, in violation of state regulations, distributes state-owned assets collectively to individuals in the name of the unit, if the amount is relatively large, the persons who are directly in charge and the other persons who are directly responsible for the offense shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention, and shall also be fined, or be fined alone; if the amount is huge, they shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years and shall also be fined.

Any judicial organ or administrative law enforcement organ that, in violation of state regulations, distributes confiscated property or fines that should be turned over to the state collectively to individuals in the name of the unit shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

### Chapter 9 Crimes of Dereliction of Duty

Article 397 Any state functionary who abuses his power or neglects his duty, causing major losses to public property or the interests of the state and the people, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years. Where this Law provides otherwise, those provisions shall apply.

Any state functionary who engages in malpractices for personal gain and commits the crime prescribed in the preceding paragraph shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years. Where this Law provides otherwise, those provisions shall apply.

Article 398 Any state functionary who, in violation of the provisions of the Law on Guarding State Secrets, intentionally or negligently divulges state secrets, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Any person who is not a state functionary and commits the crime prescribed in the preceding paragraph shall be punished as the circumstances may require in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 399 Any judicial officer who, engaging in malpractices for personal gain or favoritism, knowingly subjects a person who is obviously innocent to prosecution, knowingly protects a person who is obviously guilty from prosecution, or, in criminal trial activities, deliberately distorts facts and law to render an unjust judgment, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are especially serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years.

Whoever, in civil or administrative trial activities, deliberately distorts facts and law to render an unjust judgment, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years.

Whoever, in the execution of judgments or rulings, is seriously irresponsible or abuses his power, fails to take preservation measures according to law, fails to perform statutory execution duties, or illegally takes preservation measures or enforcement measures, causing major losses to the interests of the parties or others, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if he causes especially major losses to the interests of the parties or others, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years.

If a judicial officer accepts bribes and commits any of the acts prescribed in the preceding three paragraphs, and at the same time constitutes the crime prescribed in Article 385 of this Law, he shall be convicted and punished in accordance with the provisions for the heavier punishment.

Article 399a Any person who is lawfully responsible for arbitration who, in arbitration activities, deliberately distorts facts and law to render an unjust award, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 400 Any judicial officer who releases a criminal suspect, defendant, or criminal in custody without authorization shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are especially serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years.

Any judicial officer who, due to serious irresponsibility, causes the escape of a criminal suspect, defendant, or criminal in custody, resulting in serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if especially serious consequences result, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Article 401 Any judicial officer who, engaging in malpractices for personal gain, grants commutation of sentence, parole, or temporary service of sentence outside prison to a criminal who does not meet the conditions for such measures, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 402 Any administrative law enforcement officer who, engaging in malpractices for personal gain, fails to transfer a case that should be transferred to a judicial organ for criminal liability according to law, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if serious consequences result, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 403 Any state functionary of the relevant competent department of the state who, engaging in malpractices for personal gain and abusing his power, approves or registers the establishment or registration application of a company, or the application for the issuance or listing of stocks or bonds, that does not meet the conditions prescribed by law, causing major losses to public property or the interests of the state and the people, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

If a superior department compels the registration authority and its staff to commit the act prescribed in the preceding paragraph, the persons who are directly in charge shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 404 Any functionary of a tax authority who, engaging in malpractices for personal gain, fails to collect or under-collect the taxes due, causing major losses to state tax revenue, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if especially major losses are caused, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Article 405 Any functionary of a tax authority who, in violation of the provisions of laws and administrative regulations, engages in malpractices for personal gain in the work of issuing invoices, deducting taxes, or handling export tax refunds, causing major losses to the interests of the state, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if especially major losses are caused to the interests of the state, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Any other state functionary who, in violation of state regulations, engages in malpractices for personal gain in the work of providing export goods customs declaration forms, export foreign exchange verification forms, or other export tax refund vouchers, causing major losses to the interests of the state, shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

Article 406 Any state functionary who, in the process of signing or performing a contract, is defrauded due to serious irresponsibility, causing major losses to the interests of the state, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if especially major losses are caused to the interests of the state, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 407 Any functionary of the competent forestry department who, in violation of the provisions of the Forest Law, issues forest tree felling licenses exceeding the approved annual felling quota, or, in violation of regulations, indiscriminately issues forest tree felling licenses, if the circumstances are serious and cause serious damage to the forest, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 408 Any state functionary responsible for environmental protection supervision and management who is seriously irresponsible, leading to a major environmental pollution accident, causing major losses to public or private property or serious consequences of personal injury or death, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 408a Any state functionary responsible for food and drug safety supervision and management who abuses his power or neglects his duty, under any of the following circumstances, causing serious consequences or having other serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if especially serious consequences are caused or there are other especially serious circumstances, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years:

(1) Concealing or falsely reporting food safety accidents or drug safety incidents;

(2) Failing to investigate and deal with serious food or drug safety illegal acts found as prescribed;

(3) Granting permits to applications that do not meet the conditions during the approval and review of drugs and special foods;

(4) Failing to transfer cases that should be transferred to judicial organs for criminal liability according to law;

(5) Having other acts of abusing power or neglecting duty.

Whoever commits the crime prescribed in the preceding paragraph by engaging in malpractices for personal gain shall be given a heavier punishment.

Article 409 Any functionary of the government health administration engaged in the prevention and treatment of infectious diseases who is seriously irresponsible, leading to the spread or epidemic of an infectious disease, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 410 Any state functionary who, engaging in malpractices for personal gain, violates land management regulations, abuses his power, illegally approves the expropriation, requisition, or occupation of land, or illegally transfers the right to use state-owned land at a low price, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if especially major losses are caused to the interests of the state or the collective, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 411 Any customs officer who, engaging in malpractices for personal gain, connives at smuggling, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Article 412 Any functionary of the state commodity inspection department or commodity inspection agency who, engaging in malpractices for personal gain, forges inspection results, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if serious consequences result, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years.

Any person mentioned in the preceding paragraph who is seriously irresponsible, fails to inspect items that should be inspected, or delays inspection certification or issues incorrect certification, causing major losses to the interests of the state, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 413 Any quarantine officer of an animal and plant quarantine authority who, engaging in malpractices for personal gain, forges quarantine results, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if serious consequences result, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years.

Any person mentioned in the preceding paragraph who is seriously irresponsible, fails to quarantine items that should be quarantined, or delays quarantine certification or issues incorrect certification, causing major losses to the interests of the state, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 414 Any state functionary responsible for investigating and dealing with criminal acts of producing or selling counterfeit or substandard goods who, engaging in malpractices for personal gain, fails to perform the investigation duties prescribed by law, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

Article 415 Any state functionary responsible for handling passports, visas, or other exit-entry documents who knowingly issues exit-entry documents to persons attempting to illegally cross the national (border) frontier, or any state functionary of the border defense or customs who knowingly allows persons illegally crossing the national (border) frontier to pass, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 416 Any state functionary responsible for rescuing abducted or kidnapped women or children who, upon receiving a request for rescue from the abducted or kidnapped women or children or their family members, or upon receiving a report from others, fails to rescue the abducted or kidnapped women or children, causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention.

Any state functionary responsible for rescue who, taking advantage of his position, obstructs rescue, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than two years but not more than seven years; if the circumstances are relatively minor, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than two years or criminal detention.

Article 417 Any state functionary with the duty to investigate and suppress criminal activities who tips off criminals, provides convenience, or helps criminals evade punishment shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Article 418 Any state functionary who, engaging in malpractices for personal gain in the recruitment of public servants or students, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 419 Any state functionary who is seriously irresponsible, causing damage or loss of valuable cultural relics, if the consequences are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

### Chapter 10 Crimes of Servicemen's Dereliction of Duty

Article 420 Any act committed by a serviceman in violation of his duties, endangering the military interests of the state, that should be subject to criminal punishment according to law, constitutes a crime of servicemen's dereliction of duty.

Article 421 Whoever disobeys orders during wartime, causing harm to combat operations, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if major losses are caused to a battle or campaign, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death.

Article 422 Whoever intentionally conceals or falsely reports military intelligence, or refuses to transmit or falsely transmits military orders, causing harm to combat operations, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if major losses are caused to a battle or campaign, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death.

Article 423 Whoever, on the battlefield, is afraid of death and voluntarily lays down his arms to surrender to the enemy shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment.

Whoever, after surrendering, serves the enemy shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death.Article 424: Whoever deserts from the battlefield during wartime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years; if the circumstances are serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if heavy losses are caused to combat or campaign operations, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death.

Article 425: Any commander or person on duty or on watch who, by leaving his post without permission or by neglecting his duty, causes serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if especially serious consequences are caused, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Whoever commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph during wartime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Article 426: Whoever, by means of violence or threat, obstructs a commander or a person on duty or on watch from performing his duties, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are especially serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment. A heavier punishment shall be imposed during wartime.

Article 427: Any person who abuses his power and instructs his subordinates to engage in activities that violate their duties, thereby causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years.

Article 428: Any commander who disobeys orders, flinches before battle, or fights passively, thereby causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years; if heavy losses are caused to combat or campaign operations, or if there are other especially serious circumstances, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Article 429: Any commander who, on the battlefield, knowing that a friendly unit is in a critical situation and requesting rescue, fails to provide rescue when able to do so, thereby causing heavy losses to the friendly unit, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years.

Article 430: Any person who, while performing official duties, leaves his post without permission, defects abroad, or defects while abroad, thereby harming the military interests of the state, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Whoever defects by piloting an aircraft or commanding a ship, or if there are other especially serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death.

Article 431: Whoever illegally obtains military secrets by means of theft, espionage, or bribery shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years; if the circumstances are serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are especially serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years.

Whoever steals, spies on, bribes, or illegally provides military secrets for institutions, organizations, or individuals outside the country shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death.

Article 432: Whoever, in violation of laws and regulations on the protection of state secrets, intentionally or negligently divulges military secrets, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years.

Whoever commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph during wartime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are especially serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment.

Article 433: Whoever, during wartime, spreads rumors to confuse the public and shake the morale of the troops shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years; if the circumstances are serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are especially serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years or life imprisonment.

Article 434: Whoever, during wartime, injures himself to evade military obligations shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years; if the circumstances are serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 435: Whoever, in violation of military service laws, deserts the armed forces, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Whoever commits the crime mentioned in the preceding paragraph during wartime shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 436: Whoever violates regulations on the use of weapons and equipment, if the circumstances are serious, thereby causing a liability accident resulting in serious injury, death, or other serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the consequences are especially serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 437: Whoever violates regulations on the management of weapons and equipment, arbitrarily changes the designated use of weapons and equipment, thereby causing serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if especially serious consequences are caused, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than seven years.

Article 438: Whoever steals or forcibly seizes weapons, equipment, or military supplies shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are especially serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death.

Whoever steals or forcibly seizes firearms, ammunition, or explosives shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 127 of this Law.

Article 439: Whoever illegally sells or transfers military weapons or equipment shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years; if a large quantity of weapons or equipment is sold or transferred, or if there are other especially serious circumstances, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death.

Article 440: Whoever disobeys orders and abandons weapons or equipment shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if important or a large quantity of weapons or equipment is abandoned, or if there are other serious circumstances, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Article 441: Whoever loses weapons or equipment and fails to report promptly, or if there are other serious circumstances, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention.

Article 442: Whoever, in violation of regulations, illegally sells or transfers military real estate, if the circumstances are serious, shall be sentenced, with respect to the person directly responsible, to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years or criminal detention; if the circumstances are especially serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than three years but not more than ten years.

Article 443: Any person who abuses his power and maltreats his subordinates, if the circumstances are flagrant, causing serious injury or other serious consequences, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if death is caused, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Article 444: Whoever intentionally abandons wounded or sick soldiers on the battlefield, if the circumstances are flagrant, shall be sentenced, with respect to the person directly responsible, to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years.

Article 445: Whoever, in a position of medical treatment during wartime, refuses to treat critically wounded or sick soldiers when conditions for treatment exist, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years or criminal detention; if severe disability or death of the wounded or sick soldier is caused, or if there are other serious circumstances, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years.

Article 446: Whoever, during wartime in a military operation area, cruelly injures innocent residents or plunders their property, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years; if the circumstances are serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years but not more than ten years; if the circumstances are especially serious, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than ten years, life imprisonment, or death.

Article 447: Whoever privately releases a prisoner of war shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than five years; if an important prisoner of war is privately released, or if multiple prisoners of war are privately released, or if there are other serious circumstances, to fixed-term imprisonment of not less than five years.

Article 448: Whoever maltreats prisoners of war, if the circumstances are flagrant, shall be sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years.

Article 449: During wartime, for a soldier who has been sentenced to fixed-term imprisonment of not more than three years and, having no real danger, is granted a suspended sentence, he may be allowed to atone for his crimes by meritorious service. If he indeed performs meritorious service, the original sentence may be revoked, and he shall not be treated as having committed a crime.

Article 450: This Chapter applies to active-duty officers, civilian cadres, soldiers, and students with military status of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as active-duty officers, civilian cadres, soldiers, and students with military status of the Chinese People's Armed Police Force, and also to civilian personnel, reservists performing military tasks, and other personnel.

Article 451: For the purposes of this Chapter, "wartime" refers to the period when the state declares a state of war, when troops receive combat missions, or when they are suddenly attacked by the enemy.

When troops perform martial law duties or handle sudden violent incidents, it shall be treated as wartime.

## Supplementary Provisions

Article 452: This Law shall come into force as of October 1, 1997.

The regulations, supplementary provisions, and decisions formulated by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress listed in Appendix I to this Law, which have been incorporated into this Law or are no longer applicable, shall be abolished as of the date this Law comes into force.

The supplementary provisions and decisions formulated by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress listed in Appendix II to this Law shall be retained. Among them, the provisions concerning administrative penalties and administrative measures shall remain effective; the provisions concerning criminal liability have been incorporated into this Law, and as of the date this Law comes into force, the provisions of this Law shall apply.
