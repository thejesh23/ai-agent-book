# Individual Income Tax Law of the People's Republic of China

Article 1 An individual who has a domicile in China, or who does not have a domicile but has resided in China for an aggregate of 183 days or more in a tax year, is a resident individual. Resident individuals shall pay individual income tax on income derived from within and outside China in accordance with the provisions of this Law.

An individual who does not have a domicile in China and does not reside in China, or who does not have a domicile but has resided in China for an aggregate of less than 183 days in a tax year, is a non-resident individual. Non-resident individuals shall pay individual income tax on income derived from within China in accordance with the provisions of this Law.

A tax year shall begin on January 1 and end on December 31 of the Gregorian calendar.

Article 2 Individual income tax shall be paid on the following categories of personal income:

(1) income from wages and salaries;
(2) income from remuneration for personal services;
(3) income from author's remuneration;
(4) income from royalties;
(5) income from business operations;
(6) income from interest, dividends, and bonuses;
(7) income from lease of property;
(8) income from transfer of property;
(9) incidental income.

Resident individuals shall calculate individual income tax on a consolidated annual basis for income derived from items (1) through (4) of the preceding paragraph (hereinafter referred to as comprehensive income); non-resident individuals shall calculate individual income tax on a monthly or per-transaction basis for income derived from items (1) through (4) of the preceding paragraph. Taxpayers shall calculate individual income tax separately for income derived from items (5) through (9) of the preceding paragraph in accordance with the provisions of this Law.

Article 3 Tax rates for individual income tax:

(1) For comprehensive income, progressive tax rates ranging from 3 percent to 45 percent shall apply (see the tax rate schedule attached);
(2) For income from business operations, progressive tax rates ranging from 5 percent to 35 percent shall apply (see the tax rate schedule attached);
(3) For income from interest, dividends, and bonuses, income from lease of property, income from transfer of property, and incidental income, a proportional tax rate of 20 percent shall apply.

Article 4 The following categories of personal income shall be exempt from individual income tax:

(1) Awards in the fields of science, education, technology, culture, health, sports, and environmental protection granted by provincial-level people's governments, ministries and commissions of the State Council, units at or above the corps level of the People's Liberation Army, as well as foreign organizations and international organizations;
(2) Interest on treasury bonds and financial bonds issued by the state;
(3) Subsidies and allowances issued in accordance with uniform state regulations;
(4) Welfare benefits, survivor pensions, and relief payments;
(5) Insurance compensation;
(6) Military severance pay, demobilization pay, and retirement pay;
(7) Settlement allowances, severance pay, basic pensions or retirement pensions, and living subsidies for retired veteran cadres issued to cadres and workers in accordance with uniform state regulations;
(8) Income of diplomatic representatives, consular officers, and other personnel of foreign embassies and consulates in China that is exempt from tax under relevant legal provisions;
(9) Income exempt from tax as stipulated in international conventions to which the Chinese government is a party or agreements it has signed;
(10) Other tax-exempt income prescribed by the State Council.

The tax exemption provisions in item (10) of the preceding paragraph shall be reported by the State Council to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for recordation.

Article 5 Under any of the following circumstances, individual income tax may be reduced, with the specific extent and period to be determined by the people's governments of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government, and reported to the standing committees of the people's congresses at the corresponding level for recordation:

(1) Income of disabled persons, orphaned elderly persons, and family members of martyrs;
(2) Income of persons who have suffered significant losses due to natural disasters.

The State Council may prescribe other circumstances for tax reduction, which shall be reported to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for recordation.

Article 6 Calculation of taxable income:

(1) For comprehensive income of resident individuals, the taxable income shall be the balance of the income for each tax year after deducting 60,000 yuan, as well as special deductions, special additional deductions, and other deductions determined in accordance with the law.
(2) For income from wages and salaries of non-resident individuals, the taxable income shall be the balance of the monthly income after deducting 5,000 yuan; for income from remuneration for personal services, author's remuneration, and royalties, the taxable income shall be the amount of each payment.
(3) For income from business operations, the taxable income shall be the balance of the total income for each tax year after deducting costs, expenses, and losses.
(4) For income from lease of property, if each payment does not exceed 4,000 yuan, 800 yuan shall be deducted; if it exceeds 4,000 yuan, 20 percent of the expenses shall be deducted, and the remaining amount shall be the taxable income.
(5) For income from transfer of property, the taxable income shall be the balance of the income from the transfer after deducting the original value of the property and reasonable expenses.
(6) For income from interest, dividends, and bonuses, and incidental income, the taxable income shall be the amount of each payment.

For income from remuneration for personal services, author's remuneration, and royalties, the income amount shall be the balance after deducting 20 percent of the expenses from the income. The income amount for author's remuneration shall be further reduced by 30 percent for calculation.

Where an individual makes donations for public welfare and charitable causes such as education, poverty alleviation, and disaster relief, the portion of the donation that does not exceed 30 percent of the taxpayer's declared taxable income may be deducted from the taxable income; if the State Council stipulates that full pre-tax deduction is allowed for donations to public welfare and charitable causes, such provisions shall prevail.

The special deductions referred to in item (1) of the first paragraph of this Article include basic pension insurance, basic medical insurance, unemployment insurance, and housing provident fund paid by resident individuals in accordance with the scope and standards prescribed by the state; special additional deductions include expenditures for children's education, continuing education, serious illness medical care, housing loan interest or housing rent, and support for elderly parents. The specific scope, standards, and implementation steps shall be determined by the State Council and reported to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for recordation.

Article 7 For income derived by resident individuals from outside China, the amount of individual income tax already paid outside China may be credited against their tax liability, but the credit amount shall not exceed the tax payable on such foreign-source income calculated in accordance with the provisions of this Law.

Article 8 Under any of the following circumstances, the tax authorities have the right to make tax adjustments using reasonable methods:

(1) Business transactions between an individual and their related parties do not comply with the arm's length principle, resulting in a reduction of the tax payable by the individual or their related parties without justifiable reasons;
(2) An enterprise controlled by a resident individual, or jointly controlled by a resident individual and a resident enterprise, established in a country (region) with a significantly low actual tax burden, without reasonable business needs, fails to distribute or reduces the distribution of profits that should be attributable to the resident individual;
(3) An individual implements other arrangements without reasonable commercial purposes to obtain improper tax benefits.

Where the tax authorities make tax adjustments in accordance with the preceding paragraph and additional tax needs to be collected, the tax shall be collected, and interest shall be charged in accordance with the law.

Article 9 For individual income tax, the income earner is the taxpayer, and the unit or individual that pays the income is the withholding agent.

If the taxpayer has a Chinese citizen identity number, that number shall be the taxpayer identification number; if the taxpayer does not have a Chinese citizen identity number, the tax authorities shall assign a taxpayer identification number. When the withholding agent withholds tax, the taxpayer shall provide the taxpayer identification number to the withholding agent.

Article 10 Under any of the following circumstances, the taxpayer shall file a tax return in accordance with the law:

(1) Obtaining comprehensive income and needing to file an annual settlement;
(2) Obtaining taxable income without a withholding agent;
(3) Obtaining taxable income for which the withholding agent fails to withhold tax;
(4) Obtaining income from outside China;
(5) Canceling Chinese household registration due to emigration;
(6) A non-resident individual obtaining income from wages and salaries from two or more sources within China;
(7) Other circumstances prescribed by the State Council.

The withholding agent shall handle full and complete withholding declarations in accordance with state regulations and provide the taxpayer with information on their personal income and tax withheld.

Article 11 Resident individuals who obtain comprehensive income shall calculate individual income tax on an annual basis; if there is a withholding agent, the withholding agent shall withhold tax on a monthly or per-transaction basis; if an annual settlement is required, it shall be handled between March 1 and June 30 of the following year after obtaining the income. The method for withholding tax in advance shall be formulated by the tax authority of the State Council.

If a resident individual provides special additional deduction information to the withholding agent, the withholding agent shall deduct it in accordance with regulations when withholding tax on a monthly basis and shall not refuse.

For non-resident individuals who obtain income from wages and salaries, remuneration for personal services, author's remuneration, and royalties, if there is a withholding agent, the withholding agent shall withhold tax on a monthly or per-transaction basis, and no annual settlement is required.

Article 12 Taxpayers who obtain income from business operations shall calculate individual income tax on an annual basis, submit a tax return to the tax authorities within 15 days after the end of each month or quarter, and pay the tax in advance; the annual settlement shall be handled before March 31 of the following year after obtaining the income.

Taxpayers who obtain income from interest, dividends, and bonuses, income from lease of property, income from transfer of property, and incidental income shall calculate individual income tax on a monthly or per-transaction basis; if there is a withholding agent, the withholding agent shall withhold tax on a monthly or per-transaction basis.

Article 13 If a taxpayer obtains taxable income without a withholding agent, they shall submit a tax return to the tax authorities and pay the tax within 15 days of the month following the month in which the income is obtained.

If a taxpayer obtains taxable income and the withholding agent fails to withhold the tax, the taxpayer shall pay the tax before June 30 of the following year after obtaining the income; if the tax authorities notify a deadline for payment, the taxpayer shall pay the tax within that deadline.

Resident individuals who obtain income from outside China shall file a tax return between March 1 and June 30 of the following year after obtaining the income.

Non-resident individuals who obtain income from wages and salaries from two or more sources within China shall file a tax return within 15 days of the month following the month in which the income is obtained.

If a taxpayer cancels Chinese household registration due to emigration, they shall settle the tax before canceling the Chinese household registration.

Article 14 The tax withheld in advance or withheld by the withholding agent each month or per transaction shall be paid into the state treasury within 15 days of the following month, and a withholding declaration for individual income tax shall be submitted to the tax authorities.

If a taxpayer handles a tax refund for an annual settlement, or a withholding agent handles a tax refund for a taxpayer, the tax authorities shall, after review, handle the tax refund in accordance with the relevant provisions of state treasury management.

Article 15 Public security, the People's Bank of China, financial regulatory authorities, and other relevant departments shall assist the tax authorities in confirming the taxpayer's identity and financial account information. Education, health, medical security, civil affairs, human resources and social security, housing and urban-rural construction, public security, the People's Bank of China, financial regulatory authorities, and other relevant departments shall provide the tax authorities with information on special additional deductions such as children's education, continuing education, serious illness medical care, housing loan interest, housing rent, and support for elderly parents.

When an individual transfers real estate, the tax authorities shall verify the individual income tax payable based on relevant information such as real estate registration; when the registration authority handles the transfer registration, it shall inspect the tax payment certificate for individual income tax related to the real estate transfer. When an individual transfers equity and handles change registration, the market entity registration authority shall inspect the tax payment certificate for individual income tax related to the equity transaction.

Relevant departments shall, in accordance with the law, include the compliance of taxpayers and withholding agents with this Law in the credit information system and implement joint incentives or penalties.

Article 16 The calculation of various types of income shall be in Renminbi. If the income is in a currency other than Renminbi, it shall be converted into Renminbi for tax payment at the central parity rate of the Renminbi exchange rate.

Article 17 A handling fee of 2 percent of the tax withheld shall be paid to the withholding agent.

Article 18 The imposition, reduction, or exemption of individual income tax on interest income from savings deposits, and the specific methods thereof, shall be prescribed by the State Council and reported to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for recordation.

Article 19 Taxpayers, withholding agents, tax authorities, and their staff who violate the provisions of this Law shall be held legally liable in accordance with the "Law of the People's Republic of China on the Administration of Tax Collection" and relevant laws and regulations.

Article 20 The administration of individual income tax collection shall be carried out in accordance with this Law and the "Law of the People's Republic of China on the Administration of Tax Collection."

Article 21 The State Council shall formulate implementing regulations based on this Law.

Article 22 This Law shall come into effect on the date of its promulgation.
