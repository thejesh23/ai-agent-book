# Vehicle and Vessel Tax Law of the People's Republic of China

Adopted at the 19th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the 11th National People's Congress on February 25, 2011

Amended in accordance with the Decision on Revising Eight Laws including the Construction Law of the People's Republic of China at the 10th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the 13th National People's Congress on April 23, 2019

Article 1 The owner or manager of vehicles and vessels (hereinafter referred to as vehicles and vessels) within the territory of the People's Republic of China that are specified in the Vehicle and Vessel Tax Item and Tax Rate Table attached to this Law shall be the taxpayer of vehicle and vessel tax and shall pay vehicle and vessel tax in accordance with this Law.

Article 2 The applicable tax rates for vehicles and vessels shall be implemented in accordance with the Vehicle and Vessel Tax Item and Tax Rate Table attached to this Law.

The specific applicable tax rates for vehicles shall be determined by the people's governments of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government within the tax rate range specified in the Vehicle and Vessel Tax Item and Tax Rate Table attached to this Law and in accordance with the provisions of the State Council.

The specific applicable tax rates for vessels shall be determined by the State Council within the tax rate range specified in the Vehicle and Vessel Tax Item and Tax Rate Table attached to this Law.

Article 3 The following vehicles and vessels are exempt from vehicle and vessel tax:

(1) Fishing boats and aquaculture boats;

(2) Vehicles and vessels exclusively used by the military and armed police forces;

(3) Police vehicles and vessels;

(4) National comprehensive fire rescue vehicles and vessels bearing special license plates for emergency rescue, and national comprehensive fire rescue specialized vessels;

(5) Vehicles and vessels of foreign embassies and consulates, representative offices of international organizations in China, and their relevant personnel that are entitled to tax exemption in accordance with legal provisions.

Article 4 Vehicle and vessel tax may be reduced or exempted for vehicles and vessels that conserve energy or use new energy; vehicle and vessel tax may be reduced or exempted for those affected by severe natural disasters that make tax payment difficult, or for other special reasons where tax reduction or exemption is genuinely necessary. Specific measures shall be formulated by the State Council and reported to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for record.

Article 5 The people's governments of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government may, based on local actual conditions, periodically reduce or exempt vehicle and vessel tax for public transportation vehicles and vessels, as well as for motorcycles, three-wheeled vehicles, and low-speed cargo vehicles owned by rural residents and primarily used in rural areas.

Article 6 Insurance institutions engaged in compulsory motor vehicle third-party liability insurance business shall serve as withholding agents for motor vehicle and vessel tax. They shall collect the vehicle and vessel tax in accordance with the law when collecting insurance premiums and issue tax collection certificates.

Article 7 The place of tax payment for vehicle and vessel tax shall be the place of registration of the vehicle or vessel, or the location of the withholding agent for vehicle and vessel tax. For vehicles and vessels that are not required to be registered in accordance with the law, the place of tax payment shall be the location of the owner or manager of the vehicle or vessel.

Article 8 The obligation to pay vehicle and vessel tax arises in the month when the ownership or management right of the vehicle or vessel is obtained.

Article 9 Vehicle and vessel tax shall be declared and paid on an annual basis. The specific deadline for tax declaration and payment shall be determined by the people's governments of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government.

Article 10 The public security, transportation, agriculture, and fishery authorities responsible for vehicle and vessel registration, vessel inspection agencies, and the competent industry authorities of withholding agents for vehicle and vessel tax shall assist tax authorities in strengthening the collection and management of vehicle and vessel tax by providing information related to vehicles and vessels.

When applying for relevant vehicle registration or periodic inspection procedures, the vehicle owner or manager shall submit to the traffic management department of the public security authority a certificate of tax payment or tax exemption. The traffic management department of the public security authority shall handle the relevant procedures after verification.

Article 11 The collection and management of vehicle and vessel tax shall be implemented in accordance with this Law and the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Administration of Tax Collection.

Article 12 The State Council shall formulate implementing regulations based on this Law.

Article 13 This Law shall come into effect on January 1, 2012. The Interim Regulations on Vehicle and Vessel Tax of the People's Republic of China promulgated by the State Council on December 29, 2006, shall be simultaneously repealed.
