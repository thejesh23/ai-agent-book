# Tobacco Leaf Tax Law of the People's Republic of China

Adopted at the 31st Meeting of the Standing Committee of the 12th National People's Congress on December 27, 2017

<!-- INFO END -->

Article 1 Within the territory of the People's Republic of China, entities that purchase tobacco leaves in accordance with the provisions of the Tobacco Monopoly Law of the People's Republic of China are the taxpayers of tobacco leaf tax. Taxpayers shall pay tobacco leaf tax in accordance with the provisions of this Law.

Article 2 For the purposes of this Law, "tobacco leaves" refer to flue-cured tobacco leaves and sun-cured or air-cured tobacco leaves.

Article 3 The tax base for tobacco leaf tax is the total amount actually paid by the taxpayer for the purchase of tobacco leaves.

Article 4 The tax rate for tobacco leaf tax is 20 percent.

Article 5 The tax payable for tobacco leaf tax shall be calculated by multiplying the total amount actually paid by the taxpayer for the purchase of tobacco leaves by the tax rate.

Article 6 Tobacco leaf tax shall be collected and administered by the tax authorities in accordance with this Law and the relevant provisions of the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Administration of Tax Collection.

Article 7 Taxpayers shall declare and pay tobacco leaf tax to the competent tax authority of the place where the tobacco leaves are purchased.

Article 8 The tax liability for tobacco leaf tax arises on the date when the taxpayer purchases the tobacco leaves.

Article 9 Tobacco leaf tax is assessed on a monthly basis. Taxpayers shall declare and pay the tax within 15 days from the end of the month in which the tax liability arises.

Article 10 This Law shall take effect as of July 1, 2018. The Interim Regulation on Tobacco Leaf Tax of the People's Republic of China promulgated by the State Council on April 28, 2006 shall be repealed simultaneously.
