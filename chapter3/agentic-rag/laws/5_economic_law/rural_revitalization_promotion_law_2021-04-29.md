# Law of the People's Republic of China on the Promotion of Rural Revitalization

Adopted at the 28th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the 13th National People's Congress on April 29, 2021

<!-- INFO END -->

## Chapter I: General Provisions

**Article 1** This Law is enacted to comprehensively implement the rural revitalization strategy, promote the comprehensive upgrading of agriculture, the overall progress of rural areas, and the all-round development of farmers, accelerate agricultural and rural modernization, and build a modern socialist country in all respects.

**Article 2** This Law applies to the comprehensive implementation of the rural revitalization strategy, including activities to promote the revitalization of rural industries, talent, culture, ecology, and organizations, and to advance integrated urban-rural development.

For the purposes of this Law, "rural areas" refer to territorial complexes outside urban built-up areas that possess natural, social, and economic characteristics and multiple functions such as production, living, ecology, and culture, including townships and villages.

**Article 3** The promotion of rural revitalization shall be carried out in accordance with the general requirements of thriving industries, livable ecosystems, civilized rural customs, effective governance, and prosperous living. It shall coordinate the advancement of rural economic, political, cultural, social, ecological, and Party building, and fully leverage the unique functions of rural areas in ensuring the supply of agricultural products and food security, protecting the ecological environment, and inheriting and developing the excellent traditional culture of the Chinese nation.

**Article 4** The comprehensive implementation of the rural revitalization strategy shall adhere to the leadership of the Communist Party of China, implement the new development philosophy of innovation, coordination, green development, openness, and sharing, follow the path of socialist rural revitalization with Chinese characteristics, promote common prosperity, and observe the following principles:

(1) Prioritize the development of agriculture and rural areas, giving priority consideration in cadre deployment, meeting factor allocation requirements first, ensuring financial investment priority, and arranging public services first;

(2) Uphold the principal position of farmers, fully respect their wishes, protect their democratic rights and other lawful rights and interests, mobilize their initiative, enthusiasm, and creativity, and safeguard their fundamental interests;

(3) Adhere to the harmonious coexistence of humans and nature, coordinate the systematic governance of mountains, waters, forests, farmlands, lakes, grasslands, and deserts, promote green development, and advance ecological civilization construction;

(4) Adhere to reform and innovation, fully leverage the decisive role of the market in resource allocation and better play the role of the government, advance agricultural supply-side structural reform and high-quality development, continuously emancipate and develop rural social productive forces, and stimulate rural development vitality;

(5) Adhere to adapting measures to local conditions, planning first, and proceeding step by step, conform to the laws of village development, and promote development by category based on the historical culture, development status, location conditions, resource endowments, and industrial foundations of rural areas.

**Article 5** The State shall consolidate and improve the two-tier management system based on household contract management and combining unified and decentralized operations, and develop and strengthen the rural collective ownership economy.

**Article 6** The State shall establish and improve the institutional mechanisms and policy systems for integrated urban-rural development, promote the orderly flow and equal exchange of urban and rural factors and the balanced allocation of public resources, adhere to using industry to support agriculture and cities to lead rural areas, and promote the formation of a new type of industrial-agricultural and urban-rural relationship characterized by mutual promotion, complementarity, coordinated development, and shared prosperity.

**Article 7** The State shall adhere to the guidance of the core socialist values, vigorously promote the national spirit and the spirit of the times, strengthen the protection of excellent traditional rural culture and the construction of the public cultural service system, and prosper and develop rural culture.

The autumn equinox of each year in the lunar calendar shall be the Chinese Farmers' Harvest Festival.

**Article 8** The State shall implement a food security strategy that is self-reliant, based on domestic production, ensures production capacity, allows for moderate imports, and is supported by science and technology. It shall adhere to storing grain in the land and storing grain in technology, take measures to continuously improve comprehensive grain production capacity, build a national food security industrial belt, improve the grain processing, circulation, and reserve systems, ensure basic self-sufficiency of grains and absolute security of rations, and safeguard national food security.

The State shall improve standards for grain processing, storage, and transportation, increase the yield rate and utilization rate of grain processing, and promote grain saving and loss reduction.

**Article 9** The State shall establish and improve a rural revitalization working mechanism with central coordination, provincial responsibility, and implementation by cities, counties, and townships.

People's governments at all levels shall incorporate rural revitalization promotion work into their national economic and social development plans, and establish a rural revitalization assessment and evaluation system, an annual work report system, and a supervision and inspection system.

**Article 10** The competent department of agriculture and rural affairs under the State Council shall be responsible for the overall coordination, macro-guidance, and supervision and inspection of the national rural revitalization promotion work. Other relevant departments under the State Council shall be responsible for related rural revitalization promotion work within their respective scopes of duties.

The competent departments of agriculture and rural affairs of local people's governments at or above the county level shall be responsible for the overall coordination, guidance, and supervision and inspection of rural revitalization promotion work within their respective administrative regions. Other relevant departments of local people's governments at or above the county level shall be responsible for related rural revitalization promotion work within their respective scopes of duties.

**Article 11** People's governments at all levels and their relevant departments shall adopt various forms to widely publicize laws, regulations, and policies related to the promotion of rural revitalization, and encourage and support people's organizations, social organizations, enterprises, institutions, and other sectors of society to participate in activities related to the promotion of rural revitalization.

Units and individuals that have made outstanding achievements in the work of promoting rural revitalization shall be commended and rewarded in accordance with relevant state regulations.

## Chapter II: Industrial Development

**Article 12** The State shall improve the rural collective property rights system, enhance the development vitality of the rural collective ownership economy, promote the preservation and appreciation of collective assets, and ensure that farmers benefit.

People's governments at all levels shall adhere to the principal role of farmers, rely on rural advantageous and characteristic resources, support and promote the integrated development of primary, secondary, and tertiary industries in rural areas, promote the establishment of a modern agricultural industrial system, production system, and management system, advance the construction of digital villages, cultivate new industries, new business formats, new models, and new types of agricultural business entities, and promote the organic connection between small farmers and modern agricultural development.

**Article 13** The State shall take measures to optimize the layout of agricultural productive forces, promote agricultural structural adjustment, develop advantageous and characteristic industries, ensure the effective supply and quality safety of grains and important agricultural products, promote variety improvement, quality enhancement, brand building, and standardized production, promote agricultural opening-up, and improve agricultural quality, efficiency, and competitiveness.

The State shall implement a strategy for ensuring the supply of important agricultural products, specifying guarantee targets by variety, and building a scientific, reasonable, safe, and efficient supply guarantee system for important agricultural products.

**Article 14** The State shall establish a classified management system for agricultural land, strictly protect cultivated land, strictly control the conversion of agricultural land to construction land, and strictly control the conversion of cultivated land to other types of agricultural land such as forest land and garden land. People's governments of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government shall take measures to ensure that the total amount of cultivated land does not decrease and its quality improves.

The State shall implement a permanent basic farmland protection system, build grain production functional zones and important agricultural product protection zones, and construct and protect high-standard farmland.

Local people's governments at all levels shall promote rural land consolidation and the scientific and safe use of agricultural land, strengthen the construction of farmland water conservancy and other infrastructure, and improve agricultural production conditions.

**Article 15** The State shall strengthen the protection and utilization of agricultural germplasm resources and the construction of germplasm resource banks, support basic, frontier, and applied technology research in breeding, implement the cultivation of improved crop and livestock varieties and key breeding technology research, encourage the transformation of seed industry scientific and technological achievements and the promotion of improved varieties, establish and implement a national security review mechanism for the seed industry, and promote the high-quality development of the seed industry.

**Article 16** The State shall take measures to strengthen agricultural scientific and technological innovation, cultivate innovation entities, build an innovation mechanism with enterprises as the main body and industry-university-research collaboration, strengthen the innovation capabilities of higher education institutions, scientific research institutions, and agricultural enterprises, establish innovation platforms, strengthen the research and development of new varieties, new technologies, new equipment, and new products, strengthen the protection of agricultural intellectual property rights, promote innovation in fields such as biological seed industry, smart agriculture, facility agriculture, agricultural product processing, and green agricultural inputs, build a modern agricultural industry technology system, and promote innovation-driven development in agriculture and rural areas.

The State shall improve the systems for reviewing agricultural scientific research projects, evaluating talent, and protecting the property rights of achievements, ensure investment in basic and public welfare research in agricultural science and technology, and stimulate the innovation enthusiasm of agricultural scientific and technological personnel.

**Article 17** The State shall strengthen the construction of the agricultural technology extension system, promote the establishment of incentive mechanisms and benefit-sharing mechanisms conducive to the transformation and promotion of agricultural scientific and technological achievements, and encourage enterprises, higher education institutions, vocational schools, scientific research institutions, scientific and technological social groups, specialized farmer cooperatives, specialized agricultural socialized service organizations, agricultural scientific and technological personnel, etc., to innovate extension methods and carry out agricultural technology extension services.

**Article 18** The State shall encourage the research, development, promotion, and application of agricultural machinery, promote the full mechanization of major crop production, improve the equipment level of facility agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry, fisheries, and primary processing of agricultural products, promote the integration of agricultural machinery and agronomy, and the integration of mechanization and informatization, and promote the adaptation of mechanized production to farmland construction and the adaptation of service models to moderate-scale agricultural operations.

The State shall encourage the construction of agricultural informatization, strengthen agricultural information monitoring, early warning, and comprehensive services, and promote the informatization of agricultural production and operation.

**Article 19** People's governments at all levels shall leverage rural resource and ecological advantages, support the development of rural industries such as specialty agriculture, leisure agriculture, modern agricultural product processing, rural handicrafts, green building materials, red tourism, rural tourism, health and wellness, and rural logistics and e-commerce; guide new business entities to rationally allocate production factors through specialized and specialized operations, and promote the deep integration of rural industries; support the construction of advantageous specialty agricultural product zones, modern agricultural industrial parks, agricultural science and technology parks, rural entrepreneurship parks, and key towns and villages for leisure agriculture and rural tourism; coordinate the construction of markets in agricultural product production areas, distribution centers, and sales areas; strengthen the construction of backbone agricultural product circulation networks and cold chain logistics systems; encourage enterprises to obtain internationally recognized agricultural product certifications, and enhance the competitiveness of rural industries.

The development of rural industries shall comply with territorial spatial planning, industrial policies, and environmental protection requirements.

**Article 20** People's governments at all levels shall improve support policies, strengthen guidance and services, support farmers and people returning to or moving to rural areas to start businesses and innovate in rural areas, and promote the development of rural industries and the employment of farmers.

**Article 21** People's governments at all levels shall establish and improve mechanisms conducive to the steady growth of farmers' income, encourage and support farmers to broaden their income channels, and promote an increase in farmers' income.

The State shall take measures to support the development of rural collective economic organizations, provide production and living services for their members, and ensure the right of members to obtain income distribution from collective operating income.

The State shall support specialized farmer cooperatives, family farms, agricultural enterprises, e-commerce enterprises, specialized agricultural socialized service organizations, etc., to establish close interest linkage mechanisms with farmers in various ways, allowing farmers to share the value-added benefits of the entire industrial chain.

**Article 22** People's governments at all levels shall strengthen the planning and construction of state-owned farms (forests, animal husbandry, fisheries), promote the modern agricultural development of state-owned farms (forests, animal husbandry, fisheries), and encourage state-owned farms (forests, animal husbandry, fisheries) to play a demonstrative and leading role in agricultural and rural modernization.

**Article 23** People's governments at all levels shall deepen the comprehensive reform of supply and marketing cooperatives, encourage supply and marketing cooperatives to strengthen their connection with farmers' interests, improve market operation mechanisms, strengthen their service functions for farmers, and leverage their role as comprehensive cooperative economic organizations serving farmers.

## Chapter III: Talent Support

**Article 24** The State shall improve the institutional mechanisms for rural talent work, take measures to encourage and support all sectors of society to provide services such as education and training, technical support, and entrepreneurship guidance, cultivate local talent, guide urban talent to go to rural areas, promote professional talent to serve rural areas, and promote the construction of the agricultural and rural talent team.

**Article 25** People's governments at all levels shall strengthen the coordination of rural education work, continuously improve the school conditions in rural areas, support the development of online distance education, improve the quality of rural basic education, increase the training of rural teachers, adopt methods such as publicly-funded teacher education to attract higher education institution graduates to teach in rural areas, give preferential treatment to teachers who have taught in rural areas for a long time in terms of professional title evaluation, etc., guarantee and improve the treatment of rural teachers, and improve the academic level, overall quality of rural teachers, and the level of rural education modernization.

People's governments at all levels shall take measures to strengthen the construction of the rural medical and health team, support medical and health personnel at the county, township, and village levels to participate in training and further education, establish a career development mechanism that connects the county, township, and village levels, implement preferential treatment for medical and health personnel working in rural areas, encourage medical school graduates to work in rural areas, support physicians to practice in rural medical and health institutions, open rural clinics, popularize medical and health knowledge, and improve the capacity of rural medical and health services.

People's governments at all levels shall take measures to cultivate agricultural science and technology talent, management talent, legal service talent, and social work talent, strengthen the construction of the rural cultural talent team, and cultivate backbone forces for rural culture.

**Article 26** People's governments at all levels shall take measures to strengthen vocational education and continuing education, organize and carry out agricultural skills training, entrepreneurship and employment training for returnees, and vocational skills training, and cultivate high-quality farmers and practical rural talent, and innovation and entrepreneurship leaders who are educated, understand technology, are good at management, and can manage.

**Article 27** People's governments at or above the county level and their education administrative departments shall guide and support higher education institutions and vocational schools to set up agriculture-related majors, increase the training of rural professional talent, and encourage graduates of higher education institutions and vocational schools to work and start businesses in rural areas.

**Article 28** The State shall encourage the flow of urban talent to rural areas, and establish and improve mechanisms for talent training cooperation and exchange between urban and rural areas, regions, and schools and localities.

People's governments at or above the county level shall establish incentive mechanisms to encourage all kinds of talent to participate in rural construction, build platforms for social work and rural construction volunteer services, and support and guide all kinds of talent to serve rural revitalization through various means.

Township people's governments, villagers' committees, and rural collective economic organizations shall provide necessary production and living services for returnees, newcomers, and all kinds of talent. Rural collective economic organizations may provide relevant welfare benefits based on actual conditions.

## Chapter IV: Cultural Prosperity

**Article 29** People's governments at all levels shall organize and carry out activities for civilized practice in the new era, strengthen the construction of spiritual civilization in rural areas, and continuously improve the level of social civilization in rural areas.

**Article 30** People's governments at all levels shall take measures to enrich the cultural and sports life of farmers, advocate scientific and healthy production and lifestyles, give play to the positive role of village rules and regulations, popularize scientific knowledge, promote the transformation of customs, eradicate outdated customs such as extravagant weddings and funerals and waste, advocate filial piety, love for the elderly, diligence, thrift, honesty, and trustworthiness, promote gender equality, create civilized villages and towns and civilized families, cultivate civilized rural customs, good family traditions, and simple folk customs, and build civilized villages.

**Article 31** People's governments at all levels shall improve and perfect the network of rural public cultural and sports facilities and service operation mechanisms, encourage the development of various forms of mass cultural and sports activities and festival folk activities for farmers, make full use of radio, television, audio-visual networks, books, and newspapers, expand rural cultural service channels, and provide convenient and accessible public cultural services.

People's governments at all levels shall support literary and artistic creation on themes of agriculture, rural areas, and farmers, and encourage the creation of excellent literary and artistic works reflecting the production and life of farmers and the practice of rural revitalization.

**Article 32** People's governments at all levels shall take measures to protect agricultural cultural heritage and intangible cultural heritage, explore the profound connotation of excellent agricultural culture, promote red culture, and inherit and develop excellent traditional culture.

Local people's governments at or above the county level shall strengthen the protection of famous historical and cultural towns and villages, traditional villages and rural landscapes, and ethnic minority characteristic villages, conduct monitoring and assessment of their protection status, and take measures to prevent and mitigate disasters such as fires, floods, and earthquakes.

**Article 33** Local people's governments at or above the county level shall adhere to planning guidance and typical demonstration, systematically build agricultural cultural exhibition areas and cultural industry characteristic villages with distinct characteristics and prominent advantages, develop rural characteristic cultural and sports industries, promote the revitalization of traditional crafts in rural areas, actively promote the construction of smart radio and television in rural areas, and enliven and prosper the rural cultural market.

## Chapter V: Ecological Protection

**Article 34** The State shall improve the protection system for important ecosystems and the ecological protection compensation mechanism, implement projects for the protection and restoration of important ecosystems, strengthen rural ecological protection and environmental governance, beautify and green the rural environment, and build beautiful villages.

**Article 35** The State shall encourage and support agricultural producers to adopt advanced planting and breeding technologies such as water-saving, fertilizer-saving, pesticide-saving, and energy-saving, promote the combination of planting and breeding and the comprehensive development of agricultural resources, and prioritize the development of ecological circular agriculture.

People's governments at all levels shall take measures to strengthen the prevention and control of agricultural non-point source pollution, promote the reduction of agricultural inputs, cleaner production, resource utilization of waste, and ecological industrial models, and guide the whole society to form production, living, and consumption patterns that are economical, moderate, green, low-carbon, civilized, and healthy.

**Article 36** People's governments at all levels shall implement comprehensive territorial consolidation and ecological restoration, strengthen the protection and restoration of forests, grasslands, wetlands, etc., carry out comprehensive management of desertification, stony desertification, and soil erosion, and improve the rural ecological environment.

**Article 37** People's governments at all levels shall establish a mechanism for joint construction, management, and sharing involving governments, village-level organizations, enterprises, farmers, and other parties, comprehensively manage rural water systems, promote sanitary toilets and simple and easy waste classification according to local conditions, manage rural garbage and sewage, strengthen the construction of barrier-free facilities in rural areas, encourage and support the use of clean energy and renewable energy, and continuously improve the rural living environment.

**Article 38** The State shall establish and improve a quality and safety management system for rural housing construction and related technical standard systems, and establish a safe housing guarantee mechanism for low-income groups in rural areas. The construction of rural housing shall avoid disaster-prone areas and meet basic safety requirements such as earthquake resistance and flood control.

Local people's governments at or above the county level shall strengthen the management and service of rural housing construction, strengthen the planning control of new rural housing, strictly prohibit the illegal occupation of cultivated land for housing construction; encourage the design of rural housing to reflect regional, ethnic, and local characteristics, encourage the use of new construction technologies and green building materials in rural housing construction, and guide farmers to build livable housing that is modern in function, safe in structure, economical in cost, green and environmentally friendly, and coordinated with the rural environment.

**Article 39** The State shall implement strict management of agricultural inputs, and take measures to prohibit or restrict the use of highly toxic, highly hazardous, and highly residual pesticides and veterinary drugs. Producers and operators of agricultural products shall not use prohibited pesticides, veterinary drugs, or other toxic and harmful substances, and shall not use agricultural inputs such as pesticides, veterinary drugs, fertilizers, and feed additives in excess of dosages or scopes in violation of agricultural product quality and safety standards and relevant state regulations.

**Article 40** The State shall implement a system for the conservation, restoration, and fallowing of cultivated land, and for the recuperation of grasslands, forests, rivers, and lakes. People's governments at or above the county level and their relevant departments shall, in accordance with the law, designate the time and areas for restricted and prohibited fishing in rivers, lakes, and seas, and may, based on the situation of groundwater over-extraction, designate areas where groundwater extraction is prohibited or restricted.It is prohibited to illegally transfer industries or enterprises that pollute the environment or damage ecosystems to rural areas. It is prohibited to illegally transfer urban waste, industrial solid waste, and untreated urban sewage to agricultural and rural areas. It is prohibited to discharge sewage or sludge containing excessive heavy metals or other toxic and hazardous substances onto agricultural land, as well as dredging sediment, tailings, slag, etc., that may cause soil pollution; it is prohibited to use toxic and hazardous waste as fertilizer or for land reclamation and restoration.

Local people's governments at all levels and their relevant departments shall take measures to promote the recycling and disposal of waste agricultural film and packaging waste from agricultural inputs such as pesticides, promote the resource utilization of crop straw and livestock and poultry manure, and strictly control cage culture with bait in rivers, lakes, reservoirs, and coastal waters.

## Chapter VI: Organizational Construction

Article 41: A modern rural social governance system shall be established and improved, led by Party committees, responsible by governments, with democratic consultation, social coordination, public participation, legal guarantees, and technological support, along with a rural social governance system combining self-governance, rule of law, and rule of virtue, to build vibrant, harmonious, and orderly well-governed villages.

Local people's governments at all levels shall strengthen the social management and service capacity building of township people's governments, making townships the centers of rural governance, rural services, and rural economy.

Article 42: Rural grassroots organizations of the Communist Party of China shall play a comprehensive leading role in accordance with the Party Constitution and relevant regulations. Villagers' committees, rural collective economic organizations, etc., shall, under the leadership of township Party committees and village Party organizations, implement villagers' self-governance, develop the collective ownership economy, safeguard the legitimate rights and interests of farmers, and shall accept supervision by villagers.

Article 43: The state shall establish and improve mechanisms for the cultivation, deployment, use, and management of the cadre team for agricultural and rural work, select outstanding cadres to strengthen this team, take measures to enhance the capacity and level of the cadre team for agricultural and rural work, implement relevant treatment guarantees for rural grassroots cadres, and build a cadre team for agricultural and rural work that understands agriculture, loves rural areas, and cares for farmers.

Article 44: Local people's governments at all levels shall build a streamlined and efficient grassroots management system, scientifically set up township institutions, strengthen the training of rural cadres, improve the rural grassroots service system, and consolidate the foundation of rural governance.

Article 45: Township people's governments shall guide and support the standardization and institutionalization of rural grassroots self-governing mass organizations, improve the democratic decision-making mechanism of villagers' committees and the system of village affairs disclosure, and enhance villagers' capacity for self-management, self-education, self-service, and self-supervision.

Article 46: People's governments at all levels shall guide and support rural collective economic organizations in playing their roles in managing collective assets according to law, rationally developing collective resources, and serving collective members, ensuring the independent operation of rural collective economic organizations.

Local people's governments at or above the county level shall support the development of various business entities such as farmers' professional cooperatives, family farms, and agricultural enterprises, and improve the agricultural and rural socialized service system.

Article 47: Local people's governments at or above the county level shall take measures to strengthen the construction of grassroots mass organizations, support, regulate, and guide the development of rural social organizations, and leverage the roles of grassroots mass organizations and rural social organizations in uniting, contacting, and serving the masses.

Article 48: Local people's governments at all levels shall strengthen the construction of grassroots law enforcement teams, encourage township people's governments to establish legal advisors and public lawyers as needed, encourage qualified places to establish public legal service studios in villagers' committees, conduct in-depth legal publicity and education and people's mediation work, improve the mechanism for resolving and mediating rural conflicts and disputes, and promote the construction of rule-of-law villages.

Article 49: Local people's governments at all levels shall improve the rural public security prevention and control system, strengthen rural police work, and promote the construction of safe villages; improve the rural public safety system, and strengthen safety management responsibilities in rural public health, production safety, disaster prevention, mitigation, and relief, emergency rescue, emergency broadcasting, food, drugs, transportation, and fire protection.

## Chapter VII: Urban-Rural Integration

Article 50: People's governments at all levels shall coordinate the implementation of the rural revitalization strategy and the new urbanization strategy, plan urban and rural development as a whole, scientifically and orderly arrange ecological, agricultural, urban, and other functional spaces, optimize the layout of urban and rural industrial development, infrastructure, and public service facilities, gradually improve a basic public service system that covers all people, is universally beneficial and shared, and integrates urban and rural areas, accelerate the integrated development of counties, cities, and rural areas, and promote high-quality and efficient agriculture, livable and workable rural areas, and prosperous and well-off farmers.

Article 51: County and township people's governments shall optimize the rural development layout within their administrative regions, arrange village layouts according to local conditions based on the principles of respecting farmers' wishes, facilitating production and daily life, and maintaining rural functions and characteristics, prepare village plans according to law, promote village construction in a classified and orderly manner, strictly regulate village mergers and removals, and prohibit the merger or removal of villages against farmers' wishes or in violation of legal procedures.

Article 52: Local people's governments at or above the county level shall coordinate the planning, construction, management, and maintenance of urban and rural roads, as well as public infrastructure and new infrastructure such as garbage and sewage treatment, water, electricity, and gas supply, logistics, passenger transport, information and communication, radio and television, fire protection, and disaster prevention and mitigation, promote the interconnection of urban and rural infrastructure, ensure the energy needs of rural development, ensure the safety of rural drinking water, and meet the production and living needs of farmers.

Article 53: The state shall develop rural social undertakings, promote the tilt of resources such as public education, medical and health care, and social security toward rural areas, improve the level of basic public services in rural areas, and promote the equalization of basic public services between urban and rural areas.

The state shall improve the rural convenience service system, enhance the digital and intelligent level of rural public services, support the improvement of village-level comprehensive service facilities and comprehensive information platforms, cultivate service institutions and service-oriented social organizations, improve the service operation mechanism, promote the effective connection between public services and self-services, and enhance the functions of production and living services.

Article 54: The state shall improve the urban-rural coordinated social security system, establish and improve guarantee mechanisms, and support rural areas in improving the level of social security management services; establish and improve mechanisms for determining basic pension insurance benefits for urban and rural residents and the normal adjustment of basic pension standards, ensuring that the basic pension insurance benefits for urban and rural residents gradually increase with economic and social development.

The state supports farmers in participating in basic pension insurance and basic medical insurance for urban and rural residents according to regulations, and encourages flexible employment personnel and agricultural industrialization practitioners who meet the conditions to participate in social insurance such as basic pension insurance and basic medical insurance for employees.

The state promotes the coordinated development of the urban and rural minimum living security system, improves the level of social assistance such as support for rural people in extreme poverty, strengthens care services for left-behind children, women, the elderly, persons with disabilities, and children in difficult circumstances, and supports the development of inclusive elderly care services and mutual aid elderly care in rural areas.

Article 55: The state shall promote the formation of a human resources market that is equal competition, standardized and orderly, and unified between urban and rural areas, and improve the public employment and entrepreneurship service system that is equal between urban and rural areas.

Local people's governments at or above the county level shall take measures to promote the voluntary and orderly settlement of farmers who have stable employment and life in cities and towns, and shall not use the withdrawal of land contract management rights, homestead use rights, or collective income distribution rights as conditions for farmers to settle in cities and towns; promote farmers who have obtained residence permits and their accompanying family members to enjoy basic public services in cities and towns.

The state encourages social capital to develop projects linked to farmers' interests in rural areas, encourages urban residents to travel to rural areas for tourism, leisure vacations, health preservation, and elderly care, but shall not damage the rural ecological environment or harm the legitimate rights and interests of rural collective economic organizations and their members.

Article 56: People's governments at or above the county level shall take measures to promote the coordinated development of urban and rural industries, improve the mechanism for linking farmers and incentivizing them on the basis of ensuring the dominant position of farmers, and achieve the diversification of the rural economy and the development of the entire agricultural industry chain.

Article 57: People's governments at all levels and their relevant departments shall take measures to encourage farmers to work in cities and towns, fully implement equal employment and equal pay for equal work for urban and rural workers, and ensure the payment of wages and social security rights and interests of migrant workers according to law.

## Chapter VIII: Support Measures

Article 58: The state shall establish and improve the agricultural support and protection system and the financial investment guarantee system for implementing the rural revitalization strategy. People's governments at or above the county level shall give priority to ensuring financial investment for rural revitalization, ensuring that the intensity of investment is continuously strengthened, the total amount continues to increase, and it is compatible with the goals and tasks of rural revitalization.

People's governments of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government may issue government bonds according to law for the construction of modern agricultural facilities and rural construction.

People's governments at all levels shall improve the long-term mechanism for the overall integration of agricultural-related funds, strengthen the supervision and management of financial funds, fully implement budget performance management, and improve the efficiency of the use of financial funds.

Article 59: People's governments at all levels shall take measures to enhance the endogenous development capacity of areas that have been lifted out of poverty, establish a long-term assistance mechanism for the rural low-income population and underdeveloped areas, and continue to promote the development of areas that have been lifted out of poverty; establish and improve a dynamic monitoring, early warning, and assistance mechanism for the population that is prone to returning to poverty or falling into poverty, and achieve an effective connection between consolidating and expanding the achievements of poverty alleviation and rural revitalization.

The state shall increase support for the implementation of the rural revitalization strategy in old revolutionary base areas, ethnic minority areas, and border areas.

Article 60: The state shall, in accordance with the principles of increasing the total amount, optimizing the stock, and improving efficiency, build a new agricultural subsidy policy system oriented toward high-quality green development.

Article 61: People's governments at all levels shall adhere to the principle of taking from agriculture and mainly using it in agriculture, adjust and improve the scope of use of land transfer income in accordance with relevant national regulations, increase the proportion of investment in agriculture and rural areas, focusing on high-standard farmland construction, farmland water conservancy construction, modern seed industry improvement, rural water supply guarantee, rural human settlement environment improvement, comprehensive rural land improvement, protection of arable land and permanent basic farmland, construction and management of village public facilities, rural education, rural culture and spiritual civilization construction, as well as ecological protection and restoration of mountains, waters, forests, fields, lakes, grasslands, and deserts directly related to agriculture and rural areas, and work-relief project construction.

Article 62: Relevant special funds and funds established by people's governments at or above the county level shall strengthen support for rural revitalization in accordance with regulations.

The state supports the establishment of rural revitalization funds in a market-oriented manner, focusing on supporting the development of rural industries and the construction of public infrastructure.

Local people's governments at or above the county level shall optimize the rural business environment, encourage innovation in investment and financing methods, and guide social capital to invest in rural areas.

Article 63: The state shall comprehensively use fiscal, financial, and other policy measures to improve the government financing guarantee mechanism, improve the mortgage guarantee rights of rural assets according to law, and improve and strengthen financial support and services for rural revitalization.

Agricultural credit guarantee institutions funded by fiscal funds shall primarily serve business entities engaged in agricultural production and those directly related to agricultural production.

Article 64: The state shall improve the multi-level capital market, promote equity financing of agricultural enterprises through multiple channels, develop and regulate the bond market, and promote agricultural enterprises to use various methods for financing; enrich agricultural futures varieties, and leverage the price discovery and risk dispersion functions of the futures market.

Article 65: The state shall establish and improve a multi-level, wide-coverage, and sustainable rural financial service system, improve the assessment and evaluation mechanism for financial support for rural revitalization, promote the development of inclusive finance in rural areas, and encourage financial institutions to allocate more resources to key areas and weak links in rural development according to law.

Policy financial institutions shall provide credit support and other financial services for rural revitalization within their business scope, and increase support for rural revitalization.

Commercial banks shall, based on their own functional positioning and business advantages, innovate financial products and service models, expand the coverage of basic financial services, increase the scale of credit to farmers and agricultural business entities, and provide financial services for rural revitalization.

Rural commercial banks, rural cooperative banks, rural credit cooperatives, and other rural small and medium-sized financial institutions shall primarily serve local agriculture, rural areas, and farmers, and the newly added loanable funds in the current year shall be mainly used for local agricultural and rural development.

Article 66: The state shall establish and improve a multi-level agricultural insurance system, improve the policy-based agricultural insurance system, encourage commercial insurance companies to carry out agricultural insurance business, and support farmers and agricultural business entities in carrying out mutual assistance and cooperative insurance according to law.

People's governments at or above the county level shall take measures such as premium subsidies to support insurance institutions in appropriately increasing insurance varieties, expanding the coverage of agricultural insurance, and promoting the development of agricultural insurance.

Article 67: Local people's governments at or above the county level shall promote economical and intensive land use, improve land use efficiency, take measures according to law to revitalize existing rural construction land, activate rural land resources, improve the guarantee mechanism for new rural construction land, and meet the reasonable land needs of rural industries, public service facilities, and farmers' housing.

Local people's governments at or above the county level shall guarantee land for rural industries, and construction land indicators shall be tilted toward rural development; new arable land indicators within the county shall be prioritized for offsetting construction land indicators needed for rural industrial development, and explore flexible and diverse new land supply methods.

For collective commercial construction land that is determined to be used for industrial, commercial, and other operational purposes in the territorial space plan and registered according to law, the land owner may, according to law, transfer or lease it to units or individuals for use, prioritizing the development of the collective ownership economy and rural industries.

## Chapter IX: Supervision and Inspection

Article 68: The state implements a target responsibility system and an assessment and evaluation system for the implementation of the rural revitalization strategy. Higher-level people's governments shall assess the completion of targets for the implementation of the rural revitalization strategy by lower-level people's governments, and the assessment results shall serve as an important part of the comprehensive assessment and evaluation of local people's governments and their responsible persons.

Article 69: Relevant departments of the State Council and people's governments of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government shall establish indicators and statistical systems that objectively reflect the progress of rural revitalization. Local people's governments at or above the county level shall evaluate the implementation of the rural revitalization strategy within their administrative regions.

Article 70: People's governments at or above the county level shall report on the work of promoting rural revitalization to the people's congresses or their standing committees at the corresponding level. Township people's governments shall report on the work of promoting rural revitalization to the people's congresses at the corresponding level.

Article 71: Local people's governments at all levels shall report on the work of promoting rural revitalization to the people's government at the next higher level each year.

People's governments at or above the county level shall regularly conduct supervision and inspection of the work of promoting rural revitalization by the people's government at the next lower level.

Article 72: The development and reform, finance, agriculture and rural affairs, audit, and other departments of people's governments at or above the county level shall, in accordance with their respective responsibilities, supervise the implementation of the priority guarantee mechanism for agricultural and rural investment, the use of rural revitalization funds, and their performance.

Article 73: If people's governments at all levels and their relevant departments fail to perform or correctly perform their duties in the work of promoting rural revitalization, they shall be held accountable in accordance with laws, regulations, and relevant national provisions, and the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be given sanctions according to law.

Violations of laws and regulations on agricultural product quality and safety, ecological environment protection, land management, etc., shall be punished by the relevant competent departments according to law; if a crime is constituted, criminal liability shall be pursued according to law.

## Chapter X: Supplementary Provisions

Article 74: This Law shall come into force on June 1, 2021.
