# Hainan Free Trade Port Law of the People's Republic of China

Adopted at the 29th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the 13th National People's Congress on June 10, 2021

<!-- INFO END -->

## Chapter I General Provisions

Article 1 This Law is enacted for the purpose of building a high-level Hainan Free Trade Port with Chinese characteristics, promoting the formation of a new pattern of higher-level reform and opening up, establishing a new system of an open economy, and facilitating the stable, healthy, and sustainable development of the socialist market economy.

Article 2 The State establishes the Hainan Free Trade Port on the entire island of Hainan, and establishes the policies and institutional systems of the free trade port step by step and stage by stage, to achieve free and convenient trade, investment, cross-border capital flow, movement of persons, and transportation, as well as orderly and secure data flow.

The construction and management activities of the Hainan Free Trade Port shall be governed by this Law. Matters not provided for in this Law shall be governed by the provisions of other relevant laws and regulations.

Article 3 The construction of the Hainan Free Trade Port shall reflect Chinese characteristics, draw on international experience, focus on Hainan's strategic positioning, leverage Hainan's advantages, promote reform and innovation, strengthen risk prevention, implement the new development philosophy of innovation, coordination, green development, openness, and sharing, adhere to high-quality development, uphold a holistic approach to national security, adhere to a people-centered development philosophy, and achieve economic prosperity, social civility, ecological livability, and people's happiness.

Article 4 The construction of the Hainan Free Trade Port shall focus on the liberalization and facilitation of trade and investment, be supported by the cross-border free, orderly, safe, and convenient flow of various production factors and a modern industrial system, and be guaranteed by special tax arrangements, an efficient social governance system, and a complete legal system. It shall continuously optimize a law-based, international, and convenient business environment and a fair, unified, and efficient market environment.

Article 5 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall implement the strictest ecological and environmental protection system, adhere to ecological priority and green development, innovate the ecological civilization system and mechanism, and build a national ecological civilization pilot zone.

Article 6 The State shall establish a leadership mechanism for the construction of the Hainan Free Trade Port to coordinate major policies and major issues concerning the construction of the Hainan Free Trade Port. The development and reform, finance, commerce, financial management, customs, taxation, and other departments of the State Council shall, according to their respective functions and duties, guide and promote the relevant work of the construction of the Hainan Free Trade Port.

The State shall establish an administrative management system compatible with the construction of the Hainan Free Trade Port and innovate regulatory models.

Hainan Province shall earnestly fulfill its responsibilities, strengthen organizational leadership, and make every effort to promote all work related to the construction of the Hainan Free Trade Port.

Article 7 The State supports the construction and development of the Hainan Free Trade Port, and supports Hainan Province in exercising its autonomy in reform in accordance with the requirements of the Central Committee and the provisions of the law. The State Council and its relevant departments shall, based on the actual needs of the construction of the Hainan Free Trade Port, promptly authorize or entrust the People's Government of Hainan Province and its relevant departments to exercise relevant management powers in accordance with the law.

Article 8 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall build a complete, scientific, standardized, and effective governance system for the Hainan Free Trade Port, promote the reform of government institutions and the transformation of functions, standardize government service standards, strengthen the construction of mechanisms for preventing and resolving social conflicts, improve the level of intelligent social governance, and perfect the social governance system of joint contribution, joint governance, and shared benefits.

The State shall promote the reform and innovation of the administrative divisions of the Hainan Free Trade Port, and optimize the setting of administrative divisions and the structure of the administrative division system.

Article 9 The State supports the Hainan Free Trade Port in actively adapting to the development of international economic and trade rules and the new trends in the reform of the global economic governance system, and actively carrying out international exchanges and cooperation.

Article 10 The People's Congress of Hainan Province and its Standing Committee may, in accordance with this Law, in light of the specific conditions and actual needs of the construction of the Hainan Free Trade Port, and following the principles of the Constitution and the basic principles of laws and administrative regulations, formulate regulations concerning trade, investment, and related management activities (hereinafter referred to as Hainan Free Trade Port regulations), which shall be implemented within the scope of the Hainan Free Trade Port.

Hainan Free Trade Port regulations shall be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the State Council for recordation; where modifications are made to the provisions of laws or administrative regulations, the circumstances and reasons for such modifications shall be explained.

Where Hainan Free Trade Port regulations involve matters that should, according to law, be governed by laws formulated by the National People's Congress or its Standing Committee, or by administrative regulations formulated by the State Council, they shall be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress or the State Council for approval before taking effect.

## Chapter II Free and Convenient Trade

Article 11 The State shall establish and improve the special customs supervision zone system for the Hainan Free Trade Port with island-wide customs clearance operations. On the basis of effective supervision according to law, a management system for the free entry and exit, safe and convenient trade in goods shall be established, and management measures for trade in services shall be optimized, to achieve the liberalization and facilitation of trade.

Article 12 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall build high-standard port infrastructure, and strengthen the control of port public health security, national biosecurity, food safety, and commodity quality safety.

Article 13 Goods and articles may freely enter and exit between overseas areas and the Hainan Free Trade Port, subject to customs supervision according to law, except for those included in the list of goods and articles prohibited or restricted from import and export in the Hainan Free Trade Port.

The list mentioned in the preceding paragraph shall be formulated by the competent department of commerce of the State Council jointly with the relevant departments of the State Council and Hainan Province.

Article 14 When goods enter other areas within the territory of China (hereinafter referred to as the mainland) from the Hainan Free Trade Port, the relevant procedures shall, in principle, be handled in accordance with import regulations. Articles entering the mainland from the Hainan Free Trade Port shall be supervised according to regulations. For means of transport traveling from the Hainan Free Trade Port to the mainland, import management shall be simplified.

When goods, articles, and means of transport enter the Hainan Free Trade Port from the mainland, they shall be managed in accordance with domestic circulation regulations.

Specific measures for the entry and exit of goods, articles, and means of transport between the Hainan Free Trade Port and the mainland shall be formulated by the relevant departments of the State Council jointly with Hainan Province.

Article 15 Various market entities may freely engage in trade in goods and related activities within the Hainan Free Trade Port according to law, and customs shall implement low-intervention, high-efficiency supervision.

Subject to meeting requirements such as environmental protection and production safety, the Hainan Free Trade Port shall not set storage periods for imported and exported goods, and the storage location of goods may be freely chosen.

Article 16 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall implement customs clearance facilitation policies, simplifying the flow process and procedures for goods. Except for goods that require inspection and quarantine or are subject to license management according to law, goods entering the Hainan Free Trade Port shall be released directly by customs in accordance with relevant regulations, providing market entities with convenient customs clearance services.

Article 17 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall implement a negative list management system for cross-border trade in services, and implement a supporting system for payment and transfer of funds. Cross-border trade in services not on the list shall be managed in accordance with the principle of equal treatment for domestic and foreign entities.

The negative list for cross-border trade in services in the Hainan Free Trade Port shall be formulated by the competent department of commerce of the State Council jointly with the relevant departments of the State Council and Hainan Province.

## Chapter III Free and Convenient Investment

Article 18 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall implement policies for the liberalization and facilitation of investment, comprehensively promote a minimalist approval investment system, improve investment promotion and protection systems, strengthen property rights protection, ensure fair competition, and create an open, transparent, and predictable investment environment.

The Hainan Free Trade Port shall fully liberalize investment access, except for areas where the State implements access management involving national security, social stability, ecological protection red lines, major public interests, etc.

Article 19 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall implement a pre-establishment national treatment plus negative list management system for foreign investment. The negative list for foreign investment access specifically applicable to the Hainan Free Trade Port shall be formulated by the relevant departments of the State Council jointly with Hainan Province, and published after being submitted to the State Council for approval.

Article 20 The State shall relax market access in the Hainan Free Trade Port. The special list (special measures) for relaxing market access in the Hainan Free Trade Port shall be formulated by the relevant departments of the State Council jointly with Hainan Province.

The Hainan Free Trade Port shall implement investment facilitation measures focusing on process supervision, and gradually implement a commitment-based market access system. Specific measures shall be formulated by Hainan Province jointly with the relevant departments of the State Council.

Article 21 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall, in accordance with the principles of convenience, efficiency, and transparency, simplify procedures, improve efficiency, optimize government services, establish systems for convenient establishment, operation, and cancellation of market entities, and optimize bankruptcy procedures. Specific measures shall be formulated by the People's Congress of Hainan Province and its Standing Committee.

Article 22 The State shall protect, according to law, the investments, income, and other lawful rights and interests of natural persons, legal persons, and unincorporated organizations within the Hainan Free Trade Port, and strengthen the protection of small and medium investors.

Article 23 The State shall protect, according to law, the intellectual property rights of natural persons, legal persons, and unincorporated organizations within the Hainan Free Trade Port, promote the improvement of the capacity for the creation, utilization, and management services of intellectual property rights, establish and improve mechanisms such as credit-based classified supervision and punishment for breach of trust in the field of intellectual property rights, and strictly pursue legal liability for intellectual property infringement according to law.

Article 24 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall establish a unified, open, competitive, and orderly market system, strengthen the fundamental role of competition policy, implement the fair competition review system, strengthen and improve the enforcement of anti-monopoly and anti-unfair competition laws, and protect fair competition in the market.

Various market entities in the Hainan Free Trade Port shall enjoy equal treatment according to law in terms of access permits, business operations, factor acquisition, standard setting, preferential policies, etc. Specific measures shall be formulated by the People's Congress of Hainan Province and its Standing Committee.

## Chapter IV Fiscal and Taxation System

Article 25 During the development and construction phase of the Hainan Free Trade Port, the central finance shall, based on actual conditions and in light of changes in the tax system, provide appropriate financial support to the Hainan Free Trade Port. Hainan Province is encouraged to issue local government bonds within the limits approved by the State Council to support the construction of Hainan Free Trade Port projects. Hainan Province shall establish a government-guided, market-oriented Hainan Free Trade Port Construction Investment Fund.

Article 26 The Hainan Free Trade Port may, according to development needs, independently reduce, exempt, or defer government funds other than those with ecological compensation nature.

Article 27 In accordance with the principles of a simple and scientific tax structure, fully optimized tax elements, significantly reduced tax burden levels, clear attribution of revenue, and basically balanced fiscal revenue and expenditure, and in conjunction with the direction of national tax system reform, a tax system suitable for the needs of the Hainan Free Trade Port shall be established.

At the time of island-wide customs clearance operations, taxes and fees such as value-added tax, consumption tax, vehicle purchase tax, urban maintenance and construction tax, and education surcharges shall be simplified and consolidated, and a sales tax shall be levied on the retail of goods and services; after island-wide customs clearance operations, the tax system shall be further simplified.

The finance department of the State Council, jointly with the relevant departments of the State Council and Hainan Province, shall promptly propose specific plans for simplifying the tax system.

Article 28 After island-wide customs clearance operations and tax simplification, the Hainan Free Trade Port shall implement a catalog management system for imported goods subject to taxation. Goods not included in the catalog entering the Hainan Free Trade Port shall be exempted from import tariffs. The catalog of imported goods subject to taxation shall be formulated by the finance department of the State Council jointly with the relevant departments of the State Council and Hainan Province.

Before island-wide customs clearance operations and tax simplification, certain imported goods shall be exempted from import tariffs, import value-added tax, and consumption tax.

Export taxable goods departing from the Hainan Free Trade Port shall be subject to export tariffs.

Article 29 When goods enter the mainland from the Hainan Free Trade Port, they shall, in principle, be taxed as imports; however, goods produced by enterprises in encouraged industries that do not contain imported materials or contain imported materials that have achieved a certain proportion of value-added processing in the Hainan Free Trade Port shall be exempted from tariffs. Specific measures shall be formulated by the relevant departments of the State Council jointly with Hainan Province.

When goods enter the Hainan Free Trade Port from the mainland, the value-added tax and consumption tax already levied shall be refunded in accordance with the relevant provisions of the State Council.

Before island-wide customs clearance operations and tax simplification, departing island passengers who purchase duty-free goods and take delivery of them for departure shall be exempted from import tariffs, import value-added tax, and consumption tax in accordance with relevant regulations. After island-wide customs clearance operations and tax simplification, the tax management measures for the entry and exit of articles between the Hainan Free Trade Port and the mainland shall be formulated by the relevant departments of the State Council jointly with Hainan Province.

Article 30 Qualified enterprises registered in the Hainan Free Trade Port shall enjoy corporate income tax preferences; qualified individuals within the Hainan Free Trade Port shall enjoy individual income tax preferences.

Article 31 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall establish an optimized, efficient, and unified tax collection and management service system, improve the scientific, information-based, international, and people-friendly level of tax collection and management services, actively participate in international tax collection and management cooperation, improve the quality and efficiency of tax collection and management services, and protect the legitimate rights and interests of taxpayers.

## Chapter V Ecological and Environmental Protection

Article 32 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall improve the ecological environment assessment and monitoring system, formulate an ecological environment access list, prevent pollution, and protect the ecological environment; improve the property rights system and paid use system for natural resource assets, and promote the conservation and efficient use of resources.

Article 33 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall promote the construction of a territorial space planning system, implement differentiated control over the use of natural ecological spaces, strictly adhere to ecological protection red lines, build a system of protected natural areas with national parks as the main body, and promote green urbanization and the construction of beautiful villages.

The Hainan Free Trade Port shall strictly protect the marine ecological environment, and establish and improve a land-sea coordinated regional linkage mechanism for ecosystem protection, restoration, and pollution prevention.

Article 34 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall implement a strict environmental security access management system for entry and exit, strengthen inspection and quarantine capacity building, prevent the invasion of alien species, prohibit the import of foreign solid waste; improve the capacity for the treatment and disposal of hazardous waste such as medical waste, enhance emergency preparedness and response capacity for sudden ecological and environmental incidents, and strengthen ecological risk prevention and control.

Article 35 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall promote the establishment of a government-led, enterprise and social participation, market-oriented, and sustainable ecological protection compensation mechanism, establish a mechanism for realizing the value of ecological products, encourage the use of market mechanisms to promote ecological and environmental protection, and achieve sustainable development.

Article 36 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall implement an environmental protection target responsibility system and an assessment and evaluation system. In the annual assessment of the departments responsible for environmental supervision and management under the people's government at the same level and their responsible persons, and of the people's government at the lower level and its responsible persons, the local people's government at or above the county level shall implement a one-vote veto system for the completion of environmental protection targets.

In areas where environmental protection targets have not been met, the approval of environmental impact assessment documents for construction projects that increase the total discharge of key pollutants in the area shall be suspended for one year; the main responsible persons of the local people's government and the departments responsible for environmental supervision and management that are responsible shall not be promoted or transferred to important positions for one year, and shall be punished according to law.

Article 37 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall implement a lifelong accountability system for ecological and environmental damage. The main responsible persons, directly responsible supervisors, and other directly responsible persons of the local people's government and relevant departments that violate the requirements of scientific development and cause serious damage to the ecological environment shall be strictly held accountable.

## Chapter VI Industrial Development and Talent Support

Article 38 The State supports the Hainan Free Trade Port in building an open, ecological, and service-oriented industrial system, and actively developing key industries such as tourism, modern services, high-tech industries, and tropical high-efficiency agriculture.

Article 39 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall promote the construction of an international tourism consumption center, promote the deep integration of tourism with culture, sports, health care, medical care, and elderly care, and cultivate new business forms and models in tourism.

Article 40 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall deepen the opening up of modern service industries to domestic and foreign markets, build an international shipping hub, promote the integrated development of ports, industries, and cities, improve marine service infrastructure, and build an internationally competitive marine service system.

Overseas high-level universities and vocational colleges may establish schools in the fields of science, engineering, agriculture, and medicine in the Hainan Free Trade Port.

Article 41 The State supports the Hainan Free Trade Port in building major scientific research infrastructure and platform facilities, and establishing a scientific and technological innovation management system and an international science and technology cooperation mechanism that conform to the laws of scientific research.

Article 42 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall, according to law, establish a safe, orderly, free, and convenient data flow management system, protect the rights and interests of individuals and organizations related to data according to law, orderly expand the opening of communication resources and services, expand the opening of the data field, and promote the development of the digital economy with data as a key element.

The State supports the Hainan Free Trade Port in exploring the implementation of regional international data cross-border flow institutional arrangements.

Article 43 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall implement highly free, convenient, and open transportation policies, establish a more open shipping system and ship management system, build the "China Yangpu Port" ship registry, and implement a special ship registration system; relax airspace control and route restrictions, optimize the allocation of air rights resources, and improve transportation facilitation and service guarantee levels.

Article 44 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall deepen the reform of the system and mechanism for talent development, innovate the talent training and support mechanism, and establish a scientific and reasonable mechanism for talent introduction, recognition, utilization, and treatment guarantee.

Article 45 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall establish an efficient and convenient entry and exit management system, gradually implement a visa-free entry policy applicable to a wider scope, extend the visa-free stay period, optimize entry and exit inspection management, and provide entry and exit customs clearance convenience.

Article 46 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall implement more open talent and residence policies, implement more relaxed temporary entry and exit policies for personnel, convenient work visa policies, implement negative list management for foreigner work permits, and further improve the residence system.

Article 47 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall relax the restrictions on foreigners taking professional qualification examinations, and implement a unilateral recognition list system for the recognition of foreign professional qualifications that meet the conditions.

## Chapter VII Comprehensive Measures

Article 48 The State Council may, based on the needs of the construction of the Hainan Free Trade Port, authorize the People's Government of Hainan Province to approve the conversion of agricultural land into construction land and land acquisition matters that are originally subject to approval by the State Council; authorize the People's Government of Hainan Province, on the condition that it does not exceed the important indicators such as the ecological protection red line, the area of permanent basic farmland, the amount of cultivated land and forest land, and the total scale of construction land as defined in the territorial space planning of Hainan Province and ensures that the quality is not reduced, to approve the layout adjustment of cultivated land, permanent basic farmland, forest land, and construction land in the province in accordance with the conditions prescribed by the State.

The Hainan Free Trade Port shall actively promote the coordinated development of urban, rural, and reclamation areas and the new model of small town construction land, and promote the capitalization of agricultural reclamation land.

The sea use needs of major national projects in the Hainan Free Trade Port shall be guaranteed according to law.

Article 49 The construction of the Hainan Free Trade Port shall effectively protect cultivated land, strengthen land management, and establish a system for intensive and economical land use, evaluation standards, and a system for revitalizing and disposing of existing construction land. For land that is idle, where land use rights are obtained through transfer for development, and the land has not been completed one year after the completion date stipulated in the transfer contract, a land idle fee of a certain proportion of the present value of the transferred land shall be levied annually before completion. Specific measures shall be formulated by Hainan Province.

Article 50 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall adhere to the principle of financial services serving the real economy, promote financial reform and innovation, and take the lead in implementing financial industry opening policies.Article 51 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall establish a cross-border capital flow management system that meets the needs of high-level trade and investment liberalization and facilitation, gradually open capital account items, progressively achieve full convertibility under non-financial enterprise external debt items, promote the facilitation of cross-border trade settlement, and orderly facilitate the free and convenient flow of funds between the Hainan Free Trade Port and overseas.

Article 52 Approved financial institutions within the Hainan Free Trade Port may conduct offshore financial business through designated accounts or in specific areas.

Article 53 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall strengthen the construction and application of its social credit system, establishing mechanisms to incentivize trustworthiness and penalize dishonesty.

Article 54 The State supports the exploration of judicial system reforms compatible with the Hainan Free Trade Port. The Hainan Free Trade Port shall establish a diversified commercial dispute resolution mechanism, improve the centralized trial mechanism for international commercial dispute cases, and support the resolution of disputes through non-litigation methods such as arbitration and mediation.

Article 55 The Hainan Free Trade Port shall establish a risk early warning and prevention and control system to prevent and mitigate major risks.

Customs is responsible for routine supervision at ports and other customs surveillance zones, conducting anti-smuggling investigations and follow-up supervision in accordance with the law. Maritime police agencies are responsible for investigating and dealing with maritime smuggling violations. The People's Government of Hainan Province is responsible for the comprehensive anti-smuggling management work across the province, strengthening control over non-designated ports, and establishing joint anti-smuggling prevention and control mechanisms with other regions. Personnel, goods, articles, and means of transport must enter and exit through ports between overseas and the Hainan Free Trade Port, as well as between the Hainan Free Trade Port and the mainland.

In the Hainan Free Trade Port, a foreign investment security review system shall be implemented in accordance with the law, conducting security reviews of foreign investments that affect or may affect national security.

The Hainan Free Trade Port shall establish and improve a financial risk prevention and control system, implement a cybersecurity level protection system, establish a personnel flow risk prevention and control system, and establish mechanisms for monitoring, early warning, prevention, control, and treatment of infectious diseases and public health emergencies, ensuring order and security in areas such as finance, network and data, personnel flow, and public health.

## Chapter 8 Supplementary Provisions

Article 56 For matters stipulated in this Law, after its implementation and before the island-wide customs closure operation of the Hainan Free Trade Port, the State Council, its relevant departments, and Hainan Province may, in accordance with the principles set forth in this Law and based on their respective responsibilities, formulate transitional specific measures to promote the construction of the Hainan Free Trade Port.

Article 57 This Law shall come into effect on the date of its promulgation.
