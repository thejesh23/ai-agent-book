# Animal Husbandry Law of the People's Republic of China

Adopted at the 19th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Tenth National People's Congress on December 29, 2005

Amended in accordance with the Decision on Revising Five Laws Including the Metrology Law of the People's Republic of China at the 14th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Twelfth National People's Congress on April 24, 2015

<!-- INFO END -->

## Chapter I General Provisions

Article 1 This Law is enacted for the purposes of regulating livestock and poultry production and operation activities, ensuring the quality and safety of livestock and poultry products, protecting and rationally utilizing livestock and poultry genetic resources, safeguarding the legitimate rights and interests of livestock and poultry producers and operators, and promoting the sustainable and healthy development of animal husbandry.

Article 2 This Law applies to activities such as the protection and utilization of livestock and poultry genetic resources, breeding, raising, operation, and transportation within the territory of the People's Republic of China.

The term "livestock and poultry" as used in this Law refers to livestock and poultry included in the catalog of livestock and poultry genetic resources published in accordance with the provisions of Article 11 of this Law.

The protection, utilization, production, and operation of bee and silkworm resources shall be governed by the relevant provisions of this Law.

Article 3 The State supports the development of animal husbandry and leverages the role of animal husbandry in developing agriculture, the rural economy, and increasing farmers' income. People's governments at or above the county level shall take measures to strengthen the construction of animal husbandry infrastructure, encourage and support the development of large-scale farming, promote industrialized operation of animal husbandry, enhance the comprehensive production capacity of animal husbandry, and develop high-quality, efficient, ecological, and safe animal husbandry.

The State assists and supports the development of animal husbandry in ethnic minority areas and poverty-stricken areas, protects and rationally utilizes grasslands, and improves animal husbandry production conditions.

Article 4 The State takes measures to cultivate professional talents in animal husbandry and veterinary medicine, develop scientific research and extension in animal husbandry and veterinary technology, carry out educational and promotional activities on animal husbandry and veterinary science and technology knowledge, and provide animal husbandry and veterinary information services, thereby promoting scientific and technological progress in animal husbandry.

Article 5 Livestock and poultry producers and operators may voluntarily establish industry associations in accordance with the law to provide members with services such as information, technology, marketing, and training, strengthen industry self-discipline, and safeguard the interests of members and the industry.

Article 6 Livestock and poultry producers and operators shall fulfill their obligations for animal epidemic prevention and environmental protection in accordance with the law and accept supervision and inspections conducted by relevant competent authorities in accordance with the law.

Article 7 The competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council shall be responsible for the supervision and management of animal husbandry nationwide. The competent administrative departments for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of local people's governments at or above the county level shall be responsible for the supervision and management of animal husbandry within their respective administrative regions.

The relevant competent authorities of people's governments at or above the county level shall, within their respective functions and duties, be responsible for work related to promoting the development of animal husbandry.

Article 8 The competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council shall guide livestock and poultry producers and operators in improving the conditions and environment for livestock and poultry breeding, raising, and transportation.

## Chapter II Protection of Livestock and Poultry Genetic Resources

Article 9 The State establishes a system for the protection of livestock and poultry genetic resources. People's governments at all levels shall take measures to strengthen the protection of livestock and poultry genetic resources, and funds for the protection of livestock and poultry genetic resources shall be included in the fiscal budget.

The protection of livestock and poultry genetic resources shall be primarily undertaken by the State, which encourages and supports relevant entities and individuals to develop the cause of protecting livestock and poultry genetic resources in accordance with the law.

Article 10 The competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council shall establish a National Committee on Livestock and Poultry Genetic Resources composed of professionals, which shall be responsible for the identification and evaluation of livestock and poultry genetic resources, the approval of new livestock and poultry breeds and supporting lines, and undertake the planning and demonstration of the protection and utilization of livestock and poultry genetic resources, as well as advisory work related to the protection of livestock and poultry genetic resources.

Article 11 The competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council shall be responsible for organizing surveys of livestock and poultry genetic resources, issuing reports on the status of national livestock and poultry genetic resources, and publishing the catalog of livestock and poultry genetic resources approved by the State Council.

Article 12 The competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council shall, based on the distribution of livestock and poultry genetic resources, formulate a national plan for the protection and utilization of livestock and poultry genetic resources, formulate and publish a national list of protected livestock and poultry genetic resources, and implement key protection for precious, rare, and endangered livestock and poultry genetic resources originating from China.

The competent administrative departments for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of provincial people's governments shall, based on the national plan for the protection and utilization of livestock and poultry genetic resources and the status of livestock and poultry genetic resources within their respective administrative regions, formulate and publish provincial lists of protected livestock and poultry genetic resources, and report them to the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council for recordation.

Article 13 The competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council shall, based on the national plan for the protection and utilization of livestock and poultry genetic resources and the national list of protected livestock and poultry genetic resources, and the competent administrative departments for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of provincial people's governments shall, based on the provincial lists of protected livestock and poultry genetic resources, respectively establish or designate livestock and poultry genetic resources conservation farms, conservation areas, and gene banks to undertake the task of protecting livestock and poultry genetic resources.

Livestock and poultry genetic resources conservation farms, conservation areas, and gene banks that receive financial support from the central and provincial fiscal budgets shall not dispose of protected livestock and poultry genetic resources without authorization without the approval of the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council or the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of the provincial people's government.

Livestock and poultry genetic resources gene banks shall, in accordance with the regulations of the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council or the competent administrative departments for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of provincial people's governments, regularly collect and update livestock and poultry genetic materials. Relevant entities and individuals shall cooperate with livestock and poultry genetic resources gene banks in collecting livestock and poultry genetic materials and shall be entitled to appropriate economic compensation.

The administrative measures for livestock and poultry genetic resources conservation farms, conservation areas, and gene banks shall be formulated by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council.

Article 14 For newly discovered livestock and poultry genetic resources, before identification by the National Committee on Livestock and Poultry Genetic Resources, the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of the provincial people's government shall formulate a protection plan, take temporary protection measures, and report to the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council for recordation.

Article 15 To introduce livestock and poultry genetic resources from abroad, an application shall be submitted to the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of the provincial people's government; the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine that accepts the application shall, after review, report to the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council for approval after evaluation and demonstration. Upon approval, relevant procedures shall be handled and quarantine shall be carried out in accordance with the provisions of the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Entry and Exit Animal and Plant Quarantine.

If livestock and poultry genetic resources introduced from abroad are found to cause or may cause harm to domestic livestock and poultry genetic resources or the ecological environment, the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council shall consult with relevant competent authorities and take corresponding safety control measures.

Article 16 To export livestock and poultry genetic resources included in the protection list or to conduct cooperative research and utilization with foreign institutions or individuals within the territory, an application shall be submitted to the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of the provincial people's government, together with a plan for national benefit-sharing; the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine that accepts the application shall, after review, report to the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council for approval.

To export livestock and poultry genetic resources, relevant procedures shall also be handled and quarantine shall be carried out in accordance with the provisions of the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Entry and Exit Animal and Plant Quarantine.

Newly discovered livestock and poultry genetic resources shall not be exported or used for cooperative research and utilization with foreign institutions or individuals before identification by the National Committee on Livestock and Poultry Genetic Resources.

Article 17 The approval procedures for the entry and exit of livestock and poultry genetic resources and for foreign cooperative research and utilization shall be formulated by the State Council.

## Chapter III Selection, Breeding, Production, and Operation of Breeding Livestock and Poultry

Article 18 The State supports the selection and breeding of livestock and poultry varieties and the promotion and use of improved varieties, and supports enterprises, colleges and universities, scientific research institutions, and technology extension units in carrying out joint breeding to establish a system for the propagation of improved livestock and poultry breeds.

Article 19 Newly cultivated livestock and poultry breeds, supporting lines, and newly discovered livestock and poultry genetic resources shall, before promotion, be approved or identified by the National Committee on Livestock and Poultry Genetic Resources and announced by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council. The measures for the approval of new livestock and poultry breeds and supporting lines and the measures for the identification of livestock and poultry genetic resources shall be formulated by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council. The costs of tests and inspections required for approval or identification shall be borne by the applicant, and the fee collection measures shall be formulated by the finance and price departments under the State Council in conjunction with the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council.

Intermediate trials for cultivating new livestock and poultry breeds or supporting lines shall be approved by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of the provincial people's government where the trials are conducted.

The legitimate rights and interests of cultivators of new livestock and poultry breeds and supporting lines shall be protected by law.

Article 20 The cultivation, testing, approval, and promotion of genetically modified livestock and poultry breeds shall comply with the relevant provisions of the State on the management of agricultural genetically modified organisms.

Article 21 Animal husbandry and veterinary technology extension institutions at or above the provincial level may organize the registration of excellent individual breeding livestock and recommend excellent breeding livestock to the public. The rules for the registration of excellent breeding livestock shall be formulated by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council.

Article 22 Entities and individuals engaged in the production and operation of breeding livestock and poultry or the production of commercial generation young livestock and poultry shall obtain a production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry.

To apply for a production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry, the following conditions shall be met:

(1) The breeding livestock and poultry produced and operated must be varieties or supporting lines that have been approved or identified by the National Committee on Livestock and Poultry Genetic Resources, or foreign varieties or supporting lines that have been approved for introduction;

(2) Having animal husbandry and veterinary technical personnel commensurate with the scale of production and operation;

(3) Having breeding facilities and equipment commensurate with the scale of production and operation;

(4) Meeting the epidemic prevention conditions for breeding livestock and poultry as stipulated by laws, administrative regulations, and the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council;

(5) Having a sound quality management and breeding record system;

(6) Meeting other conditions stipulated by laws and administrative regulations.

Article 23 To apply for a production and operation license for producing genetic materials such as livestock oocytes, frozen semen, and embryos, in addition to meeting the conditions specified in paragraph 2 of Article 22 of this Law, the following conditions shall also be met:

(1) Meeting the laboratory, storage, and transportation conditions stipulated by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council;

(2) Meeting the quantity and quality requirements for breeding livestock stipulated by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council;

(3) The embryos obtained through in vitro fertilization and the oocytes used have clear sources, and the donor livestock meet the national health standards and quality requirements for breeding livestock;

(4) Meeting other technical requirements stipulated by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council.

Article 24 To apply for a production and operation license for producing genetic materials such as livestock oocytes, frozen semen, and embryos, an application shall be submitted to the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of the provincial people's government. The competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine that accepts the application shall decide whether to issue the production and operation license within sixty working days from the date of receiving the application in accordance with the law.

Production and operation licenses for other breeding livestock and poultry shall be reviewed and issued by the competent administrative departments for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of local people's governments at or above the county level, and the specific review and issuance measures shall be formulated by the provincial people's government.

The format of the production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry shall be formulated by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council, and the license shall be valid for three years. A fee for the cost of the certificate may be charged for issuing the production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry, and the specific fee management measures shall be formulated by the finance and price departments under the State Council.

Article 25 The production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry shall indicate the name of the producer and operator, the site (factory) address, the scope of production and operation, and the start and end dates of the license validity period.

No entity or individual shall produce or operate breeding livestock and poultry without a production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry or in violation of the provisions of the production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry. Forging, altering, transferring, or lending the production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry is prohibited.

Article 26 Farmers who raise breeding livestock and poultry for self-breeding and self-raising and have a small surplus of young livestock and poultry for sale, and farmers who raise male breeding livestock for mutual breeding services, are not required to obtain a production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry.

Article 27 Personnel specialized in artificial insemination, embryo transfer, and other breeding work for livestock shall obtain the corresponding national vocational qualification certificate.

Article 28 When publishing advertisements for breeding livestock and poultry, the advertiser shall provide the production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry and the business license. The content of the advertisement shall comply with the provisions of relevant laws and administrative regulations, and shall indicate the approved or identified name of the breeding livestock and poultry variety or supporting line; the description of main traits shall conform to the standards of the variety or supporting line.

Article 29 Breeding livestock and poultry sold and male breeding livestock used by livestock breeding stations (points) must meet the breeding standards. When selling breeding livestock and poultry, a certificate of conformity for breeding livestock and poultry issued by the breeding livestock and poultry farm and a quarantine certificate issued by the animal epidemic prevention supervision institution shall be attached; for breeding livestock sold, a livestock pedigree issued by the breeding livestock and poultry farm shall also be attached.

For the production of genetic materials such as livestock oocytes, frozen semen, and embryos, complete records of collection, sales, and transplantation shall be maintained, and the records shall be kept for two years.

Article 30 When selling breeding livestock and poultry, the following acts are prohibited:

(1) Passing off other livestock and poultry varieties or supporting lines as the breeding livestock and poultry varieties or supporting lines being sold;

(2) Passing off lower-generation breeding livestock and poultry as higher-generation breeding livestock and poultry;

(3) Passing off livestock and poultry that do not meet breeding standards as breeding livestock and poultry;

(4) Selling breeding livestock and poultry imported without approval;

(5) Selling breeding livestock and poultry without attaching the certificate of conformity for breeding livestock and poultry or the quarantine certificate as required by Article 29 of this Law, or selling breeding livestock without attaching the livestock pedigree;

(6) Selling breeding livestock and poultry varieties or supporting lines that have not been approved or identified.

Article 31 To apply for the import of breeding livestock and poultry, a production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry shall be held. The validity period of the approval document for importing breeding livestock and poultry is six months.

Imported breeding livestock and poultry shall meet the technical requirements stipulated by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council. For breeding livestock and poultry imported for the first time, an evaluation of breeding performance shall also be conducted by the National Committee on Livestock and Poultry Genetic Resources.

In addition to the provisions of the preceding two paragraphs, the import and export management of breeding livestock and poultry shall also be governed by the relevant provisions of Articles 15 and 16 of this Law.

The State encourages livestock and poultry breeders to select and breed new varieties and supporting lines from imported livestock and poultry; new varieties and supporting lines selected and bred shall be approved by the National Committee on Livestock and Poultry Genetic Resources before promotion.

Article 32 Breeding livestock and poultry farms and hatcheries (factories) that sell commercial generation young livestock and poultry shall provide buyers with the main production performance indicators, immunization status, feeding technical requirements, and related consulting services for the commercial generation young livestock and poultry they sell, and attach a quarantine certificate issued by the animal epidemic prevention supervision institution.

If losses are caused to livestock and poultry breeders due to quality issues in the sale of breeding livestock and poultry or commercial generation young livestock and poultry, compensation for losses shall be paid in accordance with the law.

Article 33 The competent administrative departments for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of people's governments at or above the county level shall be responsible for the supervision and management of the quality and safety of breeding livestock and poultry. The supervision and inspection of the quality and safety of breeding livestock and poultry shall be entrusted to a legally qualified quality inspection institution for breeding livestock and poultry; the required inspection costs shall be disbursed in accordance with the provisions of the State Council and shall not be collected from the inspected party.

Article 34 The protection of silkworm egg resources, selection and breeding of new varieties, production, operation, and promotion shall be governed by the relevant provisions of this Law, and the specific administrative measures shall be formulated by the competent agricultural administrative department under the State Council.

## Chapter IV Livestock and Poultry Farming

Article 35 The competent administrative departments for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of people's governments at or above the county level shall, based on animal husbandry development plans and market demand, guide and support the adjustment of the animal husbandry structure, develop advantageous livestock and poultry production, and enhance the market competitiveness of livestock and poultry products.

The State supports grassland pastoral areas in carrying out basic grassland construction such as grassland fencing, grassland water conservancy, grassland improvement, and forage grass bases, optimizing herd structure, improving livestock breeds, transforming production methods, developing shed feeding and rotational grazing, gradually achieving a balance between livestock and grass, and improving the grassland ecological environment.

Article 36 The State Council and provincial people's governments shall allocate funds such as improved breed subsidies and discounted loan subsidies within their fiscal budgets to support the development of animal husbandry, and encourage relevant financial institutions to provide loans, insurance services, and other forms of support to assist livestock and poultry breeders in purchasing improved livestock and poultry, breeding improved breeds, improving production facilities, expanding farming scale, and increasing farming efficiency.

Article 37 The State supports rural collective economic organizations, farmers, and animal husbandry cooperative economic organizations in establishing livestock and poultry farms and farming communities to develop large-scale and standardized farming. The township (town) land use master plan shall, based on local actual conditions, arrange land for livestock and poultry farming. Land used by rural collective economic organizations, farmers, and animal husbandry cooperative economic organizations for establishing livestock and poultry farms and farming communities in accordance with the township (town) land use master plan shall be managed as agricultural land. When the term of the land use right for livestock and poultry farms and farming communities expires and the land needs to be restored to its original use, the land use right holder of the livestock and poultry farm or farming community shall be responsible for the restoration. If permanent buildings (structures) need to be constructed within the land area of livestock and poultry farms and farming communities, involving the conversion of agricultural land, the matter shall be handled in accordance with the provisions of the Land Administration Law of the People's Republic of China.

Article 38 Animal husbandry and veterinary technology extension institutions established by the State shall provide farmers with services such as training in livestock and poultry farming techniques, promotion of improved breeds, and disease prevention and control. People's governments at or above the county level shall ensure the operating funds for animal husbandry and veterinary technology extension institutions established by the State to engage in public welfare technical services.

The State encourages livestock and poultry product processing enterprises and other related producers and operators to provide services needed by livestock and poultry breeders.

Article 39 Livestock and poultry farms and farming communities shall meet the following conditions:

(1) Having production sites and supporting production facilities commensurate with their farming scale;

(2) Having animal husbandry and veterinary technical personnel serving them;

(3) Meeting the epidemic prevention conditions stipulated by laws, administrative regulations, and the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council;

(4) Having facilities such as biogas digesters or other harmless treatment facilities for the comprehensive utilization of livestock and poultry manure, wastewater, and other solid waste;

(5) Meeting other conditions stipulated by laws and administrative regulations.

The establisher of a farm or farming community shall report the name, farming address, livestock and poultry breed, and farming scale of the farm or farming community to the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine of the county people's government where the farm or farming community is located for recordation, and obtain a livestock and poultry identification code.

Provincial people's governments shall, based on the development status of animal husbandry within their respective administrative regions, formulate scale standards and recordation procedures for livestock and poultry farms and farming communities.

Article 40 It is prohibited to establish livestock and poultry farms or farming communities within the following areas:

(1) Drinking water source protection areas, scenic spots, and the core areas and buffer zones of nature reserves;

(2) Urban residential areas, cultural, educational, and scientific research areas, and other densely populated areas;

(3) Other areas where farming is prohibited as stipulated by laws and regulations.

Article 41 Livestock and poultry farms shall establish farming records, which shall include the following:

(1) The breed, quantity, breeding records, identification status, source, and dates of entry and exit of livestock and poultry;

(2) The source, name, target, time, and dosage of inputs such as feed, feed additives, and veterinary drugs;

(3) Quarantine, immunization, and disinfection status;

(4) The incidence, mortality, and harmless treatment of livestock and poultry diseases;

(5) Other contents stipulated by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council.

Article 42 Livestock and poultry farms shall provide appropriate breeding conditions and a suitable living and growing environment for the livestock and poultry they raise.**Article 43** In engaging in livestock and poultry breeding, the following acts are prohibited:

(1) Using feed, feed additives, or veterinary drugs in violation of the provisions of laws, administrative regulations, and the mandatory requirements of national technical specifications;

(2) Feeding livestock with swill from restaurants or canteens that has not been treated at high temperatures;

(3) Raising livestock and poultry in garbage dumps or using substances from garbage dumps;

(4) Other acts that harm the health of humans, livestock, and poultry as stipulated by laws, administrative regulations, and the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council.

**Article 44** Persons engaged in livestock and poultry breeding shall, in accordance with the provisions of the Law of the People's Republic of China on Animal Epidemic Prevention, carry out the prevention and control of livestock and poultry epidemic diseases.

**Article 45** Livestock and poultry breeders shall, in accordance with the state regulations on the management of livestock and poultry identification, affix identification marks on the designated parts of livestock and poultry that require such marks. The competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine shall not charge fees for providing identification marks, and the necessary expenses shall be included in the fiscal budget of the people's government at the provincial level.

Livestock and poultry identification marks shall not be reused.

**Article 46** Livestock and poultry farms and breeding communities shall ensure the normal operation of facilities for the comprehensive utilization or harmless treatment of livestock and poultry manure, wastewater, and other solid waste, ensure that pollutants are discharged up to standard, and prevent environmental pollution.

If livestock and poultry farms or breeding communities illegally discharge livestock and poultry manure, wastewater, or other solid waste, causing environmental pollution hazards, they shall eliminate the hazards and compensate for losses in accordance with the law.

The state supports livestock and poultry farms and breeding communities in constructing facilities for the comprehensive utilization of livestock and poultry manure, wastewater, and other solid waste.

**Article 47** The state encourages the development of beekeeping and safeguards the legitimate rights and interests of beekeeping producers.

Relevant departments shall actively publicize and promote agricultural measures for bee pollination.

**Article 48** During the production process, beekeeping producers shall not use drugs or containers that harm the quality and safety of bee products, ensuring the quality of bee products. Beekeeping equipment shall comply with the mandatory requirements of national technical specifications.

**Article 49** When beekeeping producers move their bees to different locations, the local public security, transportation, animal husbandry and veterinary medicine, and other relevant departments shall provide them with necessary conveniences.

When beekeeping producers move their bees domestically, they shall transport the bee colonies using a quarantine certificate printed in a unified format by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council. No duplicate quarantine inspection shall be conducted within the validity period of the quarantine certificate.

## Chapter V: Trade and Transportation of Livestock and Poultry

**Article 50** People's governments at or above the county level shall promote the construction of open, unified, competitive, and orderly livestock and poultry trading markets.

The competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine and other relevant departments under the people's governments at or above the county level shall organize the collection, collation, and dissemination of livestock and poultry production and marketing information to provide information services to producers.

**Article 51** Local people's governments at or above the county level shall, in accordance with the development plan for wholesale markets of agricultural products, provide support for the establishment of livestock and poultry wholesale markets in livestock and poultry collection and distribution centers.

The site selection for livestock and poultry wholesale markets shall comply with the animal epidemic prevention conditions stipulated by laws, administrative regulations, and the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council, and shall be located at least three kilometers away from breeding livestock and poultry farms and large-scale livestock and poultry farms.

**Article 52** Livestock and poultry traded must comply with the mandatory requirements of national technical specifications.

Livestock and poultry that are required by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council to bear identification marks but do not have them shall not be sold or purchased.

**Article 53** The transportation of livestock and poultry must comply with the animal epidemic prevention conditions stipulated by laws, administrative regulations, and the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council. Measures shall be taken to protect the safety of livestock and poultry, and necessary space and feeding and watering conditions shall be provided for the transported livestock and poultry.

Inspections of livestock and poultry during transportation by relevant departments must have a basis in laws or administrative regulations.

## Chapter VI: Quality and Safety Assurance

**Article 54** People's governments at or above the county level shall organize the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine and other relevant departments, in accordance with the provisions of this Law and relevant laws and administrative regulations, to strengthen the supervision and management of the livestock and poultry breeding environment, the quality of breeding livestock and poultry, the use of inputs such as feed and veterinary drugs, and the trade and transportation of livestock and poultry.

**Article 55** The competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council shall formulate management measures for livestock and poultry identification and breeding files, and take measures to implement a system of accountability for the quality of livestock and poultry products.

**Article 56** The competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the people's governments at or above the county level shall formulate plans for the supervision and inspection of livestock and poultry quality and safety, and carry out supervision and spot-check work according to the plans.

**Article 57** The competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the people's governments at or above the provincial level shall organize the formulation of livestock and poultry production standards to guide safe livestock and poultry production.

## Chapter VII: Legal Liability

**Article 58** Anyone who, in violation of the provisions of paragraph 2 of Article 13 of this Law, disposes of protected livestock and poultry genetic resources without authorization, causing losses to such resources, shall be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the people's government at or above the provincial level.

**Article 59** Anyone who commits any of the following acts in violation of the relevant provisions of this Law shall be ordered by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the people's government at or above the provincial level to cease the illegal act, and the livestock and poultry genetic resources and illegal gains shall be confiscated, and a fine of not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 50,000 yuan shall be imposed:

(1) Introducing livestock and poultry genetic resources from abroad without examination and approval;

(2) Cooperating with foreign institutions or individuals within China to research and utilize livestock and poultry genetic resources listed in the protection catalogue without examination and approval;

(3) Cooperating with foreign institutions or individuals within China to research and utilize newly discovered livestock and poultry genetic resources that have not been appraised by the National Livestock and Poultry Genetic Resources Committee.

**Article 60** Anyone who exports livestock and poultry genetic resources abroad without the approval of the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council shall be investigated for legal liability in accordance with the relevant provisions of the Customs Law of the People's Republic of China. The customs shall transfer the detained livestock and poultry genetic resources to the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the people's government at the provincial level for handling.

**Article 61** Anyone who, in violation of the relevant provisions of this Law, sells or promotes livestock and poultry breeds that have not been approved or appraised shall be ordered by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the people's government at or above the county level to cease the illegal act, and the livestock and poultry and illegal gains shall be confiscated; if the illegal gains are 50,000 yuan or more, a fine of not less than one time but not more than three times the illegal gains shall also be imposed; if there are no illegal gains or the illegal gains are less than 50,000 yuan, a fine of not less than 5,000 yuan but not more than 50,000 yuan shall also be imposed.
**Article 62** Anyone who, in violation of the relevant provisions of this Law, produces or operates breeding livestock and poultry without a production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry, or in violation of the provisions of such a license, or transfers or lends such a license, shall be ordered by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the people's government at or above the county level to cease the illegal act, and the illegal gains shall be confiscated; if the illegal gains are 30,000 yuan or more, a fine of not less than one time but not more than three times the illegal gains shall also be imposed; if there are no illegal gains or the illegal gains are less than 30,000 yuan, a fine of not less than 3,000 yuan but not more than 30,000 yuan shall also be imposed. If the circumstances are serious, the production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry shall also be revoked.

**Article 63** Anyone who violates the provisions of Article 28 of this Law shall be investigated for legal liability in accordance with the relevant provisions of the Advertising Law of the People's Republic of China.

**Article 64** Anyone who, in violation of the relevant provisions of this Law, uses breeding livestock and poultry that do not meet the breeding standards shall be ordered by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the local people's government at or above the county level to cease the illegal act, and the illegal gains shall be confiscated; if the illegal gains are 5,000 yuan or more, a fine of not less than one time but not more than two times the illegal gains shall also be imposed; if there are no illegal gains or the illegal gains are less than 5,000 yuan, a fine of not less than 1,000 yuan but not more than 5,000 yuan shall also be imposed.

**Article 65** Anyone who sells breeding livestock and poultry and commits any of the illegal acts specified in items (1) to (4) of Article 30 of this Law shall be ordered by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the people's government at or above the county level or the administrative department for industry and commerce to cease the sale, and the illegally sold livestock and poultry and illegal gains shall be confiscated; if the illegal gains are 50,000 yuan or more, a fine of not less than one time but not more than five times the illegal gains shall also be imposed; if there are no illegal gains or the illegal gains are less than 50,000 yuan, a fine of not less than 5,000 yuan but not more than 50,000 yuan shall also be imposed; if the circumstances are serious, the production and operation license for breeding livestock and poultry or the business license shall also be revoked.

**Article 66** If a livestock and poultry farm, in violation of the provisions of Article 41 of this Law, fails to establish breeding files or fails to preserve breeding files in accordance with regulations, the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the people's government at or above the county level shall order it to make corrections within a specified time limit and may impose a fine of not more than 10,000 yuan.

**Article 67** Anyone who breeds livestock and poultry in violation of the provisions of Article 43 of this Law shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of relevant laws and administrative regulations.

**Article 68** Anyone who, in violation of the relevant provisions of this Law, sells breeding livestock and poultry without attaching a certificate of conformity for breeding livestock and poultry, a quarantine certificate, or a livestock pedigree, sells or purchases livestock and poultry that are required by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the State Council to bear identification marks but do not have them, or reuses livestock and poultry identification marks, shall be ordered by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the local people's government at or above the county level or the administrative department for industry and commerce to make corrections and may be fined not more than 2,000 yuan.

Anyone who, in violation of the relevant provisions of this Law, uses forged or altered livestock and poultry identification marks shall have the forged or altered identification marks and illegal gains confiscated by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the people's government at or above the county level and shall be fined not less than 3,000 yuan but not more than 30,000 yuan.

**Article 69** Anyone who sells livestock and poultry that do not comply with the mandatory requirements of national technical specifications shall be ordered by the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine under the local people's government at or above the county level or the administrative department for industry and commerce to cease the illegal act, and the illegally sold livestock and poultry and illegal gains shall be confiscated, and a fine of not less than one time but not more than three times the illegal gains shall be imposed; if the circumstances are serious, the administrative department for industry and commerce shall also revoke the business license.

**Article 70** If a staff member of the competent administrative department for animal husbandry and veterinary medicine takes advantage of their position to accept property or seek other benefits from others, issues a license or relevant approval document to a unit or individual that does not meet the statutory conditions, fails to perform supervisory duties, or fails to investigate illegal acts discovered, they shall be given administrative sanctions in accordance with the law.

**Article 71** Anyone who violates the provisions of this Law and constitutes a crime shall be investigated for criminal liability in accordance with the law.

## Chapter VIII: Supplementary Provisions

**Article 72** For the purposes of this Law, "livestock and poultry genetic resources" refers to genetic materials such as livestock and poultry and their ova (eggs), embryos, semen, and genetic substances.

For the purposes of this Law, "breeding livestock and poultry" refers to livestock and poultry that have been selected and bred, have breeding value, and are suitable for reproducing offspring, as well as their ova (eggs), embryos, semen, etc.

**Article 73** This Law shall come into force as of July 1, 2006.
