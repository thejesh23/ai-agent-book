# Urban Maintenance and Construction Tax Law of the People's Republic of China

Adopted at the 21st Meeting of the Standing Committee of the 13th National People's Congress on August 11, 2020

<!-- INFO END -->

Article 1 Units and individuals that pay value-added tax (VAT) and consumption tax within the territory of the People's Republic of China are taxpayers of the urban maintenance and construction tax and shall pay the urban maintenance and construction tax in accordance with this Law.

Article 2 The urban maintenance and construction tax shall be based on the amount of VAT and consumption tax actually paid by the taxpayer in accordance with the law.

The tax base for the urban maintenance and construction tax shall be determined after deducting the amount of VAT refunded due to the end-of-period VAT refund for retained credits in accordance with the provisions.

The specific method for determining the tax base of the urban maintenance and construction tax shall be formulated by the State Council in accordance with this Law and relevant tax laws and administrative regulations, and shall be reported to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for recordation.

Article 3 The urban maintenance and construction tax shall not be levied on the amount of VAT and consumption tax paid on imported goods or on the sale of labor services, services, and intangible assets by units and individuals outside the territory to within the territory.

Article 4 The tax rates for the urban maintenance and construction tax are as follows:

(1) If the taxpayer's location is in a city, the tax rate is 7 percent;

(2) If the taxpayer's location is in a county or town, the tax rate is 5 percent;

(3) If the taxpayer's location is not in a city, county, or town, the tax rate is 1 percent.

The term "taxpayer's location" as mentioned in the preceding paragraph refers to the taxpayer's domicile or other places related to the taxpayer's production and business activities, and the specific location shall be determined by the province, autonomous region, or municipality directly under the Central Government.

Article 5 The tax payable for the urban maintenance and construction tax shall be calculated by multiplying the tax base by the applicable tax rate.

Article 6 Based on the needs of national economic and social development, the State Council may provide for reductions or exemptions of the urban maintenance and construction tax for major public infrastructure construction, special industries and groups, and responses to major emergencies, and shall report such provisions to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for recordation.

Article 7 The time when the obligation to pay the urban maintenance and construction tax arises shall be the same as the time when the obligation to pay VAT and consumption tax arises, and the tax shall be paid simultaneously with VAT and consumption tax, respectively.

Article 8 The withholding agents for the urban maintenance and construction tax shall be units and individuals that have the obligation to withhold VAT and consumption tax, and shall withhold the urban maintenance and construction tax at the same time as withholding VAT and consumption tax.

Article 9 The urban maintenance and construction tax shall be collected and managed by the tax authorities in accordance with this Law and the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Administration of Tax Collection.

Article 10 Taxpayers, tax authorities, and their staff who violate the provisions of this Law shall be held legally liable in accordance with the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Administration of Tax Collection and relevant laws and regulations.

Article 11 This Law shall come into effect on September 1, 2021. The Interim Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Urban Maintenance and Construction Tax promulgated by the State Council on February 8, 1985, shall be simultaneously repealed.
