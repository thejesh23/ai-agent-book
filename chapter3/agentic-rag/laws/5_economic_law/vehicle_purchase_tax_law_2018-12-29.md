# Vehicle Acquisition Tax Law of the People's Republic of China

Adopted at the Seventh Session of the Standing Committee of the Thirteenth National People's Congress on December 29, 2018

<!-- INFO END -->

Article 1 Units and individuals that purchase automobiles, trams, automobile trailers, and motorcycles with an engine displacement exceeding 150 milliliters (hereinafter collectively referred to as taxable vehicles) within the territory of the People's Republic of China are taxpayers of vehicle acquisition tax and shall pay vehicle acquisition tax in accordance with the provisions of this Law.

Article 2 For the purposes of this Law, "acquisition" refers to the act of obtaining and using taxable vehicles for personal use through purchase, import, self-production, donation, award, or other means.

Article 3 Vehicle acquisition tax is levied once. No vehicle acquisition tax shall be levied on vehicles for which the tax has already been paid.

Article 4 The tax rate for vehicle acquisition tax is ten percent.

Article 5 The amount of vehicle acquisition tax payable shall be calculated based on the taxable price of the taxable vehicle multiplied by the tax rate.

Article 6 The taxable price of a taxable vehicle shall be determined in accordance with the following provisions:

(1) For a taxpayer who purchases a taxable vehicle for personal use, the taxable price shall be the total price actually paid by the taxpayer to the seller, excluding value-added tax;

(2) For a taxpayer who imports a taxable vehicle for personal use, the taxable price shall be the customs duty-paid price plus customs duty and consumption tax;

(3) For a taxpayer who self-produces a taxable vehicle for personal use, the taxable price shall be determined based on the selling price of similar taxable vehicles produced by the taxpayer, excluding value-added tax;

(4) For a taxpayer who obtains a taxable vehicle for personal use through donation, award, or other means, the taxable price shall be determined based on the price specified in the relevant documents at the time of acquisition, excluding value-added tax.

Article 7 If the taxable price declared by a taxpayer for a taxable vehicle is obviously low without justifiable reasons, the tax authority shall determine the amount of tax payable in accordance with the provisions of the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Administration of Tax Collection.

Article 8 If a taxpayer settles the price of a taxable vehicle in foreign currency, the tax shall be calculated and paid by converting the foreign currency into Renminbi at the central parity rate of the Renminbi on the date of tax declaration.

Article 9 The following vehicles are exempt from vehicle acquisition tax:

(1) Vehicles for personal use by foreign embassies, consulates, and international organizations in China and their relevant personnel that are exempt from tax in accordance with legal provisions;

(2) Vehicles included in the equipment ordering plans of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Armed Police Force;

(3) National comprehensive fire rescue vehicles bearing special emergency rescue license plates;

(4) Non-transport special operation vehicles with fixed devices;

(5) Public buses and electric vehicles purchased by urban public transport enterprises.

Based on the needs of national economic and social development, the State Council may provide for tax reductions or other exemptions from vehicle acquisition tax, and shall report such provisions to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for recordation.

Article 10 Vehicle acquisition tax shall be collected by the tax authorities.

Article 11 A taxpayer who acquires a taxable vehicle shall declare and pay vehicle acquisition tax to the competent tax authority at the place of vehicle registration; for taxable vehicles that do not require registration, the taxpayer shall declare and pay vehicle acquisition tax to the competent tax authority at the taxpayer's location.

Article 12 The tax liability for vehicle acquisition tax arises on the date when the taxpayer acquires the taxable vehicle. The taxpayer shall declare and pay vehicle acquisition tax within sixty days from the date the tax liability arises.

Article 13 A taxpayer shall pay vehicle acquisition tax before applying for vehicle registration with the public security traffic management department.

When handling vehicle registration, the public security traffic management department shall verify the vehicle information declared by the taxpayer against the electronic information on tax payment or tax exemption for taxable vehicles provided by the tax authority, and after verification, shall handle vehicle registration in accordance with the law.

Article 14 If tax-exempt or tax-reduced vehicles are no longer within the scope of tax exemption or tax reduction due to transfer, change of use, or other reasons, the taxpayer shall pay vehicle acquisition tax before applying for vehicle transfer registration or change registration. The taxable price shall be based on the taxable price determined at the time of the initial tax declaration for the tax-exempt or tax-reduced vehicle, with a ten percent reduction for each full year.

Article 15 If a taxpayer returns a vehicle for which vehicle acquisition tax has already been paid to the vehicle manufacturer or seller, the taxpayer may apply to the competent tax authority for a refund of the vehicle acquisition tax. The refund amount shall be based on the tax already paid, with a ten percent reduction for each full year from the date of tax payment to the date of application for tax refund.

Article 16 Tax authorities and departments such as public security, commerce, customs, and industry and information technology shall establish a mechanism for sharing information on taxable vehicles and cooperating in their work, and shall promptly exchange information on taxable vehicles and tax payment data.

Article 17 The collection and administration of vehicle acquisition tax shall be carried out in accordance with the provisions of this Law and the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Administration of Tax Collection.

Article 18 Taxpayers, tax authorities, and their staff who violate the provisions of this Law shall be held legally liable in accordance with the provisions of the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Administration of Tax Collection and relevant laws and regulations.

Article 19 This Law shall come into effect on July 1, 2019. The Interim Regulation of the People's Republic of China on Vehicle Acquisition Tax promulgated by the State Council on October 22, 2000 shall be simultaneously repealed.
