# Public Security Administration Punishments Law of the People's Republic of China

Adopted at the 17th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the 10th National People's Congress on August 28, 2005

Amended in accordance with the Decision on Amending the Public Security Administration Punishments Law of the People's Republic of China adopted at the 29th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the 11th National People's Congress on October 26, 2012

## Chapter I General Provisions

Article 1 This Law is enacted for the purposes of maintaining public security order, safeguarding public safety, protecting the lawful rights and interests of citizens, legal persons and other organizations, and regulating and ensuring that public security organs and their people's police lawfully perform their duties of public security administration.

Article 2 Anyone who disrupts public order, endangers public safety, infringes upon personal rights or property rights, or obstructs social administration, where such act is harmful to society and constitutes a crime under the Criminal Law of the People's Republic of China, shall be investigated for criminal liability in accordance with the law; where the act is not serious enough for criminal punishment, the public security organ shall impose a public security administration punishment in accordance with this Law.

Article 3 The procedures for public security administration punishments shall be governed by the provisions of this Law; where this Law does not provide, the relevant provisions of the Administrative Punishment Law of the People's Republic of China shall apply.

Article 4 Any act committed within the territory of the People's Republic of China in violation of public security administration shall be subject to this Law, except where otherwise provided by law.

Any act committed on board a vessel or aircraft of the People's Republic of China in violation of public security administration shall be subject to this Law, except where otherwise provided by law.

Article 5 Public security administration punishments must be based on facts and shall be commensurate with the nature, circumstances and degree of social harm of the act violating public security administration.

Public security administration punishments shall be imposed openly and impartially, respecting and safeguarding human rights, and protecting the personal dignity of citizens.

The principle of combining education with punishment shall be adhered to in handling public security cases.

Article 6 People's governments at all levels shall strengthen comprehensive measures for maintaining public security, take effective measures to resolve social conflicts, enhance social harmony, and maintain social stability.

Article 7 The public security department under the State Council shall be responsible for public security administration throughout the country. The public security organs of local people's governments at or above the county level shall be responsible for public security administration within their respective administrative areas.

The jurisdiction over public security cases shall be prescribed by the public security department under the State Council.

Article 8 Where an act in violation of public security administration causes harm to another person, the actor or his guardian shall bear civil liability in accordance with the law.

Article 9 Where an act in violation of public security administration, such as fighting and brawling or damaging another person's property, arises from a civil dispute and the circumstances are minor, the public security organ may mediate and handle the matter. If the parties reach an agreement through mediation by the public security organ, no punishment shall be imposed. If mediation fails to reach an agreement or the agreement is not performed after being reached, the public security organ shall impose a punishment on the violator in accordance with the provisions of this Law, and shall inform the parties that they may bring a civil lawsuit in a people's court regarding the civil dispute in accordance with the law.

## Chapter II Types and Application of Punishments

Article 10 The types of public security administration punishments are:

(1) Warning;
(2) Fine;
(3) Administrative detention;
(4) Revocation of permits issued by public security organs.

For foreigners who violate public security administration, an additional punishment of leaving the country within a specified time limit or deportation may be imposed.

Article 11 Any drugs, pornographic articles or other prohibited items, gambling equipment and gambling funds, instruments for drug use, and tools directly used for committing acts in violation of public security administration and owned by the actor, which are seized in handling public security cases, shall be confiscated and disposed of in accordance with relevant regulations.

Any property obtained from acts in violation of public security administration shall be recovered and returned to the victim; if there is no victim, it shall be registered, publicly auctioned or disposed of in accordance with relevant state regulations, and the proceeds shall be turned over to the state treasury.

Article 12 Where a person who has reached the age of 14 but not the age of 18 violates public security administration, a lighter or mitigated punishment shall be imposed; where a person under the age of 14 violates public security administration, no punishment shall be imposed, but his guardian shall be ordered to strictly discipline him.

Article 13 Where a mental patient violates public security administration when he is unable to recognize or control his own conduct, no punishment shall be imposed, but his guardian shall be ordered to strictly watch over him and provide medical treatment. Where an intermittent mental patient violates public security administration when he is in a normal mental state, a punishment shall be imposed.

Article 14 Where a blind person or a deaf-mute person violates public security administration, a lighter, mitigated or no punishment may be imposed.

Article 15 Where a person in a state of intoxication violates public security administration, a punishment shall be imposed.

Where a person in a state of intoxication poses a danger to himself or threatens the personal safety or property of others or public safety, protective measures shall be taken to restrain him until he sobers up.

Article 16 Where a person commits two or more acts in violation of public security administration, decisions shall be made separately and executed concurrently. Where administrative detention punishments are executed concurrently, the maximum term shall not exceed 20 days.

Article 17 Where two or more persons jointly violate public security administration, they shall be punished separately according to their respective roles in the violation.

Whoever instigates, coerces or induces another person to violate public security administration shall be punished according to the act he instigates, coerces or induces.

Article 18 Where a unit violates public security administration, the persons directly in charge and other persons directly responsible shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of this Law. Where other laws or administrative regulations provide for punishment of the unit for the same act, such provisions shall apply.

Article 19 A lighter or no punishment shall be imposed for an act in violation of public security administration under any of the following circumstances:

(1) The circumstances are extremely minor;
(2) The actor voluntarily eliminates or mitigates the harmful consequences and obtains the understanding of the victim;
(3) The actor acts under the coercion or inducement of another person;
(4) The actor voluntarily surrenders and truthfully confesses his illegal act to the public security organ;
(5) The actor has performed meritorious services.

Article 20 A heavier punishment shall be imposed for an act in violation of public security administration under any of the following circumstances:

(1) The act has relatively serious consequences;
(2) The actor instigates, coerces or induces another person to violate public security administration;
(3) The actor retaliates against the person who reported the case, the accuser, the informant or the witness;
(4) The actor has been subjected to a public security administration punishment within the previous six months.

Article 21 Where a person who violates public security administration falls under any of the following circumstances and should be given administrative detention in accordance with this Law, the administrative detention punishment shall not be executed:

(1) The person has reached the age of 14 but not the age of 16;
(2) The person has reached the age of 16 but not the age of 18 and violates public security administration for the first time;
(3) The person is 70 years of age or older;
(4) The person is pregnant or is breastfeeding her own infant under one year of age.

Article 22 Where an act in violation of public security administration is not discovered by the public security organ within six months, no punishment shall be imposed.

The time limit specified in the preceding paragraph shall be calculated from the date on which the act in violation of public security administration occurs; where the act is continuous or ongoing, it shall be calculated from the date on which the act ends.

## Chapter III Acts in Violation of Public Security Administration and Their Punishments

### Section 1 Acts Disrupting Public Order and Their Punishments

Article 23 Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be given a warning or a fine of not more than 200 yuan; where the circumstances are relatively serious, he shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan:

(1) Disrupting the order of a state organ, a public organization, an enterprise or a public institution, thereby preventing the normal conduct of work, production, business, medical treatment, teaching or scientific research, but not causing serious losses;
(2) Disrupting the order of a railway station, port, dock, airport, shopping mall, park, exhibition hall or other public place;
(3) Disrupting the order on a bus, trolleybus, train, ship, aircraft or other public transport vehicle;
(4) Illegally intercepting or forcibly boarding or clinging to a motor vehicle, ship, aircraft or other means of transport, thereby affecting the normal operation of the transport;
(5) Disrupting the order of an election conducted in accordance with the law.

Where a crowd is gathered to commit any of the acts specified in the preceding paragraph, the ringleader shall be detained for not less than 10 days but not more than 15 days, and may also be fined not more than 1,000 yuan.

Article 24 Whoever commits any of the following acts, thereby disrupting the order of a large-scale cultural, sports or other mass activity, shall be given a warning or a fine of not more than 200 yuan; where the circumstances are serious, he shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan:

(1) Forcibly entering the venue;
(2) Setting off fireworks or other articles in the venue in violation of regulations;
(3) Displaying insulting slogans, banners or other articles;
(4) Surrounding and attacking referees, athletes or other staff members;
(5) Throwing debris into the venue and refusing to stop;
(6) Other acts disrupting the order of a large-scale mass activity.

Where a person is detained for disrupting the order of a sports competition, he may also be ordered not to enter the sports venue to watch similar competitions within 12 months; if he enters the sports venue in violation of the order, he shall be forcibly removed from the scene.

Article 25 Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan; where the circumstances are minor, he shall be detained for not more than 5 days or fined not more than 500 yuan:

(1) Spreading rumors, falsely reporting dangerous situations, epidemic situations or police situations, or intentionally disrupting public order by other means;
(2) Disrupting public order by placing false dangerous substances such as explosive, toxic, radioactive or corrosive substances or pathogens of infectious diseases;
(3) Threatening to set fires, cause explosions or place dangerous substances to disrupt public order.

Article 26 Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan; where the circumstances are relatively serious, he shall be detained for not less than 10 days but not more than 15 days, and may also be fined not more than 1,000 yuan:

(1) Forming a gang to engage in affrays;
(2) Chasing or intercepting others;
(3) Forcibly taking or demanding property, or willfully damaging or occupying public or private property;
(4) Other acts of picking quarrels and provoking trouble.

Article 27 Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than 10 days but not more than 15 days, and may also be fined not more than 1,000 yuan; where the circumstances are minor, he shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan:

(1) Organizing, instigating, coercing, inducing or inciting others to engage in cult or secret society activities, or using cults, secret societies or superstitious activities to disrupt social order or harm the health of others;
(2) Using the name of religion or qigong to engage in activities that disrupt social order or harm the health of others.

Article 28 Whoever, in violation of state regulations, intentionally interferes with the normal operation of radio services, or causes harmful interference to a normally operating radio station (station), and after being pointed out by the relevant competent authority, refuses to take effective measures to eliminate the interference, shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days; where the circumstances are serious, he shall be detained for not less than 10 days but not more than 15 days.

Article 29 Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not more than 5 days; where the circumstances are relatively serious, he shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days:

(1) In violation of state regulations, intruding into a computer information system, thereby causing harm;
(2) In violation of state regulations, deleting, modifying, adding or interfering with the functions of a computer information system, thereby causing the computer information system to fail to operate normally;
(3) In violation of state regulations, deleting, modifying or adding data and application programs stored, processed or transmitted in a computer information system;
(4) Intentionally creating or spreading destructive programs such as computer viruses, thereby affecting the normal operation of a computer information system.

### Section 2 Acts Endangering Public Safety and Their Punishments

Article 30 Whoever, in violation of state regulations, manufactures, buys, sells, stores, transports, mails, carries, uses, provides or disposes of dangerous substances such as explosive, toxic, radioactive or corrosive substances or pathogens of infectious diseases, shall be detained for not less than 10 days but not more than 15 days; where the circumstances are minor, he shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days.

Article 31 Where dangerous substances such as explosive, toxic, radioactive or corrosive substances or pathogens of infectious diseases are stolen, robbed or lost, and the matter is not reported as required, the person responsible shall be detained for not more than 5 days; where the matter is intentionally concealed and not reported, he shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days.

Article 32 Whoever illegally carries a firearm, ammunition, crossbow, dagger or other controlled instruments prescribed by the state shall be detained for not more than 5 days, and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan; where the circumstances are minor, he shall be given a warning or a fine of not more than 200 yuan.

Whoever illegally carries a firearm, ammunition, crossbow, dagger or other controlled instruments prescribed by the state into a public place or on a public transport vehicle shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan.

Article 33 Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than 10 days but not more than 15 days:

(1) Stealing or damaging oil and gas pipeline facilities, power and telecommunications facilities, radio and television facilities, water conservancy and flood control engineering facilities, or public facilities such as hydrological monitoring, surveying, meteorological forecasting, environmental monitoring, geological monitoring or earthquake monitoring;
(2) Moving or damaging boundary markers, boundary pillars or other border markers, border facilities, or territorial land or sea markers of the state;
(3) Illegally engaging in activities affecting the direction of the national boundary (border) line or constructing facilities that obstruct the management of the national boundary (border).

Article 34 Whoever steals, damages or without authorization moves an aviation facility in use, or forcibly enters the cockpit of an aircraft, shall be detained for not less than 10 days but not more than 15 days.

Whoever uses an appliance or tool on an aircraft in use that may affect the normal function of the navigation system, and refuses to stop after being advised, shall be detained for not more than 5 days or fined not more than 500 yuan.

Article 35 Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan; where the circumstances are minor, he shall be detained for not more than 5 days or fined not more than 500 yuan:

(1) Stealing, damaging or without authorization moving railway facilities, equipment, locomotive or rolling stock parts, or safety signs;
(2) Placing obstacles on railway tracks, or intentionally throwing objects at trains;
(3) Digging pits or quarries, or extracting sand, on railway lines, bridges or culverts;
(4) Privately setting up level crossings or grade crossings on railway lines.

Article 36 Whoever without authorization enters a railway protective fence, or walks, sits or lies down on railway tracks, or rushes across a railway when a train is approaching, thereby affecting traffic safety, shall be given a warning or a fine of not more than 200 yuan.

Article 37 Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not more than 5 days or fined not more than 500 yuan; where the circumstances are serious, he shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan:

(1) Installing or using an electric grid without approval, or installing or using an electric grid that does not meet safety requirements;
(2) When constructing in a place where vehicles or pedestrians pass, failing to set up covers, fences or warning signs over ditches, wells, pits or caves, or intentionally damaging or moving such covers, fences or warning signs;
(3) Stealing or damaging public facilities such as manhole covers or street lighting on roads.

Article 38 Where a large-scale cultural, sports or other mass activity is held in violation of relevant regulations, creating a risk of a safety accident, the activity shall be ordered to stop and the participants shall be immediately evacuated; the organizer shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and shall also be fined not less than 200 yuan but not more than 500 yuan; where the circumstances are minor, he shall be detained for not more than 5 days or fined not more than 500 yuan.

Article 39 Where the operator or manager of a hotel, restaurant, theater, entertainment venue, sports ground, exhibition hall or other place for public activities violates safety regulations, thereby creating a risk of a safety accident at the place, and after being ordered by the public security organ to rectify, refuses to do so, he shall be detained for not more than 5 days.

### Section 3 Acts Infringing upon Personal Rights and Property Rights and Their Punishments

Article 40 Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than 10 days but not more than 15 days, and shall also be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 1,000 yuan; where the circumstances are minor, he shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and shall also be fined not less than 200 yuan but not more than 500 yuan:

(1) Organizing, coercing or inducing a person under the age of 16 or a disabled person to engage in terrifying or cruel performances;
(2) Forcing another person to work by violence, threat or other means;
(3) Illegally restricting another person's personal freedom, illegally entering another person's residence, or illegally searching another person's body.

Article 41 Whoever coerces, induces or uses another person to beg shall be detained for not less than 10 days but not more than 15 days, and may also be fined not more than 1,000 yuan.

Whoever repeatedly pesters, forcibly begs or begs by other means of harassing others shall be detained for not more than 5 days or given a warning.

Article 42 Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not more than 5 days or fined not more than 500 yuan; where the circumstances are relatively serious, he shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan:

(1) Writing threatening letters or threatening another person's personal safety by other means;
(2) Publicly insulting another person or fabricating facts to defame another person;
(3) Fabricating facts to falsely accuse or frame another person, attempting to cause that person to be subject to criminal investigation or public security administration punishment;
(4) Threatening, insulting, beating or retaliating against a witness or his close relatives;
(5) Repeatedly sending obscene, insulting, threatening or other information, thereby interfering with another person's normal life;
(6) Peeping, secretly photographing, eavesdropping on or disseminating another person's privacy.

Article 43 Whoever beats another person, or intentionally injures another person's body, shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and shall also be fined not less than 200 yuan but not more than 500 yuan; where the circumstances are minor, he shall be detained for not more than 5 days or fined not more than 500 yuan.

Under any of the following circumstances, the person shall be detained for not less than 10 days but not more than 15 days, and shall also be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 1,000 yuan:

(1) Forming a gang to beat or injure another person;
(2) Beating or injuring a disabled person, a pregnant woman, a person under the age of 14 or a person over the age of 60;
(3) Repeatedly beating or injuring another person, or beating or injuring multiple persons at one time.

Article 44 Whoever indecently assaults another person, or intentionally exposes his body in a public place, where the circumstances are flagrant, shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days; whoever indecently assaults a mentally disabled person, a mental patient, a person under the age of 14, or where there are other serious circumstances, shall be detained for not less than 10 days but not more than 15 days.

Article 45 Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not more than 5 days or given a warning:

(1) Maltreating a family member, where the maltreated person requests handling;
(2) Abandoning a dependent who has no ability to live independently.

Article 46 Whoever forcibly buys or sells commodities, forces another person to provide services, or forces another person to accept services, shall be detained for not less than 5 days but not more than 10 days, and shall also be fined not less than 200 yuan but not more than 500 yuan; where the circumstances are minor, he shall be detained for not more than 5 days or fined not more than 500 yuan.

Article 47 Whoever incites ethnic hatred or ethnic discrimination, or publishes content of ethnic discrimination or insult in publications or computer information networks, shall be detained for not less than 10 days but not more than 15 days, and may also be fined not more than 1,000 yuan.

Article 48 Whoever fraudulently claims, conceals, destroys, privately opens or illegally inspects another person's mail shall be detained for not more than 5 days or fined not more than 500 yuan.**Article 49** Whoever steals, fraudulently obtains, forcibly takes, snatches, extorts, or intentionally damages public or private property shall be detained for not less than five days but not more than ten days and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan; if the circumstances are relatively serious, he shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and may also be fined not more than 1,000 yuan.

### Section 4: Acts that Impair Social Administration and the Penalties Therefor

**Article 50** Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be given a warning or fined not more than 200 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be detained for not less than five days but not more than ten days and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan:

(1) refusing to carry out the decisions or orders issued according to law by the people's government in a state of emergency;

(2) obstructing a state organ functionary from performing his duties according to law;

(3) obstructing the passage of fire engines, ambulances, engineering rescue vehicles, police cars, etc., which are on an emergency mission; or

(4) forcibly breaking through the warning cordon or warning area set up by a public security organ.

If a person obstructs a people's policeman from performing his duties according to law, he shall be given a heavier penalty.

**Article 51** Whoever impersonates a state organ functionary or cheats by other false identities shall be detained for not less than five days but not more than ten days and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan; if the circumstances are relatively minor, he shall be detained for not more than five days or fined not more than 500 yuan.

Whoever impersonates a serviceman or a people's policeman to cheat shall be given a heavier penalty.

**Article 52** Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and may also be fined not more than 1,000 yuan; if the circumstances are relatively minor, he shall be detained for not less than five days but not more than ten days and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan:

(1) forging, altering, or buying or selling official documents, certificates, certification documents, or seals of state organs, people's organizations, enterprises, institutions, or other organizations;

(2) buying or selling, or using forged or altered official documents, certificates, or certification documents of state organs, people's organizations, enterprises, institutions, or other organizations;

(3) forging, altering, or scalping train tickets, ship tickets, airline tickets, tickets for theatrical performances, tickets for sports competitions, or other negotiable tickets or vouchers; or

(4) forging or altering vessel registration plates, buying or selling, or using forged or altered vessel registration plates, or altering vessel engine numbers.

**Article 53** If a vessel, without permission, enters or berths in waters or at islands which the state prohibits or restricts entry into or berthing at, the person in charge of the vessel and the persons directly responsible shall be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 1,000 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, they shall be detained for not more than five days and shall also be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 1,000 yuan.

**Article 54** Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and shall also be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 1,000 yuan; if the circumstances are relatively minor, he shall be detained for not more than five days or fined not more than 500 yuan:

(1) violating state regulations by carrying out activities in the name of a social organization without registration, and continuing such activities after being banned;

(2) a social organization whose registration has been revoked according to law continuing to carry out activities in the name of a social organization; or

(3) without permission, operating an industry which, according to state regulations, requires permission from the public security organ.

If a person commits the act specified in subparagraph (3) of the preceding paragraph, the operation shall be banned.

If an operator who has obtained permission from the public security organ violates the relevant state regulations on administration and the circumstances are serious, the public security organ may revoke the permit.

**Article 55** Whoever incites or plans an illegal assembly, procession, or demonstration, and refuses to heed dissuasion, shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days.

**Article 56** If a staff member of the hotel industry fails to register, according to regulations, the name, type of identity document, and number of the identity document of a guest, or fails to stop a guest who he knows has brought dangerous substances into the hotel, he shall be fined not less than 200 yuan but not more than 500 yuan.

If a staff member of the hotel industry knows that a guest is a criminal suspect or a person wanted by a public security organ but fails to report to the public security organ, he shall be fined not less than 200 yuan but not more than 500 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be detained for not more than five days and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan.

**Article 57** If a house lessor leases his house to a person without a valid identity document, or fails to register, according to regulations, the name, type of identity document, and number of the identity document of the lessee, he shall be fined not less than 200 yuan but not more than 500 yuan.

If a house lessor knows that the lessee uses the leased house for criminal activities but fails to report to the public security organ, he shall be fined not less than 200 yuan but not more than 500 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be detained for not more than five days and may also be fined not more than 500 yuan.

**Article 58** Whoever violates the legal provisions on the prevention of noise pollution in social life and creates noise that interferes with the normal life of others shall be given a warning; if he fails to correct after being warned, he shall be fined not less than 200 yuan but not more than 500 yuan.

**Article 59** Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 1,000 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be detained for not less than five days but not more than ten days and shall also be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 1,000 yuan:

(1) a staff member of the pawnbroking industry, when accepting pawned articles, fails to examine the relevant certificates, fails to go through the registration procedures, or fails to report to the public security organ although he knows that the articles are from a criminal suspect or are stolen goods;

(2) violating state regulations by purchasing used special equipment from railways, oil fields, power supply, telecommunications, mines, water conservancy, surveying, and urban public utilities;

(3) purchasing stolen goods or goods suspected of being stolen which are notified by the public security organ for tracing; or

(4) purchasing other goods which the state prohibits purchasing.

**Article 60** Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than five days but not more than ten days and shall also be fined not less than 200 yuan but not more than 500 yuan:

(1) concealing, transferring, selling, or damaging property that has been seized, sealed up, or frozen according to law by an administrative law enforcement organ;

(2) forging, concealing, or destroying evidence, or providing false testimony or giving a false report of a case, thereby affecting the administrative law enforcement organ's handling of a case according to law;

(3) knowingly harboring, transferring, or selling on commission stolen goods; or

(4) a criminal who is under public surveillance, deprived of political rights, on probation, or serving a sentence outside prison temporarily, or a person against whom compulsory criminal measures are taken according to law, who violates the laws, administrative regulations, or the supervision and administration regulations of the relevant departments of the State Council.

**Article 61** Whoever assists in organizing or transporting others to illegally cross the national (border) boundary shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and shall also be fined not less than 1,000 yuan but not more than 5,000 yuan.

**Article 62** Whoever provides conditions for persons to illegally cross the national (border) boundary shall be detained for not less than five days but not more than ten days and shall also be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 2,000 yuan.

Whoever illegally crosses the national (border) boundary shall be detained for not more than five days or fined not more than 500 yuan.

**Article 63** Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be given a warning or fined not more than 200 yuan; if the circumstances are relatively serious, he shall be detained for not less than five days but not more than ten days and shall also be fined not less than 200 yuan but not more than 500 yuan:

(1) intentionally damaging cultural relics or places of historic interest and scenic beauty protected by the state by carving, scribbling, or other means; or

(2) violating state regulations by carrying out blasting, excavation, or other activities near a unit of cultural relics under protection, thereby endangering the safety of the cultural relics.

**Article 64** Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 1,000 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and shall also be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 1,000 yuan:

(1) driving another person's motor vehicle without permission; or

(2) driving an aircraft or a motor vessel without a license, or driving another person's aircraft or motor vessel without permission.

**Article 65** Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than five days but not more than ten days; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and may also be fined not more than 1,000 yuan:

(1) intentionally destroying or defiling another person's grave, or destroying or discarding another person's remains or ashes; or

(2) placing a corpse in a public place, or placing a corpse in a way that interferes with the normal life or work order of others, and refusing to heed dissuasion.

**Article 66** Whoever engages in prostitution or patronizes prostitution shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and may also be fined not more than 5,000 yuan; if the circumstances are relatively minor, he shall be detained for not more than five days or fined not more than 500 yuan.

Whoever solicits prostitution in a public place shall be detained for not more than five days or fined not more than 500 yuan.

**Article 67** Whoever lures, shelters, or introduces another person to engage in prostitution shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and may also be fined not more than 5,000 yuan; if the circumstances are relatively minor, he shall be detained for not more than five days or fined not more than 500 yuan.

**Article 68** Whoever produces, transports, reproduces, sells, or rents out pornographic books, periodicals, pictures, films, audio-visual products, or other pornographic articles, or disseminates pornographic information through computer information networks, telephones, or other communication tools, shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and may also be fined not more than 3,000 yuan; if the circumstances are relatively minor, he shall be detained for not more than five days or fined not more than 500 yuan.

**Article 69** Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and shall also be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 1,000 yuan:

(1) organizing the playing of pornographic audio-visual products;

(2) organizing or performing obscene performances; or

(3) participating in group licentious activities.

Whoever knowingly provides conditions for another person to engage in the activities mentioned in the preceding paragraph shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.

**Article 70** Whoever, for the purpose of profit, provides conditions for gambling, or participates in gambling with relatively large stakes, shall be detained for not more than five days or fined not more than 500 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, he shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and shall also be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 3,000 yuan.

**Article 71** Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and may also be fined not more than 3,000 yuan; if the circumstances are relatively minor, he shall be detained for not more than five days or fined not more than 500 yuan:

(1) illegally cultivating poppy plants of less than 500 plants, or a small quantity of other narcotic plants;

(2) illegally buying, selling, transporting, carrying, or possessing a small quantity of seeds or seedlings of narcotic plants such as poppy which have not been inactivated; or

(3) illegally transporting, buying, selling, storing, or using a small quantity of poppy husks.

If a person commits the act specified in subparagraph (1) of the preceding paragraph and voluntarily uproots the plants before they mature, he shall not be punished.

**Article 72** Whoever commits any of the following acts shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and may also be fined not more than 2,000 yuan; if the circumstances are relatively minor, he shall be detained for not more than five days or fined not more than 500 yuan:

(1) illegally possessing opium of less than 200 grams, heroin or methamphetamine of less than 10 grams, or a small quantity of other narcotics;

(2) providing narcotics to another person;

(3) ingesting or injecting narcotics; or

(4) coercing or deceiving a medical worker into prescribing narcotic or psychotropic substances.

**Article 73** Whoever instigates, lures, or deceives another person into ingesting or injecting narcotics shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days and shall also be fined not less than 500 yuan but not more than 2,000 yuan.

**Article 74** If a staff member of the hotel industry, catering service industry, cultural entertainment industry, taxi industry, or other such unit tips off a person who commits an illegal or criminal act when the public security organ is investigating drug abuse, gambling, prostitution, or patronizing prostitution, he shall be detained for not less than ten days but not more than fifteen days.

**Article 75** Whoever keeps animals that interfere with the normal life of others shall be given a warning; if he fails to correct after being warned, or allows the animals to intimidate others, he shall be fined not less than 200 yuan but not more than 500 yuan.

Whoever drives animals to injure another person shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of Article 43, paragraph 1 of this Law.

**Article 76** If a person commits the acts specified in Articles 67, 68, and 70 of this Law and refuses to correct despite repeated admonition, compulsory educational measures may be taken against him in accordance with state regulations.

## Chapter IV: Procedures for Penalties

### Section 1: Investigation

**Article 77** A public security organ shall promptly accept and register cases reported, accused, or informed of, or cases where a person who commits an act against the administration of public security voluntarily surrenders, as well as cases of acts against the administration of public security transferred by other competent administrative departments or judicial organs.

**Article 78** After accepting a case reported, accused, informed of, or surrendered for, if the public security organ deems that it constitutes an act against the administration of public security, it shall immediately conduct an investigation; if it deems that it does not constitute an act against the administration of public security, it shall inform the reporter, accuser, informant, or surrenderer and explain the reasons.

**Article 79** A public security organ and its people's policemen shall conduct investigations into public security cases according to law. The use of torture to extort confessions or the use of illegal means such as threats, enticement, or deception to collect evidence is strictly prohibited.

Evidence collected by illegal means shall not be used as a basis for penalties.

**Article 80** When handling public security cases, a public security organ and its people's policemen shall keep confidential any state secrets, commercial secrets, or personal privacy involved.

**Article 81** Under any of the following circumstances, a people's policeman shall withdraw from handling a public security case; the person who commits the act against the administration of public security, the victim, or their legal representatives also have the right to request his withdrawal:

(1) he is a party to the case or a close relative of a party;

(2) he or his close relative has an interest in the case; or

(3) he has another relationship with a party to the case that may affect the impartial handling of the case.

The withdrawal of a people's policeman shall be decided by the public security organ to which he belongs; the withdrawal of the head of a public security organ shall be decided by the public security organ at the next higher level.

**Article 82** If it is necessary to summon a person who commits an act against the administration of public security for investigation, a summons shall be issued with the approval of the person in charge of the case-handling department of the public security organ. For a person who commits an act against the administration of public security discovered at the scene, a people's policeman may summon him orally after producing his work ID, but this shall be noted in the interrogation record.

The public security organ shall inform the summoned person of the reasons and basis for the summons. A person who, without justifiable reasons, refuses to accept the summons or evades the summons may be compelled to appear.

**Article 83** After summoning a person who commits an act against the administration of public security, the public security organ shall promptly interrogate and verify the facts, and the time for interrogation and verification shall not exceed eight hours; if the circumstances are complex and administrative detention penalties may be applicable according to the provisions of this Law, the time for interrogation and verification shall not exceed 24 hours.

The public security organ shall promptly notify the family members of the summoned person of the reasons and place of the summons.

**Article 84** The interrogation record shall be checked by the person being interrogated; if he is unable to read, it shall be read to him. If there are omissions or errors in the record, the person being interrogated may request additions or corrections. After the person being interrogated confirms that the record is correct, he shall sign or affix his seal, and the people's policeman conducting the interrogation shall also sign the record.

If the person being interrogated requests to provide written materials on his own regarding the matters under interrogation, permission shall be granted; when necessary, the people's policeman may also request the person being interrogated to write such materials himself.

When interrogating a person who commits an act against the administration of public security and is under the age of 16, his parents or other guardians shall be notified to be present.

**Article 85** When a people's policeman interrogates a victim or other witness, he may do so at the victim's or witness's place of work or residence; when necessary, he may also notify the victim or witness to come to the public security organ to provide testimony.

When interrogating a victim or other witness outside the public security organ, the people's policeman shall produce his work ID.

The provisions of Article 84 of this Law shall also apply to the interrogation of victims or other witnesses.

**Article 86** When interrogating a deaf-mute person who commits an act against the administration of public security, a victim, or other witness who is deaf-mute, a person proficient in sign language shall be provided to assist, and this shall be noted in the record.

When interrogating a person who commits an act against the administration of public security, a victim, or other witness who is not proficient in the spoken and written language commonly used in the locality, an interpreter shall be provided, and this shall be noted in the record.

**Article 87** A public security organ may inspect places, articles, and persons related to an act against the administration of public security. When conducting an inspection, there shall be no fewer than two people's policemen, and they shall produce their work IDs and the inspection certificate issued by the public security organ of the people's government at or above the county level. If it is truly necessary to conduct an immediate inspection, the people's policemen may conduct an on-the-spot inspection after producing their work IDs, but for inspecting a citizen's residence, the inspection certificate issued by the public security organ of the people's government at or above the county level shall be produced.

The inspection of a woman's body shall be conducted by female staff.

**Article 88** An inspection record shall be made of the inspection, which shall be signed or affixed with seals by the inspectors, the person inspected, and witnesses; if the person inspected refuses to sign, the people's policeman shall note this in the record.

**Article 89** When handling a public security case, a public security organ may seize articles related to the case that need to be used as evidence; property lawfully possessed by the victim or a bona fide third party shall not be seized but shall be registered. Articles unrelated to the case shall not be seized.

Seized articles shall be checked and counted in the presence of witnesses at the scene and the holder of the seized articles, and a list shall be made in duplicate on the spot, which shall be signed or affixed with seals by the investigators, witnesses, and the holder. One copy shall be given to the holder, and the other copy shall be kept in the file for reference.

Seized articles shall be properly kept and shall not be used for other purposes; articles that are not suitable for long-term storage shall be handled in accordance with relevant regulations. If it is found that the articles are unrelated to the case, they shall be returned promptly; if it is verified that they are the lawful property of another person, they shall be returned immediately after registration; if no one claims the property or the owner cannot be identified within six months, the property shall be sold by public auction or handled in accordance with relevant state regulations, and the proceeds shall be turned over to the state treasury.

**Article 90** In order to ascertain the facts of a case, if it is necessary to resolve a disputed specialized issue in the case, a person with specialized knowledge shall be designated or engaged to conduct an appraisal; after the appraisal, the appraiser shall write an appraisal opinion and sign it.

### Section 2: Decision

**Article 91** A decision on a public security penalty shall be made by the public security organ of the people's government at or above the county level; however, a warning or a fine of not more than 500 yuan may be decided by a police station.

**Article 92** If a person is decided to be given an administrative detention penalty, the time during which he has been subjected to compulsory measures restricting his personal freedom before the penalty shall be offset. One day of restriction of personal freedom shall offset one day of administrative detention.

**Article 93** When investigating a public security case, if the facts of the case can be proved by other evidence even without a statement from the person concerned, the public security organ may make a decision on a public security penalty. However, if there is only a statement from the person concerned and no other evidence to prove it, a decision on a public security penalty shall not be made.

**Article 94** Before making a decision on a public security penalty, the public security organ shall inform the person who commits the act against the administration of public security of the facts, reasons, and basis for the public security penalty, and shall inform him of the rights he enjoys according to law.

The person who commits the act against the administration of public security has the right to make statements and defenses. The public security organ must fully listen to the opinions of the person who commits the act against the administration of public security, and shall review the facts, reasons, and evidence presented by him; if the facts, reasons, or evidence presented by the person who commits the act against the administration of public security are established, the public security organ shall adopt them.

The public security organ shall not impose a heavier penalty because the person who commits the act against the administration of public security has made statements or defenses.

**Article 95** After the investigation of a public security case is concluded, the public security organ shall, based on the different circumstances, handle the matter as follows:

(1) if there is indeed an illegal act for which a public security penalty should be given according to law, a decision on a penalty shall be made based on the seriousness of the circumstances and the specific situation;

(2) if a penalty should not be given according to law, or the facts of the illegal act cannot be established, a decision of no penalty shall be made;

(3) if the illegal act is suspected of constituting a crime, the case shall be transferred to the competent organ for criminal liability according to law; and

(4) if it is found that the person who commits the act against the administration of public security has committed other illegal acts, the relevant administrative department shall be notified for handling while the decision on the penalty for the act against the administration of public security is made.**Article 96** When a public security organ decides on a public security penalty, it shall produce a written decision on the public security penalty. The decision shall contain the following:

(1) The name, gender, age, name and number of identification document, and address of the person penalized;

(2) The facts of the violation and the evidence;

(3) The type and legal basis of the penalty;

(4) The method and time limit for execution of the penalty;

(5) The channels and time limits for applying for administrative reconsideration or instituting administrative proceedings if the person penalized refuses to accept the penalty decision;

(6) The name of the public security organ that made the penalty decision and the date of the decision.

The decision shall be affixed with the seal of the public security organ that made the penalty decision.

**Article 97** The public security organ shall announce the written decision on the public security penalty to the person penalized and deliver it to him/her on the spot; if it is impossible to announce it to the person penalized on the spot, it shall be served on the person penalized within two days. If a decision is made to impose administrative detention, the family members of the person penalized shall be notified promptly.

If there is a victim, the public security organ shall send a copy of the decision to the victim.

**Article 98** Before making a decision on a public security penalty involving the revocation of a permit or a fine of more than 2,000 yuan, the public security organ shall inform the person who violates public security administration of the right to request a hearing; if the person who violates public security administration requests a hearing, the public security organ shall promptly hold a hearing in accordance with the law.

**Article 99** The time limit for a public security organ to handle a public security case shall not exceed 30 days from the date of acceptance; if the case is major or complex, it may be extended by 30 days with the approval of the public security organ at the next higher level.

The period for conducting an appraisal to ascertain the facts of the case shall not be counted in the time limit for handling the public security case.

**Article 100** If the facts of a violation of public security administration are clear, the evidence is conclusive, and the penalty is a warning or a fine of not more than 200 yuan, a decision on the public security penalty may be made on the spot.

**Article 101** When making a decision on a public security penalty on the spot, the people's police officer shall show his/her service identification to the person who violates public security administration and fill out a penalty decision. The penalty decision shall be delivered to the person penalized on the spot; if there is a victim, a copy of the decision shall be sent to the victim.

The penalty decision referred to in the preceding paragraph shall contain the name of the person penalized, the illegal act, the legal basis for the penalty, the amount of the fine, the time, the place, and the name of the public security organ, and shall be signed or sealed by the people's police officer handling the case.

When a decision on a public security penalty is made on the spot, the people's police officer handling the case shall report it to the public security organ to which he/she belongs for the record within 24 hours.

**Article 102** If a person penalized refuses to accept a decision on a public security penalty, he/she may apply for administrative reconsideration or institute administrative proceedings in accordance with the law.

### Section 3 Execution

**Article 103** A person who is decided to be given administrative detention shall be sent by the public security organ that made the decision to a detention center for execution.

**Article 104** A person who is fined shall pay the fine to a designated bank within 15 days from the date of receipt of the penalty decision. However, under any of the following circumstances, the people's police officer may collect the fine on the spot:

(1) The fine is not more than 50 yuan and the person penalized has no objection to the fine;

(2) In remote areas, on water, or in areas with inconvenient transportation, after the public security organ and its people's police officer make a fine decision in accordance with the provisions of this Law, the person penalized has difficulty paying the fine to the designated bank, and the person penalized so requests;

(3) The person penalized has no fixed domicile locally, and it would be difficult to enforce the fine later if it is not collected on the spot.

**Article 105** Fines collected on the spot by a people's police officer shall be turned over to the public security organ to which he/she belongs within two days from the date of collection; fines collected on the spot on water or on passenger trains shall be turned over to the public security organ to which he/she belongs within two days from the date of docking or arrival at the station; the public security organ shall pay the fines to a designated bank within two days from the date of receipt.

**Article 106** When a people's police officer collects a fine on the spot, he/she shall issue to the person penalized a fine receipt uniformly produced by the financial department of the province, autonomous region, or municipality directly under the Central Government; if a uniformly produced fine receipt is not issued, the person penalized has the right to refuse to pay the fine.

**Article 107** If a person penalized refuses to accept a decision on administrative detention and applies for administrative reconsideration or institutes administrative proceedings, he/she may apply to the public security organ for a suspension of the execution of the administrative detention. If the public security organ believes that the suspension of the execution of the administrative detention will not cause social danger, and the person penalized or his/her close relative provides a guarantor who meets the conditions specified in Article 108 of this Law, or pays a deposit at the rate of 200 yuan per day of administrative detention, the execution of the decision on administrative detention may be suspended.

**Article 108** A guarantor shall meet the following conditions:

(1) Having no involvement in the case;

(2) Enjoying political rights and not having personal freedom restricted;

(3) Having a permanent household registration and a fixed domicile locally;

(4) Having the ability to fulfill the guarantee obligations.

**Article 109** The guarantor shall ensure that the guaranteed person does not evade the execution of the administrative detention penalty.

If the guarantor fails to fulfill the guarantee obligations, causing the guaranteed person to evade the execution of the administrative detention penalty, the public security organ shall impose a fine of not more than 3,000 yuan on him/her.

**Article 110** If a person who is decided to be given administrative detention pays a deposit, and after the administrative detention is suspended, evades the execution of the administrative detention penalty, the deposit shall be confiscated and turned over to the state treasury, and the administrative detention decision already made shall still be executed.

**Article 111** If the decision on administrative detention is revoked, or the execution of the administrative detention penalty begins, the deposit collected by the public security organ shall be promptly returned to the person who paid it.

## Chapter V Supervision over Law Enforcement

**Article 112** Public security organs and their people's police officers shall handle public security cases in accordance with the law, fairly, strictly, and efficiently, enforce the law in a civilized manner, and shall not engage in malpractice for personal gain.

**Article 113** When handling public security cases, public security organs and their people's police officers are prohibited from beating, abusing, or insulting persons who violate public security administration.

**Article 114** When handling public security cases, public security organs and their people's police officers shall consciously accept supervision from society and citizens.

If public security organs and their people's police officers fail to strictly enforce the law or have illegal or disciplinary violations when handling public security cases, any unit or individual has the right to report or accuse them to the public security organ, the people's procuratorate, or the administrative supervision organ; the organ receiving the report or accusation shall handle it promptly according to its duties.

**Article 115** When a public security organ imposes a fine penalty in accordance with the law, it shall implement the separation of the fine decision and the fine collection in accordance with relevant laws and administrative regulations; all fines collected shall be turned over to the state treasury in full.

**Article 116** If a people's police officer, when handling a public security case, commits any of the following acts, he/she shall be given administrative sanctions in accordance with the law; if a crime is constituted, criminal liability shall be investigated in accordance with the law:

(1) Extorting confessions by torture, or physically punishing, abusing, or insulting others;

(2) Exceeding the time limit for interrogation and investigation to restrict personal freedom;

(3) Failing to implement the system of separating the fine decision and the fine collection, or failing to turn over confiscated property to the state treasury or handle it in accordance with the law;

(4) Illegally dividing, embezzling, misappropriating, or intentionally damaging collected or seized property;

(5) Using property of the victim in violation of regulations or failing to promptly return it;

(6) Failing to promptly return a deposit in violation of regulations;

(7) Taking advantage of his/her position to accept property from others or seek other benefits;

(8) Failing to issue a fine receipt when collecting a fine on the spot, or failing to truthfully fill in the amount of the fine;

(9) Failing to promptly dispatch police after receiving a report requesting the cessation of acts violating public security administration;

(10) Providing advance information to persons who have committed illegal or criminal acts when investigating activities violating public security administration;

(11) Other circumstances involving malpractice for personal gain, abuse of power, or failure to perform statutory duties in accordance with the law.

If a public security organ handling a public security case commits any of the acts listed in the preceding paragraph, the directly responsible supervisor and other directly responsible personnel shall be given corresponding administrative sanctions.

**Article 117** If a public security organ or its people's police officer exercises its/his/her powers illegally, infringing upon the lawful rights and interests of citizens, legal persons, or other organizations, it/he/she shall make an apology; if damage is caused, it/he/she shall bear compensation liability in accordance with the law.

## Chapter VI Supplementary Provisions

**Article 118** The terms "or more," "or less," and "within" used in this Law include the figure itself.

**Article 119** This Law shall come into force as of March 1, 2006. The "Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Administrative Penalties for Public Security" promulgated on September 5, 1986, and revised on May 12, 1994, shall be repealed simultaneously.
