# National Defense Education Law of the People's Republic of China

Adopted at the 21st Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Ninth National People's Congress on April 28, 2001

Amended in accordance with the Decision on Revising Six Laws including the Frontier Health and Quarantine Law of the People's Republic of China at the 2nd Meeting of the Standing Committee of the 13th National People's Congress on April 27, 2018

<!-- INFO END -->

## Chapter I General Provisions

Article 1 This Law is enacted in accordance with the National Defense Law and the Education Law for the purpose of popularizing and strengthening national defense education, carrying forward the spirit of patriotism, and promoting national defense construction and the building of socialist spiritual civilization.

Article 2 National defense education is the foundation for building and consolidating national defense and an important way to enhance national cohesion and improve the quality of the entire population.

Article 3 The State, by carrying out national defense education, enables citizens to strengthen their concept of national defense, acquire basic knowledge of national defense, learn necessary military skills, stimulate patriotic enthusiasm, and consciously fulfill their national defense obligations.

Article 4 National defense education shall implement the policy of universal participation, long-term persistence, and emphasis on practical results, and adhere to the principles of combining regular education with concentrated education, universal education with key-point education, and theoretical education with behavioral education, and determine corresponding educational content and organize implementation by category for different targets.

Article 5 All citizens of the People's Republic of China have the right and obligation to receive national defense education.

Popularizing and strengthening national defense education is the common responsibility of the whole society.

All state organs, armed forces, political parties, social organizations, enterprises, institutions, and grassroots mass autonomous organizations shall, according to their actual conditions, organize national defense education in their respective regions, departments, and units.

Article 6 The State Council shall lead the national defense education work nationwide. The Central Military Commission shall coordinate with the State Council in carrying out national defense education among the people.

Local people's governments at all levels shall lead the national defense education work within their respective administrative regions. The local military commands shall assist and support the local people's governments in carrying out national defense education.

Article 7 The national defense education working body of the State shall plan, organize, guide, and coordinate the national defense education work nationwide.

The bodies responsible for national defense education work at or above the county level shall organize, guide, coordinate, and inspect the national defense education work within their respective administrative regions.

Article 8 The departments of education, veterans affairs, culture and publicity, among others, shall be responsible for national defense education work within their respective functions and duties.

The competent departments for conscription, national defense scientific research and production, national economic mobilization, civil air defense, national defense transportation, and protection of military installations shall be responsible for national defense education work in accordance with this Law and relevant laws and regulations.

Trade unions, the Communist Youth League, women's federations, and other relevant social organizations shall assist the people's governments in carrying out national defense education.

Article 9 The Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Armed Police Force shall carry out national defense education in accordance with the relevant provisions of the Central Military Commission.

Article 10 The State supports and encourages social organizations and individuals to carry out activities beneficial to national defense education.

Article 11 The State and society shall commend and reward, in various forms, organizations and individuals that have made outstanding contributions to national defense education work.

Article 12 The State shall establish a National Defense Education Day for the whole people.

## Chapter II National Defense Education in Schools

Article 13 National defense education in schools is the foundation of national defense education for the whole people and an important part of implementing quality education.

The administrative departments for education shall include national defense education in their work plans, strengthen the organization, guidance, and supervision of national defense education in schools, and conduct regular assessments of national defense education work in schools.

Article 14 Primary schools and junior secondary schools shall incorporate the content of national defense education into relevant courses, combine classroom teaching with extracurricular activities, and conduct national defense education for students.

Primary schools and junior secondary schools with the necessary conditions may organize students to carry out youth military school activities with national defense education as the theme. The administrative departments for education, the Communist Youth League organizations, and other relevant departments shall strengthen the guidance and management of youth military school activities.

Primary schools and junior secondary schools may, as needed, employ off-campus counselors to assist the schools in carrying out various forms of national defense education activities.

Article 15 Institutions of higher education, senior secondary schools, and schools equivalent to senior secondary schools shall combine classroom teaching with military training to conduct national defense education for students.

Institutions of higher education shall offer appropriate national defense education courses. Senior secondary schools and schools equivalent to senior secondary schools shall arrange special national defense education content in relevant courses and may carry out various forms of national defense education activities among students.

The military training of students in institutions of higher education, senior secondary schools, and schools equivalent to senior secondary schools shall be organized and implemented by the school's military training body or military instructors in accordance with relevant state regulations. Military organs shall assist schools in organizing students' military training.

Article 16 Schools shall include national defense education in their work and teaching plans and take effective measures to ensure the quality and effectiveness of national defense education.

When organizing military training activities, schools shall take measures to strengthen safety guarantees.

Article 17 All types of educational institutions responsible for training state functionaries shall incorporate national defense education into their training plans and offer appropriate national defense education courses.

The State shall, as needed, select and send responsible persons from local authorities and departments to relevant military academies for training, to learn and master the national defense knowledge necessary for performing leadership duties.

## Chapter III National Defense Education in Society

Article 18 State organs shall, based on the nature and characteristics of their respective work, adopt various forms to conduct national defense education for their staff.

Staff of state organs shall possess basic knowledge of national defense. Staff of state organs engaged in national defense construction must learn and master the national defense knowledge necessary for performing their duties.

Leading personnel of various regions and departments shall perform their duties of organizing and leading the conduct of national defense education in their respective regions and departments in accordance with the law.

Article 19 Enterprises and institutions shall include national defense education in their employee education plans and conduct national defense education for employees in conjunction with political education, professional training, cultural and sports activities, and other activities.

Enterprises and institutions undertaking tasks such as national defense scientific research and production, national defense facility construction, and national defense transportation support shall, based on the tasks they undertake, formulate corresponding national defense education plans and conduct targeted national defense education for employees.

Social organizations shall conduct national defense education based on the characteristics of their respective activities.

Article 20 The theater-level military commands, provincial military commands (garrison commands), sub-military commands (garrison commands), and the people's armed forces departments of counties, autonomous counties, cities, and municipal districts shall, in accordance with the relevant provisions of the State and the military, conduct national defense education for militia and reserve personnel in conjunction with political education, organizational consolidation, military training, duty performance, conscription work, and activities on major festivals and anniversaries.
National defense education for militia and reserve personnel shall focus on core militia members, first-category reserve personnel, and militia and reserve personnel holding leadership positions. Systems shall be established and improved to ensure the implementation of the number of personnel receiving education, the time for education, and the content of education.

Article 21 Urban residents' committees and rural villagers' committees shall incorporate national defense education into the content of community and rural socialist spiritual civilization construction and conduct national defense education for residents and villagers in conjunction with conscription work, supporting the military and giving preferential treatment to families of servicemen and martyrs, and activities on major festivals and anniversaries.

Urban residents' committees and rural villagers' committees may employ demobilized servicemen to assist in carrying out national defense education.

Article 22 Departments and units in culture, news, publishing, radio, film, and television shall, based on the requirements of the situation and tasks, adopt various forms to carry out national defense education.

Radio stations, television stations, and newspapers of the central level, provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government, and cities divided into districts shall open national defense education programs or columns to popularize national defense knowledge.

Article 23 Places such as martyrs' cemeteries, revolutionary sites, and other museums, memorial halls, science and technology museums, cultural centers, and youth palaces with national defense education functions shall provide convenience for citizens to receive national defense education and offer preferential treatment or free access for organized national defense education activities; those named as national defense education bases in accordance with Article 28 of this Law shall provide free access to organized primary and secondary school students; and shall be open to the public free of charge on the National Defense Education Day for the whole people.

## Chapter IV Guarantees for National Defense Education

Article 24 People's governments at all levels shall incorporate national defense education into the national economic and social development plan and, based on the needs for carrying out national defense education, ensure the funds required for national defense education in the fiscal budget.

Article 25 The funds required for state organs, institutions, and social organizations to carry out national defense education shall be covered within their own budget funds; the funds required for enterprises to carry out national defense education shall be covered within their own employee education funds.

The funds required for schools to organize students' military training shall be handled in accordance with relevant state regulations.

Article 26 The State encourages social organizations and individuals to donate property to fund the development of national defense education.

Property donated by social organizations and individuals to fund national defense education shall be managed in accordance with the law by a legally established national defense education fund organization or other public welfare social organizations.

The State encourages social organizations and individuals to provide or donate physical objects with national defense education significance in their collections for use in national defense education. The units using the provided physical objects shall properly keep them and return them promptly after use.

Article 27 Funds for national defense education and property donated by social organizations and individuals to fund national defense education must be used for the cause of national defense education. No unit or individual may misappropriate or withhold them.

Article 28 Places specified in Article 23 of this Law that meet the following conditions may, upon approval by the people's government of a province, autonomous region, or municipality directly under the Central Government, be named as national defense education bases:

(1) Having a clear thematic content of national defense education;
(2) Having sound management institutions and rules and regulations;
(3) Having corresponding national defense education facilities;
(4) Having necessary funding guarantees;
(5) Having significant social education effects.

National defense education bases shall strengthen their construction, continuously improve themselves, and fully exert their national defense education functions. If a named national defense education base no longer meets the conditions specified in the preceding paragraph, the original approving authority shall revoke the naming.

Article 29 People's governments at all levels shall strengthen the planning, construction, and management of national defense education bases and provide necessary guarantees for them to play their roles.

People's governments at all levels shall strengthen the collection, sorting, and protection of cultural relics with national defense education significance.

Article 30 National defense education for the whole people shall use a unified national defense education syllabus. The national defense education syllabus shall be formulated by the national defense education working body of the State.

National defense education textbooks applicable to different regions and different types of education targets shall be compiled by relevant departments or local authorities based on the national defense education syllabus and in conjunction with the characteristics of their respective regions and departments.

Article 31 National defense education working bodies at all levels shall organize and coordinate relevant departments to do a good job in the selection, training, and management of national defense education instructors and strengthen the construction of the national defense education teaching staff.

National defense education instructors shall be selected from among persons who are enthusiastic about the cause of national defense education and possess basic national defense knowledge and necessary military skills.

Article 32 The Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Armed Police Force shall, as needed and possible, select military instructors for organized national defense education activities in their garrison areas and provide necessary military training venues, facilities, and other conveniences.

On National Day, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Day, and the National Defense Education Day for the whole people, approved military camps may be opened to the public. The measures for opening military camps shall be stipulated by the Central Military Commission.

## Chapter V Legal Liability

Article 33 If a state organ, social organization, enterprise, institution, or other social organization violates the provisions of this Law and refuses to carry out national defense education activities, the relevant department of the people's government or the superior organ shall give criticism and education and order correction within a time limit; if it refuses to correct and causes adverse effects, the person in charge directly responsible shall be given administrative sanctions in accordance with the law.

Article 34 If anyone violates the provisions of this Law by misappropriating or withholding funds for national defense education, the relevant competent department shall order the return of the funds within a time limit; the person in charge directly responsible and other persons directly responsible shall be given administrative sanctions in accordance with the law; if the act constitutes a crime, criminal liability shall be pursued in accordance with the law.

Article 35 If anyone encroaches upon or damages the facilities of a national defense education base or damages exhibits, the relevant competent department shall give criticism and education and order correction within a time limit; the person responsible shall bear corresponding civil liability in accordance with the law.

If an act listed in the preceding paragraph violates public security administration regulations, the public security organ shall impose public security administration penalties in accordance with the law; if the act constitutes a crime, criminal liability shall be pursued in accordance with the law.

Article 36 If anyone creates disturbances, disrupts the order of national defense education work and activities, or uses the name of national defense education to defraud money, the relevant competent department shall give criticism and education and stop the act; if the act violates public security administration regulations, the public security organ shall impose public security administration penalties in accordance with the law; if the act constitutes a crime, criminal liability shall be pursued in accordance with the law.

Article 37 If a state functionary responsible for national defense education neglects duty, abuses power, or engages in malpractice for personal gain, administrative sanctions shall be given in accordance with the law; if the act constitutes a crime, criminal liability shall be pursued in accordance with the law.

## Chapter VI Supplementary Provisions

Article 38 This Law shall come into force on the date of its promulgation.
