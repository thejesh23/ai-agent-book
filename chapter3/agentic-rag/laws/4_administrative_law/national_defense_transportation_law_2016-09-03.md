# National Defense Transportation Law of the People's Republic of China

Adopted at the 22nd Meeting of the Standing Committee of the 12th National People's Congress on September 3, 2016

<!-- INFO END -->

## Chapter I: General Provisions

**Article 1** This Law is enacted to strengthen national defense transportation construction, promote the integrated development of military and civilian sectors in the transportation field, and ensure the smooth progress of national defense activities.

**Article 2** This Law applies to activities of planning, construction, management, and resource utilization carried out in the transportation fields of railways, roads, waterways, aviation, pipelines, and postal services for the purpose of meeting national defense needs.

**Article 3** The State adheres to the strategy of integrated military and civilian development, promotes the optimal allocation and reasonable sharing of military and civilian resources, enhances the capabilities of national defense transportation in peacetime service, emergency response, and wartime operations, and fosters the coordinated development of economic construction and national defense construction.

National defense transportation work follows the principles of unified leadership, hierarchical responsibility, overall planning, and the combination of peacetime and wartime needs.

**Article 4** The national competent authority for national defense transportation is responsible for planning, organizing, guiding, and coordinating national defense transportation work nationwide. The establishment and functions of the national competent authority for national defense transportation shall be stipulated by the State Council and the Central Military Commission.

The competent authority for national defense transportation of a local people's government at or above the county level shall be responsible for national defense transportation work within its administrative region.

The relevant departments of a people's government at or above the county level and relevant military organs shall, according to their respective functions and duties, be responsible for related national defense transportation work.

The relevant departments of a people's government at or above the provincial level and the relevant departments of the military shall establish a consultation mechanism for the integrated military and civilian development of national defense transportation, mutually inform each other of transportation construction and national defense requirements, and study and resolve major issues concerning national defense transportation.

**Article 5** Citizens and organizations shall fulfill their obligations for national defense transportation in accordance with the law.

The State encourages citizens and organizations to participate in national defense transportation construction in accordance with the law and provides policy and financial support in accordance with relevant regulations.

**Article 6** Funds for national defense transportation shall be included in the government budget according to the principle of division of powers and responsibilities.

Reasonable expenditures incurred by enterprises and institutions for carrying out routine national defense transportation work shall be included in their own budgets and accounted for as costs.

**Article 7** A people's government at or above the county level may, according to national defense needs, lawfully requisition civilian transportation resources such as civilian transport vehicles, transportation facilities, and transportation materials. Relevant organizations and individuals shall cooperate and fulfill the relevant obligations.

The organization, implementation, and compensation for the requisition of civilian transportation resources shall be carried out in accordance with relevant laws and administrative regulations.

**Article 8** People's governments at all levels shall incorporate national defense transportation education into national defense education for the entire population, conduct national defense transportation publicity activities in various forms, popularize knowledge of national defense transportation, and enhance citizens' awareness of national defense transportation.

The administrative departments for railways, roads, waterways, aviation, pipelines, and postal services (hereinafter collectively referred to as the competent transportation departments) at all levels and relevant enterprises and institutions shall conduct national defense transportation education for personnel within their respective systems and units.

Institutions of higher learning with transportation-related majors shall incorporate knowledge of national defense transportation into relevant professional courses or offer separate courses on national defense transportation.

**Article 9** Any organization or individual has the obligation to keep confidential any State secrets and commercial secrets learned during national defense transportation work.

**Article 10** Organizations and individuals that have made outstanding contributions to national defense transportation work shall be commended and rewarded in accordance with relevant State regulations.

**Article 11** The State strengthens the informatization construction of national defense transportation to provide support for improving the support capability of national defense transportation.

**Article 12** During wartime or under special peacetime circumstances, when it is necessary to take national defense mobilization measures in the transportation field, such as industry regulation or providing priority transportation support for the armed forces, the provisions of the *National Defense Law of the People's Republic of China*, the *National Defense Mobilization Law of the People's Republic of China*, and other relevant laws shall apply.

When the armed forces organize military exercises or training that require temporary control measures over transportation, the relevant regulations of the State Council and the Central Military Commission shall apply.

**Article 13** During wartime or under special peacetime circumstances, the State may, as needed, establish a joint command organization for national defense transportation to coordinate transportation resources nationwide or in specific regions, and to uniformly organize and command the transportation of personnel and materials, as well as the emergency repair, construction, and protection of transportation facilities and equipment. Relevant organizations and individuals shall obey the unified command.

## Chapter II: National Defense Transportation Planning

**Article 14** National defense transportation planning includes planning for the construction of national defense transportation engineering facilities, planning for the construction of specialized national defense transportation support teams, planning for the reserve of national defense transportation materials, and planning for national defense transportation scientific research.

The formulation of national defense transportation planning shall meet the following requirements:

(1) Meeting national defense needs, facilitating rapid transition between peacetime and wartime, and ensuring the smooth progress of national defense activities;
(2) Taking into account the needs of economic and social development, highlighting key points, focusing on efficiency, and promoting the integration and sharing of resources;
(3) Conforming to urban and rural planning and the overall land use plan, and coordinating with the national comprehensive transportation system development plan;
(4) Facilitating the strengthening of border and coastal defense transportation infrastructure construction, and supporting the development of transportation in economically underdeveloped border and coastal areas;
(5) Protecting the environment and conserving resources such as land and energy.

**Article 15** A people's government at or above the county level shall incorporate national defense transportation construction into the national economic and social development plan.

When formulating development strategies, industrial policies, and planning the layout of transportation networks for the transportation industry and related fields, the State Council and its relevant departments, as well as the people's governments of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government, shall take into account national defense needs to enhance the capability of the national comprehensive transportation system to support national defense activities.

The relevant departments of the State Council shall incorporate relevant national defense requirements into the technical standards and specifications for transportation facilities and equipment. The relevant national defense requirements shall be summarized and proposed by the national competent authority for national defense transportation after soliciting opinions from the relevant departments of the military.

**Article 16** The plan for the construction of national defense transportation engineering facilities shall be formulated by the competent authority for national defense transportation of a people's government at or above the county level jointly with the competent transportation department of the same level, reviewed by the development and reform department of the same level, and submitted to the people's government at the same level for approval.

The plan for the construction of lower-level national defense transportation engineering facilities shall be formulated based on the plan for the construction of higher-level national defense transportation engineering facilities.

When formulating the plan for the construction of national defense transportation engineering facilities, opinions shall be solicited from relevant military organs and the relevant departments of the people's government at the same level. When formulating the comprehensive transportation system development plan and the plan for the construction of transportation engineering facilities, the relevant departments of a people's government at or above the county level shall solicit opinions from the competent authority for national defense transportation of the same level and incorporate relevant content regarding the construction of national defense transportation engineering facilities.

**Article 17** The plan for the construction of specialized national defense transportation support teams shall be formulated by the national competent authority for national defense transportation jointly with the relevant departments of the State Council and the relevant departments of the military.

**Article 18** The plan for the reserve of national defense transportation materials shall be formulated by the competent authority for national defense transportation jointly with the relevant military and civilian departments.

The reserve plan for centrally reserved national defense transportation materials shall be formulated by the national competent authority for national defense transportation jointly with the competent transportation department of the State Council and the relevant departments of the military.

The reserve plan for locally reserved national defense transportation materials shall be formulated by the competent authority for national defense transportation of the people's government of a province, autonomous region, or municipality directly under the Central Government jointly with the relevant departments of the people's government at the same level and relevant military organs.

**Article 19** The plan for national defense transportation scientific research shall be formulated by the national competent authority for national defense transportation jointly with the relevant departments of the State Council and the relevant departments of the military.

## Chapter III: Transportation Engineering Facilities

**Article 20** The construction of national defense transportation engineering facilities shall be based on the plan for the construction of national defense transportation engineering facilities to ensure the smooth flow of national defense transportation during wartime and under special peacetime circumstances.

The construction of other transportation engineering facilities shall implement national defense requirements in accordance with the law, adopt engineering and technical measures to enhance their national defense functions during construction, and improve the capability of national defense transportation support.

**Article 21** National defense transportation engineering facilities shall be designed, constructed, and undergo completion acceptance in accordance with the capital construction procedures, relevant technical standards and specifications, and national defense requirements. The relevant competent authority for national defense transportation of the people's government shall organize the relevant departments of the military to participate in the design review, completion acceptance, and other work of the project.

Projects added to enhance national defense functions during the construction of transportation engineering facilities shall be designed, constructed, and accepted simultaneously with the main project.

**Article 22** National defense transportation engineering facilities shall, while meeting the needs of national defense activities, provide convenience for economic and social activities.

**Article 23** The management unit of a national defense transportation engineering facility shall be responsible for the maintenance and management of the facility, preserving its national defense function.

If a national defense transportation engineering facility needs to change its use or be decommissioned, the management unit of the facility shall report it level by level to the national competent authority for national defense transportation or its authorized competent authority for national defense transportation for approval.

A people's government at or above the county level shall strengthen the supervision and inspection of the maintenance and management of national defense transportation engineering facilities.

**Article 24** No organization or individual shall, during production or other activities, affect the normal use of national defense transportation engineering facilities or endanger the safety of such facilities.

**Article 25** The competent authority for national defense transportation of a people's government at or above the county level shall be responsible for understanding from the competent transportation department of the same level and relevant enterprises and institutions the project initiation, design, construction, and other aspects of transportation engineering facility construction projects; the relevant competent transportation department of the people's government and relevant enterprises and institutions shall cooperate.

**Article 26** The competent authority for national defense transportation of a people's government at or above the county level shall promptly inform the relevant military organs of the construction status of transportation engineering facilities, solicit their opinions on implementing national defense requirements, and summarize and propose specific projects that need to implement national defense requirements.

**Article 27** For transportation engineering facility construction projects that need to implement national defense requirements, the competent authority for national defense transportation of the relevant people's government, jointly with the development and reform department, finance department, competent transportation department of the same level, and relevant military organs, shall negotiate with the construction unit to determine the specific matters for implementing the national defense requirements.

The increased costs incurred by new construction, renovation, or expansion projects of transportation engineering facilities due to the implementation of national defense requirements shall be borne by the State. The relevant departments shall provide support and guarantee for the implementation of the projects.

**Article 28** People's governments at all levels shall provide policy support in terms of land use, urban and rural planning, finance, taxes and fees, etc., in accordance with relevant State regulations, for projects constructing national defense transportation engineering facilities and transportation engineering facility construction projects implementing national defense requirements.

## Chapter IV: Civilian Transport Vehicles

**Article 29** The national competent authority for national defense transportation shall, based on national defense needs, jointly with the relevant departments of the State Council and the relevant departments of the military, determine the categories and scope of civilian transport vehicles that need to implement national defense requirements, and promptly announce them to the public.

The State encourages citizens and organizations to build, purchase, and operate civilian transport vehicles and related equipment within the categories and scope specified in the preceding paragraph.

**Article 30** The competent authority for national defense transportation of a people's government at or above the county level shall understand from the registration management department of civilian transport vehicles and the builders or purchasers the construction, purchase, use, and other aspects of civilian transport vehicles that need to implement national defense requirements; relevant citizens and organizations shall cooperate.

**Article 31** The competent authority for national defense transportation of a people's government at or above the county level shall promptly inform the relevant military organs of the basic information of the civilian transport vehicles it has obtained, solicit their opinions on implementing national defense requirements, and summarize and propose specific projects for civilian transport vehicles that need to implement national defense requirements.

**Article 32** For specific projects of civilian transport vehicles that need to implement national defense requirements, the competent authority for national defense transportation of a people's government at or above the county level, jointly with the finance department, competent transportation department of the same level, and relevant military organs, shall negotiate with the relevant citizens and organizations to determine the specific matters for implementing the national defense requirements and sign relevant agreements.

**Article 33** The increased costs incurred by civilian transport vehicles due to the implementation of national defense requirements shall be borne by the State. The relevant departments shall provide support and guarantee for the implementation of national defense requirements on civilian transport vehicles.

People's governments at all levels shall provide policy support in terms of service procurement, operation scope, etc., in accordance with relevant regulations, for civilian transport vehicles implementing national defense requirements.

**Article 34** The owner, lessee, or operator of a civilian transport vehicle implementing national defense requirements shall be responsible for the maintenance and management of the civilian transport vehicle, ensuring its operational effectiveness.

## Chapter V: National Defense Transportation

**Article 35** The competent transportation department of a people's government at or above the county level, jointly with the relevant transportation departments of the military, shall organize national defense transportation in accordance with the principles of unified planning, centralized command, speed and accuracy, and security and confidentiality.

Citizens and organizations undertaking national defense transportation tasks shall prioritize the arrangement of national defense transportation tasks.

**Article 36** The State, relying mainly on large and medium-sized transportation enterprises, organizes the construction of strategic projection support forces, enhances strategic projection capabilities, and provides effective support for the rapid organization of long-distance, large-scale national defense transportation.

Enterprises undertaking strategic projection support tasks shall be responsible for organizing personnel and equipment, formulating implementation plans according to relevant regulations, conducting necessary training and exercises, and improving their ability to perform strategic projection tasks.

**Article 37** People's governments at all levels and military organs shall strengthen the construction of support facilities such as supply and loading/unloading for national defense transportation.

Local people's governments at or above the county level and relevant enterprises and institutions shall, according to the needs of national defense transportation, provide necessary services or support in areas such as food and water supply, loading/unloading operations, medical rescue, passage and rest, and security guard.

**Article 38** China's overseas institutions and Chinese enterprises engaged in international transportation business, as well as their overseas institutions, shall provide assistance for the replenishment and rest of ships, aircraft, vehicles, and personnel involved in China's international rescue, maritime escort, and military operations to safeguard national overseas interests.

The relevant departments of the State shall provide relevant facilitation for the entry and exit of personnel, transport vehicles, and goods required by the institutions and enterprises specified in the preceding paragraph for providing assistance to overseas military operations.

**Article 39** The expenses incurred by citizens and organizations in completing national defense transportation tasks shall be paid by the using unit at a price not lower than the market price. The specific measures shall be stipulated by the finance department and the competent transportation department of the State Council, and the Logistics Support Department of the Central Military Commission.

**Article 40** The military may, as needed, station military representatives in relevant transportation enterprises or areas where transportation enterprises are concentrated, to jointly complete national defense transportation and transportation support tasks with relevant units.

The units where military representatives are stationed and the relevant departments of the local people's government shall provide convenience for the military representatives to carry out their work.

The stationing and duties of military representatives shall be carried out in accordance with the relevant regulations of the State Council and the Central Military Commission.

## Chapter VI: National Defense Transportation Support

**Article 41** The competent authorities for national defense transportation at all levels shall organize the relevant departments of the people's government and relevant military organs to formulate national defense transportation support plans, specifying key transportation targets, routes, and support principles, tasks, technical measures, and organizational measures.

**Article 42** The relevant departments of the State Council and local people's governments at or above the county level shall, according to their respective functions and duties, organize relevant enterprises and institutions to carry out the emergency repair and construction of transportation engineering facilities and the emergency repair of transport vehicles, ensuring the smooth progress of national defense activities. Relevant military organs shall provide support and assistance.

**Article 43** The management units of key transportation targets identified in the national defense transportation support plan and the units scheduled to undertake support tasks shall, according to relevant regulations, formulate support plans for the key transportation targets and make relevant preparations.

**Article 44** When a key transportation target is threatened with destruction, its management unit and the units scheduled to undertake support tasks shall immediately activate the support plan and make corresponding preparations; when a key transportation target is destroyed, they shall, according to the division of tasks, quickly organize the implementation of engineering reinforcement, emergency repair, and construction to restore transportation as soon as possible.

If other transportation engineering facilities related to national defense transportation are destroyed, their management units shall promptly report to the higher authorities according to the management relationship and simultaneously organize repairs.

**Article 45** The competent authority for national defense transportation of a people's government at or above the county level, jointly with the competent departments for land and resources, urban and rural planning, etc., of the same level, shall determine the land for the scheduled emergency construction of important national defense transportation engineering facilities as the national defense transportation control area, and incorporate it into the overall land use plan and urban and rural planning.

Without the approval of the competent departments for land and resources, urban and rural planning, and the competent authority for national defense transportation of a people's government at or above the county level, no organization or individual may occupy the land designated as a national defense transportation control area.

**Article 46** The air and sea defense of key transportation targets shall be included in the air and sea defense plans by the relevant departments of the military and implemented uniformly.

The ground defense of key transportation targets shall be jointly organized and implemented by the local people's government at or above the county level and the relevant military organs.

The engineering and technical protection of key transportation targets shall be organized and guided by the competent transportation department of the local people's government at or above the county level, jointly with the competent authority for national defense transportation and the civil air defense department of the same level, to be implemented by their management units and support units.

The protection of transportation facilities other than key transportation targets shall be carried out by the local people's government at or above the county level in accordance with relevant regulations.

**Article 47** Due to special needs such as major military operations, national defense scientific research and production tests, and the transportation of confidential materials and dangerous goods related to national defense, the relevant departments of a people's government at or above the county level shall, within the prescribed authority and procedures, take necessary traffic management measures and safety protection measures in the relevant land, water, and air areas. Relevant military organs shall provide assistance.

**Article 48** The competent transportation department of a people's government at or above the county level, relevant military organs, and the competent authority for national defense transportation shall, as needed, organize relevant enterprises and institutions to conduct training and exercises for specialized national defense transportation support teams.

Specialized national defense transportation support teams shall be formed by enterprises and institutions in accordance with relevant regulations.

The living welfare benefits of personnel participating in the training and exercises of specialized national defense transportation support teams shall be handled with reference to the relevant regulations on militia participation in military training.

**Article 49** When specialized national defense transportation support teams perform tasks such as emergency repair, construction, and protection of national defense transportation engineering facilities, emergency repair of civilian transport vehicles, and emergency transport of personnel and materials, they shall be uniformly deployed by the competent authority for national defense transportation of a people's government at or above the county level jointly with the competent transportation department of the same level.

Vehicles, ships, and other motorized equipment of specialized national defense transportation support teams, when performing tasks, shall be marked with a unified symbol as stipulated by the national competent authority for national defense transportation and may be given priority passage.

**Article 50** People's governments at all levels shall provide policy support to enterprises and individuals undertaking national defense transportation support tasks in accordance with relevant regulations.

## Chapter VII: Reserve of National Defense Transportation Materials

**Article 51** The State establishes a system for the reserve of national defense transportation materials to ensure the smooth flow of national defense transportation during wartime and under special peacetime circumstances.

The reserve of national defense transportation materials shall be reasonably distributed and appropriately scaled, and the reserved materials shall meet the quality standards stipulated by the State.

The varieties of nationally reserved national defense transportation materials shall be stipulated by the national competent authority for national defense transportation jointly with the relevant departments of the State Council and the relevant departments of the military.**Article 52** The competent transportation department of the State Council and the national defense transportation authorities of the people's governments of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government shall, in accordance with relevant regulations, designate the units responsible for the storage and management of national defense transportation reserve materials, and supervise and inspect the management of such materials.

The units responsible for the storage and management of national defense transportation reserve materials shall establish and improve management systems, and shall store, maintain, and update the reserve materials in accordance with national regulations and standards, ensuring their effectiveness and safety. They shall not misappropriate, damage, or lose the reserve materials.

**Article 53** During wartime or under special peacetime circumstances, when tasks such as transportation protection, emergency repair, or emergency construction are required, or when organizing major military exercises, disaster relief operations, or training and drills for specialized national defense transportation support units, national defense transportation reserve materials may be deployed.

The deployment of centrally reserved national defense transportation materials shall be approved by the national defense transportation authority of the state; the deployment of locally reserved national defense transportation materials shall be approved by the national defense transportation authority of the people's government of the relevant province, autonomous region, or municipality directly under the Central Government.

The units responsible for the storage and management of national defense transportation reserve materials shall strictly execute deployment orders and shall not refuse or delay such orders.

No organization or individual may deploy national defense transportation reserve materials without approval.

**Article 54** If national defense transportation reserve materials lose their reserve value due to technological upgrades, generational replacement, or because their main technical performance falls below the requirements for use and maintenance, they may be repurposed or disposed of as scrap.

If centrally reserved national defense transportation materials need to be repurposed or disposed of as scrap, the national defense transportation authority of the state shall organize a technical appraisal and review, and then submit the matter to the finance department of the State Council for approval.

If locally reserved national defense transportation materials need to be repurposed or disposed of as scrap, the national defense transportation authority of the people's government of the relevant province, autonomous region, or municipality directly under the Central Government shall organize a technical appraisal and review, and then submit the matter to the finance department of the corresponding people's government for approval.

Any income generated from the repurposing or scrapping of centrally or locally reserved national defense transportation materials shall be turned over to the treasury of the corresponding level and incorporated into fiscal budget management.

## Chapter VIII: Legal Liability

**Article 55** Anyone who violates the provisions of this Law by committing any of the following acts shall be ordered by the competent transportation department or the national defense transportation authority of a people's government at or above the county level to make corrections within a specified time limit; the directly responsible person in charge and other directly responsible personnel shall be given sanctions in accordance with the law; any illegal income shall be confiscated, and a fine of not less than one time but not more than five times the illegal income shall be imposed:

(1) Unauthorizedly changing the use of national defense transportation engineering facilities or disposing of them as scrap;
(2) Refusing or intentionally delaying the execution of national defense transportation tasks;
(3) Refusing or intentionally delaying the execution of emergency repair or construction tasks for key transportation targets;
(4) Refusing or intentionally delaying the execution of orders for the deployment of national defense transportation reserve materials;
(5) Unauthorizedly changing the use of national defense transportation reserve materials or disposing of them as scrap;
(6) Unauthorizedly deploying national defense transportation reserve materials;
(7) Failing to store or maintain national defense transportation reserve materials in accordance with regulations, resulting in damage or loss.

If the above illegal acts cause property damage, the liable party shall bear compensation liability in accordance with the law.

**Article 56** If staff members of the national defense transportation authority, relevant military organs, competent transportation departments, or other relevant departments violate the provisions of this Law under any of the following circumstances, the directly responsible person in charge and other directly responsible personnel shall be given sanctions in accordance with the law:

(1) Abusing power or neglecting duties, causing serious losses to national defense transportation work;
(2) Embezzling or misappropriating national defense transportation funds or materials;
(3) Disclosing state secrets or commercial secrets learned during national defense transportation work;
(4) Infringing upon the legitimate rights and interests of citizens or organizations during national defense transportation work.

**Article 57** Anyone who violates the provisions of this Law and constitutes a violation of public security administration shall be given a public security administration penalty in accordance with the law; if a crime is constituted, criminal liability shall be pursued in accordance with the law.

## Chapter IX: Supplementary Provisions

**Article 58** For the purposes of this Law, "national defense transportation engineering facilities" refer to transportation infrastructure constructed by the state for national defense purposes, as well as engineering facilities dedicated to national defense transportation, such as command, repair, loading/unloading, and storage facilities.

For the purposes of this Law, "national defense transportation" refers to activities carried out by the government and the military for national defense purposes, utilizing military-civilian transportation resources to transport personnel, equipment, and materials. Transportation activities carried out by the military using its own resources shall be executed in accordance with the relevant regulations of the Central Military Commission.

**Article 59** This Law applies to the construction, management, and use of information facilities, equipment, and specialized support units closely related to national defense transportation.

If the state has other regulations concerning information mobilization, those regulations shall prevail.

**Article 60** This Law shall come into effect on January 1, 2017.
