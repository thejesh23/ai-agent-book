# People's Republic of China Military Personnel Insurance Law

Adopted at the 26th Meeting of the Standing Committee of the 11th National People's Congress on April 27, 2012

<!-- INFO END -->

## Chapter I General Provisions

**Article 1** This Law is enacted to regulate military personnel insurance relations, safeguard the legitimate rights and interests of military personnel, and promote national defense and military construction.

**Article 2** The State establishes a military personnel insurance system.

This Law applies to the establishment, contribution, transfer and continuation of military casualty insurance, retirement pension insurance, retirement medical insurance, and insurance for unemployed military spouses accompanying the military.

**Article 3** The military personnel insurance system shall reflect the occupational characteristics of military personnel, align with the social insurance system, and be commensurate with the level of economic and social development.

The State shall, in accordance with the development of the social insurance system, supplement and improve the military personnel insurance system in a timely manner.

**Article 4** The State promotes the development of military personnel insurance and provides financial appropriations and policy support for military personnel insurance.

**Article 5** The competent authority for military personnel insurance of the Chinese People's Liberation Army is responsible for military personnel insurance work throughout the armed forces. The social insurance administrative department, the finance department of the State Council, and other relevant departments of the armed forces are responsible for related military personnel insurance work within their respective duties.

The financial departments of military logistics (joint logistics) organs are responsible for handling military personnel insurance registration, recording individual rights and interests, and paying military personnel insurance benefits.

The financial departments of military logistics (joint logistics) organs and local social insurance agencies shall, in accordance with their respective duties, handle the procedures for the transfer and continuation of military personnel insurance and social insurance relations.

**Article 6** Military personnel shall participate in military personnel insurance in accordance with the law and enjoy corresponding insurance benefits.

Military personnel have the right to inquire about and verify their personal contribution records and individual rights and interests records, and to request that the financial departments of military logistics (joint logistics) organs and local social insurance agencies handle the procedures for the transfer and continuation of pension, medical and other insurance relations in accordance with the law, and provide relevant services such as military personnel insurance and social insurance consultation.

## Chapter II Military Casualty Insurance

**Article 7** If a military person dies in combat or due to official duties, a military death insurance benefit shall be paid according to the determined nature of death and the corresponding insurance benefit standard.

**Article 8** If a military person becomes disabled due to combat, official duties, or illness, a military disability insurance benefit shall be paid according to the assessed disability grade and the corresponding insurance benefit standard.

**Article 9** The determination of the nature of death and disability of military personnel, the assessment of disability grades, and the corresponding insurance benefit standards shall be implemented in accordance with the relevant provisions of the State and the armed forces.

**Article 10** Military personnel who die or become disabled due to any of the following circumstances shall not enjoy military casualty insurance benefits:

(1) Intentional crime;
(2) Drunkenness or drug use;
(3) Self-harm or suicide;
(4) Other circumstances stipulated by laws, administrative regulations, and military regulations.

**Article 11** Military personnel who have been assessed with a disability grade due to combat or official duties and who suffer a recurrence of an old injury after retiring from active service and taking up employment shall enjoy corresponding work-related injury benefits in accordance with the law.

**Article 12** The funds required for military casualty insurance shall be borne by the State, and individuals shall not pay insurance premiums.

## Chapter III Retirement Pension Insurance

**Article 13** When military personnel retire from active service and participate in basic pension insurance, the State shall provide retirement pension insurance subsidies.

**Article 14** The standards for military personnel retirement pension insurance subsidies shall be formulated by the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army jointly with the relevant departments of the State Council, based on factors such as the basic pension insurance contribution standards prescribed by the State and the salary levels of military personnel, and shall be submitted to the State Council and the Central Military Commission for approval.

**Article 15** If military personnel have already participated in basic pension insurance before enlistment, the local social insurance agency and the financial department of the military logistics (joint logistics) organ shall handle the procedures for the transfer and continuation of the basic pension insurance relationship.

**Article 16** When military personnel participate in employee basic pension insurance after retiring from active service, the financial department of the military logistics (joint logistics) organ shall transfer the military personnel's retirement pension insurance relationship and corresponding funds to the local social insurance agency, and the local social insurance agency shall handle the corresponding transfer and continuation procedures.

The period of active service of military personnel shall be combined with the contribution periods for employee basic pension insurance before enlistment and after retirement from active service.

**Article 17** When military personnel participate in the new rural social pension insurance or urban resident social pension insurance after retiring from active service, the transfer and continuation procedures shall be handled in accordance with the relevant provisions of the State.

**Article 18** For military personnel who retire from active service and take up civil servant positions or positions of staff managed by reference to the Civil Servant Law, as well as active-duty officers and civilian cadres who retire from active service and choose their own employment, their pension insurance methods shall be implemented in accordance with the relevant provisions of the State.

**Article 19** For military personnel who retire from active service and are placed in a retirement manner, their pension methods shall be implemented in accordance with the relevant provisions of the State Council and the Central Military Commission.

## Chapter IV Retirement Medical Insurance

**Article 20** Officers, civilian cadres, and non-commissioned officers who participate in military personnel retirement medical insurance shall pay military personnel retirement medical insurance premiums, and the State shall provide subsidies equal to the amount of the individual contributions.

Conscripts and cadets on a supply system shall not pay military personnel retirement medical insurance premiums, and the State shall provide military personnel retirement medical insurance subsidies according to prescribed standards.

**Article 21** The standards for individual contributions and State subsidies for military personnel retirement medical insurance shall be determined by the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army jointly with the relevant departments of the State Council, based on factors such as the contribution ratios prescribed by the State and the salary levels of military personnel.

**Article 22** If military personnel have already participated in basic medical insurance before enlistment, the local social insurance agency and the financial department of the military logistics (joint logistics) organ shall handle the procedures for the transfer and continuation of the basic medical insurance relationship.

**Article 23** When military personnel participate in employee basic medical insurance after retiring from active service, the financial department of the military logistics (joint logistics) organ shall transfer the military personnel's retirement medical insurance relationship and corresponding funds to the local social insurance agency, and the local social insurance agency shall handle the corresponding transfer and continuation procedures.

The period of active service of military personnel shall be deemed as the contribution period for employee basic medical insurance, and shall be combined with the contribution periods for employee basic medical insurance before enlistment and after retirement from active service.

**Article 24** When military personnel participate in the new rural cooperative medical care or urban resident basic medical insurance after retiring from active service, the matter shall be handled in accordance with the relevant provisions of the State.

## Chapter V Insurance for Unemployed Military Spouses Accompanying the Military

**Article 25** The State establishes pension insurance, medical insurance, etc., for unemployed military spouses accompanying the military. Unemployed military spouses accompanying the military who participate in insurance shall pay pension insurance premiums and medical insurance premiums, and the State shall provide corresponding subsidies.

The standards for individual contributions and State subsidies for insurance for unemployed military spouses accompanying the military shall be implemented in accordance with the relevant provisions of the State.

**Article 26** If unemployed military spouses accompanying the military have already participated in social insurance before accompanying the military, the local social insurance agency and the financial department of the military logistics (joint logistics) organ shall handle the procedures for the transfer and continuation of the insurance relationship.

**Article 27** When an unemployed military spouse accompanying the military becomes employed or the military person retires from active service, the financial department of the military logistics (joint logistics) organ shall transfer the pension insurance and medical insurance relationships and corresponding funds to the local social insurance agency, and the local social insurance agency shall handle the corresponding transfer and continuation procedures.

The contribution periods for pension insurance and medical insurance of the military spouse during the period of unemployment while accompanying the military shall be combined with their contribution periods for employee basic pension insurance and employee basic medical insurance in the locality.

**Article 28** When an unemployed military spouse accompanying the military reaches the retirement age prescribed by the State, the place of retirement shall be determined in accordance with the relevant provisions of the State, and the financial department of the military logistics (joint logistics) organ shall transfer the pension insurance relationship and corresponding funds to the social insurance agency in the place of retirement, enabling the spouse to enjoy corresponding basic pension insurance benefits.

**Article 29** Local people's governments and relevant departments shall provide services such as employment guidance and training for unemployed military spouses accompanying the military.

If an unemployed military spouse accompanying the military, without justifiable reasons, refuses to accept employment placement by the local people's government, or refuses to accept suitable work introduced or employment training provided by the designated departments or agencies of the local people's government, the provision of insurance contribution subsidies shall be stopped.

## Chapter VI Military Personnel Insurance Fund

**Article 30** The military personnel insurance fund includes the military casualty insurance fund, the military retirement pension insurance fund, the military retirement medical insurance fund, and the insurance fund for unemployed military spouses accompanying the military. Each military personnel insurance fund shall be separately accounted for and accounted for separately according to the type of military personnel insurance, and shall implement the military accounting system.

**Article 31** The military personnel insurance fund shall consist of individual contributions, military personnel insurance funds borne by the central finance, and interest income.

**Article 32** Insurance premiums payable by military personnel shall be withheld and paid by their units on their behalf.

Insurance premiums payable by unemployed military spouses accompanying the military shall be withheld and paid by the military person's unit on their behalf.

**Article 33** The military personnel insurance funds borne by the central finance shall be included in the annual national defense expenditure budget by the finance department of the State Council.

**Article 34** The military personnel insurance fund shall be managed through budgeting and final accounts in accordance with the budget management system of the State and the armed forces.

**Article 35** The military personnel insurance fund shall be stored in special accounts, and the specific management measures shall be implemented in accordance with the relevant provisions of the State and the armed forces.

**Article 36** The military personnel insurance fund shall be centrally managed by the military personnel insurance fund management institution of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

The military personnel insurance fund management institution shall strictly manage the military personnel insurance fund to ensure its safety.

**Article 37** The military personnel insurance fund shall be used exclusively for its designated purposes, and expenditures shall be made according to the prescribed items, scope, and standards. No unit or individual may embezzle, misappropriate, or divert the fund, nor may they change the expenditure items, expand the expenditure scope, or alter the expenditure standards.

## Chapter VII Insurance Handling and Supervision

**Article 38** The financial departments of military logistics (joint logistics) organs and local social insurance agencies shall establish and improve the management system for handling military personnel insurance.

The financial departments of military logistics (joint logistics) organs shall pay military personnel insurance benefits in full and on time.

The financial departments of military logistics (joint logistics) organs and local social insurance agencies shall promptly handle the procedures for the transfer and continuation of military personnel insurance and social insurance relations.

**Article 39** The financial departments of military logistics (joint logistics) organs shall establish insurance files for military personnel and unemployed military spouses accompanying the military, promptly, completely, and accurately record their individual contributions and State subsidies, as well as individual rights and interests records such as the enjoyment of military personnel insurance benefits, and regularly deliver individual rights and interests statements to the individuals.

The financial departments of military logistics (joint logistics) organs and local social insurance agencies shall provide relevant services such as military personnel insurance and social insurance consultation for military personnel and unemployed military spouses accompanying the military.

**Article 40** The military personnel insurance information system shall be uniformly constructed by the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

**Article 41** The finance department of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the audit institutions of the Chinese People's Liberation Army shall, within their respective duties, supervise the revenue, expenditure, and management of the military personnel insurance fund.

**Article 42** Military logistics (joint logistics) organs and local social insurance administrative departments shall supervise and inspect the compliance of units and individuals with this Law.

When military logistics (joint logistics) organs and local social insurance administrative departments conduct supervision and inspection, the units and individuals being inspected shall truthfully provide materials related to military personnel insurance and shall not refuse the inspection or make false reports or conceal information.

**Article 43** The financial departments of military logistics (joint logistics) organs, local social insurance agencies, and their staff shall keep confidential the information of military units and military personnel in accordance with the law and shall not disclose it in any form.

**Article 44** Any unit or individual has the right to report or complain about acts violating the provisions of this Law.

Relevant departments and agencies of the armed forces and the locality shall handle reports and complaints within their duties in accordance with the law; if the matter is not within the duties of the department or agency, it shall notify in writing and transfer it to the department or agency with the authority to handle it. The department or agency with the authority to handle it shall handle it promptly and shall not shirk responsibility.

## Chapter VIII Legal Liability

**Article 45** If the financial departments of military logistics (joint logistics) organs or social insurance agencies have any of the following circumstances, the military logistics (joint logistics) organs or social insurance administrative departments shall order them to make corrections; the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be given sanctions in accordance with the law; if losses are caused, compensation liability shall be borne in accordance with the law:

(1) Failing to establish or transfer and continue military personnel insurance relations in accordance with regulations;
(2) Failing to collect or remit individual insurance premiums in accordance with regulations;
(3) Failing to pay military personnel insurance benefits in accordance with regulations;
(4) Tampering with or losing military personnel insurance file materials such as individual contribution records;
(5) Disclosing information of military units and military personnel;
(6) Allocating or storing military personnel insurance funds in violation of regulations;
(7) Other acts that violate laws and regulations and harm the insurance rights and interests of military personnel.

**Article 46** If military personnel insurance funds are embezzled, misappropriated, or diverted, the military logistics (joint logistics) organs shall order the return of the funds within a specified period, and the directly responsible supervisors and other directly responsible personnel shall be given sanctions in accordance with the law.

**Article 47** If military personnel insurance benefits are obtained by means such as fraud or forging certification materials, the military logistics (joint logistics) organs and social insurance administrative departments shall order the return of the benefits within a specified period and impose sanctions in accordance with the law.

**Article 48** Anyone who violates the provisions of this Law and constitutes a crime shall be investigated for criminal liability in accordance with the law.

## Chapter IX Supplementary Provisions

**Article 49** When military personnel participate in unemployment insurance after retiring from active service, their period of active service shall be deemed as the contribution period for unemployment insurance, and shall be combined with the contribution periods for unemployment insurance before enlistment and after retirement from active service.

**Article 50** The provisions of this Law regarding the rights and obligations of military personnel insurance shall apply to the People's Armed Police; the management of the insurance fund of the Chinese People's Armed Police Force shall be implemented in accordance with the fund management system of the Chinese People's Armed Police Force.

**Article 51** This Law shall come into effect on July 1, 2012.
