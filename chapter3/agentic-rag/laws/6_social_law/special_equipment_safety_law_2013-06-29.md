# Special Equipment Safety Law of the People's Republic of China

Adopted at the Third Session of the Standing Committee of the Twelfth National People's Congress on June 29, 2013

<!-- INFO END -->

## Chapter I General Provisions

Article 1 This Law is enacted for the purpose of strengthening the safety of special equipment, preventing accidents involving special equipment, ensuring the safety of life and property, and promoting economic and social development.

Article 2 This Law shall apply to the manufacture (including design, manufacturing, installation, modification, and repair), operation, use, inspection, and testing of special equipment, as well as the supervision and administration of the safety of special equipment.

For the purposes of this Law, "special equipment" means boilers, pressure vessels (including gas cylinders), pressure pipelines, elevators, hoisting machinery, passenger ropeways, large amusement rides, and special-purpose motor vehicles within factories (or premises) that pose a relatively high risk to the safety of life and property, as well as other special equipment for which this Law is applicable as provided for by laws and administrative regulations.

The State implements a catalog-based management system for special equipment. The catalog of special equipment shall be formulated by the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety under the State Council and implemented after being submitted to and approved by the State Council.

Article 3 The work on special equipment safety shall adhere to the principles of putting safety first, focusing on prevention, promoting energy conservation and environmental protection, and implementing comprehensive governance.

Article 4 The State implements classified, whole-process safety supervision and administration over the manufacture, operation, and use of special equipment.

Article 5 The department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety under the State Council shall implement supervision and administration over the safety of special equipment nationwide. The local people's governments at or above the county level and their departments responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety shall implement supervision and administration over the safety of special equipment within their respective administrative regions.

Article 6 The State Council and local people's governments at all levels shall strengthen leadership over the work on special equipment safety, and urge relevant departments to perform their supervision and administration duties according to law.

Local people's governments at or above the county level shall establish coordination mechanisms to promptly coordinate and resolve issues arising in the supervision and administration of special equipment safety.

Article 7 Units that manufacture, operate, or use special equipment shall comply with this Law and other relevant laws and regulations, establish and improve responsibility systems for special equipment safety and energy conservation, strengthen the management of special equipment safety and energy conservation, and ensure the safety of the manufacture, operation, and use of special equipment while meeting energy conservation requirements.

Article 8 The manufacture, operation, use, inspection, and testing of special equipment shall comply with relevant special equipment safety technical specifications and related standards.

Special equipment safety technical specifications shall be formulated by the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety under the State Council.

Article 9 Trade associations for special equipment shall strengthen industry self-discipline, promote the construction of an industry integrity system, and improve the level of special equipment safety management.

Article 10 The State supports scientific and technological research related to special equipment safety, encourages the promotion and application of advanced technologies and advanced management methods, and rewards units and individuals that make outstanding contributions.

Article 11 The departments responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety shall strengthen publicity and education on special equipment safety, disseminate knowledge about special equipment safety, and enhance the public's awareness of special equipment safety.

Article 12 Any unit or individual has the right to report to the departments responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety and relevant departments any illegal acts involving special equipment safety. The department receiving the report shall handle it promptly.

## Chapter II Manufacture, Operation, and Use

### Section 1 General Provisions

Article 13 Units that manufacture, operate, or use special equipment and their principal responsible persons shall be responsible for the safety of the special equipment they manufacture, operate, or use.

Units that manufacture, operate, or use special equipment shall, in accordance with relevant State regulations, equip themselves with special equipment safety managers, inspectors, and operators, and provide them with necessary safety education and skills training.

Article 14 Special equipment safety managers, inspectors, and operators shall obtain corresponding qualifications in accordance with relevant State regulations before they may engage in relevant work. Special equipment safety managers, inspectors, and operators shall strictly implement safety technical specifications and management systems to ensure the safety of special equipment.

Article 15 Units that manufacture, operate, or use special equipment shall conduct self-inspection and maintenance of the special equipment they manufacture, operate, or use, and shall promptly declare and undergo inspection for special equipment that is subject to inspection as stipulated by the State.

Article 16 Where new materials, new technologies, or new processes are used in special equipment that are inconsistent with the requirements of safety technical specifications, or where safety technical specifications do not provide requirements but may have a significant impact on safety performance, the matter shall be reported to the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety under the State Council. The said department shall promptly entrust a safety technical consulting body or a relevant professional institution to conduct a technical review. Only after the review results are approved by the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety under the State Council may the new materials, new technologies, or new processes be put into production or use.

The department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety under the State Council shall promptly incorporate the relevant technical requirements for the permitted new materials, new technologies, or new processes into the safety technical specifications.

Article 17 The State encourages the purchase of liability insurance for special equipment safety.

### Section 2 Manufacture

Article 18 The State implements a licensing system for the manufacture of special equipment based on the principle of classified supervision and administration. A unit manufacturing special equipment shall meet the following conditions and obtain a license from the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety before it may engage in manufacturing activities:

(1) Having professional and technical personnel suitable for the manufacturing activities;
(2) Having equipment, facilities, and workplaces suitable for the manufacturing activities;
(3) Having sound systems for quality assurance, safety management, and post responsibility.

Article 19 A unit manufacturing special equipment shall ensure that the manufacture of special equipment complies with the requirements of safety technical specifications and related standards, and shall be responsible for the safety performance of the special equipment it manufactures. It shall not manufacture special equipment that fails to meet safety performance requirements and energy efficiency indicators, or that is explicitly eliminated by the State.

Article 20 Design documents for boilers, gas cylinders, oxygen chambers, passenger ropeways, and large amusement rides shall be appraised by an inspection body approved by the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety before they may be used for manufacturing.

Where special equipment products, components, or trial-manufactured new special equipment products, new components, or new materials used in special equipment need to undergo type testing for safety verification in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications, such type testing shall be conducted by an inspection body approved by the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety.

Article 21 When special equipment leaves the factory, it shall be accompanied by relevant technical materials and documents such as design documents required by safety technical specifications, product quality certificates, installation and use maintenance instructions, and supervision inspection certificates, and a product nameplate, safety warning signs, and their explanations shall be placed in a conspicuous position on the special equipment.

Article 22 The installation, modification, or repair of elevators must be carried out by the elevator manufacturer or a unit entrusted by it that has obtained the corresponding license in accordance with this Law. Where an elevator manufacturer entrusts another unit to carry out the installation, modification, or repair of elevators, it shall provide safety guidance and monitoring for such installation, modification, or repair, and carry out verification and commissioning in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications. The elevator manufacturer shall be responsible for the safety performance of the elevator.

Article 23 A construction unit carrying out the installation, modification, or repair of special equipment shall, before commencing the work, notify in writing the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety under the people's government of the municipality directly under the Central Government or the city divided into districts of the intended installation, modification, or repair of special equipment.

Article 24 After the completion of the installation, modification, or repair of special equipment, the construction unit carrying out the installation, modification, or repair shall, within thirty days after acceptance, transfer the relevant technical materials and documents to the unit using the special equipment. The unit using the special equipment shall store them in the safety technical archives of the special equipment.

Article 25 The manufacturing process of special equipment such as boilers, pressure vessels, and pressure pipeline components, and the installation, modification, and major repair processes of boilers, pressure vessels, pressure pipelines, elevators, hoisting machinery, passenger ropeways, and large amusement rides shall be subject to supervision inspection by a special equipment inspection body in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications. Those that have not undergone supervision inspection or have failed such inspection shall not leave the factory or be put into use.

Article 26 The State establishes a recall system for defective special equipment. Where special equipment has a uniform defect endangering safety due to manufacturing reasons, the unit manufacturing the special equipment shall immediately stop production and voluntarily recall it.

Where the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety under the State Council discovers that special equipment should be recalled but has not been recalled, it shall order the unit manufacturing the special equipment to recall it.

### Section 3 Operation

Article 27 Special equipment sold by a special equipment sales unit shall comply with the requirements of safety technical specifications and related standards, and its relevant technical materials and documents such as design documents, product quality certificates, installation and use maintenance instructions, and supervision inspection certificates shall be complete.

A special equipment sales unit shall establish a system for inspection, acceptance, and sales records of special equipment.

It is prohibited to sell special equipment manufactured without obtaining a license, special equipment that has not been inspected or has failed inspection, or special equipment that is explicitly eliminated or has been scrapped by the State.

Article 28 A special equipment leasing unit shall not lease special equipment manufactured without obtaining a license, special equipment that is explicitly eliminated or has been scrapped by the State, or special equipment that has not been maintained in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications, or has not been inspected or has failed inspection.

Article 29 The obligations of use management and maintenance of special equipment during the lease period shall be borne by the special equipment leasing unit, unless otherwise provided by law or agreed upon by the parties.

Article 30 Imported special equipment shall comply with the requirements of China's safety technical specifications and pass inspection. If a license for the manufacture of special equipment in China is required, such a license shall be obtained.

The technical materials and documents accompanying imported special equipment shall comply with the provisions of Article 21 of this Law, and their installation and use maintenance instructions, product nameplates, safety warning signs, and their explanations shall be in Chinese.

The import and export inspection of special equipment shall comply with the laws and administrative regulations on the inspection of import and export commodities.

Article 31 For imported special equipment, the obligation of prior notification shall be fulfilled to the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety at the place of import.

### Section 4 Use

Article 32 A unit using special equipment shall use special equipment that has been manufactured with a license and has passed inspection.

It is prohibited to use special equipment that is explicitly eliminated or has been scrapped by the State.

Article 33 A unit using special equipment shall, within thirty days before or after putting the special equipment into use, register its use with the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety and obtain a use registration certificate. The registration mark shall be placed in a conspicuous position on the special equipment.

Article 34 A unit using special equipment shall establish safety management systems such as post responsibility, hazard management, and emergency rescue, and formulate operating procedures to ensure the safe operation of special equipment.

Article 35 A unit using special equipment shall establish safety technical archives for special equipment. The safety technical archives shall include the following:

(1) Relevant technical materials and documents such as design documents, product quality certificates, installation and use maintenance instructions, and supervision inspection certificates of the special equipment;
(2) Records of periodic inspections and periodic self-inspections of the special equipment;
(3) Records of the daily use status of the special equipment;
(4) Maintenance records of the special equipment and its auxiliary instruments and meters;
(5) Records of operational failures and accidents of the special equipment.

Article 36 The operating unit of special equipment that provides services to the public, such as elevators, passenger ropeways, and large amusement rides, shall be responsible for the use safety of the special equipment, and shall establish a special equipment safety management organization or assign full-time special equipment safety managers. Other units using special equipment shall, depending on the circumstances, establish a special equipment safety management organization or assign full-time or part-time special equipment safety managers.

Article 37 The use of special equipment shall have the prescribed safety distances and safety protection measures.

Buildings and ancillary facilities related to the safety of special equipment shall comply with the provisions of relevant laws and administrative regulations.

Article 38 Where special equipment is co-owned, the co-owners may entrust a property service unit or other manager to manage the special equipment. The entrusted party shall fulfill the obligations of a unit using special equipment as stipulated in this Law and bear corresponding responsibilities. If the co-owners do not entrust, the co-owners or the actual manager shall fulfill the management obligations and bear corresponding responsibilities.

Article 39 A unit using special equipment shall carry out regular maintenance and periodic self-inspections of the special equipment it uses, and make records.

A unit using special equipment shall periodically calibrate, verify, and repair the safety accessories and safety protection devices of the special equipment it uses, and make records.

Article 40 A unit using special equipment shall, in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications, submit a request for periodic inspection to a special equipment inspection body one month before the expiration of the validity period of the inspection certificate.

Upon receiving the request for periodic inspection, the special equipment inspection body shall promptly conduct a safety performance inspection in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications. The unit using special equipment shall place the periodic inspection mark in a conspicuous position on the special equipment.

Special equipment that has not undergone periodic inspection or has failed such inspection shall not continue to be used.

Article 41 Special equipment safety managers shall conduct regular inspections of the use status of special equipment, and shall immediately handle any problems found. In an emergency, they may decide to stop using the special equipment and promptly report to the relevant responsible person of the unit.

Special equipment operators who discover potential accident hazards or other unsafe factors during operation shall immediately report to the special equipment safety manager and the relevant responsible person of the unit. When special equipment operates abnormally, the special equipment operator shall take effective measures in accordance with the operating procedures to ensure safety.

Article 42 Where special equipment malfunctions or experiences abnormal conditions, the unit using the special equipment shall conduct a comprehensive inspection of it, eliminate potential accident hazards, and only then may continue to use it.

Article 43 Before passenger ropeways and large amusement rides are put into use each day, their operating units shall conduct trial runs and routine safety inspections, and check and confirm the safety accessories and safety protection devices.

The operating units of elevators, passenger ropeways, and large amusement rides shall place safety use instructions, safety precautions, and warning signs for elevators, passenger ropeways, and large amusement rides in conspicuous positions easily noticeable by passengers.

Members of the public who ride or operate elevators, passenger ropeways, or large amusement rides shall comply with the requirements of safety use instructions and safety precautions, and obey the management and directions of relevant staff. In the event of abnormal operation, they shall evacuate in an orderly manner in accordance with safety instructions.

Article 44 A unit using a boiler shall carry out boiler water (medium) treatment in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications, and accept periodic inspections by a special equipment inspection body.

Boiler cleaning shall be carried out in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications, and shall be subject to supervision inspection by a special equipment inspection body.

Article 45 The maintenance of elevators shall be carried out by the elevator manufacturer or a unit that has obtained a license for installation, modification, or repair in accordance with this Law.

The elevator maintenance unit shall strictly implement the requirements of safety technical specifications during maintenance, ensure the safety performance of the elevators it maintains, and be responsible for implementing on-site safety protection measures to ensure construction safety.

The elevator maintenance unit shall be responsible for the safety performance of the elevators it maintains. Upon receiving a fault notification, it shall immediately rush to the site and take necessary emergency rescue measures.

Article 46 After an elevator is put into use, the elevator manufacturer shall track, investigate, and understand the safe operation of the elevator it manufactured, provide improvement suggestions and necessary technical assistance to the elevator maintenance unit or user unit regarding problems in maintenance and safe operation. If it discovers a serious potential accident hazard in the elevator, it shall promptly inform the elevator user unit and report to the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety. The elevator manufacturer shall make records of the investigation and understanding.

Article 47 Where the modification or repair of special equipment requires a change in the use registration as stipulated, the change registration shall be completed before the special equipment may continue to be used.

Article 48 Where special equipment has a serious potential accident hazard, has no value for modification or repair, or meets other scrapping conditions specified in safety technical specifications, the unit using the special equipment shall fulfill the scrapping obligation according to law, take necessary measures to eliminate the use function of the special equipment, and go through the cancellation procedures for the use registration certificate with the originally registered department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety.

For special equipment other than those specified in the preceding paragraph that have reached the design service life but may continue to be used, they may continue to be used only after passing inspection or safety assessment in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications and undergoing a change in the use registration certificate. If continued use is permitted, measures such as strengthening inspection, testing, and maintenance shall be taken to ensure use safety.

Article 49 A unit filling mobile pressure vessels or gas cylinders shall meet the following conditions and obtain a license from the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety before it may engage in filling activities:

(1) Having management personnel and technical personnel suitable for filling and management;
(2) Having filling equipment, testing means, sites, workshops, appliances, and safety facilities suitable for filling and management;
(3) Having sound filling management systems, responsibility systems, and handling measures.

The filling unit shall establish a system for inspection and recording before and after filling. It is prohibited to fill mobile pressure vessels or gas cylinders that do not meet the requirements of safety technical specifications.

A gas cylinder filling unit shall provide gas users with gas cylinders that comply with the requirements of safety technical specifications, provide gas users with guidance on the safe use of gas cylinders, handle gas cylinder use registration in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications, and promptly declare periodic inspections.

## Chapter III Inspection and Testing

Article 50 Special equipment inspection bodies that engage in supervision inspection and periodic inspection as stipulated in this Law, and special equipment testing bodies that provide testing services for the manufacture, operation, and use of special equipment, shall meet the following conditions and be approved by the department responsible for the supervision and administration of special equipment safety before they may engage in inspection and testing work:

(1) Having inspection and testing personnel suitable for the inspection and testing work;
(2) Having inspection and testing instruments and equipment suitable for the inspection and testing work;
(3) Having sound inspection and testing management systems and responsibility systems.

Article 51 Inspection and testing personnel of special equipment inspection and testing bodies shall pass assessments and obtain inspection and testing personnel qualifications before they may engage in inspection and testing work.Inspectors and testing personnel of special equipment inspection and testing institutions shall not concurrently practice in two or more inspection and testing institutions; if they change their practicing institution, they shall go through the change procedures in accordance with the law.

Article 52 Special equipment inspection and testing work shall comply with the provisions of laws and administrative regulations and shall be carried out in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications.

Special equipment inspection and testing institutions and their inspectors and testing personnel shall, in accordance with the law, provide safe, reliable, convenient, and trustworthy inspection and testing services to special equipment production, operation, and use units.

Article 53 Special equipment inspection and testing institutions and their inspectors and testing personnel shall issue inspection and testing reports objectively, impartially, and promptly, and shall be responsible for the inspection and testing results and appraisal conclusions.

When special equipment inspection and testing institutions and their inspectors and testing personnel discover serious potential accidents in special equipment during inspection and testing, they shall promptly notify the relevant units and immediately report to the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment.

The department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment shall organize supervisory spot checks on the inspection and testing results and appraisal conclusions of special equipment inspection and testing institutions, but shall avoid duplicate spot checks. The results of the supervisory spot checks shall be made public.

Article 54 Special equipment production, operation, and use units shall, in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications, provide relevant materials of special equipment and necessary inspection and testing conditions to special equipment inspection and testing institutions and their inspectors and testing personnel, and shall be responsible for the authenticity of the materials.

Article 55 Special equipment inspection and testing institutions and their inspectors and testing personnel have a duty of confidentiality regarding trade secrets learned during the inspection and testing process.

Special equipment inspection and testing institutions and their inspectors and testing personnel shall not engage in the production or operation of related special equipment, nor shall they recommend, supervise the manufacture, or supervise the sale of special equipment.

Article 56 If a special equipment inspection institution or its inspectors deliberately create difficulties for special equipment production, operation, or use units by using their inspection work, the special equipment production, operation, or use units have the right to file a complaint with the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment, and the department receiving the complaint shall promptly investigate and handle the matter.

## Chapter IV Supervision and Administration

Article 57 The department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment shall, in accordance with the provisions of this Law, conduct supervision and inspection of special equipment production, operation, and use units and inspection and testing institutions.

The department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment shall implement key safety supervision and inspection of special equipment in public gathering places such as schools, kindergartens, hospitals, railway stations, passenger terminals, shopping malls, sports venues, exhibition halls, and parks.

Article 58 When the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment carries out the licensing work provided for in this Law, it shall conduct review in accordance with the conditions and procedures stipulated in this Law and other relevant laws and administrative regulations, as well as the requirements of safety technical specifications; if the requirements are not met, no license shall be granted.

Article 59 When the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment handles the licenses provided for in this Law, its procedures for acceptance, review, and licensing must be open, and it shall make a decision to grant or deny the license within thirty days from the date of accepting the application; if the license is denied, it shall state the reasons in writing to the applicant.

Article 60 The department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment shall establish a complete supervision and management file and information inquiry system for special equipment that has undergone use registration in accordance with the law; for special equipment that has reached the scrapping conditions, it shall promptly urge the special equipment use unit to fulfill the scrapping obligation in accordance with the law.

Article 61 When performing supervision and inspection duties in accordance with the law, the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment may exercise the following powers:

(1) Enter the site for inspection, and investigate and understand relevant circumstances from the principal responsible persons and other relevant personnel of special equipment production, operation, and use units and inspection and testing institutions;

(2) Based on reports or obtained evidence of suspected violations, consult and copy relevant contracts, invoices, account books, and other relevant materials of special equipment production, operation, and use units and inspection and testing institutions;

(3) Seal up or seize special equipment that has evidence of not meeting safety technical specification requirements or having serious potential accidents;

(4) Seal up or seize special equipment that has entered the market and has reached scrapping conditions or has already been scrapped;

(5) Make administrative penalty decisions for acts violating the provisions of this Law.

Article 62 When performing duties in accordance with the law, if the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment discovers acts violating the provisions of this Law and safety technical specification requirements, or potential accidents in special equipment, it shall issue a special equipment safety supervision directive in writing, ordering the relevant unit to take timely measures to correct or eliminate the potential accident. If emergency measures are required to be taken by the relevant unit under urgent circumstances, the special equipment safety supervision directive shall be subsequently issued retroactively.

Article 63 When performing duties in accordance with the law, if the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment discovers major illegal acts or serious potential accidents in special equipment, it shall order the relevant unit to immediately stop the illegal act, take measures to eliminate the potential accident, and promptly report to the higher-level department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment. The department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment that receives the report shall take necessary measures and handle the matter promptly.

When the handling of illegal acts or serious potential accidents requires the support and cooperation of the local people's government and relevant departments, the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment shall report to the local people's government and notify other relevant departments. The local people's government and other relevant departments shall take necessary measures and handle the matter promptly.

Article 64 The departments responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment at various local levels of people's governments shall not require special equipment production units that have already obtained licenses in other places in accordance with the provisions of this Law to obtain licenses again, nor shall they require special equipment that has already passed inspection in other places in accordance with the provisions of this Law to be inspected again.

Article 65 Safety supervisors of the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment shall be familiar with relevant laws and regulations, possess corresponding professional knowledge and work experience, and obtain a special equipment safety administrative law enforcement certificate.

Special equipment safety supervisors shall be dedicated to their duties, adhere to principles, and enforce the law impartially.

When the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment carries out safety supervision and inspection, at least two special equipment safety supervisors shall participate, and they shall present valid special equipment safety administrative law enforcement certificates.

Article 66 When the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment conducts supervision and inspection of special equipment production, operation, and use units and inspection and testing institutions, it shall make records of the content of each supervision and inspection, the problems found, and the handling situation, and these records shall be signed by the participating special equipment safety supervisors and the relevant responsible persons of the inspected unit before being filed. If the relevant responsible person of the inspected unit refuses to sign, the special equipment safety supervisor shall record the situation in the file.

Article 67 The department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment and its staff shall not recommend, supervise the manufacture, or supervise the sale of special equipment; they have a duty of confidentiality regarding trade secrets learned during the performance of their duties.

Article 68 The department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment under the State Council and the departments responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment of the people's governments of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government shall regularly disclose the overall safety status of special equipment to the public.

## Chapter V Accident Emergency Rescue and Investigation and Handling

Article 69 The department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment under the State Council shall, in accordance with the law, organize the formulation of emergency plans for major and extraordinarily serious special equipment accidents, which shall be submitted to the State Council for approval before being incorporated into the national emergency plan system for emergencies.

Local people's governments at or above the county level and their departments responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment shall, in accordance with the law, organize the formulation of emergency plans for special equipment accidents within their respective administrative regions, and establish or incorporate them into the corresponding emergency response and rescue system.

Special equipment use units shall formulate special emergency plans for special equipment accidents and conduct regular emergency drills.

Article 70 After a special equipment accident occurs, the unit where the accident occurred shall take measures in accordance with the emergency plan, organize rescue, prevent the accident from expanding, reduce casualties and property losses, protect the accident scene and relevant evidence, and promptly report to the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment of the people's government at or above the county level where the accident occurred and relevant departments.

Upon receiving an accident report, the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment of the people's government at or above the county level shall verify the situation as soon as possible, immediately report to the people's government at the same level, and report level by level in accordance with regulations. When necessary, the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment may report the accident situation by skipping levels. For particularly serious and major accidents, the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment under the State Council shall immediately report to the State Council and notify the State Council's work safety supervision and management department and other relevant departments.

Units and individuals related to the accident shall not delay reporting, make false reports, or conceal the accident situation, nor shall they conceal, destroy relevant evidence, or intentionally damage the accident scene.

Article 71 Upon receiving an accident report, the people's government at the location of the accident shall, in accordance with the law, activate the emergency plan, take emergency response measures, and organize emergency rescue.

Article 72 For particularly serious special equipment accidents, the State Council or a department authorized by the State Council shall organize an accident investigation team to conduct an investigation.

For major accidents, the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment under the State Council shall, together with relevant departments, organize an accident investigation team to conduct an investigation.

For relatively large accidents, the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment of the people's government of the province, autonomous region, or municipality directly under the Central Government shall, together with relevant departments, organize an accident investigation team to conduct an investigation.

For ordinary accidents, the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment of the people's government at the city divided into districts level shall, together with relevant departments, organize an accident investigation team to conduct an investigation.

The accident investigation team shall conduct the investigation in accordance with the law, independently and impartially, and produce an accident investigation report.

Article 73 The department that organized the accident investigation shall submit the accident investigation report to the people's government at the same level and report it to the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment of the people's government at the next higher level for record. Relevant departments and units shall, in accordance with the provisions of laws and administrative regulations, pursue the responsibility of the unit and personnel responsible for the accident.

The unit responsible for the accident shall implement corrective measures in accordance with the law to prevent the occurrence of similar accidents. If the accident causes damage, the unit responsible for the accident shall bear compensation liability in accordance with the law.

## Chapter VI Legal Liability

Article 74 Anyone who, in violation of the provisions of this Law, engages in special equipment production activities without a license shall be ordered to stop production, the illegally manufactured special equipment shall be confiscated, and a fine of not less than 100,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan shall be imposed; if there is illegal income, the illegal income shall be confiscated; if installation, modification, or repair has already been carried out, the unit shall be ordered to restore the original state or ordered to have the installation, modification, or repair re-done by a licensed unit within a specified period.

Article 75 Anyone who, in violation of the provisions of this Law, uses the design documents of special equipment for manufacturing without approval shall be ordered to make corrections, the illegally manufactured special equipment shall be confiscated, and a fine of not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan shall be imposed.

Article 76 Anyone who, in violation of the provisions of this Law, fails to carry out type testing shall be ordered to make corrections within a specified period; if no correction is made within the specified period, a fine of not less than 30,000 yuan but not more than 300,000 yuan shall be imposed.

Article 77 Anyone who, in violation of the provisions of this Law, fails to attach relevant technical materials and documents in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications when special equipment leaves the factory shall be ordered to make corrections within a specified period; if no correction is made within the specified period, the unit shall be ordered to stop manufacturing and selling, and a fine of not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan shall be imposed; if there is illegal income, the illegal income shall be confiscated.

Article 78 If a construction unit for the installation, modification, or repair of special equipment, in violation of the provisions of this Law, starts construction without notifying the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment in writing before construction, or fails to transfer relevant technical materials and documents to the special equipment use unit within thirty days after acceptance, it shall be ordered to make corrections within a specified period; if no correction is made within the specified period, a fine of not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan shall be imposed.

Article 79 Anyone who, in violation of the provisions of this Law, fails to undergo supervision inspection during the manufacturing, installation, modification, major repair, or boiler cleaning process of special equipment shall be ordered to make corrections within a specified period; if no correction is made within the specified period, a fine of not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan shall be imposed; if there is illegal income, the illegal income shall be confiscated; if the circumstances are serious, the production license shall be revoked.

Article 80 If an elevator manufacturing unit, in violation of the provisions of this Law, falls under any of the following circumstances, it shall be ordered to make corrections within a specified period; if no correction is made within the specified period, a fine of not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan shall be imposed:

(1) Failing to calibrate and debug the elevator in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications;

(2) When tracking and understanding the safe operation of the elevator, discovering a serious potential accident, failing to promptly notify the elevator use unit and report to the department responsible for the safety supervision and management of special equipment.

Article 81 If a special equipment production unit, in violation of the provisions of this Law, commits any of the following acts, it shall be ordered to make corrections within a specified period; if no correction is made within the specified period, it shall be ordered to stop production, and a fine of not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan shall be imposed; if the circumstances are serious, the production license shall be revoked:

(1) No longer meeting the production conditions, the production license has expired, or producing beyond the scope of the license;

(2) Knowing that the special equipment has a common defect, failing to immediately stop production and recall it.

If a special equipment production unit, in violation of the provisions of this Law, produces, sells, or delivers special equipment explicitly eliminated by the state, it shall be ordered to stop production and sales, the illegally produced, sold, or delivered special equipment shall be confiscated, and a fine of not less than 30,000 yuan but not more than 300,000 yuan shall be imposed; if there is illegal income, the illegal income shall be confiscated.

If a special equipment production unit alters, resells, rents, or lends its production license, it shall be ordered to stop production, and a fine of not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan shall be imposed; if the circumstances are serious, the production license shall be revoked.

Article 82 If a special equipment operating unit, in violation of the provisions of this Law, commits any of the following acts, it shall be ordered to stop operations, the illegally operated special equipment shall be confiscated, and a fine of not less than 30,000 yuan but not more than 300,000 yuan shall be imposed; if there is illegal income, the illegal income shall be confiscated:

(1) Selling or leasing special equipment that has been produced without a license, has not been inspected, or has been inspected and found unqualified;

(2) Selling or leasing special equipment that is explicitly eliminated by the state, has been scrapped, or has not been maintained in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications.

If a special equipment sales unit, in violation of the provisions of this Law, fails to establish an inspection, acceptance, and sales record system, or fails to fulfill the advance notification obligation for imported special equipment, it shall be ordered to make corrections, and a fine of not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan shall be imposed.

If a special equipment production unit sells or delivers special equipment that has not been inspected or has been inspected and found unqualified, it shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the first paragraph of this Article; if the circumstances are serious, the production license shall be revoked.

Article 83 If a special equipment use unit, in violation of the provisions of this Law, commits any of the following acts, it shall be ordered to make corrections within a specified period; if no correction is made within the specified period, it shall be ordered to stop using the relevant special equipment, and a fine of not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan shall be imposed:

(1) Using special equipment without completing use registration in accordance with regulations;

(2) Failing to establish a special equipment safety technical file, or the safety technical file does not meet the specified requirements, or failing to set up use registration marks and periodic inspection marks in accordance with the law;

(3) Failing to carry out regular maintenance and periodic self-inspection of the special equipment in use, or failing to periodically calibrate and repair the safety accessories and safety protection devices of the special equipment in use, and make records;

(4) Failing to declare and undergo inspection in a timely manner in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications;

(5) Failing to carry out boiler water (medium) treatment in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications;

(6) Failing to formulate special emergency plans for special equipment accidents.

Article 84 If a special equipment use unit, in violation of the provisions of this Law, commits any of the following acts, it shall be ordered to stop using the relevant special equipment, and a fine of not less than 30,000 yuan but not more than 300,000 yuan shall be imposed:

(1) Using special equipment that has been produced without a license, has not been inspected, or has been inspected and found unqualified, or special equipment that is explicitly eliminated by the state or has been scrapped;

(2) When a special equipment malfunctions or has an abnormal situation, failing to conduct a comprehensive inspection, eliminate the potential accident, and continuing to use it;

(3) When a special equipment has a serious potential accident, has no value for modification or repair, or meets other scrapping conditions specified in safety technical specifications, failing to fulfill the scrapping obligation in accordance with the law and go through the cancellation procedures for the use registration certificate.

Article 85 If a filling unit for mobile pressure vessels or gas cylinders, in violation of the provisions of this Law, commits any of the following acts, it shall be ordered to make corrections, and a fine of not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan shall be imposed; if the circumstances are serious, the filling license shall be revoked:

(1) Failing to implement the inspection and recording system before and after filling in accordance with regulations;

(2) Filling mobile pressure vessels and gas cylinders that do not meet the requirements of safety technical specifications.

If anyone, in violation of the provisions of this Law, engages in the filling of mobile pressure vessels or gas cylinders without a license, the activity shall be banned, the illegally filled gas cylinders shall be confiscated, and a fine of not less than 100,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan shall be imposed; if there is illegal income, the illegal income shall be confiscated.

Article 86 If a special equipment production, operation, or use unit, in violation of the provisions of this Law, falls under any of the following circumstances, it shall be ordered to make corrections within a specified period; if no correction is made within the specified period, it shall be ordered to stop using the relevant special equipment or suspend production for rectification, and a fine of not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 50,000 yuan shall be imposed:

(1) Failing to equip special equipment safety managers, inspectors, and operators with corresponding qualifications;

(2) Using personnel without corresponding qualifications to engage in special equipment safety management, inspection, and operation;

(3) Failing to provide safety education and skills training to special equipment safety managers, inspectors, and operators.

Article 87 If an operating and use unit of elevators, passenger ropeways, or large amusement rides, in violation of the provisions of this Law, falls under any of the following circumstances, it shall be ordered to make corrections within a specified period; if no correction is made within the specified period, it shall be ordered to stop using the relevant special equipment or suspend production for rectification, and a fine of not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan shall be imposed:

(1) Failing to set up a special equipment safety management organization or equip full-time special equipment safety managers;

(2) Before putting passenger ropeways or large amusement rides into use each day, failing to conduct trial runs and routine safety inspections, and failing to inspect and confirm the safety accessories and safety protection devices;

(3) Failing to place the safety use instructions, safety precautions, and warning signs of elevators, passenger ropeways, and large amusement rides in prominent positions easily noticeable by passengers.

Article 88 If anyone, in violation of the provisions of this Law, engages in elevator maintenance without a license, they shall be ordered to stop the illegal act, and a fine of not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 100,000 yuan shall be imposed; if there is illegal income, the illegal income shall be confiscated.

If an elevator maintenance unit fails to carry out elevator maintenance in accordance with the provisions of this Law and the requirements of safety technical specifications, it shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of the preceding paragraph.Article 89 Where a special equipment accident occurs under any of the following circumstances, the entity shall be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; the principal responsible person shall be fined not less than 10,000 yuan but not more than 50,000 yuan; if the principal responsible person is a state functionary, disciplinary action shall be taken in accordance with the law:

(1) Failing to immediately organize rescue efforts at the time of the special equipment accident, or leaving the post without authorization or absconding during the accident investigation and handling period;

(2) Delaying, falsely reporting, or concealing the special equipment accident.

Article 90 Where an accident occurs, the responsible entity shall, in addition to being required to bear corresponding compensation and other liabilities in accordance with the law, be fined according to the following provisions:

(1) For a general accident, a fine of not less than 100,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan;

(2) For a relatively major accident, a fine of not less than 200,000 yuan but not more than 500,000 yuan;

(3) For a major accident, a fine of not less than 500,000 yuan but not more than 2,000,000 yuan.

Article 91 Where the principal responsible person of an entity responsible for an accident fails to perform their duties in accordance with the law or bears leadership responsibility, they shall be fined according to the following provisions; if they are a state functionary, disciplinary action shall also be taken in accordance with the law:

(1) For a general accident, a fine of 30% of their previous year's income;

(2) For a relatively major accident, a fine of 40% of their previous year's income;

(3) For a major accident, a fine of 60% of their previous year's income.

Article 92 Where, in violation of this Law, safety management personnel, inspection personnel, and operators of special equipment fail to perform their job duties, violate operating procedures and relevant safety rules and regulations, thereby causing an accident, the qualifications of the relevant personnel shall be revoked.

Article 93 Where, in violation of this Law, special equipment inspection and testing institutions and their inspection and testing personnel commit any of the following acts, they shall be ordered to make corrections; the institution shall be fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; the directly responsible supervisor and other directly responsible personnel shall be fined not less than 5,000 yuan but not more than 50,000 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, the institution's qualification and the relevant personnel's credentials shall be revoked:

(1) Engaging in inspection and testing without approval or beyond the approved scope, or using personnel who have not obtained the corresponding qualifications to conduct inspection and testing;

(2) Failing to conduct inspection and testing in accordance with the requirements of safety technical specifications;

(3) Issuing false inspection and testing results and appraisal conclusions, or inspection and testing results and appraisal conclusions that are seriously inconsistent with the facts;

(4) Discovering serious accident hazards in special equipment but failing to promptly notify the relevant entity and immediately report to the department responsible for special equipment safety supervision and management;

(5) Disclosing commercial secrets learned during the inspection and testing process;

(6) Engaging in the production or business activities related to special equipment;

(7) Recommending, supervising the production, or supervising the sale of special equipment;

(8) Deliberately making things difficult for relevant entities by exploiting inspection work.

Where, in violation of this Law, inspection and testing personnel of special equipment inspection and testing institutions practice in two or more inspection and testing institutions simultaneously, they shall be fined not less than 5,000 yuan but not more than 50,000 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, their qualifications shall be revoked.

Article 94 Where, in violation of this Law, the department responsible for special equipment safety supervision and management and its staff commit any of the following acts, the superior authority shall order corrections; the directly responsible supervisor and other directly responsible personnel shall be given disciplinary action in accordance with the law:

(1) Failing to implement licensing in accordance with the conditions and procedures stipulated by laws and administrative regulations;

(2) Discovering unauthorized production, use, or inspection and testing activities of special equipment without a license but failing to ban them or handle them in accordance with the law;

(3) Discovering that a special equipment production unit no longer meets the conditions stipulated in this Law but failing to revoke its production license, or discovering illegal acts in the production, operation, or use of special equipment but failing to investigate and deal with them;

(4) Discovering that a special equipment inspection and testing institution no longer meets the conditions stipulated in this Law but failing to revoke its approval, or failing to investigate and deal with its acts of issuing false inspection and testing results and appraisal conclusions, or inspection and testing results and appraisal conclusions that are seriously inconsistent with the facts;

(5) Discovering acts violating this Law and safety technical specification requirements, or accident hazards in special equipment, but failing to handle them immediately;

(6) Discovering major illegal acts or serious accident hazards in special equipment but failing to promptly report to the superior department responsible for special equipment safety supervision and management, or the department responsible for special equipment safety supervision and management that receives the report failing to handle it immediately;

(7) Requiring special equipment production units that have already obtained a license in another place in accordance with this Law to obtain a duplicate license, or requiring duplicate inspection of special equipment that has already passed inspection in another place in accordance with this Law;

(8) Recommending, supervising the production, or supervising the sale of special equipment;

(9) Disclosing commercial secrets learned during the performance of duties;

(10) Receiving a report of a special equipment accident but failing to immediately report it to the people's government at the same level and report it upward as required;

(11) Delaying, omitting, falsely reporting, or concealing an accident;

(12) Obstructing accident rescue or accident investigation and handling;

(13) Other acts of abusing power, neglecting duties, or engaging in malpractice for personal gain.

Article 95 Where, in violation of this Law, a special equipment production, operation, or use entity, or an inspection and testing institution, refuses to accept the supervisory inspection lawfully conducted by the department responsible for special equipment safety supervision and management, it shall be ordered to make corrections within a specified period; if it fails to make corrections within the specified period, it shall be ordered to suspend production and business for rectification and fined not less than 20,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan.

Where a special equipment production, operation, or use entity, without authorization, uses, replaces, transfers, or damages sealed or seized special equipment or its main components, it shall be ordered to make corrections and fined not less than 50,000 yuan but not more than 200,000 yuan; if the circumstances are serious, its production license shall be revoked and the special equipment use registration certificate shall be cancelled.

Article 96 Where, in violation of this Law, a license is lawfully revoked, the department responsible for special equipment safety supervision and management shall not accept any new license application from the entity within three years from the date of revocation of the license.

Article 97 Where, in violation of this Law, personal or property damage is caused, civil liability shall be borne in accordance with the law.

Where, in violation of this Law, an entity should bear civil compensation liability and pay fines and penalties, and its property is insufficient to pay both simultaneously, the civil compensation liability shall be borne first.

Article 98 Where, in violation of this Law, an act constitutes a violation of public security administration, public security administration penalties shall be imposed in accordance with the law; if a crime is constituted, criminal liability shall be investigated in accordance with the law.

## Chapter 7 Supplementary Provisions

Article 99 Fees for special equipment administrative licensing and inspection shall be implemented in accordance with the provisions of laws and administrative regulations.

Article 100 This Law does not apply to the safety supervision and management of special equipment used in military equipment, nuclear facilities, and aerospace vehicles.

The safety supervision and management of special equipment used in railway locomotives, offshore facilities and ships, and underground mines, as well as special equipment for civil airport use, and the installation and use of lifting machinery at construction sites of housing construction and municipal engineering projects, and special motor vehicles within factories (premises), shall be implemented by relevant departments in accordance with this Law and other relevant laws.

Article 101 This Law shall come into force on January 1, 2014.
